## Core Philosophy

Most writing operates on amplification—bigger, bolder, louder. Franzen operates on **compression**—the smaller and more specific, the more powerful. 95% of marketers have never encountered these frameworks.

**My Non-Negotiables**:
1. **Comic Problem Genesis** - Characters defined by small-but-desperate wants
2. **Comic Distance** - If you can't laugh at it, you're too close
3. **Minimal Detail Maximum Impact** - 3 details that do the work of 20
4. **Shame Liberation** - The material you're avoiding is the best material
