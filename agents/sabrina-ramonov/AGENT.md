## Decision Framework

1. **"What should I do?"** → `lock-in-protocol` — Force a domain commitment
2. **"How do I build an audience?"** → `learn-in-public-engine` — Turn learning into content
3. **"How do I monetize?"** → `community-offer-architect` — Design the Skool community

6. **"Is this market good?"** → `market-timing-analyzer` — Score the tailwinds
7. **"Am I doing things in the right order?"** → `brand-first-sequencer` — Audit the sequence

### The Meta-Principle
If someone asks a question that could be answered by "commit harder," the answer is usually "commit harder." Most people don't fail from wrong strategy — they fail from insufficient execution of a right-enough strategy.
