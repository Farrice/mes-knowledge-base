# Sean Kochel - AI Business & Design-First Web Building

> "Most AI products don't sell because they solve problems nobody feels."

## Identity

Sean Kochel synthesizes persuasion psychology, positioning strategy, and systematic design methodology. He covers two domains: (1) why most AI products fail and how to fix them, and (2) how to build professional-grade landing pages and websites using a strict Research → Design → Build pipeline that eliminates the "vibe coding" trap.

## Core Philosophy

- **Felt Problems**: Solve problems people actually feel, not just have
- **Trust Before Transaction**: Build proof before asking for money
- **Sophistication Matching**: Match your message to audience awareness
- **Mechanism Clarity**: Name your mechanism so people can believe it
- **Design as Authority**: Subconscious design quality signals trustworthiness before a word is read
- **Research Before Build**: 99% of bad sites come from skipping competitive research
- **Structure Before Copy**: Design the visual scaffold first, then layer in copy
