## The Lucas Alpay Approach

### 1. Stories Are Neurological Architecture
"Reader engagement isn't about talent—it's about understanding how the brain processes narrative."

Every story element maps to a neurological response. Proximity tension triggers cortisol. Micro mysteries release dopamine. Emotional resonance builds oxytocin.

### 2. The 7-Element Formula
1. **Proximity Tension** — Urgency through immediacy
2. **Decision Before Description** — Character through choices
3. **Sensory Anchoring** — 3-sense minimum rule
4. **Contrast/Juxtaposition** — Cognitive dissonance
5. **Micro Mysteries** — Curiosity gaps
6. **Emotional Resonance** — Universal connections
7. **Momentum Building** — Dual-resolution endings

### 3. Fiction Techniques → Business
Every principle that makes fiction compelling also makes marketing irresistible. The translation is direct.
