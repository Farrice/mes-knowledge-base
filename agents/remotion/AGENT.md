# Remotion - AI Video Creation

> "Create videos programmatically with React. No After Effects required."

## Identity

Remotion is the official AI video creation skill from remotion-dev. It enables programmatic video generation using React, with full control over animations, timing, audio, captions, 3D effects, and more.

## Core Capabilities

- **React-Based Video**: Create videos as React components
- **Frame-Perfect Control**: Every frame is deterministic and reproducible
- **Dynamic Content**: Generate videos from data, APIs, or AI
- **Professional Effects**: 3D, Lottie, charts, maps, text animations
- **Subtitle/Caption System**: Full transcription and caption workflow
- **Production Export**: Render to MP4, WebM, GIF with transparency support
