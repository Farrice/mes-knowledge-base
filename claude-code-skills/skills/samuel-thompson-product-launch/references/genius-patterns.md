# Samuel Thompson - Genius Patterns

14 unconscious mastery behaviors decoded into executable actions.

## Pattern 1: Shadow Market Detection
**What He Does**: Identifies massive markets, then finds the adjacent "problem market" that's underserved.

**Execute**: When evaluating any market, ask: "What's the opposite or aftermath market?" Wedding → Divorce. Fitness → Injury recovery. Success → Failure recovery.

**Success Metric**: Markets with 50%+ the size but 10% of the competition.

---

## Pattern 2: Validation Before Polish
**What He Does**: Launches deliberately "messy" first versions—testing market response before investing in quality.

**Execute**: Create the ugliest functional version in 1-2 hours. Run $50-100 in ads. Only improve what gets purchases.

**Success Metric**: First sales within 48 hours of idea conception.

---

## Pattern 3: Rigged Slot Machine Framing
**What He Does**: Views every business as a mathematical equation, removing emotional attachment.

**Execute**: Before launching: "I put $X in → I get $Y out." If Y < X after optimization, shut down immediately.

**Success Metric**: Minimum 1.3x return on ad spend or immediate pivot.

---

## Pattern 4: CAC-Based Pricing Architecture
**What He Does**: Sets prices based on customer acquisition costs, not "what feels right."

**Execute**: Determine CAC first ($30-50 for info products). Set minimum price at CAC + product cost + margin. Work backward from math.

**Success Metric**: Price allows breakeven at worst, 30%+ margin at best.

---

## Pattern 5: Compare-At Price Testing
**What He Does**: Uses "holiday sales" to systematically test price elasticity.

**Execute**: List at high anchor price. Create rotating "sales" at test prices. Measure conversion rate change vs. AOV change.

**Success Metric**: Identify price point where (conversion rate × AOV) peaks.

---

## Pattern 6: Screenshot Mockup Hack
**What He Does**: Uses AI tool preview images as final product photography.

**Execute**: Generate avatar videos in HeyGen. Screenshot preview images before rendering. Use as product photos.

**Success Metric**: Complete product imagery in under 30 minutes with zero cost.

---

## Pattern 7: Perceived Value Stacking
**What He Does**: Adds bonus gifts so each buyer sees at least one "I need that" item.

**Execute**: Create 5+ digital bonuses targeting different concerns. Include variety: checklist, template, guide, resource list.

**Success Metric**: At least one bonus resonates with each buyer segment.

---

## Pattern 8: 80% Head Start Prompting
**What He Does**: Uses AI for rapid first drafts, never expecting perfect output.

**Execute**: Prompt for quantity and ideas, not perfection. Grab the 80%, edit the 20%.

**Success Metric**: Usable first draft in under 2 minutes per asset.

---

## Pattern 9: Mobile-Only Optimization
**What He Does**: Ignores desktop entirely—99% of traffic is mobile.

**Execute**: Build and test everything on mobile view only. All conversion elements must work on phone screens.

**Success Metric**: Full conversion flow testable on mobile in under 60 seconds.

---

## Pattern 10: Conversion Theme Leverage
**What He Does**: Uses pre-built conversion-optimized themes instead of designing from scratch.

**Execute**: Purchase proven themes (Solo Drop, Elixir). Use templates exactly. Focus on copy and offer.

**Success Metric**: Functional store live in under 2 hours.

---

## Pattern 11: Prompt-to-Page Pipeline
**What He Does**: Feeds live website URLs to ChatGPT to generate all copy.

**Execute**: Share Shopify preview link. Say "Look at this website and give me all the copy based on [product context]."

**Success Metric**: Full page copy generated in single conversation.

---

## Pattern 12: Voice Clone Authenticity
**What He Does**: Records himself, clones voice, applies to AI avatars.

**Execute**: Record 30-60 seconds of natural speech. Clone in 11Labs. Apply to HeyGen avatars.

**Success Metric**: AI video that sounds natural enough to not trigger skepticism.

---

## Pattern 13: Deep Research Iteration
**What He Does**: Uses initial sales as validation, then improves product quality substantially.

**Execute**: Launch minimal version. After 10-20 sales, use ChatGPT deep research to create improvement brief. Upgrade.

**Success Metric**: 2-3x improvement in quality after initial validation.

---

## Pattern 14: LTV Roadmap Thinking
**What He Does**: Maps future LTV plays while focused on immediate profitability.

**Execute**: For any successful front-end, brainstorm: What's book #2? Membership? High-ticket coaching?

**Success Metric**: Clear LTV roadmap for any offer hitting profitability.
