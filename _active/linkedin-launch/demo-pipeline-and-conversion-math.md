## Part 1: Unsolicited Demo Pipeline — How to Run It

### The Play

While your 5 posts are going out, simultaneously run unsolicited demos on target coaches. Each demo produces:
1. A **warm lead** (you've already proven you can do the work)
2. **Content for your LinkedIn** (before/after posts are powerful proof)
3. **System practice** (sharpens your voice-matching engine)

### Target Selection (Run During Week 1-2)

Find 5 coaching accounts that match these criteria:

| Criteria | Why |
|----------|-----|
| 2K-30K followers | Big enough to have content, small enough to be accessible |
| Posts at least 2x/month | Shows intent but not mastery — your ideal client |
| Coaching methodology visible | You need frameworks to demonstrate voice matching |
| Content quality: C+ to B | Great ideas, mediocre execution = maximum demo impact |
| NOT already working with a ghostwriter | Check for credits or @mentions |

### Execution (Per Demo Target)

1. **Collect** their last 5-10 posts + About section + comments
2. **Build abbreviated Voice Profile** (30-45 min per coach)
3. **Score posts** on Idea Quality vs Execution Quality
4. **Rewrite** the 2-3 posts with highest Gap Score
5. **Package** as a demo using `outreach` mode
6. **DM** using the permission-first approach: "I rewrote a couple of your posts — want to see?"

### Timeline

| Week | Demos to Run | Total Pipeline |
|------|:---:|:---:|
| Week 1 (Commenting only) | 2 demos | 2 warm leads |
| Week 2 (Posts 1-2) | 2 demos | 4 warm leads |
| Week 3 (Posts 3-4) | 1 demo | 5 warm leads |

**Target**: 5 demos in 3 weeks = 5 warm leads who've SEEN your work before you ever pitch.
