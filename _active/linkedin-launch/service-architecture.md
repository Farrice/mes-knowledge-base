## The Nicolas Cole Operating System (Applied to Services)

Cole made $5M+ ghostwriting via **one service** (800-word thought leadership articles), then scaled to $180K/month with premium positioning. His Ghostwriter Income Staircase has 5 levels — we're building at Level 4-5 (per asset / per outcome).

### Rule 1: One Service Focus
You ghostwrite LinkedIn posts. Not websites, not emails, not social media management. LinkedIn posts in the client's voice. Everything else is an **add-on** — never the core.

### Rule 2: Per-Outcome Pricing (Level 4-5)
Never charge hourly. Never charge per-word. Charge **per asset delivered** (posts, VSL scripts, newsletters). The client pays for the outcome, not your time. Your AI advantage means you produce faster — that margin is YOUR profit, not their discount.

> **Cole's Income Staircase**: (1) per word → (2) per hour → (3) per month → (4) per asset → (5) per outcome. Below $3K/month retainer = **undercharging** per Cole.

### Rule 3: Free Consulting Strategy
On discovery/sales calls, give away the strategy completely:
> "Whether or not we work together, I want you to know exactly what you should be posting, when, and why."

Give them everything. The insight is: *they still won't do it themselves*. Strategy is free. Execution is what costs money.

### Rule 4: Context Repackaging
The Voice Profile you build for a client is a **reusable asset**. Once captured:
- LinkedIn posts = Context 1
- Newsletter drafts = Context 2
- VSL scripts = Context 3
- Email sequences = Context 4

Same IP (their voice), different context, different price. Cole's multiplier in action.

### Rule 5: You Are Your Own Case Study
Cole's method for justifying premium rates **without a portfolio**:
1. **Create the asset you sell** — Your IP Flywheel content IS the proof. You're using the exact methodology on yourself.
2. **Write about the industry** — Post about ghostwriting trends, LinkedIn strategy, content creation. Prospects see your expertise before you pitch.
3. **Do strategic free work** — The Unsolicited Demo IS this. Zero risk for the prospect, infinite upside for you.

> "You are your own case study. You should be actively creating and demonstrating the power of the assets you ghostwrite for clients." — Cole

### Rule 6: Package as Outcomes (Cole's Naming Formula)
Name every service using: **[Problem/Person/Way] + [Desirable Outcome] + "Package"**
- ❌ "12 posts per month"
- ✅ "The LinkedIn Authority Package" / "The Thought Leader Engine"

**Why 8 posts + 1 VSL draft (not 5 posts + roadmap)**:
- 8 posts = 2-3 weeks of content (enough to SEE results, not just read drafts)
- VSL script draft = the "high-value bonus" that makes this feel like $3K+ value for $750
- The roadmap was a filler deliverable. Posts + VSL = tangible assets they can deploy immediately.

**The $750 Price Point Logic** (Cole's $350 Threshold):
- $500 sits in "considered purchase" territory but feels cheap for done-for-you content
- $750 is solidly above the threshold — signals premium without corporate pricing
- Market context: most ghostwriters charge $50-$500/post. 8 posts + VSL at $750 = ~$83/post. That's **below market for mid-tier quality but includes a VSL that alone costs $750-$1,000 elsewhere**

**First Client Pricing Strategy** (Cole-validated):
1. **Pilot Rate**: First 3 clients get the Proof Run at $500 (50% of future $1,000 target)
2. **Testimonial Exchange**: Reduced rate in exchange for a detailed testimonial + case study
3. **Results Guarantee**: Full refund if not satisfied after delivery
4. **Escalation Clause**: "After the pilot, the rate moves to $750. This pilot rate is a one-time offer."

**The Free Consulting Play**:
Before anyone pays, run the **Unsolicited Demo Pipeline** (GVE Workflow 03). This IS the Free Consulting — show them what their content COULD sound like. No call needed. Public content only. This is the most powerful sales tool in the stack.

## Client Acquisition: "Comments as Consulting" Strategy

Cole's signature acquisition method — adapted for LinkedIn:

### The Warm Pipeline
1. **Comment on ideal clients' posts** — add genuine value, frameworks, or a reframe. This is "free consulting" in public.
2. **After 3-5 valuable comments**, send the **Unsolicited Demo** (GVE Workflow 03) — rewrite one of their weaker posts in their voice.
3. **Let the demo sell itself** — "Saw your post on [topic]. Here's how I'd sharpen it. No strings. Just thought you'd find it useful."
4. **If they engage** → Discovery call (give strategy for free, charge for execution)
5. **If they don't** → Keep commenting. The public value builds your reputation regardless.

### The Cold Pipeline (Cole's Outreach Template)
> "Hi [Name], I've been following your work on [topic]. I noticed your LinkedIn posts are solid but your content frequency has dropped. I ghostwrite LinkedIn for [type of professional] like you — here's what [a recent post of yours] would look like with some strategic polish: [LINK TO REWRITE]. No pitch. Just wanted to share."

### Handling "Do You Have a Portfolio?"
Cole's response framework:
> "Because of the nature of ghostwriting, I can't share specific client names — however I can tell you I've ghostwritten for coaches and consultants in [relevant niche]. More importantly, look at my own LinkedIn — I use the exact methodology I'd apply to yours."
