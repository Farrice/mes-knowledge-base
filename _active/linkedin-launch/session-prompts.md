MY BACKGROUND: 18+ years as a personal trainer and holistic life coach. I understand these coaches from the inside — I've been in that room. That's my moat.

WHAT I NEED REWRITTEN:
1. **Headline** — Must follow Lara's Pattern 20 (pain + for whom + proof). LinkedIn truncates at ~60 chars on mobile. I need 2-3 variants.
2. **Banner copy** — The text/concept for my LinkedIn banner image. Social proof, positioning, or clear value prop.
3. **About section** — Reader-as-protagonist (second person). The reader is an elite S&C coach who feels invisible online. I enter as the answer to THEIR problem. Show don't tell. The About section IS the portfolio piece — the writing must demonstrate the service. Run the full writers' room treatment (`.agent/workflows/writers-room.md`). Give me 3 variants.
4. **Featured section** — What should be pinned? (The audit post? A lead magnet? A case study placeholder?)

CRITICAL RULES FROM PAST SESSIONS:
- "Structurally sound but flat" = 5/10 max. No structure without heartbeat.
- Writers' room is the DEFAULT, not a fallback.
- Content ecosystem awareness: map what language the audit post already claims BEFORE writing profile copy. Each piece has a different role.
- Show > Tell at the sentence level. "I get into the specifics of your methodology" = TELL (dead). "The thing you say in a session that makes someone go quiet because it landed" = SHOW (alive).
- Voice test: "Would Farrice say this to a friend?" If no → rewrite.
- No forced jargon. No corporate. No guru. No motivational speaker.

Give me the full profile rewrite with 3 variants for headline and About, then let me voice-pass before we finalize.
