## QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST

For each prospect, check:
- [ ] Posts 0-4x per month (sporadic)
- [ ] Last post was 5-30 days ago
- [ ] Posts get 50-500 impressions (check engagement)
- [ ] Has an offer/service mentioned in profile
- [ ] Profile looks established (not brand new)
- [ ] Content quality: C+ to B (good ideas, mediocre execution)

**✅ = Qualified → Add to list below**
**❌ = Skip → Move to next**

## COLD OUTREACH TEMPLATE

**Subject:** Quick question about your LinkedIn

**Message:**
```
Hey [Name],

I've been reading your posts about [specific framework/topic] and I can tell it comes from real client work, not theory.

Random question: is your coaching methodology all organized in one place, or scattered across Google Docs, Notes, and your head like 90% of the coaches I talk to?

I ask because I build voice-matched LinkedIn content systems for coaches. Basically taking everything that makes you different and turning it into consistent posts that sound like YOU (not ChatGPT wearing a costume).

Your [specific insight from their last post] stood out to me. That's the kind of thing prospects need to see more of.

Would it be helpful to see a 10-min preview of what your LinkedIn content system would look like? No pitch, just showing you the approach.

- Farrice
```

**Personalization Checklist:**
- [ ] Name correct
- [ ] Referenced specific framework/topic from their content
- [ ] Mentioned specific insight from their last post
- [ ] Feels genuine (not copy/paste)
