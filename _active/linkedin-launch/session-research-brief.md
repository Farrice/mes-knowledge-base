## Key Decisions Made (And Why)

### Decision 1: Consolidate Under LinkedIn Ghostwriting

**What**: Shelved the "AI Brain Build" concept. All revenue efforts now focused on LinkedIn ghostwriting for coaches/consultants.

**Why**:
- AI Brain Build was too conceptual — hard to explain, harder to sell
- LinkedIn ghostwriting has proven demand (89% of B2B marketers use LinkedIn for leads)
- The GVE (Ghostwriting Voice Engine) was already built and battle-tested
- Your lived experience in coaching gives you domain credibility
- Nicolas Cole proved a single-service focus scales faster ($10K → $180K/month with one service)

**Risk acknowledged**: Narrower offering = fewer potential clients. Mitigated by depth of vertical expertise and add-on menu.

---

### Decision 2: Two-System Separation (IP Flywheel vs. Service)

**What**: Your content (IP Flywheel) and client content (Ghostwriting Service) are completely separate systems.

**Why**:
- Previous versions conflated the two — prompt kits were being treated as client deliverables
- Your IP Flywheel posts build YOUR audience and generate leads
- Your ghostwriting service produces content FOR clients in THEIR voice
- Prompt kits are lead magnets for YOUR funnel, not deliverables for client packages

**Files affected**: LAUNCH-GUIDE.md (now IP Flywheel only), SERVICE-SALES-GUIDE.md (new, service only)

---

### Decision 3: Proof Run at $750 (8 Posts + VSL)

**What**: The door-opener project is $750, delivering 8 LinkedIn posts + 1 VSL script draft in 7 days. Pilot rate of $500 for first 3 clients.

**Why (pricing)**:
- Cole's $350 Threshold: anything below $350 feels "cheap" for done-for-you. $750 is solidly premium but accessible
- Old $500/5-post version was below market rate for mid-tier quality
- At $750 for 8 posts + VSL, the effective per-post cost is ~$83 (below market) but the VSL alone is worth $750-$1,000 standalone — massive perceived value

**Why (deliverables)**:
- 5 posts = 1.5 weeks of content (too short to see results)
- 8 posts = 2-3 weeks of content (enough to track impressions, DMs, engagement)
- VSL script is the "high-value bonus" that makes $750 feel like $3K+ value
- Content roadmap was removed — it was a filler deliverable. Posts + VSL = tangible assets

**Why ($500 pilot)**:
- Cole validates "50% of target rate" for first 3 clients
- Testimonial exchange removes the "no portfolio" objection
- Escalation clause creates urgency: "This rate goes to $750 after the pilot"

---

### Decision 4: Tier 1 at $2,500/month, Tier 2 at $3,500/month

**What**: Monthly retainers set at $2,500 (The Voice) and $3,500 (The Engine).

**Why**:
- **Cole's rule**: Below $3K/month is undercharging. $2,500 is the bridge — top of "Starter" tier
- **Market data**: Budget tier is $500-$1,500/mo (template-driven). Mid-tier is $2,000-$4,000/mo (custom voice). Premium is $5,000-$10,000+
- **Your positioning**: Mid-tier quality at fair pricing, with AI arbitrage giving you 60-70% time savings
- **AI Arbitrage math**: 12 posts/month at traditional speed = 12-18 hours. With GVE = 3-5 hours. Effective rate = $500-$625/hour
- **Tier 2 at $3,500**: Cole's sweet spot ($3K-$5K where "vast majority" of retainers live). VSL included makes it feel like a steal. 3-month minimum ensures ROI demonstration period.

**Future path**: Raise Tier 1 to $3,000 after 3 clients with 30-60 day notice (Cole's rate increase protocol).

---

### Decision 5: Coaches & Consultants as Primary Vertical

**What**: Start with health/wellness coaches, then business/executive coaches, then career coaches.

**Why**:
- **Lived experience**: You've been in the fitness/health/wellness space — you understand the language, pain points, and transformation stories
- **Willingness to pay**: Business/executive coaches earn $122K-$160K/year (ICF 2023). They can afford $2,500/month retainers
- **LinkedIn ROI**: LinkedIn is THE platform for B2B service providers. Career coaches especially — their entire pipeline is LinkedIn
- **Content quality gap**: Most coaches post C+ to B content. Good ideas, mediocre execution. Maximum demo impact zone

**Testing protocol**: Run 3 Unsolicited Demos per sub-niche. Track response rate. If 2/3 engage → pursue. If 0/3 → move on.

---

### Decision 6: "Comments as Consulting" as Primary Acquisition

**What**: Daily engagement on ideal clients' posts (15-20 min/day) → build familiarity → Unsolicited Demo → conversion.

**Why**:
- Cole's signature method — proven at scale ($180K/month agency)
- Demonstrates expertise publicly before any pitch
- Builds warm pipeline (cold outreach is backup, not primary)
- Free consulting strategy: give away strategy on calls, charge for execution. They still won't do it themselves.
- Supported by GVE Workflow 03 (Unsolicited Demo pipeline)

---

### Decision 7: "You Are Your Own Case Study" Positioning

**What**: Use your own LinkedIn content as proof of methodology, rather than needing a traditional portfolio.

**Why**:
- Cole landed 300+ high-paying clients without sharing a single client name
- Ghostwriting NDA reality: "Because of the nature of ghostwriting, I can't share specific names" — every client satisfied with this response
- Your IP Flywheel posts ARE the proof — you're using the exact methodology on yourself
- The Proof Run + guarantee removes remaining risk
