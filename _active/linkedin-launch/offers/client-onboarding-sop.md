# Client Onboarding SOP
## Payment → Call → Delivery → Follow-Up

> **This SOP covers everything from the moment a client pays to the moment you pitch the retainer.**
> For the full delivery process, see [proof-run-delivery-template.md](./proof-run-delivery-template.md).

---

## Stage 1: Payment Received (Day 0)

### Immediate Actions (within 1 hour of payment)

- [ ] **Send confirmation email:**

> Subject: You're in — let's capture your voice
>
> Hey [Name],
>
> Payment confirmed. Here's what happens next:
>
> 1. **Book your call** → [CALENDLY LINK] (pick any time that works)
> 2. **Fill out the intake form** → [INTAKE FORM LINK] (takes 5 min)
> 3. Within 7 business days after our call, you'll have 8 posts + a VSL script in your inbox.
>
> Looking forward to hearing how you think.
>
> — Farrice

- [ ] **Send intake form** — Use the questions from `_active/offers/intake-form-questions.md`
- [ ] **Create client folder**: `_active/clients/[client-name]/`
  - `voice-profile.md`
  - `posts/`
  - `vsl-script.md`
  - `notes.md`
  - `tracking.md`

### Client Intake Form (Pre-Call)

Send these questions via Google Form or Notion form:

1. What's your coaching/consulting niche?
2. Who is your ideal client? (Be specific — job title, industry, pain point)
3. What's your main offer and what does it cost?
4. Share 3 LinkedIn posts you've written that you liked (links)
5. Share 3 LinkedIn creators whose style you admire
6. What do you want LinkedIn to DO for you? (book calls, build authority, attract partnerships)
7. Any words, phrases, or tones you want me to avoid?

---

## Stage 2: Pre-Call Prep (15 min before the call)

- [ ] Review client's intake form answers
- [ ] Scan their LinkedIn profile (headline, About, recent posts)
- [ ] Check their website/bio page
- [ ] Write 3 observations:
  1. Something about their methodology
  2. Something their content is missing
  3. A question about how they work with clients
- [ ] Open the Voice Profile Template: `_active/offers/voice-profile-template.md`

---

## Stage 3: The Call (60 min)

Follow the full call script in [proof-run-delivery-template.md](./proof-run-delivery-template.md), Phase 2.

**Record the call** (with permission) for reference during production.

---

## Stage 4: Production (Days 1-5)

Follow the full production process in [proof-run-delivery-template.md](./proof-run-delivery-template.md), Phases 3-4.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Voice Profile completed
- [ ] 8 post angles identified
- [ ] 8 posts drafted using GVE
- [ ] VSL script drafted
- [ ] All content passes 10-Point Authenticity Standard
- [ ] Humanize pass complete (read aloud, no AI tells)
- [ ] Posting strategy notes written

---

## Stage 5: Delivery (Day 6-7)

- [ ] **Send delivery email** — Template in [proof-run-delivery-template.md](./proof-run-delivery-template.md), Phase 5
- [ ] Include: 8 posts + VSL script + posting strategy notes
- [ ] Log delivery date in `_active/clients/[client-name]/tracking.md`

---

## Stage 6: Follow-Up & Conversion

| Day After Delivery | Action | Message |
|-------------------|--------|---------|
| **Day 3** | Check-in | "Have you had a chance to read the posts?" |
| **Day 5** | Revision reminder | "Any feedback? You have 2 revision rounds." |
| **Day 7** | After they post | "Saw you posted [X]. How'd it feel?" |
| **Day 14** | Retainer pitch | See script below |

### Day 14 Retainer Pitch

> "Hey [Name], curious — after seeing the results, is ongoing content something you'd want? I'm taking on [X] monthly clients at $2,500/month. 12 posts/month, monthly strategy call, content calendar. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. No pressure — just wanted you to know."

If they want Tier 2, present the $3,500 Engine package first (anchor high).

---

## Stage 7: Retainer Onboarding (If They Upgrade)

- [ ] Send retainer agreement/invoice
- [ ] Schedule recurring monthly voice capture call
- [ ] Create content calendar for Month 1
- [ ] Set up monthly performance reporting cadence
- [ ] Add to client roster tracking
