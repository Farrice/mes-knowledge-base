### 4. THE 5 ARCHETYPES OF CLIENT VOICES FRAMEWORK

Cole's revolutionary framework for training AI on client voice:

**Archetype #1: The Storyteller**
- Loves dates, times, locations, real-life examples
- Trigger phrases: "I remember...", "Back when I was...", "A few years ago..."
- Uses two 1/3/1 blocks: first summarizes story, second brings takeaway to reader
- Creative, eccentric personalities, natural speakers

**Archetype #2: The Opinionator**
- Emphasizes strong opinions, loves to prove points
- Uses words like "never" and "always"
- Trigger phrases: "There's a reason why...", "It's unbelievable how..."
- Secret weapon: ADVERBS (really, rarely, honestly) = emphasis

**Archetype #3: The Fact Presenter**
- Backs up opinions with stats, studies, surveys
- Academic or aspirationally academic
- Golden rule: ONE source per section
- Avoids "fluff" and "naked opinions"

**Archetype #4: The Frameworker**
- Gives actionable step-by-step processes
- Subtitles: "A Proven Process...", "A Timeless Framework..."
- Key: Packaging and naming frameworks
- Examples: "The Endless Idea Generator", "The 1/3/1 Writing Framework"

**Archetype #5: The F-Bomber**
- Abrasive, pushes boundaries with language
- Uses cursing, ranting, sarcasm
- 1 expletive per section or paragraph is enough
- Stands out by being brash

### 5. THE 3-PHASE CLIENT VOICE TRAINING PROCESS

**Phase I: Transcription**
- Record client calls using Zoom, Otter.ai
- Get call transcribed (Rev.com or AI tools)
- Alternative: Use client's existing content (YouTube, podcasts, speeches)

**Phase II: Editing Down**
- Hack away at the clay—remove everything not relevant
- Organize and polish what's left
- Don't add more, subtract to essence

**Phase III: Read Aloud (Approval)**
- Read work out loud before sending
- Ask: "Can I say this in fewer words?"
- Ask: "Would this voice say this word, phrase, or story?"

### 7. THE 4A CONTENT FRAMEWORK (FOR AI IDEA GENERATION)

Every idea can be expressed in 4 different ways:

**Actionable (Here's how)**
- Tips, hacks, resources, ultimate guides
- Help reader put ideas into practice

**Analytical (Here's a breakdown)**
- Trends, reasons, numbers, examples
- Help reader unlock new ways of thinking

**Aspirational (Yes, you can)**
- Traits, stories, quotes, lessons, mistakes
- Help reader understand benefits of new lens

**Anthropological (Here's why)**
- Fears, failures, struggles, limiting beliefs
- Create urgency to embrace core idea

### 8. KEY WRITING FRAMEWORKS FOR AI PROMPTS

**The 1/3/1 Writing Rhythm:**
- 1 sentence opener (strong, declarative)
- 3 sentences of description/value in middle
- 1 sentence closer (summary/takeaway)
- Variations: 1/4/1, 1/5/1, 1/2/5/2/1

**The Rate of Revelation:**
- How quickly you introduce new information to reader
- Low score: 1 idea stretched across hundreds of paragraphs
- High score: 10+ ideas compressed into handful of paragraphs
- Digital age readers want information FAST

**Prep The Page Framework:**
- Headline: What, who, benefit, credibility
- Introduction: Repeat headline with more detail
- Main Points: Proven approach (steps, lessons, mistakes)
- Conclusion: Final takeaway, moral of story

**The Endless Idea Generator:**
- Combine 4A Framework + proven approaches
- Turn 1 idea into 12+ pieces of content in 10 minutes
- Paired with AI inside Typeshare platform

### 9. CONVERSATIONAL VS STRUCTURED PROMPTING

Cole teaches two approaches to AI prompting:

**Conversational Prompting:**
- Break things down step by step
- Engage in dialogue with AI
- Iterate and refine through back-and-forth

**Structured Prompting:**
- Think through all situations/hypotheticals ahead of time
- Build massive, well-thought-out prompt
- Feed into model for single comprehensive output

## (b) TOP 5 KEY INSIGHTS

1. **The 5 Archetypes Framework is Revolutionary for AI Voice Training**
   - Instead of trying to teach AI a "unique" voice for every client, Cole discovered 99% of clients fit into 5 archetypes
   - This makes AI voice training systematic and repeatable
   - Master these 5 and you can ghostwrite for anyone

2. **AI Should Handle Lower Leverage Tasks, Humans Handle High-Level Decisions**
   - The key to 10x output is NOT having AI do everything
   - Use AI for legwork (research, headlines, expansion, editing)
   - Humans focus on creativity, strategy, and final polish
   - This is the "Editor-in-Chief" model

3. **The 3-Phase Transcription Process is the Secret to Voice Matching**
   - Record client calls, transcribe with AI (Otter.ai)
   - Edit down to essence (don't add, subtract)
   - Read aloud for approval
   - This process captures authentic voice better than any prompt engineering

4. **The 4A Framework Multiplies Content Output from Single Ideas**
   - One idea → 4 different content types (Actionable, Analytical, Aspirational, Anthropological)
   - Combined with Endless Idea Generator = 12+ pieces from 1 idea in 10 minutes
   - This is how Cole generates millions of views consistently

5. **The Ultimate Ghostwriting Voice Combines All 5 Archetypes**
   - Don't wait for clients to ask—give them everything in first draft
   - Story + Opinion + Study + Named Framework + (optional) Abrasive Rant
   - This approach consistently delivers 95/100 first drafts
