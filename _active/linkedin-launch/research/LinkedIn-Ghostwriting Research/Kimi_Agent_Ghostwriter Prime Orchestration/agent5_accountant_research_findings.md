### 4. PRICING TIER MATH: STARTER VS. MID VS. PREMIUM PACKAGES

**2026 LinkedIn Ghostwriter Pricing Tiers:**

#### BUDGET/STARTER TIER: $500 - $1,500/month
- 8-12 posts per month
- Basic research, template-based writing
- Newer freelancers or AI-assisted with light editing
- Limited strategy, minimal voice matching
- **Best for**: Testing LinkedIn as a channel

#### MID-TIER: $1,500 - $3,500/month
- 8-16 posts per month
- Voice matching, strategic topic selection
- Content calendar, light strategy

### 5. RETAINER VS. PROJECT-BASED VS. HYBRID INCOME MODELS

**RETAINER MODEL (Most Common):**
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Predictable income for both parties
- Easy to scope and manage
- Typical range: $1,000-$5,000+/month per client
- **Pros**: Predictable cash flow, long-term relationships
- **Cons**: Client dependency, scope creep risk

**PROJECT-BASED MODEL:**
- One-time engagements
- Educational Email Courses (EECs): $5,000+ per course
- Book projects: $15,000-$150,000+
- Website copy: $3,000-$15,000
- **Pros**: Higher per-project fees, clear boundaries
- **Cons**: Feast-or-famine cycle, constant new business needed

**HYBRID MODEL (Recommended by Cole):**
- Base retainer + project add-ons
- Monthly fee ($2,000-$5,000) + additional projects
- Example: $3,000/month retainer + $5,000 EEC projects
- **Pros**: Best of both worlds, maximizes revenue per client
- **Cons**: More complex to manage

**Cole's "5 Levels of Ghostwriter Income Staircase":**
1. Charging per word
2. Charging per hour
3. Charging per month (retainer)
4. Charging per asset (project)
5. Charging per outcome (value-based)

**Recommendation**: Accelerate to Level 4 (per asset/project) as quickly as possible

## (b) TOP 5 KEY INSIGHTS

1. **The $100K/year threshold is achievable with just 3 clients at $2,800/month** - This is the foundational math that makes LinkedIn ghostwriting attractive. The barrier to six figures is lower than most service businesses.

2. **Nicolas Cole's 60-day path from $0 to $20K/month is documented and replicable** - The key was "packaging upgrades" - moving from $100/article to $1,000/article by repositioning the service as strategic value, not writing labor.

3. **The 8 ghostwriters making $253K/month average $31,625/month each** - This validates that high earnings are not just outliers but achievable for dedicated practitioners who fix their "faulty beliefs" about pricing and value.

4. **Retainer pricing follows a clear 3-tier structure** ($1,000-$1,500 entry, $2,000-$3,500 mid, $5,000+ premium) - The market has standardized around these tiers, making it easy to position services.

5. **The fastest path to $5K-$10K/month is through cold outreach and paid test months, not audience building** - Cole explicitly states: "If you're under $20K/month, focus solely on cold outreach and free consulting" - audience building is "playing the game on hard mode."

## (c) ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Start with the "3 clients at $2,800" formula** - This is the proven path to $100K/year. Don't overcomplicate the math.

2. **Offer a paid test month at 1/3 of your full package price** - This reduces client risk and gives you a clean off-ramp if fit is poor.

3. **Implement "packaging upgrades" immediately** - Move from per-hour or per-word pricing to monthly retainers or project-based pricing. Anchor on outcomes, not effort.

4. **Follow the 8-step path to fix faulty beliefs** - Especially: stop thinking you need more clients (raise prices instead), and stop waiting to be "more experienced" to charge more.

5. **Use the "Leaks & Faucets" strategy for lead generation** - Focus on finding where your ideal clients already gather, rather than building an audience from scratch.

6. **Document your own LinkedIn presence as a case study** - Post 10-20 strong posts demonstrating your voice-matching and strategic thinking abilities.

7. **Create 3 spec portfolio samples in your target niche** - Use hypothetical executives if needed. Label them as samples.

8. **Plan for 3-month minimum commitments** - LinkedIn content takes 2-3 months to build momentum. Set this expectation upfront.

9. **Track ROI metrics for clients** - Impressions, engagement, profile views, inbound DMs, qualified calls booked, revenue closed. This justifies premium rates.

10. **Scale through agency model only after hitting $20K/month solo** - Don't hire until your one-person business is efficient and profitable.
