### CORE METHODOLOGY & FRAMEWORKS

**1. The 7 Key Skills of Digital Writing**
1. Viral Writing
2. Newsletter Writing
3. Offer Creation
4. Landing Page Copywriting
5. Sales Email Marketing
6. Advanced Offer Creation
7. (Additional business skills)

**2. The Endless Idea Generator**
- 4A Framework for content types:
  - **Actionable**: Here's how (tips, hacks, resources)
  - **Analytical**: Here are the numbers (trends, stats)
  - **Aspirational**: Yes, you can (lessons, habits, mistakes)
  - **Anthropological**: Here's why (fears, failures, struggles)

**3. The 5 Headline Styles**
1. **The List**: "20 Things Most People Learn Too Late In Life" (5M+ views)
2. **The Story**: "What I Learned Being Betrayed By My 6 Closest Friends"
3. **The Opinion**: Pick a side, plant your flag, stand for something
4. **The Question**: Create curiosity gap, don't answer in headline
5. **The Framework**: "The Endless Idea Generator: A Simple 5-Step Process..."

**4. Perfect Headline Formula**
- What this story is about
- Who this story is for
- The PROMISE (problem solved or results achieved)
- Write headline FIRST, then the article

**5. Post Structure Frameworks**
- 1/3/1 or 1/5/1 or 1/1/2/1 (sentence patterns)
- One-sentence paragraphs create rhythm
- Structure: Introduction → X main points → Strong conclusion

**6. Ghostwriting Pricing Strategy**
- Freelance writers charge per word or per hour
- Premium Ghostwriters charge per project or outcome
- Example: Instead of $30/hour for emails, charge $5,000 for Educational Email Course
- Decouple from time - charge for pattern recognition and experience

**7. Finding Ghostwriting Clients (4-Part Checklist)**
1. Does this person have a business?
2. How long have they been in business?
3. How digital is their business (1-10 scale)?
4. Where do I have an Information Advantage?

**8. The 2-Year Test**
- Framework for finding topics you're credible to write about
- What have you learned in the last 2 years?
- Use that as foundation for content buckets

## (c) ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Start a Daily Writing Practice**: Commit to writing and publishing one piece of content every single day for 90 days minimum. Choose one platform (LinkedIn, X, Medium) and show up consistently. Track metrics to learn what resonates.

2. **Write Headlines First**: Before writing any content, spend 80% of your time crafting the headline. Use Cole's formula: Name the Audience + Name the Outcome + Name the Process. Write 10 headline variations before choosing one.

3. **Build Your Information Advantage**: Identify a niche where you have specific knowledge or experience. Don't chase "trendy" niches - leverage what you already know. Document your learning journey publicly.

4. **Create Your First Digital Product**: Once you have 100+ followers and engagement, create a simple eBook or guide solving a specific problem your audience has asked about. Price it at $27-$97 to test demand.

5. **Practice the 4A Framework**: For every topic, create content in all 4 formats: Actionable (how-to), Analytical (data/numbers), Aspirational (lessons/stories), Anthropological (human nature/psychology).

6. **Shift to Project-Based Pricing**: If freelancing, stop charging hourly. Package your services as outcomes: "Educational Email Course" vs. "email writing." Start at $1,500-$5,000 per project.

7. **Use the 2-Year Test**: List everything you've learned in the last 2 years. These are your content buckets. You have credibility to teach what you've recently learned.

8. **Do Strategic Free Work**: Offer to help 3 people in your network for free. Document results, get testimonials, build case studies. This becomes your proof for paid clients.

9. **Study the 5 Headline Styles**: Analyze viral content in your niche. You'll find they all use: Lists, Stories, Opinions, Questions, or Frameworks. Create templates for each style.

10. **Measure in Decades, Not Days**: Set a 10-year vision for your writing career. Commit to the daily practice knowing that exponential results come from compound consistency.
