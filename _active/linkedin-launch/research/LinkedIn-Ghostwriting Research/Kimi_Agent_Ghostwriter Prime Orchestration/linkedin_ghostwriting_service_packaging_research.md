## (a) RAW RESEARCH FINDINGS

### 1. SERVICE PACKAGING MODELS USED BY TOP LINKEDIN GHOSTWRITING AGENCIES

#### Three-Tier Pricing Structure (Industry Standard)

**BUDGET TIER: $500 - $1,500/month**
- Target: Newer freelancers, offshore writers, AI-assisted services
- Deliverables: 8-12 posts/month, basic research, template-based writing
- Trade-off: Lacks personality and strategic depth; checks box for consistency but rarely generates pipeline
- Typical clients: Founders testing LinkedIn as a channel

**MID TIER: $2,000 - $4,000/month** (Most Popular)
- Target: Serious founders, established businesses
- Deliverables: 12-16 posts/month, voice matching, strategic topic selection, engagement optimization, performance tracking
- Includes: Content pillars, interview-based voice capture, basic engagement strategy
- Typical agencies: Column Content ($2,000/mo for 15 posts + design + research), Dan Fogarty ($3,000/mo)

**PREMIUM TIER: $5,000 - $10,000+/month**
- Target: High-profile executives, celebrity founders, funded startups
- Deliverables: 16-20+ posts/month, comprehensive content strategy, done-for-you engagement, audience research, lead gen integration
- Team structure: Dedicated strategist, writer, account manager
- Add-ons: Multi-platform repurposing (Twitter, newsletters, short-form video), pipeline attribution tracking
- Examples: NUOPTIMA ($3,000-$5,000), Drumlin Creative ($5,000+/mo), Media Engine ($3,000+/mo)

### 2. DOCUMENTED "PRODUCTIZED SERVICE" FRAMEWORKS FOR GHOSTWRITING

#### The Nicolas Cole Framework (Grew agency to $180k/month in 18 months)

**Core Principle:** A Service = "task" | A Packaged Service = "bundle of outcomes"

**3-Step Packaging Method:**

**Step 1: Name Your Packaged Service**
Formula: [Problem/Person/Way] + [Desirable Outcome] + "Package"
Examples:
- Twitter Thought Leadership Package
- Organic LinkedIn Book-A-Call Funnel Package  
- Newsletter Autopilot Package

**Step 2: Build Your Packaged Service**
Add as much value as possible to feel like a complete solution:
- A/B test headlines
- Write and publish content
- Optimize landing pages
- Offer all together for one price

**Step 3: Upsell to Premium**
Additional offerings:
- Build surveys
- Design digital products
- Create Educational Email Courses

#### The Assembly.com Productized Service Framework

**Key Characteristics:**
- Clear packages (clients know exactly what they'll receive and at what cost)
- Repeatable SOPs (same documented process works across multiple client types)
- Fixed pricing (no more "I'll send you a custom quote")
- Defined deliverables (crystal clear on what's included and what's not)
- Self-serve purchasing (storefront where clients can buy without sales call)

**7-Step Productization Framework:**
1. Identify your most repeatable service
2. Define clear deliverables (create your own framework)
3. Standardize your process like other industries
4. Map out 4-6 delivery milestones with timelines
5. Create tiered packages (basic/standard/premium)
6. Build add-ons for customization
7. Create a storefront for self-serve purchasing

#### The Backend Process (What Clients Don't See)

According to industry expert Ash Andilya:

1. **Discovery** - Brief call, questionnaire, understanding the client
2. **Research & Strategy** - Market/competitor research, goal alignment

4. **Content Creation** - Writing, graphics, video, etc.
5. **Scheduling & Distribution** - Calendar-based posting, multi-platform

This is why "rate per deliverable" pricing doesn't work - the effort behind each post extends far beyond writing.

## (b) TOP 5 KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Outcome-Based Packaging Commands Premium Prices**
   - Agencies selling "bundles of outcomes" (not tasks) charge 3-5x more
   - Naming convention: [Problem] + [Outcome] + "Package" increases perceived value
   - Example: "Organic LinkedIn Book-A-Call Funnel Package" vs. "LinkedIn Post Writing"

2. **Three-Tier Structure is Universal**
   - Budget ($500-1,500), Mid ($2,000-4,000), Premium ($5,000-10,000+)
   - Mid-tier captures 60-70% of serious buyers
   - Premium tier includes strategy, not just content creation

3. **Content Strategy + Ghostwriting Bundle is the Winning Model**
   - Standalone ghostwriting commoditizes the service
   - Bundling strategy, creation, and optimization justifies higher prices
   - Clients pay for pipeline attribution, not just posts

4. **ROI Guarantees Create Differentiation**
   - Media Engine's 2x ROI guarantee sets them apart
   - Guarantees reduce client risk perception
   - Only confident, experienced agencies can offer this

5. **The Backend Process is the Real Value**
   - Discovery, research, strategy, ideation = 70% of the work
   - Writing is only the final 30%
   - Clients who understand this pay premium prices

## (c) ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Package Services as Outcomes, Not Tasks**
   - Rename "LinkedIn Ghostwriting" to "LinkedIn Authority Builder Package"
   - Lead with business outcomes (leads, pipeline, authority) not deliverables
   - Create tiered packages: Starter/Growth/Authority or Silver/Gold/Platinum

2. **Implement the 3-Step Nicolas Cole Framework**
   - Step 1: Name packages using [Problem] + [Outcome] + "Package" formula
   - Step 2: Add maximum value to feel like complete solutions
   - Step 3: Create premium upsells (strategy, training, additional platforms)

3. **Bundle Content Strategy with Ghostwriting**
   - Never sell ghostwriting without strategy
   - Include: content pillars, editorial calendar, competitor analysis
   - Price strategy layer separately to show value

4. **Add High-Value Deliverables for Premium Tiers**
   - Graphics and carousels
   - Cross-platform repurposing
   - Analytics and ROI tracking
   - Profile optimization
   - Engagement management

5. **Consider ROI Guarantees for Differentiation**
   - Start with conservative guarantee (1.5x ROI)
   - Track pipeline attribution rigorously
   - Use guarantee as marketing differentiator

6. **Create Clear Delivery Milestones**
   - Map 4-6 milestones with timelines
   - Example: Week 1 (Discovery), Week 2 (Strategy), Week 3-4 (Content Creation)
   - Creates constraints that become selling points

7. **Build Add-On Menu for Customization**
   - Core packages remain standardized
   - Add-ons: additional posts, video scripts, media pitching, book ghostwriting
   - Allows upsells without custom proposals

8. **Document Your Process as a Framework**
   - Create proprietary methodology (e.g., "The Authority Builder Method")
   - Visual roadmaps showing client journey
   - Frameworks justify premium pricing
