# Pricing Architecture for LinkedIn Ghostwriting

## The Price Ladder

| Tier | Price Range | Posts/Month | Services | Target Client |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $1,500-$2,500/mo | 8-12 | Profile optimization, content calendar, monthly call | Solopreneurs, new founders |
| Growth | $3,000-$5,000/mo | 12-16 | + Engagement strategy, bi-weekly calls, performance reports | Established founders, executives |
| Authority | $6,000-$10,000+/mo | 16-20+ | + Thought leadership, custom visuals, weekly calls, dedicated manager | C-suite, industry leaders |

## Deal Structures (Nicolas Cole Framework)

### Flat-Rate, Per Project
Best for defined-scope projects with clear endpoints.

- **5-Day Educational Email Course**: Starting at $5,000
- **Website Copy**: $3,000-$10,000
- **Automated Email Sequences**: $2,000-$5,000
- **eBooks**: $5,000-$15,000

Payment terms: Under $5,000 = 100% upfront. Over $5,000 = 50/50 split.

### Monthly Retainer
Best for ongoing content creation and strategy.

- Predictable recurring revenue
- Clear deliverables per month
- Built-in rate increase mechanism

### Hybrid (Upfront + Performance)
Best for experienced ghostwriters with significant leverage.

- Reduced upfront fee + share of long-term upside
- Example: $2,000/month + 10% of revenue from leads generated
- Only use when you have strong track record

## Pricing Psychology

### Value-Based Framing Script
"Let me ask you — what's the average value of a new client for your business? [$X]. And how many clients do you think a strong LinkedIn presence could generate per month? [Y]. So we're looking at $[X*Y] in potential revenue per month. My fee of $[Z] is a fraction of that return."

### Prestige Positioning
High prices signal high quality. A $5,000/month ghostwriter is perceived as fundamentally different from a $500/month one. Price is a filter that attracts serious clients and repels tire-kickers.

### Price Anchoring
Always present the Authority package first. This makes the Growth package look like a bargain by comparison. Most clients will choose the middle option.

## Rate Increase Strategy

1. Give 30-60 days notice
2. Frame as investment in quality: "This allows me to dedicate more strategic time to your account"
3. Offer grandfather clause for 2-3 months
4. State new rates with confidence — no apologizing

## First-Client Pricing Strategy

For your first 1-3 clients when you have zero proof:

1. **Pilot Rate**: Offer 30-day pilot at 50% of your target rate
2. **Testimonial Exchange**: Reduced rate in exchange for a detailed testimonial and case study
3. **Results Guarantee**: Full refund if not satisfied after 30 days
4. **Escalation Clause**: "After the pilot, the rate moves to $[full rate]. This pilot rate is a one-time offer."
