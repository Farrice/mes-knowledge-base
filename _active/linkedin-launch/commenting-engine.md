## The 30-Day Roadmap

### Daily Routine: The 60-Minute LinkedIn Block

| Time Block | Activity | Targets |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Minutes 1-10** | Find 5-8 posts from target creators (use the 4 categories above) | Sort by "recent" to catch posts in first 2 hours |
| **Minutes 10-45** | Leave 5-8 substantive comments using the matrix archetypes | Rotate: 2 Expanders, 2 Challengers, 2 Humanizers per day |
| **Minutes 45-55** | Check notifications → reply to replies on your comments | Every reply is another visibility impression |
| **Minutes 55-60** | Log the day — which comment got the most profile views? | Track in a simple spreadsheet: Comment Topic / Platform Views / Inbound DMs |

### Weekly Promotion Cycle

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| **Monday** | Review last week's comment log. Identify the "Winning Comment" (most profile views or reactions) |
| **Tuesday** | Expand the winning comment into a full LinkedIn post (Trapdoor Hook format) |
| **Wednesday** | 60-minute commenting block (Lab Testing) |
| **Thursday** | Post #2 of the week (from your 5-post launch schedule OR a new expansion) |
| **Friday** | 60-minute commenting block + DM follow-ups on anyone who engaged this week |

### 30-Day Phase Plan

| Week | Focus | Publishing | Commenting |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Week 1 (Pre-Launch)** | Warm the algorithm. Comment only. No posts yet. | 0 posts | 5-8 comments/day × 5 days = 25-40 comments |
| **Week 2 (Soft Launch)** | Start posting. Posts 1-2 from your launch schedule. | 2 posts (Tue/Thu) | 5-8 comments/day × 5 days |
| **Week 3 (Full Launch)** | Posts 3-4. Comment volume stays high. | 2 posts (Tue/Thu) | 5-8 comments/day × 5 days |
| **Week 4 (Close + Optimize)** | Post 5 + best winning comment expanded into Post 6. | 2 posts (Tue/Thu) | 5-8 comments/day × 5 days |
