## Client Acquisition: 3 Pipelines

### Pipeline 1: Comments as Consulting (Warm)

This is Cole's signature method. You give away strategy in public → demonstrate expertise → prospects come to you.

**Daily rhythm (15-20 min):**
1. Find 3-5 ideal clients' posts (coaches/consultants with 500+ connections, posting inconsistently)
2. Leave comments that add genuine value — a framework, a reframe, a specific insight
3. After 3-5 valuable comments on someone's posts over 1-2 weeks → send Unsolicited Demo

**What a valuable comment looks like:**
- ❌ "Great post!" / "Love this!"
- ✅ "This reminds me of something I've noticed with my clients — [specific insight]. The pattern is usually [framework]. Have you seen that too?"

### Pipeline 2: Unsolicited Demo (Warm-Cold)

The GVE Workflow 03 — rewrite one of their weaker posts in their voice, no pitch attached.

**The outreach message:**
> "Hey [Name], been following your work on [topic]. Saw your post about [X] and thought it had a killer insight buried under too much framing. Took 10 minutes and rewrote it as I'd approach it. No strings — just thought you'd find it useful. [LINK TO REWRITE]"

**If they respond positively:**
> "Glad it landed. That rewrite came from studying your other posts — the way you explain [methodology], the phrases you use. If you ever want to see what a full month of that looks like, I do a Proof Run — 8 posts + a VSL script, $750. But honestly, even if we never work together, keep posting about [topic]. You've got a perspective people need to hear."

### Pipeline 3: Cold Outreach (Cold)

Use only after Pipelines 1-2 are running. Requires at least 1 case study.

> "Hi [Name], I ghostwrite LinkedIn for [coaches/consultants] who have deep expertise but inconsistent content. I noticed your posting cadence dropped off in [month] — happens to most coaches I work with. Here's what your last post would look like with some strategic polish: [LINK]. No pitch. Just wanted to share."

## The Conversion Sequence

```
Comment/Demo → Response → Discovery Call → Proof Run → Results → Retainer
```

### Step 1: Discovery Call (30 min, "Free Consulting")

Give away everything. Strategy, content angles, posting schedule, engagement tactics. All free.

**The key line:**
> "Whether or not we work together, I want you to know exactly what you should be posting, when, and why."

They will NOT do it themselves. Strategy is free. Execution costs money.

### Step 2: Proof Run

Send the offer page: `offers/proof-run-offer-page.md`
- First 3 clients: $500 pilot rate (testimonial exchange)
- Standard rate: $750
- Delivery SOP: `offers/proof-run-delivery-template.md`

### Step 3: Follow-Up (After Proof Run Delivery)

| Day | Message |
|-----|---------|
| **Day 3** | "Have you had a chance to read through the posts?" |
| **Day 7** | "Saw you posted [X]. How'd it feel? Any engagement you didn't expect?" |
| **Day 14** | "After seeing the results — is ongoing content something you'd want? I'm taking on [X] monthly clients at $2,500/mo. 12 posts/week, strategy call, content calendar. No pressure." |

### Step 4: Retainer Upgrade

Present the Service Menu (see below). Always present Tier 2 first — anchor high, let them choose Tier 1.

## Handling Objections

**"Do you have a portfolio?"**
> "Because of the nature of ghostwriting, I can't share specific client names — but look at my own LinkedIn. I use the exact methodology I'd apply to yours. That's the proof."

**"That's expensive."**
> "What's the average value of a new client for you? [They answer]. And how many do you think a strong LinkedIn presence could generate per month? So we're looking at $[X] in potential revenue. My fee is a fraction of that."

**"Can I see a sample first?"**
> "That's exactly what the Proof Run is. $750, 8 posts in your voice. If they don't sound like you, full refund. Zero risk."

**"I tried a ghostwriter before and it didn't work."**
> "Most ghostwriters start guessing from draft one. I start by capturing your voice — your rhythm, your phrases, the way you build an argument. That's the difference between content that sounds like you and content that sounds like a writer trying to be you."

## Vertical Targeting

### Start Here: Coaches & Consultants

**Sub-niches to test first:**
1. Health & wellness coaches (your lived experience)
2. Business/executive coaches ($5K-$15K/month revenue, can afford retainers)
3. Career coaches (high LinkedIn ROI, hungry for visibility)
4. Leadership consultants (B2B, LinkedIn is their primary platform)

### Testing Protocol
1. Run 3 Unsolicited Demos in a vertical
2. Track: response rate, interest, willingness to pay
3. If 2/3 engage → pursue that vertical
4. If 0/3 → move to next

### Explore After 3 Clients
- Tech founders (highest willingness to pay)
- Agency owners (want personal brand separate from agency)
- Real estate agents (growing LinkedIn presence)
