## The Primary Weakness: Rule 5 (The Profitability Gate)

**Diagnosis**: All 5 kits diagnose brilliantly — they reveal the gap. But none of them explicitly **create the demand** for the Proof Run. The user finishes the diagnostic, sees their score, gets 3 generic action items... and then what? The kits tell them to "start posting" or "build content now" — but they don't say "and there's someone who will do this for you in 48 hours."

**This is a "profitable-looking failure."** The kits generate goodwill and authority, but the conversion to $500 Proof Run is left to initiative, not architecture.

## Kit-by-Kit Bridge Fixes

### Kit 01: Invisible Expert Diagnostic
**Current ending**: "The fix isn't more content — it's better TRANSLATION of the expertise you already have."
**Problem**: This is insightful, but it leaves the user thinking "so how do I translate better?" rather than "I need someone to do this translation for me."

**Rule 5 Fix — Add after the closing line**:
```
---

You now know your exact gap score. Here's what you can do about it:

over the next 30 days.

**Done-For-You Route**: If you want 5 LinkedIn posts written in YOUR 
voice — based on YOUR methodology — in 48 hours, reply "PROOF RUN" 
to the person who sent you this diagnostic. 

They'll ghostwrite your first 5 posts using the same voice-matching 
AI that identified your gap. No templates. Your frameworks. Your stories. 
Your voice. $500 for the first 5 posts — and if they don't sound like 
you wrote them, you don't pay.

The gap between your expertise and your presence doesn't have to stay 
open for another 30 days.
```

### Kit 02: Deployment Gap Calculator
**Current ending**: "The gap between your Notion and your LinkedIn isn't creativity — it's deployment. And deployment is a skill, not a talent."
**Problem**: Beautiful line. But it positions deployment as a learnable skill — which means they'll try to learn it themselves. You want them to conclude "I need someone to deploy FOR me."

**Rule 5 Fix — Add after the closing line**:
```
---

## THE DEPLOYMENT QUESTION

You're sitting on [X] posts. You could deploy them yourself — 
but here's what usually happens:

Week 1: You write 2 posts. Week 2: You write 1. Week 3: You're 
"too busy with clients." Week 4: Back to zero.

Alternatively: someone extracts the posts from YOUR frameworks, 
writes them in YOUR voice, and hands you 5 ready-to-paste LinkedIn 
essays by Friday.

Reply "PROOF RUN" to the person who sent you this → they'll deploy 
your first 5 posts for $500. Your frameworks. Your voice. Your 
proof that deployment works.
```

### Kit 03: Pipeline Fragility Score
**Current ending**: "You need a second acquisition channel — and LinkedIn is the fastest one to build."
**Problem**: The ending prescribes "3 specific actions" — which positions the coach as the one who needs to act. It needs to position ghostwriting as the fastest way TO build that channel.

**Rule 5 Fix — Replace the high-score prescription**:
```
If score 7+: "Your business is running on hope, not a pipeline. 
LinkedIn is the fastest second channel to build — but building it 
yourself while serving clients is a Catch-22.

Reply "PROOF RUN" to the person who sent you this. They'll 
ghostwrite 5 LinkedIn posts from your existing frameworks in 48 hours. 
You keep coaching. Your pipeline starts diversifying. $500."
```

The content was there all along. You just needed someone to mine it.

**Rule 5 Fix — Add after the closing line**:

## Psychological Journey Map (All 5 Kits)

| Stage | What the Coach Feels |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Before** | "I probably should post more, but I'm too busy coaching" — mild guilt, no urgency |
| **During** | "Oh. My gap score is a 3/10. I have 96 posts sitting in my head. My pipeline would dry up in 6 weeks." — quantified anxiety |
| **After** | "I can't keep ignoring this. I need to act." — urgency activated |
| **The Bridge** | "But I don't have time to do this myself... wait, someone will ghostwrite 5 posts for $500? Using MY voice? That's the play." — Proof Run becomes the obvious solution |

**Without the Bridge**: They feel urgent → they try to write posts themselves → they fail in 2 weeks → the urgency fades. You lose the sale.
**With the Bridge**: They feel urgent → the Proof Run is right there → path of least resistance → $500 collected.
