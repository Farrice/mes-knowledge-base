## Gig Description:

### Tired of marketing to a made-up avatar?

Most "ideal customer profiles" are fiction. A name, an age, a salary bracket, and some pain points you brainstormed over coffee. They don't work because they're not connected to how real people actually think, talk, and buy.

**I build consumer intelligence profiles grounded in real human behavior.**

### What makes this different:

**I mine real consumer conversations** — Reddit threads, Amazon reviews, forums, Quora, and niche communities — to extract the exact language, frustrations, desires, and identity patterns of your ideal customer. Not surveys. Not guesswork. Real, unfiltered human data.

Then I apply **consumer psychology frameworks** to build a 3-dimensional portrait of your ideal consumer:
- How they occupy space in your brand's world
- What behaviors reinforce their identity
- The internal logic driving their decisions
- The words that would stop them mid-scroll

### What you'll use it for:
- Write copy and content that converts (using their actual language)
- Build offers that feel custom-designed for them
- Create ads that resonate on an emotional level
- Develop products they didn't know they needed

## FAQs:

**Q: How is this different from a regular buyer persona?**
A: Buyer personas are fictional composites. This is a psychological intelligence report built from real consumer data — actual quotes, behaviors, and language mined from live communities. You'll know exactly what your customer says, thinks, and feels.

**Q: What do you need from me?**
A: Just 15 minutes to complete a 7-question intake about your brand, your customers, and what's not working. I handle everything else.

**Q: What industries/niches do you work with?**
A: Any B2C or B2B service business, e-commerce brand, coaching/consulting practice, or SaaS company. If you have customers, this works.

**Q: Can I use this for ads/copy/content?**
A: Absolutely — that's the primary use case. The Voice of Customer library alone gives you months of copy ammunition. The content pieces are ready to publish.
