# ICP Intelligence System — Service Delivery Workflow

> **Purpose**: Step-by-step execution workflow for delivering the ICP Intelligence Service.
> Each stage maps to a Dai Media prompt enhanced with live deep research.
> **Delivery time**: 48-72 hours per client.

---

## Pre-Work: Client Intake (30 min)

### Collect From Client:
1. **Brand Name & Category**
2. **Brand Essence** (2-3 sentences — what they stand for, their vibe)
3. **Price Point / Positioning** (where they sit in market)
4. **Current Consumer Description** (how they describe their customer today — demographics, psychographics, whatever they have)
5. **Known Consumer Signals** (successful customers, engagement patterns, intuitions)
6. **Top 3 Business Challenges** (what's not working — marketing, messaging, conversion, retention)
7. **Competitor List** (3-5 competitors they watch)

### Intake Format:
Send client a Google Form or Notion intake template. 15-20 min to complete.

---

## STAGE 1: Consumer Posture Gap Diagnostic (2-3 hours)

### Prompt: Dai Media Crown Jewel #3 — Gap Diagnostic

**What It Does**: Exposes exactly what the client doesn't know about their consumer. Produces a diagnostic report with 25 probing questions, gap analysis, strategic consequence map, and discovery priorities.

### Enhancement Layer — Deep Research:
Before running the diagnostic prompt, conduct research to fill in context:

1. **Review client's existing content** (website, social media, emails) to understand their current consumer messaging
2. **Perplexity research**: Industry-specific consumer behavior trends, market positioning
3. **Competitor analysis**: How 3-5 competitors describe and speak to their audiences

### Deliverable:
- Gap Diagnostic Report (the 25 questions + gap analysis + strategic consequences)
- Understanding Score (0-100 with dimension breakdown)
- Priority Discovery List

### Client Value: *"Here's what you don't know about your customer — and here's exactly what it's costing you."*

---

## STAGE 2: Voice of Customer Deep Research (3-4 hours)

### Enhancement Layer — This is where AI system advantage lives

**What It Does**: Mines real consumer language, frustrations, desires, and behaviors from live sources. This is NOT available from the Dai Media prompts alone — this is the deep research layer that makes the output categorically different from any "avatar worksheet."

### Research Protocol:

1. **Reddit Mining** (1-2 hours)
   - Search subreddits relevant to client's industry
   - Find threads where consumers express frustration, desire, confusion
   - Extract verbatim quotes (the "language of pain")
   - Identify recurring themes, unspoken needs, anti-preferences
   - Target: 30-50 raw consumer quotes

2. **Review Mining** (30-45 min)
   - Amazon reviews, G2 reviews, Trustpilot, app store reviews for competitors
   - Extract: What people love, what they hate, what they wish existed
   - Look for the "finally" moments — "I finally found..."

3. **Forum/Community Mining** (30-45 min)
   - Facebook groups, Quora, niche forums
   - Find conversations where the consumer discusses their problem in their own words
   - Extract: Decision-making language, objections, what they've tried

4. **Perplexity Research** (30 min)
   - Industry trends and shifts affecting consumer behavior
   - Emerging needs and underserved segments
   - Competitive landscape consumer positioning

### Deliverable:
- Voice of Customer Data Library (organized by theme)
- Consumer Language Glossary (exact words and phrases consumers use)
- Anti-Preference Map (what consumers actively reject)
- Unmet Needs Inventory

---

## STAGE 3: Consumer Posture Complete Profile (2-3 hours)

### Prompt: Dai Media Crown Jewel #1 — Consumer Posture Profile Generator

**What It Does**: Builds the full 3D psychographic portrait — Occupation, Activity, and Thought Process in the brand's world. Not a generic persona. ONE vivid, specific individual.

### Enhancement Layer:
- Feed ALL Stage 2 research data into the prompt as context
- Cross-reference with Gap Diagnostic findings (Stage 1) — specifically fill the critical gaps identified
- Validate profile against real VoC data (does the profile match what actual consumers say?)

### Deliverable:
- The Individual (vivid description)
- Occupation in Your World (role, energy, what makes the brand world tick)
- Activity in Your World (rituals, routines, identity-reinforcing behaviors)
- Thought Process (internal logic, alignments, refusals)
- Posture Synthesis (unified portrait)
- Strategic Implications (content, voice, products, experience)
- The Prediction Test (5 hypothetical brand decisions with predicted consumer responses)

### Client Value: *"This is your customer. Not a demographic. A human being you can now predict."*

---

## STAGE 4: Individual Consumer Finder (1-2 hours)

### Prompt: Dai Media Crown Jewel #2 — Individual Consumer Finder

**What It Does**: Creates a search methodology to find ONE real, specific person who embodies the ideal consumer. Not a persona — a findable human being.

### Enhancement Layer:
- Use Consumer Posture Profile (Stage 3) as input
- Cross-reference with VoC data to identify signal environments
- Provide platform-specific search instructions (Reddit usernames to look for, LinkedIn search strings, community forums)

### Deliverable:
- Signal Environments (where to find this person)
- Recognition Criteria (how to identify them)
- Search Methodology (step-by-step 7-day search plan)
- 3 Candidate Profiles
- Validation Protocol (questions to confirm you found the right person)
- The "Coffee Test"

### Client Value: *"Here's exactly where to find your ideal customer and how to recognize them."*

---

## STAGE 5: Internal World Storytelling (2-3 hours)

### Prompt: Dai Media Crown Jewel #4 — Consumer Internal World Storytelling

**What It Does**: Creates content that enters the consumer's mind and narrates their unspoken experience. Entry → Recognition → Permission → Possibility.

### Enhancement Layer:
- Use Consumer Posture Profile as character foundation
- Use VoC verbatim quotes as language calibration
- Produce format-specific variations (LinkedIn, email, ad, landing page)

### Deliverable:
- Internal World Map (the landscape of their mind)
- 3 Complete Story Drafts (different use cases — ad, email, and social post)
- Recognition Markers (moments designed for "how did they know?" reaction)
- Language Library (phrases calibrated to their internal world)

### Client Value: *"Here's content that makes your customer feel like you've read their mind."*
