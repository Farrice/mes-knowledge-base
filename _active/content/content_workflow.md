# AI Brain Content Workflow
## How to Produce Proof-Based Content Daily (30-45 min)

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT

> **You can't sell an AI Brain without showing one working.**
> Every piece of content should contain proof: screenshots, before/afters, screen recordings, or live demos. Words alone won't convert this audience — they've been burned by promises.

---

## STEP 1: Build Your Own AI Brain (One-Time, Today)

You already HAVE an AI Brain. Your Antigravity system extracts expertise, deploys frameworks, and produces content in your voice. You just haven't *framed* it as your product demo.

**Action:**
1. Open your Antigravity system
2. Ask it to produce a LinkedIn post on a coaching topic using your voice and frameworks
3. Screenshot the input and the output
4. That screenshot IS your proof asset

**The reframe:** Every time you use your system to produce content, you're creating demo material. Your daily workflow IS your marketing.

---

## STEP 2: Daily Content Production — The IP Flywheel (45 min)

### The 4-Asset Flywheel (Nate B. Jones Method)

Every topic you run produces FOUR assets, not one. Use the `/ip-flywheel` command to generate them all:

| Asset | What It Is | Where It Goes |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Video Script** | 2-3 min talking-head, contrarian hook → framework → CTA | LinkedIn native video, Reels, TikTok |
| **LinkedIn Essay** | Structured, skimmable deep-dive with proof | LinkedIn text post (same day, accompanies video) |
| **Substack Article** | Long-form, personal, exploratory essay with inline Prompt Kit | Substack newsletter |
| **Prompt Kit** | Interactive XML-tagged diagnostic prompt | Notion page, linked from all platforms |

### Why This Is 10x More Leveraged

The Prompt Kit is the secret weapon. It's **software disguised as text.** When a coach copies your prompt into ChatGPT and runs the diagnostic:
- They experience the VALUE of your framework in real-time
- They see exactly what's missing from their current AI setup
- They self-qualify as a buyer
- The prompt itself soft-sells the AI Brain Build

One flywheel cycle = 3 pieces of content + a lead magnet + a self-qualifying tool.

### Daily Workflow

| Step | Time | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1. Pick a pain point | 2 min | From your validated list (red team research) |
| 2. Feed to Antigravity | 5 min | Use the IP Flywheel prompt to generate all 3 assets |
| 3. Record the video | 15 min | Phone, raw, no editing |
| 4. Post the essay | 5 min | Copy from the generated package |
| 5. Host the Prompt Kit | 10 min | Add to Notion page, drop link in comments |
| 6. Screenshot results | 5 min | For tomorrow's proof content |

## STEP 3: Before/After Screenshot Method

For every post, create this format:

```
┌─────────────────────────┐
│  BEFORE: Generic AI     │
│  "Unlock your potential  │
│   with this powerful     │
│   framework..."          │
├─────────────────────────┤
│  AFTER: AI Brain         │
│  [Actual output using    │
│   your frameworks,       │
│   your voice,            │
│   your specific client   │
│   stories]               │
└─────────────────────────┘
```

Take a real coaching concept. Run it through ChatGPT (generic). Run it through your system (calibrated). Screenshot both. Post the comparison.

## RECORDING YOUR DEMO LOOM (One-Time, 5-7 Min)

**Script structure:**

1. **The problem** (30 sec): "If you're a coach with 10 years of expertise and you still spend 2 hours writing a single LinkedIn post — this is for you."
2. **The demo** (3-4 min): Share screen. Open your system. Type a real prompt. Watch it produce content in your voice. Narrate what's happening.
3. **The gap** (30 sec): "This is what's possible when your AI actually knows your methodology. Generic ChatGPT can't do this because it doesn't know YOU."
4. **The CTA** (30 sec): "If you want to see what yours would look like, DM me 'BRAIN' on LinkedIn."

**Keep it raw.** Don't overpolish. Coaches trust authenticity over production value.
