## 5. Expert Roundtable Analysis

### Samuel Thompson (Shadow Market Lens)
> **Verdict: Classic Shadow Market.** Coaches are searching for AI solutions but being served by (a) generic chatbot builders, (b) $10K+ enterprise platforms, or (c) YouTube tutorials. The "language of pain" is visible on Reddit: "I'm paying for 5 AI tools and none of them talk to each other." The AI Brain Builder offer is a **Minimum Viable Offer** that can be tested for under $300 in ad spend.

### April Dunford (Positioning Lens)
> **Verdict: Strong positioning IF differentiated correctly.** "AI consultant" is becoming commoditized. "AI Brain Builder" works because it creates a **new category** in the buyer's mind — not "consultant," not "developer," but someone who builds a *brain* for your business. Risk: if competitors adopt the term, the advantage dissolves. **Recommendation:** Lock in the "AI Brain Builder" terminology through content, IP, and repetition before the market catches up.

### Dan Koe (Solopreneur Lens)
> **Verdict: Perfectly aligned with the one-person business model.** This is a "Sovereignty Vessel" — Farrice can deliver high-value, high-margin work without hiring a team. The key is the **Permission Gate**: the DM "BRAIN" CTA functions as a low-friction entry point that lets the buyer self-select. Don't over-productize too early — the human touch IS the premium.

### Lindsay (AI Consulting Sales Lens)
> **Verdict: Go, with a proof stack priority.** The biggest blocker for new AI consultants is the "zero testimonial" problem. The pricing is right, the demand is there, but **before scaling ads or cold outreach**, Farrice needs 3-5 case studies. Recommendation: offer the first 3-5 builds at a discount or as "founding client" packages in exchange for testimonials + video reviews.

### Monk.AI (Offer Architecture Lens)
> **Verdict: The 3-gig Fiverr structure is solid.** The tiered offer (Brain Build → Workflow System → Stack Audit) functions as an **Offer Pyramid** — the audit is the low-friction entry, the brain build is the premium anchor, and the workflow system is the upsell path. This is textbook offer architecture for a marketplace.
