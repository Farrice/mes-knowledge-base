# AI Brain Builder — Content & Outreach System

This document contains the complete **90-Day Content Engine** and the **Outbound/Inbound DM Frameworks** to generate and capture demand for the AI Brain Builder offer. 

Engineered using frameworks from **Lara Acosta** (4-3-2-1 Ratio), **Tom Noske** (90/10 Value Model), **Lindsay** (Robot-Speak Elimination), and **AI Chris Lee** (Zero-Testimonial Proof).

---

## PART 1: The 90-Day Content Matrix

**Frequency:** 5 Posts / Week (Monday - Friday)
**Core Ratio (Lara Acosta 4-3-2-1):** 4 Education, 3 Story, 2 Opinion, 1 Promotion across a 2-week rolling window.
**Tom Noske Rule:** 90% pure value, 10% direct ask. Never hard sell.

### Core Pillars
1. **AI Delegation (Education/Tactical):** How to offload 40% of your week to AI.
2. **Solopreneur Sovereignty (Story/Aspirational):** Building a business that doesn't rely on your typing speed.
3. **High-Leverage Automations (Opinion/Proof):** Why doing it all yourself is a trauma response, not a badge of honor.

### Month 2 & 3: The Proof & Escalation Cycles
*For Weeks 5-12, repeat the 4-week structure above, injecting the following rotating themes:*

- **Weeks 5-6 (The "How It's Built" Cycle):** Focus heavily on Education/Tactics. Show the *process* of building a custom AI brain. Screen recordings, Prompt dissections, Zapier flowcharts. Prove expertise by teaching everything.
- **Weeks 7-8 (The Case Study Cycle):** Focus on Story elements. Even without direct client testimonials, use "Audit Proof" (how you *would* fix a known industry leader's bottleneck) and personal case studies. 
- **Weeks 9-10 (The Objection Handling Cycle):** Focus on Opinions. Directly attack buyer fears: Data privacy, technical overwhelm, "will it sound like me?", and the cost of doing nothing.
- **Weeks 11-12 (The Scarcity Cycle):** Ramp up the Promotional frequency slightly (moving to 90/10 across the whole month). Focus on the "Cost of Inaction" and highlight limited capacity for custom builds.

## PART 2: DM Outreach Frameworks

*Engineered using Lindsay's "Robot-Speak Elimination" and AI Chris Lee's "Testimonial-Free Proof" systems.*

### 1. The "Audit Proof" Cold DM (Chris Lee Framework)
*Use this when reaching out to high-value ICPs you have no prior relationship with. ZERO permission-asking. Lead with indisputable value.*

> **Subject/Opener:** Your current intake bottleneck
> 
> Hey [Name],
> 
> I’ve been following your scale-up over the last few months. Noticed you’re pushing hard on [Specific Initiative/Content Pillar], but it looks like the backend intake/follow-up is still highly manual. 
> 
> I audited how top coaches in [Niche] are automating this, and built a quick 3-min Loom showing an AI workflow that cuts that admin time by 80% while retaining your exact voice. 
> 
> [Insert Loom Link]
> 
> Is this relevant to how you're tracking things right now?
> 
> — Farrice

### 2. The Warm Inbound Nurture (Lindsay Framework)
*When someone views your profile, likes a post, or votes on a poll. Do NOT pitch immediately.*

> **Opener:** Thanks for dropping by
> 
> Hey [Name], saw you checked out my profile / liked my post on [Topic]. Appreciate the support! 
> 
> Noticed you’re building out [Specific thing from their profile] at the moment. How’s the current tech stack holding up for you as you scale that? 

### 3. The Value-Drop Follow-Up
*When they reply to the warm inbound, or accept a connection request but go silent.*

> **Opener:** Quick resource for [Their Company]
> 
> Hey [Name] - no pressure to reply, but based on your recent post about [Specific Pain Point], I thought this might be useful. 
> 
> I just mapped out the exact AI prompt sequence to solve [Pain Point] without having to buy new software. 
> 
> [Link to Notion Doc / Loom]
> 
> Hope it helps you reclaim a few hours this week.
