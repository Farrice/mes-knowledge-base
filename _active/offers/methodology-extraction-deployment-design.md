## 1. The Pivot

**From**: AI Brain Build (selling the system concept — too abstract, requires market education)
**To**: Methodology Extraction & Deployment (selling the *outputs* of the system as a LinkedIn ghostwriting service)

**Why**: The Antigravity system with 80+ expert agents, extraction pipelines, and skill architecture is a 10x production advantage. But the market doesn't buy systems — they buy results. LinkedIn ghostwriting is a $4.2B market growing 7.5% annually with validated demand at $1K-$5K/month retainers.

## 2. Positioning

### Category: Methodology Extraction & Deployment (CREATE, don't compete)

**Do NOT position as "LinkedIn Ghostwriter"** — commoditized, race-to-bottom pricing, evaluated on posts/month.

**Position as**: The only service that systematically extracts coaching methodologies and deploys them as LinkedIn content using AI-powered agents trained on real persuasion and positioning frameworks.

**The line**: *"I don't write posts in your voice. I deploy your methodology as content."*

**Competitive moat**: MES pipeline + 80+ expert agent library + structured knowledge extraction. Competitors would need to rebuild their entire service model to match.

### Why This Category Wins

| Dimension | "Ghostwriting" | "Methodology Extraction & Deployment" |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------------------------|
| Pricing logic | Cost per post | Value of owning your category |
| Client evaluation | "Did you write enough posts?" | "Is my methodology being deployed?" |
| Qualification filter | Attracts everyone | Attracts only coaches with real IP |
| Expansion path | More posts | Courses, workshops, books, certifications |
| Competition | Thousands of freelancers | Nobody (new category) |

## 3. Target Customer

### Primary ICP: "The Proven Expert in Scaling Mode"

- **Revenue**: $100K-$500K/year (have a working offer, need demand)
- **Has**: Proven frameworks, methodologies, signature processes — trapped in their head
- **Charges**: $3K-$15K per client
- **Psychographic**: "I know what I teach works. I just can't scale myself."
- **Platform behavior**: Posts 1-3x/week OR sporadic, engagement under 100 likes average
- **Follower range**: 1,000-10,000 (hungry to grow, can afford services)

### Disqualified

- "Vibes-based" coaches with no structured methodology (nothing to extract)
- Price shoppers comparing Fiverr rates
- Coaches under $50K/year (can't afford retainers)

## 5. Go-to-Market: Dual-Track Strategy

### Track A: Outbound (Revenue in 14 days)

**Daily routine**: 2.5 hours/day

| Activity | Volume | Time |
|----------|--------|------|
| Pre-warm comments on prospects' posts | 10/day | 30 min |
| Connection requests (personalized) | 10-15/day | 30 min |
| DM sequences to accepted connections | 10-20/day | 60 min |
| Follow-ups | 10-15/day | 20 min |

**Pre-warm protocol** (Nate Herk — non-negotiable):
1. Day 1: Follow their profile (don't connect)
2. Days 2-5: Leave 2-3 substantive comments on their posts
3. Day 5: View their profile
4. Day 6: Send personalized connection request

**DM sequence**: 5 messages over 14 days (see DM Script Playbook)

**Conversion math**:
- 75 messages → 22 responses (30%) → 6 interested → 3 proof runs
- 3 proof runs → 1 retainer (33% conversion)

### Track B: Content (Compounding authority over 90 days)

**Posting cadence**: 3-5x/week on own LinkedIn

**3 content pillars**:
1. **Extraction Methodology (40%)**: How you turn coaching calls into LinkedIn content
2. **Algorithm Mechanics (30%)**: Transparent testing of what works on LinkedIn in 2026
3. **Proof in Public (30%)**: Documenting the journey from zero — every post IS the portfolio

**Format priority** (2026 algorithm):
- Document posts (PDF carousels): 6.6% engagement rate — 2x/week
- Text posts (SLAY formatted): core content
- Screenshots (before/after rewrites): authenticity signal
- Zero external links (60% distribution penalty)

**Daily commenting**: 30 min/day, 5 substantive comments (50-100 words each) on target prospects' posts

### How the Tracks Reinforce Each Other

Content makes DMs warmer → prospects check profile, see authority content, respond at higher rates.
DMs drive proof runs → proof runs generate case studies → case studies become content.
Content attracts inbound → reduces outbound dependency over time.

## 6. Sales Mechanics

### Qualification Framework (Jason Fladlien)

**3 questions that separate buyers from browsers**:
1. "Are you actively posting on LinkedIn, or is it one of those 'I know I should but I don't' situations?"
2. "How long has LinkedIn been on your 'should do' list?"
3. "What happens if it stays broken for another 6 months?"

**Buyer signals**: 6+ months of pain, specific consequences, process-focused questions
**Browser signals**: mild curiosity, vague answers, price-focused questions

### Guarantee Stack (3 layers)

1. **Proof run**: Full refund if voice isn't captured. Keep the posts.
2. **Monthly**: Month-to-month. No contract.
3. **Engagement**: Free messaging workshop if no traction in 60 days.

### Top Objections + Reframes

| Objection | Reframe |
|-----------|---------|
| "Tried ghostwriters before" | "They templated. I extract YOUR methodology from a live call." |
| "Can't afford monthly" | "Proof run is $250. You're testing, not committing." |
| "Should do it myself" | "Do you write your own contracts? Your $500/hr brain shouldn't do $50/hr work." |
| "No testimonials" | "You're right — that's why it's $250 not $500. You're the proof I'm building." |
| "Will it sound like me?" | "That's what the proof run tests. Full refund if it doesn't." |
