# DM SCRIPT PLAYBOOK — Complete Outreach System

> **Date**: 2026-02-28
> **Sources**: Nicolas Cole (client acquisition), Nate Herk (pre-warm + Trojan Horse), Jason Fladlien (objection handling + sales flow)
> **Rule**: Personalize EVERY message. Reference their actual content. If you can't personalize it, don't send it.

---

## PHASE 0: PRE-WARM PROTOCOL (Days 1-5 Before First DM)

**This is non-negotiable.** Do NOT skip this. Pre-warmed prospects accept connections at 40-45% vs 15-20% cold.

### Day 1
- Follow their profile (don't connect yet)
- Turn on notification bell for their posts

### Days 2-4
- Leave 2-3 substantive comments on their posts
- Each comment: 50+ words, adds a unique insight, asks a follow-up question
- **Not**: "Great post!" / "Love this!" / "So true!"
- **Instead**: "This mirrors what I'm seeing with [related observation]. The pattern: [your insight]. Curious if you've noticed [related thing]?"

### Day 5
- View their profile (they see the notification)

### Day 6
- Send connection request (they recognize your name from comments)

## PHASE 1: CONNECTION REQUEST (Day 6)

### Template A — Content Reference (Best conversion)
```
Hey [Name], your post on [specific topic] hit different — especially [specific detail or quote from their post]. Most coaches I see talk about [generic thing], but you actually understand [specific insight they shared]. Would love to connect.
```

### Template B — Methodology Recognition
```
Hey [Name], been following your content on [topic]. You clearly have a structured approach to [their area of expertise] — not just talking points but an actual methodology. That's rare. Would love to connect.
```

### Template C — Peer-Level (for well-known coaches)
```
Hey [Name], your framework on [specific framework they teach] is the clearest version of [concept] I've seen on LinkedIn. I study how experts package their methodologies — yours is exceptionally well-structured. Let's connect.
```

**Length**: 40-80 words max. No pitch. Pure recognition that you paid attention.

## PHASE 2: OPENING DM (Send after they accept)

**Wait 2-4 hours after acceptance before DMing.** Immediate DMs feel automated.

### Script A — The Diagnostic Question (Highest conversion)

```
[Name], quick question —

Are you actively posting on LinkedIn,
or is it one of those "I know I should but I don't" situations?
```

**Why it works**: Binary question forces engagement. No pitch. Opens diagnosis.

**Expected responses**:
- **"I post but not consistently"** → Go to Script 2A
- **"I know I should but don't have time"** → Go to Script 2B
- **"Not really, why?"** → Go to Script 2C
- **No response after 3 days** → Go to Follow-Up Sequence

---

### Script B — The Value-First Opener

```
Hey [Name], just read your post about [topic].

Strong insight on [specific thing].

One quick thought — your hook could hit harder
if you led with the outcome instead of the setup.

Something like:

"[Rewritten hook example using their actual post]"

Then drop into your story.

Anyway, that's my ghostwriter brain — can't turn it off.
Keep posting.
```

**Why it works**: Demonstrates skill immediately. No ask. Pure value. If they engage, you've already proven you can improve their content.

**If they respond positively** → Go to Script 2D

---

### Script C — The Trojan Horse (Nate Herk)

```
Hey [Name],

I ran your last 3 posts through my content analysis system.

Your methodology is strong.
The hook architecture just needs restructuring.

Happy to show you what the system flagged.
Takes 15 min. Zero obligation.
```

**Why it works**: Curiosity + specificity. You're offering to diagnose, not sell. Frame: you're doing them a favor.

```
Want me to send you the details?
```

```
One 30-min call — you talk about your methodology,

then turn it into posts that sound like you.

Not a ghostwriter guessing at your voice.
A system that actually captures it.

Worth testing?
```

## PHASE 4: FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE (When they don't reply)

**Critical**: 50-70% of responses come from follow-ups. If you stop at Message 1, you're leaving most of your revenue on the table.

### Follow-Up 1 (3 days after no reply)

```
[Name], no worries if now's not the right time.

I just wrapped a test — coach went from
2 posts/week (4 hours each) to 5 posts/week
(15 min review time). Same voice, 10x output.

If you ever want to see how this would work
for [their specific niche], happy to run it.

No strings.
```

### Follow-Up 2 (3 days later)

```
[Name], last thought on this —

I analyzed the top 50 [their niche] coaches on LinkedIn.

Found a pattern: the ones with 20K+ followers
post 4-5x/week using a 3-part framework:
1. Personal story (hook)
2. Tactical insight (value)
3. Soft CTA (engagement)

Your content already has #2 and #3.
If you leaned into #1 more, I think you'd
3x your reach in 90 days.

Worth testing?

P.S. — If you want the full breakdown,
I can send it over. Totally free.
Just think your work deserves a bigger audience.
```

### Follow-Up 3 — The Breakup (4 days later — FINAL)

```
[Name], circling back one last time.

If LinkedIn content isn't a priority right now,
all good — genuinely no hard feelings.

But if it IS a priority and you're just swamped,
let's do a 15-min call. I'll show you
the system, you tell me if it's
worth your time.

Calendar link: [Calendly]

If I don't hear back, I'll assume
it's a no and stop reaching out.

Either way, rooting for you.
```

**After the breakup**: STOP. Move to next prospect. If they come back later, they come back.

```

from a live conversation.
```

```
You're not committing to monthly —
```

```
If after 5 posts you don't feel like

You keep the posts.

Zero risk.
```

```
You're not betting on my track record.
```

## RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

1. **Personalize every message.** Reference their actual posts, frameworks, or content. Generic = delete.
2. **Never pitch in the connection request.** Connection = permission to talk. Not permission to sell.
3. **Lead with value or diagnosis, never the offer.** The offer comes after they've confirmed the problem.
4. **Follow up. Then follow up again.** 50-70% of responses come from follow-ups 2-5.
5. **Respect the breakup.** After Follow-Up 3, stop. Desperation kills deals.
6. **Track everything.** If you can't measure it, you can't improve it.
7. **Voice notes boost response rates 2x.** Use them on high-value prospects (mobile LinkedIn app → hold mic icon).
8. **Never send more than 20 connection requests/day.** LinkedIn will restrict your account.
9. **Post on your own LinkedIn daily.** When prospects check your profile (they will), they need to see activity.
10. **The proof run IS the sales tool.** Don't oversell. Let the work close the deal.
