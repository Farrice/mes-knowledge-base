# ⚡️ The 7-Day / $500 Revenue Sprint

We've assembled a specialized 3-agent swarm to attack this specific goal: **Chris Do** (Offer Architecture), **Stockton Walbeck** (Lead Magnets), and **Lara Acosta** (LinkedIn Conversion).

To hit $500 in 7-14 days, you do *not* need to sell a full $2,500 build to a cold lead. You need to leverage **urgency, exclusivity, and a low-friction "foot-in-the-door" offer.**

Here is the exact playbook the swarm devised for you.

---

## 1. The "$500 Cash Collected" Offer (Chris Do)
*The $2,500 main offer requires trust. $500 simply requires intense curiosity and a clear ROI. We have two ways to do this:*

**Option A: The "Beta Build" Trade (Highest Conversion Probability)**
You offer the full $2,500 sprint for $500 (80% off) in exchange for a video testimonial. 
> *"I'm taking on 2 beta clients this week to test the final workflow for my AI Brain Build Sprint. Normal price is $2,500. Your price is $500 out-of-pocket, plus a video testimonial when we're done."*
*Why it works:* It removes all price friction for warm leads. You only need to send the Outreach Scripts to your warm network to close ONE of these.

**Option B: The "Extraction & Blueprint" Session (High Value, Low Delivery Time)**
You sell just the *first step* of the sprint. 
> *"For $500, we do a 90-minute deep-dive extraction of your core coaching methodology. I will organize your scattered frameworks and hand you the exact structural Blueprint for an AI Brain. You can build it yourself, or apply the $500 toward me building it."*
*Why it works:* It's a paid discovery call. It pays you $500 today, and creates a highly-qualified lead for the $2,000 upsell later.

## 2. The Lead Magnet (Stockton Walbeck)
*The goal is to get "hand-raisers"—people admitting they have the problem you solve.*

**The Concept:** "The Scattered Expert Audit" (or "The AI Brain Blueprint")
Do not write a 40-page PDF. Create a simple, 3-page Notion document or a 5-minute Loom video.

**The Hook:**
"Are you a coach with a $50K course graveyard and 47 abandoned Google Docs? I recorded a 5-minute Loom showing exactly how I organize 10 years of coaching methodology into a single 'AI Brain'."

**The Delivery:**
A short video showing a glimpse of an AI Brain in action. It proves it's not just "prompting ChatGPT." It shows a real system. 
**The CTA in the video:** "If you want me to build this for you, here's my calendar."

---

## 3. The LinkedIn Featured Section Architecture (Lara Acosta)
*Your Featured section should act as a funnel. Right now, a user landing on your profile needs to know EXACTLY what to click.*

**The 3-Slot Setup:**

1. **Slot 1 (The Hook): A High-Performing Post**
   - *What to pin:* Pin the "Day 1 Launch Post" (The 'I spent 8 months' post from your playbook). 
   - *Why:* It tells the story of *why* you do this and agitates the core pain point.

2. **Slot 2 (The Hand-Raiser): The Lead Magnet**
   - *What to pin:* A link to your Lead Magnet Loom video or Notion doc.
   - *Title:* "Free: Inside an AI Brain 🧠"
   - *Why:* Gives cold traffic a zero-risk way to sample your expertise.

3. **Slot 3 (The Offer): The $500 Quick Win**
   - *What to pin:* A direct link to a Stripe checkout or Calendly booking for the Beta Build or Extraction Session.
   - *Title:* "Apply: AI Brain Beta Build ($500)"
   - *Why:* Captures the high-intent buyers immediately.
