## Gig 2: "I Will Create an AI Workflow System for Your Coaching or Consulting Business"

**Tagline:** *Stop re-explaining your business to AI every time you open a new chat.*

---

**Description:**

You know the drill.

Open ChatGPT. Paste your notes. Write 4 paragraphs of context. Get an okay response. Close the tab. Do it again tomorrow. From scratch.

That's not a workflow. That's a hamster wheel.

I build **AI workflow systems** — custom-built around how YOU actually work, grounded in YOUR content, running on YOUR expertise.

**What a workflow system looks like in practice:**

→ **Content workflow**: You say "draft a LinkedIn post on [topic]." Your AI pulls from your frameworks, case studies, and voice — and writes something that sounds like you, not like a robot summarizing a blog post.

→ **Client prep workflow**: Before a coaching session, your AI reviews the client's notes and surfaces the three questions you'd want to ask. Based on YOUR methodology, not generic coaching prompts.

→ **Sales follow-up workflow**: After a discovery call, your AI drafts a follow-up email in your voice — referencing what was actually discussed, using your frameworks, closing the way YOU close.

Each workflow is built on **your knowledge base**. Not prompt templates you bought for $27. Your real content, your real frameworks, your real language.

**This is built for you if:**
- You spend 5-10 hours/week manually feeding context to AI tools
- You have expertise that AI should already know about your business — but doesn't
- You want AI that runs your business processes, not just answers random questions

**My background:**
18+ years building systems for how humans actually think and work. Now I build the AI systems that match.
