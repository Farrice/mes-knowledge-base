## 1. LinkedIn Headline

> [!TIP]
> LinkedIn gives you ~220 characters. The headline shows everywhere — search results, comments, DMs. It's your billboard.

### Option A — The Pattern Interrupt
```
Your AI doesn't know your business. I fix that. → Custom AI Brains for Coaches & Consultants
```

**Three Rules Test:**
- Visualization ✅ — "AI doesn't know your business" is a scene Marcus lives daily
- Falsifiability ✅ — Either the AI knows their business or it doesn't
- Uniqueness ✅ — No competitor can sign this because no one else builds "AI Brains"

### Option B — The Juxtaposition
```
18 years studying how people change. Now I build AI that thinks like you do. → AI Brain Builder
```

**Three Rules Test:**
- Visualization ✅ — "18 years" and "AI that thinks like you" are concrete
- Falsifiability ✅ — Verifiable background claim
- Uniqueness ✅ — The psychology-to-AI bridge is his alone

### Option C — The Direct Offer
```
I build your AI Brain → Your expertise + your voice + your frameworks, in one custom AI. DM "BRAIN"
```

**Three Rules Test:**
- Visualization ✅ — "Your expertise, your voice, your frameworks" paints the deliverable
- Falsifiability ✅ — Either it has their voice/frameworks or it doesn't
- Uniqueness ✅ — Specific deliverable no one else offers by name

> **Recommendation:** **Option A** for launch. It leads with the felt pain Marcus experiences every time he opens ChatGPT and gets generic output. The pattern interrupt ("I fix that") creates a micro-curiosity loop that earns the click. Option B is your credibility play if you want to lean on authority. Option C is the conversion play once you have social proof to back the direct claim.

## 3. LinkedIn Banner Spec

> Visual brief for a designer or AI image tool (Canva, Midjourney, etc.)

**Concept**: Dark, minimal, premium — not "tech startup blue"

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| **Background** | Deep charcoal/near-black (#0A0A0A to #161616 gradient) |
| **Accent color** | Warm amber (#F5A623) — matches lead magnet HTML |
| **Typography** | Space Grotesk or Inter — clean, modern, no serifs |
| **Primary text** | "I build AI Brains for coaches & consultants" |
| **Secondary text** | "Your expertise. Your voice. One custom AI." |
| **CTA element** | Small pill/badge: `DM "BRAIN" to start` |
| **Visual element** | Subtle neural network or constellation pattern (abstract, not literal brain imagery) |
| **Avoid** | Stock photos, headshots on the banner, neon colors, busy layouts, robot imagery |
| **Dimensions** | 1584 × 396px (LinkedIn recommended) |

**Mood**: The banner should feel like the interface of something premium and custom-built. Think Notion's brand energy — dark, confident, no noise.
