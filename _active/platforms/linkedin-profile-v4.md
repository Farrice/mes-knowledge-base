## WHY THIS VERSION IS DIFFERENT

- **V3 said "that's the gap I close." V4 says "I write your LinkedIn posts for you."** Plain English. A 12-year-old understands it. No metaphors for the service -- just the service, stated directly.

- **V3 used "Curse of Knowledge" and described the process ("I capture your frameworks, your stories, your specific language"). V4 cuts all process language and replaces it with outcomes.** "People DM you. They book calls. They say 'I feel like I already know you.'" Coaches don't care HOW it works. They care WHAT happens.

- **V3 was a story. V4 is a sales page compressed into 1,070 characters.** It follows PAS exactly: Pain (blank screen, 45 minutes, generic), Agitation (less-qualified coaches winning), Solution (I write your posts), Dream Outcome (DMs, calls, "I already know you"), Differentiator (your voice, not ghostwriter filler), CTA (DM me POSTS). Every section does one job and moves to the next.
