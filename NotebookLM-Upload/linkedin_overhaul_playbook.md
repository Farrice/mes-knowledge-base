## COMMENT ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

### Where to Engage (Daily, 15-20 min)

1. **Top coaching voices** on LinkedIn — comment with genuine insight, not flattery
2. **Posts about AI + coaching** — add your contrarian take ("generic AI isn't the answer...")
3. **Posts about content creation struggles** — drop a raw truth from the Bullseye research
4. **Your own connections' posts** — build reciprocity

### How to Comment (Caleb Ralston Rule: Optimize for Trust)

**Template**: [Specific observation about their post] + [Your unique insight or experience] + [Optional micro-CTA]

**Example**:
```
This resonates hard. I've been building AI Brains for coaches and the #1 thing I've learned is that the block isn't technical. It's identity. They're hiring for the organization of their own expertise.
```

**What NOT to do**:
- ❌ "Great post!" (zero value)
- ❌ Long paragraphs about your offer (feels salesy)
- ❌ Tagging people randomly
- ✅ Add a genuine insight that makes people click your profile
