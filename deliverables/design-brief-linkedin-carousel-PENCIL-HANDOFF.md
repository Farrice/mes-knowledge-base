## Troubleshooting

- **Frames don't appear**: Make sure Pencil canvas is open and MCP connection is active. Try `/mcp` in Claude Code to verify.
- **Agent stalls mid-slide**: Break the slide into 2 prompts — structure first (text elements), then styling second (colors, fonts, spacing).
- **Fonts not rendering**: DM Serif Display and Inter are Google Fonts. If unavailable in Pencil, substitute with any serif + sans-serif pairing.
- **Colors look different**: Pencil may interpret hex codes differently at various opacity levels. The background should be very dark, the text should be light/warm.
