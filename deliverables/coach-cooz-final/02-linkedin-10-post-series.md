## DISTRIBUTED COMMENTING STRATEGY (Zero-to-Authority Growth Engine)

For a new LinkedIn account, posting alone is not enough. The primary audience acquisition mechanism is strategic commenting on other creators' content.

### Daily Commenting Protocol
- **Volume:** 30-50 substantive comments per day (30-60 minute block)
- **Target creators:** 20-30 LinkedIn profiles whose audience overlaps with Coach Cooz's ICP — executive coaches, leadership thought leaders, C-suite content creators, health/performance content
- **Comment quality:** Each comment adds a NEW insight, challenges the premise constructively, or tells a relevant micro-story. Never "Great post!" Never a pitch.
- **Goal:** The comment itself should be so valuable that people click through to the profile

### Comment Templates

**The Diagnostic Comment:**
> "This reminds me of something I see in every executive I work with — [specific pattern]. The missing piece most people don't address is [body-first insight]. Curious if you've noticed this too."

**The Micro-Story Comment:**
> "Had a client who said almost exactly this. He was running a $10M company on 4 hours of sleep. The fix wasn't what he expected — it started with his body, not his business. [1-2 sentence result]."

**The Reframe Comment:**
> "Interesting take. I'd push back slightly — most executives aren't burned out. Something went dormant. The distinction matters because the fix is completely different."
