## CRITICAL RULES FOR MONTH 1

1. **Post consistency > post perfection.** A good post published beats a perfect post in drafts.
2. **DMs > Comments.** Real conversations happen in DMs. Comments are visibility.
3. **Strategy Calls > Everything.** If you have to choose between posting and conducting a Strategy Call, take the call.
4. **Don't discount.** If someone can't afford $10K, offer the $4,997 Intensive. Never go below.
5. **Weekly AAR is non-negotiable.** Every Sunday. Numbers. Diagnosis. Adjustment.
6. **Record the wins.** Screenshot every positive DM, every comment that says "this is me," every Strategy Call booking. You'll need these for momentum and testimonials.
7. **Lead with confidence, not guarantees.** Don't offer percentage-based refunds. Use the confidence statement: "The system works if you do. You'll feel it in your sleep by Day 7." A conditional guarantee creates more friction than having no guarantee at all for this buyer.
8. **Single-offer presentation on Strategy Calls.** Present only The Resurrection ($10K). Introduce the Intensive ($4,997) ONLY as a down-sell if the prospect explicitly signals price resistance. Never present both options side by side — it creates decision paralysis.
