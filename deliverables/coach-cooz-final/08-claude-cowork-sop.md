## PROMPT 2: STRATEGY CALL SCRIPT

**What it produces:** A complete 45-60 minute Strategy Call script with the dormant power framing

**Attach:** `01-narrative-and-positioning.md`

**Prompt:**
```
Write a complete Strategy Call script for Coach Cooz (The Resurrection Coach).

This is a 45-60 minute diagnostic call with high-performing executives ($150K+). The goal is to diagnose what went dormant, demonstrate value through the AAR framework, and naturally transition to the $10,000 Resurrection offer (or $4,997 Intensive as bridge).

POSITIONING: Resurrection = Dormant Power Reactivation. Not crisis recovery. These executives aren't broken — something went dormant while they built the empire.

Script structure:

PHASE 1: RAPPORT (10 min)
- Warm opening — "Thanks for being here. Before we dive in, what made you decide to book this call?"
- Listen for dormancy signals (lost edge, empty despite success, body declining)
- Mirror back what they said using dormant power language

PHASE 2: DIAGNOSIS / AAR (20 min)
- Run the After-Action Report framework:
  - "Let's look at the last 12 months. What was SUPPOSED to happen?"
  - "What ACTUALLY happened?"
  - "Why the gap?"
- Identify which archetype they are (Hollow Emperor, Exhausted Operator, Fading Titan, Isolated Builder)
- Name what went dormant (body, edge, connection, purpose)
- Ask body-state questions: sleep, training, energy, nutrition
- Bridge: "Here's what I've seen in 17 years — the body is always the bottleneck"

PHASE 2.5: IDENTITY BRIDGE (5 min)
- Deliver the Binary Identity Lock: "I see two versions of you. The one on this call... and the one your wife fell in love with. Which one do you want to reactivate?"

PHASE 3: THE REACTIVATION PATH (10 min)
- Present the 3-phase approach: Stabilization → Installation → Integration
- Share the 8 Tenets briefly
- Case study: "I had a client, CEO, came in at 215 lbs..." (28 lbs lost, $3M deal)
- Personal proof: "I was a college quarterback. That leader went dormant. Fitness brought it back."

PHASE 4: THE OFFER (15 min)
- "Based on what we've diagnosed today, here's what I'd recommend..."
- Present The Resurrection ($10,000, 90 days) — lay out exactly what they get
- If price resistance → offer The Intensive ($4,997, 30 days) with $2,500 credit
- Payment plan option: $5,500 x 2 or $3,750 x 3

PHASE 5: CLOSE (5 min)
- "Do you want to reactivate? Or keep operating with the hardware offline?"
- Handle objections (time, money, "I can do this myself")
- Set next step: onboarding call within 48 hours if yes
- If not ready: "No pressure. You have the AAR. Execute it yourself. My door's open."

Include 6-7 objection handling scripts:
- "I don't have time"
- "$10K is a lot"
- "I can do this myself"
- "I need to think about it"
- "I need to talk to my wife"
- "Let me talk to my wife"

Voice: Direct, diagnostic, never salesy. Questions > statements. Make THEM realize the need.
```

**Prompt:**

7. THE FILTER (6:00-6:30): "This isn't for everyone. If you want motivation, hire a speaker. If you want reactivation, book the call."

Voice: Speaking directly to camera. Personal, not polished. Like he's talking to a friend at a bar who happens to be a CEO.

No emojis. No hype. No "Are you ready to CRUSH it?" energy.

Write it as a spoken script with [PAUSE] and [BEAT] directions.
