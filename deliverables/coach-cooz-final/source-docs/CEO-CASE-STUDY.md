# EXECUTIVE RESURRECTION: CASE STUDY
## The CEO Who Lost 28 Pounds and Closed a $3M Deal in 90 Days

---

## CLIENT PROFILE

- **Role:** CEO, tech company
- **Age:** 42
- **Archetype:** Fading Titan (dormant edge, archived decisiveness)

---

## BEFORE STATE (Day 0)

| Metric | Starting Point |
|--------|---------------|
| Weight | 215 lbs |
| Sleep | 4 hours/night |
| Work hours | 75 hours/week |

**What went dormant:**
His edge. The version of him that built the company — the one who made fast calls, walked into rooms and changed the energy, closed deals on instinct — got archived under a decade of scaling, managing, and optimizing for safety.

**How it showed up:**
- Brain fog by 2 PM every day
- Defaulting to "safe plays" instead of bold calls
- Irritable at home, distracted at work
- Living on caffeine and adrenaline
- Body felt like it belonged to someone else

---

## THE PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Stabilization (Weeks 1-4) — Reactivate the Hardware

**Sleep Protocol:**
- No screens after 8 PM
- Magnesium supplementation
- Cold room (65-67°F)
- Result: 4 hours → 7 hours in 2 weeks

**Flexible Nutrition:**
- 200g protein/day target
- No meal plans, no restrictions
- Compliance over perfection
- Result: Sustained energy, brain fog clearing

**Training:**
- 3x/week full-body strength training
- 45-minute sessions
- Progressive overload (strength-focused, not cardio)
- Calibrated to current capacity, not ego

**Daily Accountability:**
- Voxer check-ins (5 min/day max)
- Weekly AAR (After-Action Report)

**Phase 1 Result:** Energy went from 3/10 to 7/10. Brain fog cleared. Decision-making improved.

---

### Phase 2: Installation (Weeks 5-8) — Install the Operating System

**The 8 Tenets installed:**
Silence, Movement, Fuel, Recovery, Accountability, Radical Self-Ownership, The Benevolent No, Purpose Calibration

**Weekly AAR framework running:**
What was supposed to happen → What actually happened → Why the gap → What to adjust

**Daily accountability continued:**
Voxer check-ins + tracking

**Phase 2 Result:** Systems on autopilot. Less reactive, more proactive. The buried edge came back.

---

### Phase 3: Integration (Weeks 9-13) — Recalibrate Purpose

**Purpose Calibration:**
"What direction am I traveling?" (not what am I achieving)

**Relationship Repair:**
Rebuilt connection with wife — presence requires fuel. When the hardware came back online, he was physically and emotionally present again.

**Business Clarity:**
With the fog cleared and decision-making restored, he operated from instinct again. Closed a $3M deal — the biggest of his career.

**Phase 3 Result:** Full integration. Body, mind, and business running on the same operating system.

## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR PROSPECTS

1. **We didn't start with mindset.** We started with sleep, nutrition, and movement. The body is the infrastructure.

2. **Results came faster than expected.** Brain fog cleared by Week 2. Energy restored by Week 4. The edge returned by Week 8.

3. **It's not a fitness program.** It's an operating system reactivation. The body is the hardware. Everything else runs on it.

4. **50 hours/week > 75 hours/week.** When the hardware works, you don't need to brute-force output. Quality decisions replace quantity of hours.

5. **The relationship repair was a side effect.** When you're physically present, energized, and clear — you show up differently at home. Not because of therapy. Because of biology.

6. **The science backs the protocol.** Chronic sleep restriction elevates cortisol by 37%, degrading executive decision-making by up to 40% (Journal of Occupational Health Psychology). The Body-First Protocol targets these biomarkers directly — sleep, cortisol regulation, inflammatory markers — before touching mindset or strategy. The results aren't motivational. They're biochemical.
