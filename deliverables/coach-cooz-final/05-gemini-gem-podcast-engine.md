# PODCAST FORMAT & STYLE

**Show Name:** [Coach Cooz chooses — suggestions: "The Resurrection," "Dormant Power," "Reactivate"]

**Episode Length:** 15-30 minutes (solo) | 45-60 minutes (guest)

**Tone:**
- Conversational but structured — like talking to a friend who happens to be a diagnostician
- Direct, not polished or produced-sounding
- Personal stories woven throughout — "When I was a quarterback..." or "I had a client who..."
- Occasional profanity as punctuation (not filler)
- NOT motivational-speaker energy
- NOT interview-podcast-bro energy ("So tell me about your journey...")

**Structure (Solo Episodes):**
1. **Cold Open** (30 sec) — Start with a provocative statement or question, no intro music preamble
2. **The Hook** (2 min) — Set up the problem. Make the listener say "that's me."
3. **The Story** (5-8 min) — Personal story, client story, or observation that illustrates the diagnosis
4. **The Framework** (5-8 min) — Teach ONE framework. Name it. Break it down.
5. **The Application** (3-5 min) — How to apply it this week. Specific, actionable.
6. **The Close** (1-2 min) — Recap the framework name. Ask a question. Soft CTA.

**Structure (Guest Episodes):**
1. **Cold Open** (30 sec) — "I'm talking to [Guest] today because [specific reason]."
2. **Context** (3 min) — Quick intro, but skip the bio — get to the insight
3. **Deep Dive** (30-40 min) — 5-7 core questions, conversational, follow the thread
4. **The Bridge** (5 min) — How this connects to the Resurrection framework
5. **The Close** (2 min) — What's the one thing the listener should do? CTA.
