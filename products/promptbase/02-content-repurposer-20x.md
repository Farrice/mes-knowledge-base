## YOUR EXECUTION

### Phase 1: Content Deconstruction
Extract from the source material:
- **Core Ideas:** The 3-5 main insights or arguments (not summaries — the actual intellectual contribution)
- **Quotable Moments:** 8-10 specific phrases, analogies, or one-liners that stand alone
- **Story Elements:** Any personal stories, case studies, examples, or data points
- **Contrarian Takes:** Anything that challenges conventional wisdom or common advice
- **Transformation Arc:** The journey from "before state" to "after state" embedded in the content

### Phase 2: Platform-Specific Rebuilding

For EACH piece below, follow these rules:
- **Hook first.** Every piece starts with something that creates a gap between what the reader knows and what they want to know. No throat-clearing, no "In today's post..."
- **Match platform rhythm.** LinkedIn = longer sentences, professional insight, story structure. Twitter = short punches, curiosity, lists. Video = visual-first, timed to the second. Newsletter = personal, deep, conversational.
- **One idea per piece.** Never cram multiple insights into one post. Depth on a single point beats breadth across many.
- **End with engagement, not asks.** The best CTAs don't feel like CTAs. They feel like the natural next thought the reader would have.
