## CROSS-PLATFORM CONSISTENCY GUIDE `<a name="cross-platform"></a>`

### Brand Voice Rules (All Platforms)

| Principle | What It Means in Practice |
|---|---|
| **Clinical, not motivational** | "Your cortisol is chronically elevated" > "Let's crush this!" |
| **Peer-level, not superior** | "Here's what I've observed in executives at your level" > "You need to stop being lazy" |
| **Mythic, not medical** | "The version of you that went dormant" > "Your BMI indicates obesity" |
| **Data-backed, not vague** | "47 days to reverse pre-diabetic markers" > "Feel better in weeks" |
| **Composed, not intense** | "I bring clinical precision" > "I'll hold you accountable no matter what!" |
| **Private, not exhibitionist** | "Confidential, executive-grade partnership" > "Tag your transformation journey!" |

### Visual Consistency

| Element | LinkedIn | Instagram | Website |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Color** | Navy (#1B2341) | Navy (#1B2341) | Navy (#1B2341) |
| **Secondary** | Slate (#64748B) | Slate (#64748B) | Slate (#64748B) |
| **Accent** | Cream (#F5F0E8) | Gold (#C4A35A) | Cream + Gold |
| **Typography** | Platform default | Playfair + Inter | Playfair + Inter |
| **Imagery** | Professional headshot, clean banner | Composed, premium feel | "Private Club" aesthetic |
| **Tone** | Authority thought leadership | Accessible education | Conversion-focused |

### The One-Line Test

Before publishing ANYTHING on any platform, it must pass this test:

> **"Would a $300K/year executive who doesn't know Acusio look at this and think: 'This person operates at my level'?"**

If the answer is no — rewrite it, reshoot it, or delete it.
