## DELEGATION PROTOCOL

**How to Hand Off a Process Using SOPs:**

**Step 1: Ensure SOP Exists**

Before delegating anything, document it. You can't delegate what isn't captured.

**Step 2: Share and Review Together**

Walk through the SOP with the person taking over. Answer questions. Identify gaps.

**Step 3: Observed Execution**

They do it while you watch. Provide feedback. Note where SOP could be clearer.

**Step 4: Independent Execution with Check**

They do it alone. You review the output. One round of feedback.

**Step 5: Full Handoff**

They own it. You check periodically but don't micromanage.

**The "Two-Week Rule":**

Any process you've documented should be fully delegatable within two weeks of assigning it. If it takes longer, the SOP is incomplete.
