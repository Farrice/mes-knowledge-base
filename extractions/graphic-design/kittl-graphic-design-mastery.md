## üîë KEY PRINCIPLES (Memorize These)

### Font Selection
- **3 mood words** ‚Üí Font search terms
- **Serif** = Ethereal, elegant, nostalgic, romantic
- **Sans-serif** = Techy, sporty, modern, brutalist
- **Height contrast pairing**: Tall headline ‚Üí Wide subtitle (and vice versa)

### Spacing
- **Tight tracking** (-20 to -60) = Modern, aggressive, dense
- **Expanded tracking** (+100 to +300) = Elegant, airy, secondary status
- **Compressed line spacing** (-20 to -40) = Headlines feel unified, impactful

### Hierarchy
- **Size** is obvious; also use **weight**, **color value**, **tracking**, **position**
- Gray text (40-60% value) = Instant secondary status without shrinking

### AI Prompting
- **Technical terms FIRST** (shot type, lighting) ‚Üí then subject
- **Line breaks** = Semantic separators (AI parses better)
- **Positive instructions** ("only blues") beat negatives ("no red")
- **Edit prompts** = Minimal words; Generation prompts = Comprehensive

### Optical Centering
- Mathematical center ‚â† Visual center
- Script tails pull visual weight ‚Üí Shift 3-5% opposite direction
- Always step back and ask: "Does this LOOK centered?"
