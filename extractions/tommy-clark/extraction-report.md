## Genius Patterns

### 1. The "How I" Narrative Pivot
- **What They Do Unconsciously**: When constructing a take, Tommy instinctively anchors it to a concrete, personal anecdote or client story rather than a generic explanation. 
- **Executable Behavior**: Replace "how to" content formats with "how I" formats. Pair every single opinion or framework with a specific narrative example (e.g., "instead of 'why LinkedIn is important,' tell the story of a founder going from 0 to 30k followers").
- **Deployment Context**: When drafting middle-of-funnel (MoF) and top-of-funnel (ToF) content that risks sounding like ChatGPT output.
- **Success Metric**: High engagement on posts that previously would have been scrolled past; comments referencing the specific story told.

### 2. The Stealth Hook
- **What They Do Unconsciously**: Tommy applies proven psychological hooks (negativity bias, specific numbers, credibility jacking) but heavily camouflages them in conversational, almost dismissive language.
- **Executable Behavior**: Write the optimal formulaic hook. Then, rewrite it to sound like a casual slack message to a peer while retaining the underlying psychological levers (e.g., specific numbers or namedropping).
- **Deployment Context**: The first 1-3 lines of any LinkedIn post.
- **Success Metric**: Increased read-more clicks; absence of "written by AI" accusations or marketing fatigue responses.

### 3. Audience Design Architecture
- **What They Do Unconsciously**: He bypasses the algorithmic feed by forcibly injecting targeted users into the content flywheel through selective outbound connections and surgical commenting.
- **Executable Behavior**: Identify specific ICP roles (e.g., "Heads of Design at Series A startups"). Send exactly 20 targeted connection requests every weekday. Leave exactly 5 high-quality comments daily on posts from overlapping ICP creators or influencers. 
- **Deployment Context**: Daily growth routine; specifically when starting a new account or pivoting an audience.
- **Success Metric**: 10 minutes spent daily generating high-quality inbound profile views from exact target roles.

### 4. The Content Interview System
- **What They Do Unconsciously**: Tommy completely separates the extraction of ideas from the drafting of content, treating the founder as the "marble" and the team/agency as the "sculptor."
- **Executable Behavior**: Schedule a bi-weekly 45-minute recording session. Use 5-10 pre-planned prompts to extract conversational insights, stories, and takes. Transcribe the recording and use it as the exclusive source material for the next two weeks of content drafting.
- **Deployment Context**: Scaling founder-led content without consuming the founder's schedule. 
- **Success Metric**: 5 weekly posts generated from a single 2-hour weekly time commitment (including editing/review).

## Hidden Knowledge

- **The AI Saturation Floor**: Everyone now has access to Claude and ChatGPT, meaning "valuable content" is no longer a differentiator—it is the baseline. The only moat left is personal narrative and idiosyncratic voice, because AI can copy formats and tone, but it cannot invent legitimate personal history.
- **The "Wonky Algorithm" Bypass**: Because LinkedIn's algorithm has extended the lifecycle of posts and become less predictable, relying solely on organic distribution is a failure point. Manually connecting with the target audience forces the algorithm to serve content to those specific individuals, turning a public feed into a targeted distribution channel.

## Methodology

### The Narrative Content Funnel (2026 Standard)
1. **Top of Funnel (1x/week)**: Broad narratives. Designed for maximum impression generation.
2. **Middle of Funnel (3x/week)**: Industry-specific thought leadership anchored firmly in "How I" stories.
3. **Bottom of Funnel (1x/week)**: Explicit sales pitches (case studies, product demos) mapped to the audience built by ToF and MoF.

### The Audience Design Protocol
1. Calculate the exact target ICP (Title + Company scale).
2. Execute 20 outbound connections per weekday to this exact ICP.
3. Identify 2-3 "hub" accounts (creators whose audience consists of your ICP).
4. Execute 5 highly thoughtful comments on these hub accounts per day.

### The 2-Hour Creation Loop
1. Document 5-10 structural prompts related to weekly themes.
2. Conduct 45-minute conversational recording.
3. Extract core transcript.
4. Sculpt transcript into 5 narrative-first posts.
5. Founder review and tone correction.
