# Swarm Synthesis: 'The Resurrection Coach' Brand Realignment

## Executive Summary
The transition from **The Rebuild** to **The Resurrection** shifts the brand from a remedial "repair shop" (low status) to an aspirational "throne room" (high status). The core psychological leverage is the redefinition of Resurrection as **Dormant Power Reactivation**. By framing the executive’s current state as "dormancy" or "latency" rather than "failure," we bypass ego-defenses and position the coach as the catalyst for reclaiming a pre-existing, elite identity.

## Conflicts & Minority Report
### **The "Religious" Conflict**
*   **The Tension:** Most agents (Miller, Mallet, Miner) worry about the word "Resurrection" sounding like a ministry or a recovery program. 
*   **Minority Position (monk-ai):** Argues that the risk isn't just in the *words* but in the *visuals*. To succeed, the branding must be "aggressive and modern" (slate, gold, obsidian) to signal a "Modern Titan" rather than a "Gothic" or "New Age" feel.
*   **Minority Position (jeremy-miner):** Warns against the "Savior Complex." The copy must emphasize that the *prospect* holds the power and the *coach* is merely the catalyst, otherwise, the client will become high-maintenance and dependent.

### **Terminology Preference**
*   While "Dormant Power" is the agreed-upon anchor, **monk-ai** recommends using the word **"Latency"** in sales calls to describe the gap between current results and potential, as it sounds more technical and less judgmental to an executive.
