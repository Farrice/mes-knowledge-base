# Swarm Synthesis: Write 3 viral hooks for an AI consulting service targeting coaches and consultants

## Executive Summary
The expert agents largely agree that effective viral hooks for AI consulting targeting coaches and consultants must aggressively address specific pain points (time scarcity, income ceilings, competitive threat, tech overwhelm) with concrete, benefit-driven AI solutions. The recommended hooks leverage urgency, specificity, and a focus on transformation, not just technology. To maximize impact and achieve sustainable B2B virality, these hooks should be integrated with strong visuals, supported by quantifiable results, and pre-tested for immediate comprehension.

## Unanimous Agreements
| Finding | Supporting Agents |
|-------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| **Pains Drive Urgency/Momentum:** Hooks must directly agitate core professional anxieties (time, money, competition, tech fear) to create immediate resonance and compel action. | cardinal-mason, harry-dry |
| **Specificity & Falsifiability:** Generic claims about AI are ineffective. Hooks require concrete numbers, specific outcomes, or clear systems to make the solution feel real, achievable, and verifiable. | cardinal-mason, harry-dry |
| **Benefit-Centric Transformation:** The focus must be on the tangible *outcome* AI delivers (e.g., more clients, more revenue, less work, increased authority), framing AI as the vehicle for professional transformation. | cardinal-mason, harry-dry |
| **B2B Relevance & Credibility:** For a professional audience, the "virality" stems from direct value, clarity, and addressing immediate professional threats/opportunities, rather than abstract or overly sensationalized claims. | cardinal-mason, harry-dry |
