# Swarm Synthesis: Alignment of 'The Resurrection Coach' Operational Systems

## Executive Summary
The brand "The Resurrection Coach" is being repositioned from a remedial "repair shop" (The Rebuild) to a premium "power plant reactivation" service. This shift leverages **Dormant Power Reactivation** to appeal to the high-status ego of executives ($250K+) who view themselves as dormant titans rather than broken projects. By grounding mythic language in "Hardware-First" systems engineering, the brand justifies its $10K price point and avoids the "crisis-coded" traps of traditional coaching.

*   **DO:** Dormant, buried edge, reclaim, reactivate, system restoration, hardware-first, operating system reset, latent power, apex-state, dormant titan.
*   **DONT:** Coward, dying slowly, broken, rock bottom, crisis, intervention, saving, fixing, repair, pathetic, help, "the rebuild."

## Conflicts & Minority Report
**The Religious/Spiritual Trap:**
There is a consensus risk that "Resurrection" may be interpreted as religious or ecclesiastical. 
*   **Jim O’Shaughnessy** argues that we must keep the imagery "secular-mythic" (Phoenix/Stoicism) to maintain the "Philosopher-Financier" edge.
*   **Nick Saraev** warns that AI safety filters might flag the word "Resurrection" as religious, requiring explicit instruction to the AI to stay within "Industrial/Systems" metaphors.
*   **Oren** posits that this risk is actually a "feature," provided the visual "Vibe" is expensive and high-tech, differentiating it from commodity fitness apps.
