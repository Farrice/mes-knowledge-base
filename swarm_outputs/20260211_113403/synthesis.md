# Swarm Synthesis: The Resurrection Coach Alignment

## Executive Summary
The brand transition from "The Rebuild" to "The Resurrection" shifts Acusio Bivona’s positioning from a "repair shop" for broken men to a "power plant" for elite performers. The core strategy replaces shame-based crisis language with a **Diagnostic/Systemic framework**, treating the executive as a high-performance machine running on "corrupted code" or "legacy hardware." This move elevates the brand to a $10K+ premium category by validating the client’s existing success while identifying the "high-functioning dormancy" that prevents their next leap.

## Conflicts & Minority Report
*   **Falsifiability vs. Poetry:** **Harry-Dry** argues for "falsifiable diagnostics" (concrete, measurable problems), while **Nicolas-Cole** emphasizes "rhythm engineering" and "terminal word power." 
    *   *Recommendation:* Use Cole’s rhythmic prose for the "Aspirational" sections and Dry’s clinical diagnostics for the "Problem/Pain" sections.
*   **The "Woo-Woo" Threshold:** **Cardinal-Mason** and **Lara-Acosta** are highly concerned that "Resurrection" will sound like a spiritual retreat. 
    *   *Minority Position:* To mitigate this, avoid the word "healing" entirely. Replace it with "restoration," "optimization," or "alignment."
