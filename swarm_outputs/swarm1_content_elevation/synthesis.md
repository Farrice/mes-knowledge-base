## DELIVERABLE 3: THE CONTENT FRAMEWORK (THE "DIAGNOSTIC LOOP")

To maintain this level, Acusio must follow the **Diagnostic-to-Optimization (DTO) Loop**:

1.  **The Symptom (The Hook):** Start with a specific, visceral physical or cognitive state (e.g., "The 3 PM slump," "Cardboard steak syndrome," "The 3 AM heart-race").
2.  **The Biological Translation (The Insight):** Explain *why* this is happening using "Hard Science" language. Move it from a "feeling" (weakness) to a "mechanic" (system error). Use terms like: *Cortisol Spikes, Vagal Tone, Latency, Biological Capital.*
3.  **The Contrast (The Archetype):** Briefly mention one of the 4 Archetypes to make the reader feel "seen" (e.g., "This is how the Fading Titan loses his edge").
4.  **The Optimization Path (The CTA):** Offer a "Body-First" solution. Never suggest "trying harder." Always suggest calibrating, resetting, or measuring.

**The "Executive Polish" Checklist:**
*   **The Eraser:** Delete "very," "really," "just," and "believe."
*   **The Terminal Check:** Ensure the last word of every paragraph is a "power word" (e.g., *atrophy, crisis, empire, failure*).
*   **Visual Pacing:** Use the 1/3/1 rhythm (One hook, three context sentences, one takeaway/CTA).
