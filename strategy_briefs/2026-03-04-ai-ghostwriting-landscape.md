The copyright issue creates an invisible premium for human-originated content. A coaching framework that was articulated by the coach (even if structured by AI) is copyrightable. The same framework generated entirely by AI is not. For coaches whose business model depends on intellectual property — courses, books, signature methodologies — the choice between AI-generated and human-originated content is a legal and financial decision, not just a quality one.
