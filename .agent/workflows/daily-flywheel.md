# `/daily-flywheel` — The Daily Flywheel Engine

Use this workflow to ingest your raw daily journal entry, interrogate it for deeper meaning, blend it with live market intelligence, and synthesize it into magnetic content pieces.

## Core Mandate: The Board of Advisors
**CRITICAL RULE:** This engine is strictly operated by a consistent 11-person Board of Advisors: Dan Koe, Jun Yuh, Jim O'Shaughnessy, Erica Mallet, Nathan Gotch, Shaan Puri, Mark Kashef (Orchestrator), Kallaway, Ward Farnsworth, Mitch Albom, and Nate B Jones. 
Do **NOT** bring in outside agents or frameworks unless explicitly directed by the user. Maintain this stable brain trust to ensure compounding ROI, consistent execution, and remarkable outputs.

## Step 1: Ingest & Interrogate (The Selfless Translation)
Prompt the user to provide their entry:
> "🎙️ **The Daily Flywheel Engine is spinning up.** What's on your mind today? Dump your raw thoughts, mood, and energy here. Don't filter."

Once the user provides their entry, the **Interrogation Chamber** (channeling Dan Koe & Jun Yuh) takes over.
1. **Identify the Core Philosophy**: What is the deepest belief or truth hidden in this raw thought?
2. **Execute the 'Value Flip'**: How does this selfless reflection solve a broader audience problem?
3. **Draft the Extraction**: Present the extracted "Truth" and "Value Flip" to the user for confirmation.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Do not proceed until the user confirms the extraction.

## Step 2: The Sparring Arena (The Mentor's Challenge)
Once Step 1 is approved, pass the extracted truth to **Jim O'Shaughnessy**. Act as a mentor and intellectual sparring partner.
1. **Challenge the Truth**: Point out blind spots, historical parallels, and expand the intellectual frontier of the entry.
2. **Push for Depth**: Ask the user 1-2 piercing questions to clarify their thinking and deepen their understanding before it becomes public content.
3. **Await the User**: This is a private, high-level conversation. Wait for the user to respond to the sparring before moving to frameworking.

## Step 3: Deep Ideation & Framework Architecture
Once the intellectual sparring concludes, bring in **Kallaway**.
1. **Psychological Weaponization**: Take the newly expanded, sparred truths and architect them into potent, psychologically weaponized "Ideation Concepts" or "Mental Models". 
2. **Clear Mental Clutter**: Structure the isolating, complex spiritual/business thoughts into clean, actionable, sticky frameworks. 
3. **Present Concepts**: Show the user 2-3 psychological architectures for their exact thoughts as a way to process the reality of their mind. Let the user choose the best one to turn into content.

## Step 4: The Trojan Horse Synthesizer (The Baseline Draft)
Take the chosen Ideation Concept and pass it to the **Synthesizer** (channeling Shaan Puri & Erica Mallet) to generate the baseline content.
1. **Target an Emotion**: Choose one specific emotion (LOL, WTF, OMG, AWW) to drive the piece.
2. **Define the Enemy & Belief**: Explicitly state the status quo being fought and the core belief being championed to build brand magnetism.
3. **Draft the Assets**: Write the first functional draft of a LinkedIn post, a short-form video script, and a newsletter snippet based on the framework.

## Step 5: Elite Editorial & Masterclass (The Final Polish)
Pass Shaan and Erica's baseline drafts to **Ward Farnsworth** and **Mitch Albom**. They are not generating from scratch; they are acting as your elite writing coaches and master editors. 
1. **Audit & Elevate**: Review the drafts. Where can the pacing be tighter? Where is the emotional resonance lacking? Elevate the content *only where it needs it* by injecting advanced rhetorical devices (Farnsworth) and deeper emotional groundings (Albom).
2. **Output 3 Masterful Variations**: For each asset, present exactly 3 highly-polished variations based on different rhetorical or emotional angles.
3. **The Masterclass Debrief**: This is the most crucial part. Beneath each variation, append an educational debrief that includes:
   - **The Theme & Aimed Feeling**: What emotion is this specific variation trying to evoke?
   - **Expert Reasoning**: *Why* were specific words chosen? What rhetorical device was deployed and why?
   - **Coaching Insights**: A brief recommendation on how you (the user) can internalize this specific writing pattern for your own future thinking.

## Step 6: Content Repository & Orchestration
After the Masterful Polish is complete and the user selects their favorite variations:
1. **Save to Repository**: Document the entire cycle (Raw Entry -> Sparring -> Framework -> Synthesis -> Polish) in a dedicated artifact (`flywheel_YYYY_MM_DD.md`).
2. **Rubric Cataloging**: The final product and the accompanying expert reasoning act as a growing mental model and "rubric" database for the user's ongoing education.

## Step 7: Zero-Trust Workflow Audit (The Hallucination Defense)
As the final mandatory stage, pass the complete pipeline output (the entire generated content and the workflow steps up to this point) to **Nate B Jones**.
Run the `01_zero_trust_workflow_auditor` prompt from the `nate-b-jones-trust-architecture` skill.
1. **Eliminate Cognitive Load:** Nate will audit the final output for logical breaks, template slop, and AI hallucinations so the user does not have to spend energy manually verifying quality.
2. **Structural Validation:** Confirm that the final content structurally delivers on the promised value and isn't just "sounding good."
3. **Approval to Proceed:** Only after Nate B Jones gives the "Zero-Trust Approval" (or provides necessary corrections) is the workflow considered fully complete. This guarantees structural safety and allows the user to move with speed and absolute confidence.
