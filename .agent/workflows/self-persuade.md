# /self-persuade

Design question chains where the prospect reaches YOUR conclusion in THEIR words — 10x more powerful than anything you could say.

## Usage

```
/self-persuade
```

## What It Does

Takes a target conclusion and reverse-engineers a question chain that makes the prospect/reader articulate that conclusion themselves. Adapts for sales calls, landing pages, email sequences, and content.

## Execution

1. **Read skill files:**
   - `skills/jeremy-miner-identity-persuasion/genius.md` — Focus on Patterns 24, 27, 26, 25
   - `skills/jeremy-miner-identity-persuasion/workflows/self-persuasion-question-designer.md`

2. **Gather inputs:**
   - What conclusion do you want them to reach?
   - Who is the audience?
   - What context? (sales call, landing page, email, content)
   - What's their current belief that blocks this conclusion?

3. **Execute the Self-Persuasion Question Designer** end-to-end

4. **Deliver:**
   - Target conclusion (in THEIR language, not yours)
   - 5-8 question chain with tonal annotations
   - Context adaptations (sales call version, copy version, email version, content version)
   - Self-persuasion checkpoints
   - Warning flags where the chain might break
