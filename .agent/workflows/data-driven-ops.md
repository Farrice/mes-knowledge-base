# Data-Driven Operations System

Turn a chaotic real estate business into a predictable, data-driven operation.

## Workflow

1. Load `skills/joshua-smith-real-estate/genius.md`
2. Load `skills/joshua-smith-real-estate/workflows/data-driven-operations-system.md`
3. Execute with user's current business state, team size, and pain points
4. Deliver:
   - KPI dashboard design (leads, appointments, conversions, revenue)
   - Daily schedule architecture (time-blocked for production)
   - Performance tracking and review cadence
   - Decision framework based on data, not feelings
5. Quality gate: system is specific enough to implement this week, with exact KPIs and tracking method
