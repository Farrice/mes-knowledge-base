# `/ip-flywheel` — The Interactive IP Flywheel (v2.1)

Takes a validated "Contrarian Truth" (usually a Mini-Brief from `/flywheel-ideas`) and produces a complete, multi-platform, low-friction content package. 

***This workflow enforces a strict Orchestrator model: The AI must generate a Creative/Content Brief and receive explicit user approval BEFORE writing the final assets.***

## When to Use
- User provides a validated Concept/Truth or selects a Mini-Brief.
- User wants short-form, text-heavy assets that are ready to post immediately.

---

## Step 1: In-Loop Ideation (If starting from scratch)
If the user invokes `/ip-flywheel` *without* a specific topic or Mini-Brief:
1. Immediately run a 3-concept Ideation sprint (similar to `/flywheel-ideas`). 
2. Present the 3 options to the user.
3. **WAIT FOR USER APPROVAL** before proceeding to Step 2.

---

## Step 2: The Creative/Content Brief Generation (Mandatory)

Once a topic or Mini-Brief is selected, build the **Master Creative Brief**.

**The Brief must include:**
1. **The Core Hook & Stakes (Kallaway):** Why should the audience care immediately?
2. **The Contrarian Angle (Dan Koe):** What is the unique perspective?
3. **The SUCCESs Framework Breakdown (Heath Bros):** How is this Simple, Unexpected, Concrete, Credible, Emotional, and Story-driven?
4. **The Outline/Beat Sheet:** A high-level view of the arguments we will make in the LinkedIn Essay and Substack Article.
5. **The Vibe/Taste Check (Oren):** The intended aesthetic and tone ("Unbothered Authority", diagnostic, anti-hype).

**Present this Creative Brief to the user.**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **HALT EXECUTION.** You must explicitly ask the user: "Does this Creative Brief look good? Do you want to tweak any of the Lego pieces before I assemble the final 4-Asset Package?" Wait for their explicit GO.

---

## Step 3: Generate the Final 4-Asset Package

Only after the Creative Brief is approved, generate the final assets based strictly on the approved blueprint.

### Asset 1: The LinkedIn Content (Rotating Format)
*Expertise: Harry Dry & Tommy Clark*
Select ONE of the following formats based on the brief:
- **Format A (Punchy Text Post):** Short paragraphs (1-2 lines). Heavy white space. Scroll-stopping headline. Argumentative and diagnostic.
- **Format B (Step-by-Step List):** Action-oriented structure.
- **Format C (Carousel Outline):** Slide-by-slide copy keeping character counts low per slide.

### Asset 2: The Substack Article
*Expertise: Mitch Albom*
- **Length:** 800 - 1500 words. Newsletter style, high thematic resonance.
- **Structure:** Personal intro -> The massive industry problem -> The specific framework -> The Interactive Prompt Kit embed.

### Asset 3: The Interactive Prompt Kit (Notion Public Page)
*Expertise: Oren (Diagnostic Framing)*
- An advanced, XML-tagged, multi-phase AI prompt acting as a ruthless diagnostic consultant.

### Asset 4: The Visual Asset Prompt Sheet (Sean Kochel)
- **Midjourney Prompt:** For a stylized, high-contrast hero image. 
- **Canva/Gamma Concept:** If it's a Carousel or Infographic, provide the exact layout instruction.
