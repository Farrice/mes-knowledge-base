## PHASE 2: GATHER INPUTS

Ask the user for:
1. **Business name** and what it sells
2. **Target customer** — who do they serve?
3. **Existing messaging** — website URL or marketing materials (optional)

If the user provides enough context, proceed. If sparse, ask 2-3 focused questions.

## PHASE 3: EXECUTE THE WORKFLOW

Follow the `01-brandscript-generator.md` workflow exactly:
1. Character Discovery (identify hero's desire)
2. Problem Architecture (3-level problem + villain)
3. Guide Positioning (empathy + authority)
4. The Plan (process + agreement)
5. Call to Action (direct + transitional)
6. Failure Stakes (3 negative consequences)
7. Success Transformation (3 positive outcomes)
8. Synthesis (formatted BrandScript)
9. Bonus (5 survival sound bites)

## PHASE 4: QUALITY GATE

Before delivering, verify:
- [ ] Customer is CLEARLY the hero
- [ ] Product appears at element 4 or later
- [ ] All three problem levels addressed
- [ ] CTA uses resolution formula
- [ ] A 12-year-old could understand every sentence
- [ ] Would stop a daydreaming brain

## OUTPUT

Deliver the complete formatted BrandScript document with one-liner and sound bites.
