### PHASE 3: EXECUTE THE WORKFLOW

Follow the Campaign Architecture Engine workflow exactly:
1. Insight Mining (Observation → Insight → Contrary Truth → POV)

Before delivering, verify:

- Overground concept is genuinely free of product features
- Underground concepts create experiences only humans can deliver
