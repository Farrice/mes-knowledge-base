Diagnose and prescribe interventions for creative innovation capacity using Greg Hoffman's methodology.

## Execution

### PHASE 1: SKILL ACQUISITION (Do this FIRST)

Read these files in order. Do not proceed until you have read ALL of them:

### PHASE 2: GATHER INPUTS

Ask the user for:
- **Who/what**: An individual creator, a team, a company, or a brand
- **Current creative output**: What are they producing? Links, examples, or descriptions
- **Pain point**: What feels broken? (Staleness, sameness, risk aversion, template-dependence)
- **Resources**: Team size, tools, budget range, time constraints

If the user has already provided context, proceed directly.

### PHASE 3: EXECUTE THE WORKFLOW

Follow the Creative Culture Diagnostic workflow exactly:
1. Culture Diagnosis across 5 dimensions (Permission, Volume, Curiosity, Risk, Taste)
2. Prescribe specific interventions for weak dimensions
3. Design a 4-Shot Innovation Offense customized for the team/person
4. Build an implementation roadmap (Week 1 → Month 1 → Quarter 1)

### PHASE 4: QUALITY GATE

Before delivering, verify:
- Prescriptions are specific enough to implement this week
- 4-Shot Offense includes genuinely risky ideas
- Taste training plan is grounded in actual cultural sources
- Root cause is named even if it's uncomfortable

## Output
A Creative Culture Diagnostic with health scorecard, prescriptions, 4-Shot Offense design, and taste training plan.
