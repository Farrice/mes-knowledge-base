# `/flywheel-ideas` — The Ideation Engine

This workflow prevents "blank page syndrome" by generating 3-5 fully validated, psychologically potent "Mini-Briefs." It operates as a research and taste-validation swarm, acting as the fuel injector for the IP and YT flywheels.

## When to Use
- You want fresh content ideas but don't want to start from scratch.
- You want to discover "Shadow Markets" and current anxieties (e.g., the AI Panic).
- You are ready to feed either `/ip-flywheel` or `/yt-flywheel`.

---

## Step 1: Market Intelligence & Shadow Market Scan (The Seena/Samuel Layer) 

**Objective:** Find the gap in the current 2026 zeitgeist.

1. **Deploy Seena Rez (Early Adopter Monitor):**
   - Run `search_web` for current anxieties, complaints, or emerging trends related to the target audience (e.g., "coaches overwhelmed by AI 2026", "solopreneur burnout tools").
   - Look for the *emotional* friction, not just the logical problem.
2. **Deploy Samuel Thompson (Shadow Market Detector):**
   - Filter the anxieties against the "Shadow Market" framework: Is there a high-intent, low-competition sub-segment experiencing this pain?
   - Identify the "flawed common belief" that the majority of the market is currently accepting as truth.

---

## Step 2: Psychological Scaffolding (The Kallaway Layer)

**Objective:** Structure the hook and the stakes so the audience cares immediately.

For the top 3-5 validated pain points identified in Step 1, generate a:
1. **The Core Hook:** What is the scroll-stopping premise?
2. **The Stakes:** What do they lose if they ignore this? (Make it emotional and urgent).
3. **The Contrarian Truth:** What is Farrice's specific, unique angle that shatters the flawed common belief and positions his "AI Brain Builder" (or other offer) as the ultimate solution?

---

## Step 3: Taste Constraints & Validation (The Oren Layer)

**Objective:** Ensure the concepts elevate the "Unbothered Authority" and aren't engagement bait.

Run the 3-5 scaffolded ideas through the following filters:
1. **The "Template Slop" Check:** Does this sound like generic ChatGPT advice? If yes, kill it or rewrite it.
2. **The Premium Standard:** Does this position Farrice as a category-of-one expert, or does it make him sound like a desperate service provider?
3. **The "Unbothered Authority" Tone:** Remove hype words. Ensure the tone is calm, diagnostic, and slightly detached.

---

## Step 4: Output The Concept Dossier (Mini-Briefs)

Present the final 3-5 validated concepts to the user in the following format:

```markdown
# ⛽ THE IDEATION FLYWHEEL — Concept Dossier

## Mini-Brief 1: [Punchy Title]
- **The Shadow Market Pain:** [What they are secretly struggling with right now]
- **The Flawed Common Belief:** [What everyone else is telling them to do]
- **Farrice's Contrarian Truth:** [The unique angle]
- **The Kallaway Stakes:** [Why they must pay attention]
- **Recommended Engine:** [Suggest `/ip-flywheel` or `/yt-flywheel`]

## Mini-Brief 2: [Punchy Title]...
(Repeat for all concepts)
```

**Include this mandatory final CTA:**
> "Which one of these Mini-Briefs should we feed into the engines as our foundation? Reply with the number, and let me know if you want the short-form `/ip-flywheel` or the long-form `/yt-flywheel`."
