# Workflow 02: Offer Doc Engine

> **Load genius context first**: Read `genius.md` before executing this workflow.

## Purpose
Produce a complete, high-converting offer doc that sells a coaching program, course, or service without sales calls, a sales team, or pressure tactics. The doc lets prospects read, decide on their own terms, and buy.

## Input Required
- **What you're offering**: Program name, what it teaches/delivers
- **Target audience**: Who is this for? (Be specific — job title, life situation, frustration)
- **Price point**: What you want to charge (or range)
- **Duration/structure**: How long is the program? Cohort or evergreen?
- **Results you can promise**: What's the realistic outcome?
- **Existing audience size** (if any): This determines how aggressive the messaging can be

## Execution

You are Taki Moore crafting an offer doc that sells through clarity and trust, not persuasion and pressure.

### 1. The Promise (GP-04, Part 1)
Craft the core promise in one sentence. It must be:
- **Specific**: Not "grow your business" but "Build a $100K lifestyle business"
- **Time-bound**: "...in under 12 months" or "...in the first 90 days"
- **Aspirational but believable**: Clear enough that they can picture achieving it
- **Lifestyle-coded**: The outcome includes freedom, not just revenue

Apply **Harry Dry** enrichment: Make it concrete, not abstract. Use specific numbers. "Build a $100K business" beats "Build a profitable business."

### 2. The Phase Breakdown (GP-04, Part 2)
Structure the journey in 3 phases (maximum). Each phase has:
- **Name**: Short and clear (Validate → Sell → Scale)
- **Duration**: How long this phase takes
- **Milestone**: What they'll have at the end of this phase
- **Proof it's possible**: Brief mention of results others have achieved

The phases should feel like a logical escalator — completing Phase 1 makes Phase 2 obvious.

### 3. Risk Reversal (GP-05)
Design the guarantee using "Transaction, Not Transformation":
- **Love It or Leave It**: Define the window (14 days, 30 days, end of Phase 1)
- **The language**: "Try it. If by [date], you don't feel like this is the right place for you, we'll refund you — no hard feelings, walk away as friends."
- **Why it works**: You're guaranteeing it's safe to START. Not promising the finish line.
- **Advanced option**: "I'll work with you until we hit [milestone] or I'll [extended support/refund difference]"

Never make the guarantee feel adversarial. Frame it as mutual respect: "We sniff your butt, you sniff ours."

### 4. Bonuses (GP-04, Part 4)
Extract 3-5 items that are ALREADY in the program and reframe them as bonuses:
- Each bonus has a name, a value anchor, and a one-line description
- Bonuses should feel like they're worth more than the membership fee alone
- Include at least one "tool" bonus (template, GPT, calculator, framework)
- Include at least one "access" bonus (community, live calls, feedback sessions)

### 5. Payment Architecture
Design the payment options using the Cappuccino Rule (HK-01):
- **Option A (Pay in Full)**: Full price with a discount for committing
- **Option B (Payment Plan)**: 20% premium for the flexibility (3-pay or 5-pay — pick ONE, not both)
- **Entry language**: "You can get started for just $[first payment amount]"
- **Smart buyer signal**: "Or save $[X] by prepaying today"

### 6. Application + Urgency Layer (GP-09, GP-06)
Build the conversion mechanics:
- **CTA**: "Apply Now" (not "Buy Now" — buying is the last step)
- **Scarcity**: "[X] spots this month" (real, not manufactured)
- **Urgency**: "Next cohort starts [date]" (real, not manufactured)
- **Application process**: Short application → Deposit → Approval
- **Abandoned cart**: "You started your application but didn't finish — did anything go wrong?"

### 7. The Founder Video
Script a 3-5 minute Loom/video for the top of the doc:
- Who you are (30 seconds — credibility, not resume)
- What this is (60 seconds — the promise and the phases)
- Who it's for / who it's NOT for (60 seconds — filtering builds trust)
- How to get started (30 seconds — apply below)

Apply **Trust Economy** (HK-08): The video is a trust deposit. Be genuine, not polished. Show your face, your space, your personality.

## Output

**THE OFFER DOC** — Complete, ready-to-publish document containing:

1. **Founder Video Script** (top of doc)
2. **Promise Statement**: One-sentence core offer
3. **Phase Breakdown**: 3-phase journey with milestones
4. **Risk Reversal**: Guarantee copy (love it or leave it)
5. **Bonuses**: 3-5 reframed program features with value anchors
6. **Payment Options**: Pay-in-full + one payment plan
7. **Application CTA**: With scarcity/urgency copy
8. **Abandoned Cart Email**: 1 follow-up email for incomplete applications

**Format**: Ready to paste into a Google Doc, Notion page, or landing page builder. Written in conversational, trust-building language.

## Quality Gate
- [ ] Does it feel like a friend explaining something great, not a salesperson pitching?
- [ ] Can someone read it in under 10 minutes and make a decision?
- [ ] Is the guarantee anxiety-free for both buyer AND seller?
- [ ] Are there zero pressure tactics? (No fake countdown timers, no "only 2 spots left" manipulation)
- [ ] Would someone share this doc with a friend who might benefit?
