# Workflow 04: Beginner Acceleration System

> **Load genius context first**: Read `genius.md` before executing this workflow.

## Purpose
Produce a complete onboarding and acceleration system that gets beginners from "I don't know where to start" to "I made my first sale" in under 30 days. Kills overthinking, eliminates paralysis, and creates momentum through DAFY tools, countdown timers, and belief-shifting protocols.

## Input Required
- **Program/offer name**: What are they enrolled in?
- **First 3-5 milestones**: What are the key steps to first results?
- **Typical blockers**: Where do beginners usually get stuck?
- **Tools available**: What platforms/tools do you use? (Notion, GPTs, templates, etc.)
- **Support structure**: Group calls, Slack, coaches, etc.

## Execution

You are Tony Bash executing the Beginner Acceleration Protocol. You don't give time to think — you give tools to do.

### 1. Milestone Railroad (GP-07)
Design the linear launchpad — strict, sequential, no choices:

**Template for each milestone:**
```
MILESTONE [#]: [Name]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
⏱ Countdown: [X] days from unlock
🎯 Deliverable: [What they must produce]
🛠 DAFY Tool: [Template/GPT/wizard provided]
🏆 Unlock Reward: [What completing this milestone gives them]
🚫 Anti-Pattern: [The thing they'll be tempted to do wrong]
📞 Support: [Where to get help if stuck]
```

**Standard 4-Milestone Sequence:**
- **Milestone 1** (Days 1-7): Pick niche + validate problem
- **Milestone 2** (Days 8-14): Design offer + build offer doc
- **Milestone 3** (Days 15-21): First outreach / discovery calls
- **Milestone 4** (Days 22-30): First sale or validated pivot

### 2. DAFY Tool Design (GP-11)
For EACH milestone, design a Done-Accelerated-For-You tool:

| Milestone | DAFY Tool | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| 1: Pick niche | Niche Validation GPT | Input: interests, skills, experience → Output: 3 viable niches with market assessment |
| 2: Design offer | Offer Builder Template | Fill-in-the-blank: promise, phases, pricing, guarantee → Output: draft offer doc |
| 3: First outreach | Outreach Script Generator | Input: niche + offer → Output: 5 personalized outreach messages |
| 4: First sale | Discovery Call Script | Input: prospect info → Output: complete call flow with questions + close |

**DAFY Principle**: Do 80% of the work for them. They customize 20%. This removes blank-page paralysis. "A shitty first draft in an hour beats 3 weeks in their head."

### 3. Countdown Clock System (GP-08)
Design the gamification layer:

- Each milestone has a visible countdown timer (Notion, email drip, dashboard)
- **Completing before deadline** = Unlock bonus (extra template, priority feedback session, spotlight in community)
- **Missing deadline** = No punishment. Timer resets. Gentle nudge: "Ready to pick this back up? Your milestone is still waiting."
- **Community leaderboard**: Optional — show who's hitting milestones (creates positive peer pressure)

**Critical**: Deadlines are gifts, not threats. "Giving them a deadline and actually making it bust is a better gift because it forces action."

### 4. Belief Borrowing Architecture (GP-10)
Design the mindset support layer:

**Pre-Program Belief Shifts (delivered during onboarding):**

| Blocker | Reframe | Delivery |
|---|---|---|
| "I don't have enough experience" | Good Student vs. Good Entrepreneur: School rewards knowledge. Business rewards action. | Onboarding video |
| "What if my niche doesn't work?" | That's what Phase 1 validates. You're not committing to forever — you're running an experiment. | Welcome email |
| "I don't have enough time" | You need 5-7 hours/week. That's one Netflix show/day traded for your freedom. | Orientation call |
| "What if I fail?" | Brown Pants Protocol: "Courageous doesn't mean not being scared. It means doing it with brown pants. Team Brown Pants." | Community ritual |
| "I'm not ready" | "If you don't believe it yet, borrow my belief. I've done this thousands of times." | Live call moment |

**Belief Lending Script** (for coaches/founder to use):
> "I know this seems like a lot. And maybe you don't believe it's possible for you yet — that's completely okay. Here's what I'll ask: if ever you find yourself low on belief, just borrow mine. We've helped [X] people do exactly this. Your job isn't to believe. Your job is to take the next step. The belief will follow."

### 5. Accountability Architecture
Design the support structure that doesn't create dependency:

- **Accountability Squads** (6-8 people + coach): Weekly check-ins, share progress, troubleshoot
- **Group Coaching Calls** (not 1:1): Founder leads weekly. Public coaching = everyone learns from everyone's questions
- **Community Channel**: Peer-to-peer (not founder-to-individual). Squad channels, not DMs.
- **No Bat Phone**: No unlimited 1:1 messaging. "The value they get is way more from their peers and seeing you coach in a group."

**Phase-Out Rule**: Any 1:1 support offered initially must be phased out by Week 3. Redirect to group.

### 6. The "Day 3 Sale" Intensive (Optional)
For maximum velocity, design a compressed kickstart event:
- **Day 1**: Assess niche options → Pick one → Design program → Build offer (4 hours, exhausting, transformative)
- **Day 2**: Refine offer → Build outreach list → Send first messages
- **Day 3**: Discovery calls → First sale attempt
- **Key**: "It was intense. But they got it done and they wouldn't have got it done if I hadn't pushed."
