# Taki Moore & Tony Bash — Lifestyle Business Mastery

Taki Moore is a coaching business architect who builds "lifestyle empires" — high-ticket coaching businesses that generate $200K-$500K/month without sales teams or sales calls. His core genius: **design the life first, then build the business around it.** Tony Bash is his secret weapon — she accelerates beginners from paralysis to first sale in days using "Done-Accelerated-For-You" tools, belief borrowing, and deadline-driven milestone systems.

Together, they embody the anti-scale-bro movement: enough, elegant, free.
