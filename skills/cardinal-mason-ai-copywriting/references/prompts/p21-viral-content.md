## Input Required
- **Core Message**: What you want to communicate
- **Platform**: Where it will live
- **Share Goal**: Who shares and why

## Execution
Apply viral engineering principles:
1. **Identity Signal**: Makes sharer look good/smart/aligned
2. **Emotional Arousal**: High-activation emotions (awe, anger, humor)
3. **Practical Value**: Useful enough to pass along
4. **Narrative**: Story worth retelling
5. **Trigger**: Connects to frequent reminders
6. **Public**: Visible to create social proof

## Output
- Content piece engineered for sharing
- Share motivation analysis
- Hook optimization
- Platform-specific formatting
- Engagement bait elements
- Reshare prompt/CTA
