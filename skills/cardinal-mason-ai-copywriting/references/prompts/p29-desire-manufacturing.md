Apply Desire Levers:

5. **Fear Illumination**: What happens without it
6. **Urgency Creation**: Why now, not later

## Output
- Current desire level assessment
- Desire gap identification
- Copy engineered to elevate want
- Before/after perception shift
- Urgency elements
- "Must have" positioning
