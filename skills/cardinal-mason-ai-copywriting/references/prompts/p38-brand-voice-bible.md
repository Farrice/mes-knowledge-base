## Role

## Input Required
- **Brand**: Company/person name
- **Industry**: Space they operate in
- **Samples**: Existing content examples
- **Target Audience**: Who they speak to

## Execution
Document:
1. **Voice Attributes**: 3-5 defining characteristics
2. **Tone Spectrum**: How voice shifts by context
3. **Vocabulary**: Words to use/avoid
4. **Sentence Style**: Structure preferences
5. **Examples**: Good vs. bad samples
6. **Guardrails**: What to never do

## Output
- Complete brand voice guide
- Quick reference card (1 page)
- Do/Don't examples
- Tone adjustments by channel
- Sample paragraphs
- Voice audit checklist

## Example Attributes
**Confident, Not Arrogant**: "We know what works" not "We're the best"
**Direct, Not Harsh**: "This won't work" not "This is terrible"
**Friendly, Not Casual**: Professional warmth, not slang-heavy
