# Cardinal Mason - Hidden Knowledge

5 tacit expertise points that separate amateurs from professionals.

---

## 1. Claude > ChatGPT for Copywriting
**The Truth**: Claude produces more natural-sounding writing, "listens better," and with memory features is the primary tool. ChatGPT relegated to research only.

**Deploy**: Default to Claude for all copywriting tasks. Use ChatGPT for research and fact-finding.

---

## 2. AI Humor Must Be Unintentional
**The Truth**: "AI is not funny" unless the humor is dry, subtle, and delivered as if the writer doesn't know they're being funny. Direct joke attempts always fail.

**Deploy**: Never prompt AI to "be funny." Instead: "Include dry, subtle humor that sounds like you don't realize you're being funny."

---

## 3. The 5-Question Context Gathering Hack
**The Truth**: Before writing first drafts, have AI ask YOU 5 questions to fill knowledge gaps. This surfaces what's missing from the brief.

**Deploy**: End every context prompt with: "Before you write your first draft, ask me five questions that will allow you to create high-quality, high-converting copy."
