# Luke Iha: Creative Strategy & Freelance Mastery v2.0

## What This Skill Does

This skill operationalizes Luke Iha's creative strategy methodology — the upstream thinking that PRECEDES copywriting. While most practitioners jump straight to writing, this skill forces strategic clarity first: audience selection, angle identification, competitive positioning, and proof-of-competence architecture.

It also deploys his complete freelance business system: the 7-step Upwork mastery sequence, the Nuclear VSL technique (portfolio pieces so good they eliminate objections), the Over-Delivery Flywheel, and — new in v2.0 — the **Offer Cycling Engine** for detecting, packaging, and rotating hot market opportunities, the **Agency Ladder** for career evolution from task vendor to growth partner, and the **12 Types of Leverage** for scaling beyond time-for-money.
