# Offer Cycling Engine

Systematically detect hot market opportunities, package your skills into an entry offer that gets you in the door, and plan the rotation cycle before the market saturates. Uses the Offer Heat Formula to score opportunities and the Agency Ladder to position your offer at the right level.

---

## Inputs Required

1. **Your Core Skills** — What are you genuinely good at? What's your T-shape?
2. **Current Niche** — Who do you serve now?
3. **Current Offer** — What are you selling? At what price? How hot is it?
4. **Market Observations** — What's changing in your niche? New platforms, formats, trends?

## Phase 4: Entry Offer Architecture

For the hottest opportunity, design the entry offer:

### 8-Point Entry Offer Checklist:
1. ☐ Solves a real, immediate problem (not "nice to have")
2. ☐ Deliverable in 3-7 days (fast turnaround)
3. ☐ Low price relative to value ($200-1,000)
4. ☐ Showcases your core skill
5. ☐ Creates natural expansion opportunity
6. ☐ Easy to say yes to (low decision weight)
7. ☐ Produces a tangible deliverable
8. ☐ Demonstrates your unique methodology
