# Joanna Wiebe — Writing Career Monetization

Joanna Wiebe is the founder of Copy Hackers and coined the term "conversion copywriting." This skill captures her **business architecture** for writer careers — the pricing models, service packaging, client positioning, and revenue strategies that separate broke writers from six-figure consultants. The throughline: writers who stay broke sell time; writers who get rich sell measurable business outcomes.

**Relationship to existing skill**: This complements `joanna-wiebe-persuasion-mastery` (copy psychology). That skill covers HOW to write persuasively. This skill covers HOW TO BUILD A WRITING BUSINESS.
