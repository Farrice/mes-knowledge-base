# Sabri Suby AI Advertising

Identify validated business opportunities, create converting funnels, write high-ROI ads, and scale to $10K/day—using AI as the execution engine.

## Overview

Sabri Suby is founder of King Kong (Australia's fastest-growing digital marketing agency) and creator of $7.8B+ in client revenue. His genius: reverse-engineering business building into an AI-executable system that follows existing money flows, validates through competition analysis, and deploys proven direct response frameworks.

**Core philosophy**: Follow the money. Competition validates demand. AI executes, you strategize.
