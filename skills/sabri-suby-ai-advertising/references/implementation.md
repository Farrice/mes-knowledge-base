# Sabri Suby Implementation Pathway

Progression from market discovery to $10K/day ad scaling.

---

## 24-Hour Quickstart

### Hour 0-2: Market Selection
- Load AI market discovery prompt into ChatGPT Deep Research
- Set parameters for target industries
- Initiate research (15-30 minutes)

### Hour 2-4: Validation Forensics
- Take top 3 opportunities from AI research
- Check Facebook Ads Library for each (minimum 20+ ads)
- Check Google Ads Transparency Center
- Select primary opportunity based on validation score

### Hour 4-8: Pain Extraction
- Deploy forum foraging prompt
- Target 3-5 relevant subreddits
- Extract 50+ verbatim pain quotes
- Organize by theme: fears, frustrations, failed solutions, desires

### Hour 8-12: Competition Deep-Dive
- Sign up for top competitor's free trial/demo
- Screenshot entire funnel
- Document pricing, promises, objection handling
- Identify 5+ specific weaknesses

### Hour 12-24: Offer & Page Foundation
- Define offer (transformation, price, guarantee)
- Draft landing page outline using pain research
- Use AI to generate first draft of landing page copy

---

## 7-Day Sprint

### Days 1-2: Foundation
- Complete 24-hour quickstart
- Refine landing page copy through 2-3 iterations
- Build landing page in platform

### Days 3-4: Ad Creation
- Generate 20+ headline variations
- Create 3-5 body copy variations
- Design 12+ ad creatives (breaking news, native, SMS)

### Days 5-6: Launch Preparation
- Set up Facebook Business Manager
- Install pixel on landing page
- Create campaign structure
- Set up conversion tracking

### Day 7: Launch & Monitor
- Launch campaign at $50-100/day
- Monitor first 24 hours for delivery issues
- Verify tracking is working

---

## 30-Day Transformation

### Week 1: Foundation
- Market validation complete
- Research database built
- Offer engineered
- Landing page live

### Week 2: Launch & Initial Data
- Ads live and collecting data
- First conversions tracked
- Winner/loser patterns emerging

### Week 3: Optimization
- Clear winners identified
- Losers eliminated
- Budget increased to $200-500/day

### Week 4: Scaling
- Scale to $500-1,000/day on proven system
- Systematic testing protocol established
- Path to $10K/day mapped
