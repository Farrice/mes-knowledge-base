## Input Required

- **[MARKET]**: Industry to research
- **[COMPETITORS]**: Known players in the space
- **[PRICE RANGE]**: Expected pricing for solutions

---

## Execution Protocol

1. **IDENTIFY** transaction volume indicators
2. **RESEARCH** competitor revenue (funding, employee count, reviews)
3. **ESTIMATE** total market transaction volume
4. **CALCULATE** obtainable slice (realistic market share)
5. **VALIDATE** money is actively being spent

---

## Output Deliverable

Revenue river assessment:
- Market size indicators
- Competitor revenue estimates
- Total transaction volume guess
- Your obtainable slice calculation
- Validation confirmation

---

## Quality Standard

Can identify specific dollar amounts already being spent. Joining existing party, not starting new one.
