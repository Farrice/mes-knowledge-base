## Hidden Knowledge

### Tacit Expertise Made Explicit

1. **Translation beats creation**: Great content restructures existing ideas rather than inventing new ones

2. **Energy reveals truth**: Pay attention to what energizes you in consumption—that's your creation direction

3. **Authenticity requires identity work**: "Be yourself" is useless advice without knowing who that self is

4. **Audience ownership through participation**: Involve them in building your ideas (IKEA effect)

5. **Cringe phases are progress markers**: Embarrassment at past work proves evolution

6. **Systems beat goals for creators**: Process orientation prevents creative burnout
