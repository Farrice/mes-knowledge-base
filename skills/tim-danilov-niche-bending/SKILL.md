# Tim Danilov: Niche Bending

Tim Danilov is the creator who coined "Niche Bending" — the strategy of combining proven viral formats with untapped markets to create content that feels brand new in saturated spaces. He used this method to take faceless channels from 0 → $56K/month in 30 days, 0 → 150M views, and 0 → $23K in under 90 days. Extracted from a VidIQ deep-dive into his methodology.
