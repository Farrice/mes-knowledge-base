## Workflow

### Phase 1: The Susan Transformation & Positioning Stack
*Objective: Claim KPI status and move from "interchangeable worker" to "category authority."*

1.  **Positioning Pivot**: Apply the **Susan Transformation**. Rewrite the user's generic title into a hyper-specific "KPI Title" (Industry + Problem + Transformation).
2.  **The 1/9/90 Filter**: Identify the "9% Market" (those with expensive problems) and define the **Stakes** (what they lose by not solving this).
3.  **Premium Pitch Suite**: Generate a 5-part pitch (10-sec, 30-sec, 2-min, 5-min, and Written Bio) that anchors on the **Demand Inversion**—focusing on the "wanting" rather than the "features."

### Phase 2: Assessment-Led Demand Architecture
*Objective: Build the "X-ray" that pre-qualifies leads and surfaces dormant tension.*

1.  **Diagnostic Design**: Create a 20-question **Assessment Architecture**.
    - 8 Problem Surface Questions (revealing hidden costs).
    - 6 Desire Amplification Questions (painting the "Desired Reality").
    - 6 Qualification Questions (budget, authority, timing).
2.  **Scoring Matrix**: Define the point values and tier definitions (A/B/C prospects) to identify "A-players" immediately.
3.  **The Signal Volume Calculator**: Calculate the required "Signal Volume" based on the **Pattern 4** formula: [CAPACITY] × 50 = Required monthly signals.

### Phase 3: The 4-Phase Oversubscribed Campaign
*Objective: Execute the 11/90 Visibility Threshold to ensure you are seen 11+ times in the launch window.*

1.  **Phase 1: Awareness (The 96% Unlock)**: 7 days of content focusing exclusively on the PROBLEM. No solution mentioned. Build tension.
2.  **Phase 2: Anticipation (The Waitlist)**: 7 days of "Something is coming." Open the waitlist. Release the Assessment.
3.  **Phase 3: Launch (The Application)**: 4 days. Doors open ONLY to the waitlist/high-scorers. Use "With or Without You" energy.
4.  **Phase 4: Close (The Scarcity Stack)**: 3 days. Hard capacity limits. Final push messaging based on remaining slots.
5.  **Tension Creative Brief**: Generate visual direction (Color Palette, Imagery, Typography) that mirrors the psychological gap between "Current Pain" and "Desired Relief."

### Phase 4: The Tension Triangle Sales Playbook
*Objective: Convert leads through diagnostic "therapy," not interrogation.*

1.  **The Tension Script**: A word-for-word call framework using the **Tension Triangle**:
    - **Current Reality Excavation**: Questions to surface the truth of their pain.
    - **Desired Reality Painting**: Questions to build a vivid image of the "Magic Wand" scenario.
    - **Obstacle Identification**: Identifying the gap that makes your solution inevitable.
2.  **Stakes-Based Closing**: Scripts for handling price objections by pivoting back to the cost of inaction (The Stakes Lens).
3.  **Tension Signal Guide**: A cheat sheet for recognizing when a prospect is at "Peak Tension" and ready for the prescription.

### Phase 5: The Solopreneur OS & 90-Day Roadmap
*Objective: Integrate the launch into a sustainable life-first business model.*

1.  **Life Design Blueprint**: Map the "Ideal Week" architecture to ensure the business serves the life, not consumes it.
2.  **90-Day Implementation Calendar**: A week-by-week breakdown of tasks, from IP development to campaign execution.
3.  **Key Metrics Dashboard**: Define the 5 KPIs that actually matter (Signal Volume, Assessment Completion Rate, Application Velocity, Conversion, Average Deal Value).
