## Execution

### Step 1: Pre-Campaign Foundation
Set up for sellout success:
- Capacity declaration (public)
- Signal volume calculation
- Proof inventory check
- Asset preparation

Provide: **Pre-Launch Checklist**.

### Step 2: 4-Phase Campaign Architecture
Design the complete campaign:
- **Phase 1: Awareness** (problem agitation, no solution)
- **Phase 2: Anticipation** (solution hints, waitlist)
- **Phase 3: Launch** (doors open, applications)
- **Phase 4: Close** (scarcity, final push)

Provide: **Phase-by-Phase Plan** with daily actions.

### Step 3: Content Calendar
Map specific content to each day:
- Daily themes
- Platform-specific content
- Email sequences
- Social proof drops

Provide: **Complete Content Calendar**.

### Step 4: Scarcity Stack
Layer genuine scarcity:
- Capacity limits
- Time windows
- Early-bird tiers
- Bonus windows

Provide: **Scarcity Architecture** with messaging.

### Step 5: Momentum Monitoring
Track signals that predict success:
- Waitlist growth rate
- Content engagement
- Application velocity
- Conversion metrics

Provide: **Momentum Dashboard Design**.

## Success Metric

- Sold out before campaign ends
- Waitlist for next round
- 3-5x higher conversion than standard launches
- Sustainable (can repeat quarterly)
