## Execution

### Step 1: Scarcity Architecture
Design genuine scarcity:
- Capacity limits (real, enforced)
- Time windows (opening/closing)
- Application requirements
- Waitlist mechanics

Provide: **Scarcity System** that creates urgency without fakery.

### Step 2: Visible Demand Engineering
Make demand visible to create more demand:
- Waitlist counter (public or semi-public)
- Social proof accumulation
- "X people are viewing this" mechanics
- Testimonial amplification

Provide: **Demand Visibility Tactics**.

### Step 3: Social Proof Velocity
Create rapid accumulation of proof:
- Early testimonial generation
- Case study fast-tracking
- Social mention monitoring
- User-generated content triggers

Provide: **Social Proof Sprint Plan**.

### Step 4: Campaign Tension Arc
Design 21-day campaign with tension build:
- Week 1: Awareness + Problem agitation
- Week 2: Solution hints + Anticipation
- Week 3: Launch + Scarcity + Close

Provide: **Day-by-Day Campaign Calendar**.

### Step 5: Sharing Trigger Design
Create reasons for organic sharing:
- Status-giving content
- Insider information
- Community identification
- FOMO triggers

Provide: **5 Sharing Triggers** with content examples.

## Success Metric

- 3-10x organic reach vs. paid
- 40-60% higher conversion
- 25-40% faster decision velocity
- Waitlist exceeds capacity
- Sold out before campaign ends

## Example Campaign Arc

**Day 1-7**: Problem content. No solution mentioned. Build tension.
**Day 8-14**: Solution hints. Waitlist opens. Visible counter.
**Day 15-18**: Case studies flood. Application opens.
**Day 19-20**: "Almost full" messaging. Urgency peak.
**Day 21**: Sold out. Waitlist for next round.
