## 24-Hour Quickstart

### Hour 1: Official Capacity Calculation
**Use**: [Demand & Supply Tension Engineering](prompts/demand-supply-tension.md)

1. Calculate maximum clients you can serve with excellence
2. Multiply by 50 = your signal volume target
3. Write your capacity statement: "I work with [X] clients maximum"

### Hour 2: Problem-Aware Rewrite
**Use**: [Problem-Aware Positioning](prompts/problem-aware-positioning.md)

1. List 10 frustrations your ideal client experiences daily
2. Rewrite your headline to start with #1 frustration
3. Rewrite your main CTA to address the frustration

### Hour 3: KPI Pitch Draft
**Use**: [KPI Positioning](prompts/kpi-positioning.md)

1. Complete the Susan transformation on your title
2. Write your 30-second pitch
3. Identify your strongest of the 5 Ps (Pitch, Publish, Product, Profile, Partnership)

### Hour 4: Assessment Outline
**Use**: [Assessment Architecture](prompts/assessment-architecture.md)

1. Draft 15 questions that reveal problems prospects don't know they have
2. Create basic scoring tiers (A/B/C prospects)
3. Outline results delivery framework
