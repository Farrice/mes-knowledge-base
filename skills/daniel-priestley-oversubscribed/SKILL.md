## Quick Start (24-Hour Impact)

### Hour 1: Calculate Your Official Capacity
Use **Demand & Supply Tension Engineering** to determine:
- How many clients you can serve excellently
- Your signal volume target (50:1 ratio)
- Your scarcity architecture

### Hour 2: Transform Your Messaging  
Use **Problem-Aware Positioning** to shift from:
- ❌ "I help businesses grow" (solution-aware, 4% market)
- ✅ "Your CEO wants pipeline numbers Monday" (problem-aware, 65% market)

### Hour 3: Draft Your KPI Pitch
Use **Key Person of Influence Positioning** to create:
- The 5-Part Pitch (Pitch, Publish, Product, Profile, Partnership)
- Your "Susan transformation" (generic → specific authority)

### Hour 4: Design Your Assessment
Use **Assessment Architecture** to replace cold selling with value-first qualification.

## The 10 Priestley Truths

1. **Demand is manufactured, not found.** Stop looking for clients. Start creating wanting.
2. **Your capacity is a feature.** Scarcity creates value. Protect it fiercely.
3. **Value lives in the buyer's situation.** Price on their stakes, not your hours.
4. **Authority is claimed, not granted.** Position as KPI. Don't wait for permission.
5. **The business exists to serve your life.** If it's consuming you, the model is wrong.
6. **"With or without you" energy is magnetic.** Full pipeline creates abundance others feel.
7. **Path of least resistance positions offers.** Make buying feel inevitable.
8. **Oversubscription is sustainable.** This isn't a launch strategy—it's an operating model.
9. **Problems are bigger than solutions.** Speak to frustrations and you'll always have demand.
10. **You're standing on a mountain you can't see.** Your "ordinary" expertise is extraordinary.
