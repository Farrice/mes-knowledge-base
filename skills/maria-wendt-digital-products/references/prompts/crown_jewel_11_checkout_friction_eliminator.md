## THE FIVE FRICTION TYPES

### Friction Type 1: SPEED
"How fast does the page load?"

**Pass Criteria**: Under 2 seconds on mobile
**Fail Signals**: Large images, embedded videos auto-loading, heavy scripts, external font loading

### Friction Type 2: COGNITIVE
"How hard do they have to think?"

**Pass Criteria**: 5th-grade reading level, offer understood in 5 seconds
**Fail Signals**: Industry jargon, long paragraphs, unclear value proposition, complex offer structure

### Friction Type 3: VISUAL
"How overwhelming does it look?"

**Pass Criteria**: Skimmable in 3 seconds, clear visual hierarchy, mobile-optimized
**Fail Signals**: Wall of text, no whitespace, tiny fonts, poor contrast, desktop-only design

### Friction Type 4: TRUST
"Do they feel safe to buy?"

**Pass Criteria**: Social proof visible immediately, guarantee clear, credibility established
**Fail Signals**: No testimonials above fold, hidden guarantee, no credentials, no purchase notifications

### Friction Type 5: TECHNICAL
"Does everything work smoothly?"

**Pass Criteria**: One-page checkout, minimal form fields, multiple payment options, clear CTA
**Fail Signals**: Multi-step checkout, unnecessary fields, single payment option, broken links, confusing buttons
