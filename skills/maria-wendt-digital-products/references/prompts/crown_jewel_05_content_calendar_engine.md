## DUPLICATION PROTOCOL

**Before EVERY Publish:**

1. Create your reel
2. Go to "Drafts"
3. Tap the reel
4. Select "Duplicate"
5. Save duplicate to drafts
6. Return to original
7. Publish original

**Why This Matters:**
- Reels that perform well can be re-posted unchanged
- Second post often gets MORE views than first
- You're building a content vault for low-effort days
- No one notices or cares about reposts

**Re-Posting Rules:**
- Wait minimum 60 days before re-posting
- Duplicate AGAIN before re-posting
- Keep your draft vault at 20+ pieces
