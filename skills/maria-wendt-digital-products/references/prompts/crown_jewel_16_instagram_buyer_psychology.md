## THE 5 INSTAGRAM BUYER TRUTHS

### Truth #1: They Skim Before They Read

**The Reality**: 
- Instagram users are trained to scroll fast
- They give your page 3 seconds before deciding to stay or go
- If they can't understand your offer in a skim, they won't read further

**Optimization Criteria**:
- Can someone understand WHAT + WHY + HOW MUCH in 3 seconds?
- Is the headline the first thing they see?
- Is there visual hierarchy (not a wall of sameness)?

**Common Violations**:
- Long paragraphs above the fold
- Headline buried below hero image
- No clear price/CTA visible without scrolling

### Truth #2: Readability Is Make-or-Break

**The Reality**:
- They're reading on a small phone screen
- They're distracted (kids, waiting in line, half-watching TV)
- Complex language = cognitive load = bounce

**Optimization Criteria**:
- Is copy at 5th-grade reading level or below?
- Are sentences short (under 15 words average)?
- Are paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)?

**Common Violations**:
- Industry jargon ("leverage," "optimize," "synergy")
- Long sentences with multiple clauses
- Dense paragraphs that look like walls of text

### Truth #3: Social Proof Is Needed Immediately

**The Reality**:
- They don't know you (saw one reel, maybe two)
- They need to feel safe before they buy
- "Other people bought this and it worked" = permission to buy

**Optimization Criteria**:
- Is there social proof visible above the fold?
- Are testimonials specific and believable?
- Is there a "others are buying" signal (Use Proof, customer count)?

**Common Violations**:
- Testimonials buried at the bottom
- Vague testimonials ("Great product!")
- No social proof at all until after the pitch

### Truth #4: Friction Kills Impulse Purchases

**The Reality**:
- Instagram buying is impulse buying
- Every extra step, field, or decision loses 20-30% of buyers
- They want: See â†’ Want â†’ Buy (that fast)

**Optimization Criteria**:
- How many steps from landing to purchase complete?
- How many form fields are required?
- Are there unnecessary decisions (size, tier, options)?

**Common Violations**:
- Multi-page checkout
- Asking for shipping address on digital products
- Requiring account creation before purchase

### Truth #5: Page Speed Is Critical

**The Reality**:
- They're on mobile data (not always fast)
- If your page takes more than 2-3 seconds to load, they're gone
- They won't waitâ€”there are 1000 other things to scroll to

**Optimization Criteria**:
- Does the page load in under 2 seconds on mobile?
- Are images compressed?
- Are videos click-to-play (not auto-loading)?

**Common Violations**:
- Large uncompressed images
- Auto-playing video
- Heavy scripts and trackers
