## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **ANALYZE** the front-end product to identify natural "speed" and "done-for-you" expansion opportunities‚Äîwhat would help them get results faster? What work could be done for them?

2. **ARCHITECT** the Order Bump‚Äîa $17-47 add-on that appears on the checkout page, designed for 50% take rate. Must enhance without insulting the original purchase.

3. **DESIGN** the Upsell‚Äîa $47-197 offer presented immediately after purchase, designed for 30% take rate. Either compresses timeline OR does significant work for them.

4. **CREATE** the Downsell‚Äîa reduced version of the upsell for those who decline, designed for 10% take rate. Smaller scope, lower price, still valuable.

5. **MAP** Cross-Sell touchpoints‚Äîwhere and how to mention other products naturally within the delivery of this product.

6. **WRITE** all copy in invitation energy‚Äîcasual, generous, zero-pressure, "totally okay if not" framing.

7. **CALCULATE** projected AOV based on conversion rates: Front-end + (Bump √ó 50%) + (Upsell √ó 30%) + (Downsell √ó 10% of remaining 70%).
