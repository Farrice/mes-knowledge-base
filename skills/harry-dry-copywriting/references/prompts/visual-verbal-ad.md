## Visual-Verbal Principles

### SPATIAL MEANING
- Separation = comparison
- Proximity = relationship
- Size = importance
- Alignment = order/chaos

### LAYOUT IS COPY
- How words are arranged changes meaning
- A single word, large = impact
- Words stacked vs. inline = different effects

### SHOW DON'T TELL
- If you can visualize it, don't just say it
- The image and the words should create something neither could alone

## Execution Protocol

1. **START** with message, not words
2. **SKETCH** layout before writing
3. **INTEGRATE** visual and verbal simultaneously
4. **ITERATE** design and copy together
5. **SIMPLIFY** until one glance captures it
6. **TEST** comprehension without reading
