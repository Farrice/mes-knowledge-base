# Dan Koe - AI Leverage Methodology

**Invocation**: `/deploy-skill dan-koe-ai-leverage`  
**Domain**: AI-Augmented Creative Work • Prompt Engineering • Knowledge Extraction • Human-AI Creative Partnership
---
Dan Koe's system for maintaining intellectual sovereignty over AI while leveraging it as a superhuman creative partner. Built on the 4C Cognitive Architecture (Context → Clarification → Creation → Concerns) — this skill transforms how you interact with AI from "glorified Google search" to context-loaded creative collaboration that produces outputs you couldn't achieve alone.

**Core Philosophy**:
- AI quality is a function of YOUR context and direction, not model intelligence
- The Concerns phase (adversarial refinement) is where the real learning happens
- Curated expert knowledge > raw AI training data
- Maintain taste, creative judgment, and direction — outsource synthesis and execution
