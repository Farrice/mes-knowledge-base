# Fascination Bullet Factory

Mass fascination production engine. Takes a product's features and benefits and generates 30+ curiosity-dense bullets. Each bullet creates an independent open loop that can only be resolved by the product.

## PHASE 1: SKILL ACQUISITION (Do this FIRST)

Read: /Users/farricecain/Google Antigravity/skills/luke-iha-vsl-leads/references/genius-patterns.md (focus on GP5: Fascination Bullet Engineering)

## PHASE 2: INPUT REQUIREMENTS

Collect from user:
- **Product/Offer**: What's being sold?
- **Feature List**: All product features (ingredients, components, modules, chapters, etc.)
- **Benefit List**: What each feature DOES for the customer
- **Mechanism** (optional): What's the unique reason it works?
- **Surprising Research** (optional): Counterintuitive findings, studies, data points

## PHASE 3: FASCINATION GENERATION

Using the formulas below, generate 30+ fascinations organized by type:

### Benefit-Driven Fascinations (10+)
Formula: "How to [desirable outcome] in [surprisingly short time] — without [common sacrifice]"
- Focus on WHAT they get
- Use specific numbers and timeframes
- Remove the most common objection/sacrifice in the same sentence

### Curiosity-Driven Fascinations (10+)
Formula: "The [specific] reason why [common thing] is actually [surprising result]"
- Focus on WHY something counterintuitive is true
- Make the reader need to know the answer
- Specificity (page numbers, percentages, exact amounts) increases pull

### Authority-Driven Fascinations (5+)
Formula: "Why [authority figure] secretly [surprising behavior] (see [location])"
- Leverage credibility of known entities
- Suggest insider behavior that contradicts public messaging
- Reference specific pages, chapters, or timestamps for believability

### Fear-Driven Fascinations (5+)
Formula: "What [unexpected thing] reveals about your [health/wealth/relationships] — and what to do about it"
- Create concern about something they're currently ignoring
- Must include implied resolution (otherwise it's just fear, not fascination)
- Connect seemingly innocuous behavior to significant consequences

## PHASE 4: QUALITY FILTER

Every fascination MUST pass these tests:
1. ✅ **Incomplete**: Resolution requires the product (can't Google the answer)
2. ✅ **Specific**: Contains concrete details (numbers, names, timeframes)
3. ✅ **Standalone**: Works as an independent curiosity trigger
4. ✅ **Believable**: Sounds credible even without proof (doesn't strain credulity)
5. ✅ **Relevant**: Target audience cares about this topic

Kill any bullet that fails 2+ tests.
