## PHASE 2: INPUT REQUIREMENTS

Collect from user:
- **Product/Offer**: What's being sold? Key features, mechanism, price
- **Target Audience**: Demographics, psychographics, awareness level (unaware → most aware)
- **Core Problem**: What pain does the audience feel?
- **Core Promise**: What's the main transformation/outcome?
- **Unique Mechanism**: What's the "reason it works"? (if using `million-dollar-mechanisms`, reference the validated mechanism)
- **Key Proof Points**: Best testimonials, data, credentials
- **Story Elements** (optional): Origin story, client story, discovery narrative
- **Competitor Landscape**: What claims/solutions has the audience already seen?

## PHASE 3: MICRO LEAD

Write the micro lead (50-200 words) using the highest-impact type for this audience's awareness level:

- **Unaware**: Contrarian Claim, Surprise Connection, or Look Closer
- **Problem-Aware**: Paradoxical Question, Invisible Difference, or Insider Confession
- **Solution-Aware**: Quiz Opening, Insider Confession, or Contrarian Claim
- **Most-Aware**: Short, direct — bypass micro lead and open with proof/offer

Provide the selected micro lead PLUS 2 alternatives of different types for testing.

## PHASE 4: BUILD BELIEF PHASE

Write the belief-building section with these elements (draw from the 15-element checklist):
1. **Story**: Personal, client, or origin narrative that makes the problem real
2. **Problem Escalation**: Make the pain vivid, urgent, and multi-dimensional
3. **Failed Solutions**: What they've tried that didn't work and WHY it didn't work
4. **Mechanism Tease**: Hint at WHY the problem persists — WITHOUT revealing the mechanism
5. **Credibility Bridge**: Why THIS source can be trusted with this revelation

Copy block velocity: Pain → Curiosity → Promise → Pain (new angle) → Curiosity at moderate speed.

## PHASE 5: HANDLE OBJECTIONS PHASE

Pre-empt the skepticism:
1. **Skepticism Acknowledgment**: "I know what you're thinking..." → validates their doubt
2. **Proof Stack**: Deploy 2-3 proof types (testimonials, data, credentials)
3. **Contrarian Positioning**: Why the mainstream approach is wrong/incomplete

Copy block velocity: Proof → Conditions → Constraints at measured speed.

## PHASE 6: CLOSE PHASE

Bridge to the mechanism reveal (the body of the VSL):
1. **Future Pacing**: Paint the post-solution life vividly and specifically
2. **Fascination Bullets**: 8-12 "you'll also discover..." bullets (see Fascination Bullet Factory)
3. **Constraints/Qualification**: Who this is for / who it's NOT for
4. **Transition**: Seamless bridge to mechanism reveal ("But first, let me show you exactly why...")

## PHASE 7: 15-ELEMENT AUDIT

Score the completed lead against all 15 elements:
Problem | Promise | Solution tease | Story | Contrarian nature | Unique mechanism | Nickname | Skepticism | Credibility | Testimonials | Constraints | Qualifiers | Fascinations | Future pacing | Failed solutions

Target: 10+/15.
