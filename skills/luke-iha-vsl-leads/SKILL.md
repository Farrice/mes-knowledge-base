# Luke Iha: VSL Lead Mastery

## What This Skill Does

This skill operationalizes the OPENING LAYER of sales copy — what happens between the hook and the body. In Luke's framework, the lead is the "movie trailer" for the VSL: it must create DESIRE to watch without spoiling the mechanism. Most copywriters either write leads that are too short (jumping to the mechanism) or too long (burying the reader in preamble). This skill gives you the architecture for leads that convert.

Two core systems:
1. **Micro Leads** — The 50-200 word opening that sits ON TOP of the lead body. 7 proven types, each triggering curiosity through a different psychological mechanism. Testable independently.
2. **Lead Body Architecture** — The full lead structure: Hook phase → Build belief → Handle objections → Close. Each phase uses specific copy blocks at specific velocities.

## When to Use

- Writing any VSL, long-form sales page, or advertorial
- When VSL watch time is low (lead isn't creating enough curiosity)
- When conversion drops off before the mechanism reveal (lead isn't building enough belief)
- When you need to test openings quickly (micro leads are independently testable)
- When you want to learn from competitors (reverse engineer their leads)

## Skill Stacking

- **→ Luke Iha Copy Blocks**: The lead body is built FROM copy blocks. This skill specifies which blocks, in what order, at what velocity for the lead specifically.
- **→ Luke Iha Million-Dollar Mechanisms**: The mechanism is TEASED in the lead (element #6 of the 15-element checklist) but not revealed. This skill handles the tease. That skill handles the mechanism itself.
- **→ Luke Iha Proof Mechanisms**: Proof appears in the lead's "Handle Objections" phase. Proof Mechanisms provides the specific proof types to deploy.
- **→ Luke Iha Unaware Ads**: Micro lead types overlap with unaware ad hook types — but micro leads are EXPANDED openings (50-200 words vs 1-2 sentences).
- **→ Luke Iha Creative Strategy**: Creative strategy decides the audience and angle. This skill executes the lead FOR that audience at that angle.
