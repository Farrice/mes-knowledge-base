# Implementation Guide: Jun Yuh

## Level 1: Foundation (The Creator Vision)
Define the intersection of What (4 Pillars: Skill, Mindset, Lifestyle, Passions), Who (Psychographics), and Why (Origin Story/3Ps). Establish that the creator *is* the niche.

## Level 2: Production Constraints (Energy Management)
Implement the Batching triad (Day 1: Ideate/Script, Day 2: Film, Day 3: Edit). Constrain the week to 5 Low-Intensity and 2 High-Intensity posts to guarantee lifelong sustainability.

## Level 3: The Assets (Format Mastery)
Master the technical inputs: Silent Film Storytelling (pain hook, music swell, text cadence) and Talking Head (depth, connection). 

## Level 4: The Leverage (Repurposing Engine)
Execute the `1-to-7` multiplication framework, fracturing single high-effort vlogs into multiple psychological formats (Green Screen, Carousels, Captions) using the exact same footage.
