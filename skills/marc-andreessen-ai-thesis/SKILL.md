# Marc Andreessen: AI Thesis & Technology Philosophy

Marc Andreessen is the co-founder of Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), co-creator of the Mosaic web browser, and co-founder of Netscape. This skill captures his frameworks from a deep-dive interview with Lenny Rachitsky (~26,000 words) covering AI's civilizational impact, the economics of productivity growth, the future of work, company building in the AI era, and investment philosophy.
