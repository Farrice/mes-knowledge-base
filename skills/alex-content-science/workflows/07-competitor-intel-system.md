# Competitor Intelligence System

> Load `skills/alex-content-science/genius.md` before executing.

## Inputs Required
- **Your niche** — what domain do you create content in?
- **Platform(s)** — YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.
- **Current competitors** (if known) — list accounts you already follow/watch
- **Goals** — what kind of content opportunities are you looking for? (topics, formats, gaps?)

## The Process

### Step 1 — Build the Competitor Database

Create a systematic intelligence operation:

**Tier 1 — Direct Competitors (5-10)**
Accounts in your exact niche with similar audience size or larger. These are accounts your audience likely also follows.

| Account | Platform | Followers | Posting Frequency | Content Focus |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------------------|---------------|
| | | | | |

**Tier 2 — Adjacent Niche Creators (10-15)**
Accounts serving the SAME audience but from a different angle. (E.g., if you're in AI productivity, these might be general productivity, business strategy, or tech review creators.)

| Account | Platform | Followers | Why Adjacent | What They Do Differently |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------------|-------------------------|
| | | | | |

**Tier 3 — Aspirational Cross-Niche (5-10)**
Accounts in COMPLETELY different niches whose FORMAT or STYLE you admire. These are format-steal sources.

| Account | Platform | Niche | What Format/Style to Study |
|---------|----------|-------|---------------------------|
| | | | |

**Setup**: Create alt/research account (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok). Subscribe to ALL accounts in Tiers 1-3. Let the algorithm's recommendations surface adjacent opportunities you haven't found yet.

### Step 2 — High-Performance Topic Scan

For Tier 1 and Tier 2 accounts, identify high-performing content:

**YouTube Method:**
- Search niche keywords → Filter: Last 3 years, Sort: View count
- Note topics with 1M+ views (or 10x the creator's average)
- Track which topics appear across MULTIPLE creators (validated demand)

**Instagram/TikTok Method:**
- Sort by most liked/viewed
- Note content that broke the account's typical performance
- Track trending audio/formats that performed above baseline

**LinkedIn Method:**
- Check engagement rates (comments are most valuable signal)
- Note topics that generated conversation (not just likes)

Build the tracking database:

| Topic | Creator | Views/Engagement | Their Average | Ratio | Competition Level |
|-------|---------|-----------------|--------------|-------|-------------------|
| | | | | | Low/Med/High |

### Step 3 — Opportunity Mapping

Cross-reference the data to find opportunities:

**High Demand + Low Competition Topics**: Topics getting strong views when covered, but few creators are covering them. These are gold.

**Format Gaps**: What format types are underrepresented in your niche? (E.g., everyone does talking head but nobody does screen recordings, animations, carousels, etc.)

**Timing Patterns**: When do high performers post? What day/time patterns show in the data?

**Content Type Distribution**: Map what TYPES of content dominate:
- Educational ("How to...")
- Personal ("How I...")
- Reactive ("Responding to...")
- Storytelling ("The time I...")
- List/Ranking ("Top 5...")
- Comparison ("X vs Y")

Which types are underrepresented but high-performing when they appear?

### Step 4 — Trend Detection

Identify emerging trends before they saturate:

**Rising Topics**: Topics that appear in Tier 2/3 but haven't hit Tier 1 yet — these are about to arrive.

**Format Migrations**: Formats trending on one platform that haven't crossed over to yours yet.

**Audience Shifts**: What is your audience ASKING for in comments that creators aren't making?

**Decay Detection**: Topics/formats that are declining in performance — the "everyone's doing it" signal to avoid.

### Step 5 — Strategic Action Plan

Convert intelligence into action:

| Opportunity | Type | Priority | Content Concept | Timeline |
|------------|------|----------|----------------|----------|
| [topic/format gap] | [Topic/Format/Timing] | [High/Med/Low] | [specific content idea] | [this week/month] |

**Monthly Cadence**:
- Week 1: Scan Tier 1 competitors for new high performers
- Week 2: Scan Tier 2 adjacencies for format inspiration
- Week 3: Check Tier 3 cross-niche for format hacking opportunities
- Week 4: Compile monthly intelligence brief, update strategy
