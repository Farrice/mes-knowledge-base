# Cross-Niche Format Scanner

> Load `skills/alex-content-science/genius.md` before executing.

## Inputs Required
- **Your niche** — what domain is your content in?
- **Source niches to scan** (suggest 5-10 unrelated niches) — pick from: fitness, cooking, fashion, gaming, travel, music, comedy, science, art, DIY, finance, real estate, tech, interior design, beauty, sports, automotive, education, parenting, pets
- **Platform** — where will you publish? (formats differ by platform)
- **What's stale in your niche** — what formats has everyone been doing that feel tired?

## The Process

### Step 1 — Format Identification in Source Niches

For each unrelated niche, identify 3-5 HIGH-PERFORMING formats that are structurally interesting:

**Niche: [e.g., Cooking]**

| Format Name | Structure | Why It Works | Example |
|-------------|----------|-------------|---------|
| "Ingredient Challenge" | Given random constraint → creative solution | Curiosity + surprise + reveals | "5 meals from $10 groceries" |
| "Taste Test React" | Try → react → teach → rank | Emotional authenticity + list structure | "Pro chef tries fast food" |
| "Speed Run" | Complete task in compressed time | Urgency + visual satisfaction + skill display | "30-second carbonara" |

Focus on the STRUCTURE, not the topic. Strip away the food and look at the skeleton.

### Step 2 — Structure Extraction

For each format, extract the pure structural pattern:

| Original Format | Structural Pattern | Why It Engages |
|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| "5 meals from $10" | **Constraint + Creative Solution** | Curiosity about whether it's possible + viewer learns resourcefulness |
| "Pro chef tries fast food" | **Expert Evaluates Accessible Thing** | Status contrast + relatable subject + authority teaching |
| "30-second carbonara" | **Compressed Time Challenge** | Urgency creates retention + visual satisfaction of completion |

### Step 3 — Niche Translation

Apply each structural pattern to YOUR niche:

| Structural Pattern | Your Niche Application | Working Title |
|-------------------|----------------------|---------------|
| Constraint + Creative Solution | [how this plays in your domain] | [specific video/post idea] |
| Expert Evaluates Accessible Thing | [how this plays in your domain] | [specific video/post idea] |
| Compressed Time Challenge | [how this plays in your domain] | [specific video/post idea] |

**Litmus Test for Each Translation:**
1. Does this exist in your niche already? (If yes → skip or remix further)
2. Can someone trace this back to the cooking channel? (If yes → good — untraceable influence)
3. Does it feel fresh while being immediately understandable? (If yes → green light)

### Step 5 — Production Blueprint

For each priority format, create a production blueprint:

**Format: [Name]**
