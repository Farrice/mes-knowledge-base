# Alex — Content Science

> Forensic content reverse-engineering. See what nobody else sees. Extract principles, not imitations. Build original from the invisible.

## Core Genius

Alex treats content creation as **forensic science**. His Detail Stack methodology reveals the 20 invisible decisions underneath any successful content — environment, wardrobe, audio design, framing, color grading, sentence structure, set dressing — and converts them into one-sentence transferable principles that generate original content in any niche.

**This skill is upstream of all content creation.** Before you create, you must learn to SEE. Before you innovate, you must understand what already works and WHY — at the invisible decision level, not the surface.

## When to Use This Skill

- You want to **study WHY specific content worked** (not just what it was about)
- You're stuck **copying competitors** and want to break free with original content
- You need **format inspiration** from outside your niche
- You have a **boring-but-valuable topic** and need to make it magnetic
- You need **systematic competitive intelligence** instead of casual scrolling
- You want to **audit your own content** for derivative patterns
