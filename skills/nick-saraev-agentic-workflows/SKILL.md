## Overview

Nick Saraev delivers the definitive guide to building, deploying, and productizing agentic AI workflows. This extraction covers the DO framework, self-annealing architectures, parallel build patterns, MCP integrations, and the business side of selling AI automation services.

**Core Principle**: Agents should self-correct, self-improve, and require minimal human intervention in production.

## Key Frameworks

**DO Framework**: Define (objectives, constraints) → Orchestrate (agent coordination)

**Self-Annealing**: Systems that detect errors, adapt, and improve without human intervention

**Horizontal Leverage**: Build once, deploy across multiple clients with minimal customization
