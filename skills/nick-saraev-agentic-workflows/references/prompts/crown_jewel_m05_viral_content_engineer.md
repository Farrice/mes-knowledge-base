## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: VIRAL MECHANICS ANALYSIS

**The Three Viral Multipliers:**

1. **Social Currency** (Makes the sharer look good)
   - Insider knowledge
   - Cutting-edge insights
   - Contrarian but defensible takes
   - "I found this first" factor

2. **Emotional Resonance** (Makes the sharer feel something)
   - Validation ("This is exactly what I've been saying!")
   - Outrage ("Can you believe this?")
   - Inspiration ("I need to try this")
   - Humor ("You have to see this")

3. **Practical Value** (Helps the sharer's network)
   - Actionable frameworks
   - Time-saving tips
   - Money-making insights
   - Problem-solving content

**The Share Test:**
Before creating, answer: "If someone shares this, what caption would they write?"
- If you can't imagine the share caption, it won't spread
