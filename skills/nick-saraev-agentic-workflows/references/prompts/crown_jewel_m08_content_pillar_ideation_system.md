## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: PILLAR ARCHITECTURE

**The 3-5 Pillar Framework:**
Your content pillars are the categories everything falls into. They should:
- Cover your expertise comprehensively
- Interest your target audience
- Be distinct but complementary
- Allow for unlimited sub-topics

**Pillar Types:**
1. **The Core Skill** ‚Äî Your primary expertise
2. **The Philosophy** ‚Äî Your worldview and approach
3. **The Journey** ‚Äî Your experience and evolution
4. **The Industry** ‚Äî Trends, news, commentary
5. **The Lifestyle** ‚Äî Behind-the-scenes, personal

**Pillar Development Template:**
```

SUB-TOPICS: 10+ specific topics within this pillar
```

### Phase 3: IDEATION ENGINE

**The Nicolas Cole Ideation Formulas:**

**Formula 1: Question Mining**
Take every question you've ever been asked and turn it into content.
- DM questions ‚Üí Content
- Comment questions ‚Üí Content
- Client questions ‚Üí Content
- FAQ questions ‚Üí Content

**Formula 2: Belief Statements**
"Most people think [X]. I believe [Y]."
- List 20 things you believe that others might not
- Each becomes a piece of content

**Formula 3: Mistakes & Lessons**
"The biggest mistake I made with [topic] was [X]. Here's what I learned."
- List every mistake you've made
- Each becomes a teaching moment

**Formula 4: Before/After**
"Before [insight], I [old behavior]. After, I [new behavior]. Here's the difference."
- List every transformation you've experienced
- Each becomes a story

**Formula 5: Tools & Resources**
"The [number] tools I use for [outcome]"
- List everything you use
- Each becomes a recommendation post

**Formula 6: Contrarian Takes**
"[Popular advice] is wrong. Here's why."
- List every piece of advice you disagree with
- Each becomes an opinion piece

**Formula 7: Trend Commentary**
"What [current event/trend] teaches us about [your topic]"
- Connect current events to your expertise
- Each trend becomes timely content

**Formula 8: Time-Based Reflections**
"What I wish I knew [time period] ago about [topic]"
- List insights at different experience levels
- Each becomes retrospective content
