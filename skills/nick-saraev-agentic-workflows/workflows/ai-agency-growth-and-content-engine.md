## Input Required
- **[AGENTIC_SERVICE]**: The specific workflow or agentic system you are selling (e.g., Autonomous Lead Gen, Multi-Agent Content Studio).
- **[TARGET_AUDIENCE]**: Who specifically buys this (e.g., Series A SaaS Founders, Boutique Agency Owners).
- **[UNIQUE_MECHANISM]**: The "secret sauce" or proprietary framework that makes your agentic approach different from "just using ChatGPT."
- **[TRANSFORMATION]**: Island A (Current Pain: manual, slow, expensive) → Island B (Desired State: autonomous, scalable, 24/7).
- **[PROOF_POINTS]**: Data, case studies, or technical benchmarks (e.g., 90% reduction in human touchpoints).
- **[PRICE_POINT]**: The investment level for the service.

## Workflow

### Phase 1: The Mechanism & Belief Mapping
*Goal: Define the "Agentic Edge" that makes competition irrelevant.*
1. **Island A/B Analysis**: Map the psychological gap. Identify the "Industrial Age" thinking the client is stuck in (e.g., "hiring more people to scale") and the "Agentic Age" reality you provide.
2. **Mechanism Naming**: Create a proprietary name for your workflow (e.g., The "Orchestrator-Executor" Protocol). 
3. **Belief Inventory**: List the 4 shifts required for a purchase:
   - The problem is the architecture, not the people.
   - Agentic workflows (your mechanism) are the only way to decouple time from output.
   - They can implement this without being technical.
   - The cost of inaction (staying manual) is business suicide.

### Phase 2: The Sales Copy Virtuoso (Conversion Core)
*Goal: Build the "Inevitable Conclusion" sales page.*
1. **The Hook-Story-Offer**:
   - **Hook**: Use a pattern interrupt regarding the "Death of the Prompt Engineer" or the "Rise of the Autonomous Agency."
   - **Story**: Narrative of the "Founder Bottleneck" and the discovery of the [UNIQUE_MECHANISM].
   - **Offer**: Value-stack the service. Include the core build, the maintenance, and the "Human-in-the-loop" safety nets.
2. **The Proof Stack**: Organize proof by specificity. Use "Logic Proof" (how the agents interact) and "Result Proof" (the 22 hours saved per week).
3. **Risk Reversal**: Draft a "Performance-Based" or "Efficiency-First" guarantee.

### Phase 3: The Headline & Hook Alchemist (Attention Entry)
*Goal: Engineer the 5-second scroll-stop for the service.*
1. **Psychology Mapping**: Generate 10 headlines using the **Credibility + Curiosity Matrix**.
   - *Example*: "How a 3-Agent Swarm Replaced a $6k/mo Marketing Manager (With 94% Accuracy)."
2. **Stress Test**: Score each headline (1-10) on Specificity, Curiosity, and Relevance. Discard anything below a 35/50 total score.
3. **Variant Generation**: Create 3 "Contrarian" hooks that attack standard AI advice (e.g., "Why your custom GPTs are actually slowing you down").

### Phase 4: The Viral Content Engineer (Distribution Layer)
*Goal: Create content designed for "Social Currency" and massive reach.*
1. **The Share Test**: Design a LinkedIn post or X Thread where the "Share Caption" makes the reader look like a visionary for finding agentic workflows early.
2. **Structure Optimization**:
   - **LinkedIn**: Focus on "Vindication" (validating the founder's exhaustion with manual Ops).
   - **X Thread**: A "How-To" breakdown of the [UNIQUE_MECHANISM] with a "Save this for your Ops team" CTA.
3. **Multiplication Guide**: Provide a 1→12 repurposing plan to turn the core insight into a week of multi-platform authority building.

### Phase 5: The Email Sequence Architect (Nurture & Loop)
*Goal: A 5-day "Soap Opera" sequence to install the Agentic Worldview.*
1. **Email 1: The Epiphany**: Why "AI Tools" failed them but "Agentic Workflows" won't.
2. **Email 2: The Hidden Cost**: The math of manual labor vs. autonomous agents.
3. **Email 3: The Mechanism Reveal**: Deep dive into the [UNIQUE_MECHANISM].
4. **Email 4: The Social Proof**: A "Day in the Life" of a client using the system.
5. **Email 5: The Hard Pivot**: Urgency-driven call to book a strategy session.
*Note: Every email must open a loop for the next day's insight.*
