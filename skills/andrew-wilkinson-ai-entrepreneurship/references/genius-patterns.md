# Andrew Wilkinson - Genius Patterns

## Pattern 1: Personal-First Product Development
**Execute**: Build solutions for YOUR problems first. If valuable to you, valuable to others. Relationship OS started as personal tool.

**Success Metric**: Product solves your own real problem before pitching to others.

---

## Pattern 2: 30 Employees for $40/day Mindset
**Execute**: Think of AI tools as staff, not software. "What would I assign to an employee?" then assign it to AI.

**Success Metric**: AI handles tasks you'd previously hire humans for.

---

## Pattern 3: Speed of Thought Execution
**Execute**: ADHD + AI = productivity unlock. Don't overthink, just build. Claude Code matches the rapid iteration style.

**Success Metric**: Ideas become working prototypes in hours, not weeks.

---

## Pattern 4: Vibe Coding Mastery
**Execute**: Use natural language to build complete products. Describe what you want, iterate rapidly, deploy quickly.

**Success Metric**: Non-technical builder creates functional software.

---

## Pattern 5: Psychological Safety Net
**Execute**: Build manipulation detection systems for high-stakes interactions. AI analyzes patterns humans miss.

**Success Metric**: Red flags surface before damage occurs.

---

## Pattern 6: Choose-Your-Own-Adventure Email
**Execute**: Multiple draft responses per email, each for different scenarios. Pick and send, don't compose.

**Success Metric**: Email response time drops 80%+.

---

## Pattern 7: MCP Extensibility Thinking
**Execute**: Connect Claude Code to external services through MCP. Expand capabilities systematically.

**Success Metric**: AI actions extend beyond text into real-world effects.

---

## Pattern 8: Software Moat Analysis
**Execute**: Evaluate businesses through AI disruption lens. Ask: "Pennies in front of steamroller or defensible moat?"

**Success Metric**: Investment decisions informed by AI-era defensibility.
