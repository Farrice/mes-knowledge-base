## Hidden Knowledge

## Tacit 1: $30K Product Recognition
Products that solve YOUR expensive problems are worth exactly what you'd pay. Relationship OS = $25-30K value because that's therapy/coaching cost.

**Deploy**: Calculate what you'd pay to solve your problem. That's the product's value.

---

## Tacit 2: Friction Kills Projects
If something creates friction, momentum dies. AI should remove friction, not add it. Build for your own workflow style.

**Deploy**: Design systems that match your natural working pattern.

---

## Tacit 3: Context Sync Across Devices
Valuable AI systems work across your entire life—phone, computer, meetings. Not siloed tools.

**Deploy**: Build systems that follow you, not tools you visit.

---

## Tacit 4: Obvious Opportunities Get Crushed
"Pennies in front of steamroller"—what looks like easy AI money gets commoditized by big players instantly.

**Deploy**: Avoid markets where OpenAI/Google/Meta will obviously compete.

---

## Tacit 5: Distribution > Software
In AI era, software is commoditized. Distribution becomes the moat. Audience > code.

**Deploy**: Build audience before building product.

---

## Tacit 6: Blue-Collar AI Adoption Curve
Knowledge workers scared of AI. Blue-collar workers will adopt faster—less ego, more practical.

**Deploy**: Consider underserved markets where AI fear is lower.

---

## Tacit 7: Compute Ownership as Hedge
Owning AI infrastructure hedges against margin compression as software value deflates.

**Deploy**: Consider compute exposure in portfolio strategy.

---

## Tacit 8: Personal Brand AI Suite
Maintain voice consistency across platforms by having AI trained on your voice, style, and preferences.

**Deploy**: Create personal AI layer that knows you.
