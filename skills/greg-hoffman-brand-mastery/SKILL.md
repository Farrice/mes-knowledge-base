# Greg Hoffman: Brand Mastery

> The complete operational system behind Nike's 27-year creative dominance — emotional brand architecture, insight-driven storytelling, multi-layer campaign design, creative culture engineering, and taste development as competitive moat. Extracted from Greg Hoffman, former Global CMO of Nike and author of *Emotion by Design.*

## What Makes This Unique

Hoffman doesn't theorize about brands — he built one of the most iconic brands in human history from the inside. This skill captures the *institutional operating system* of brand building at scale: the Self-Reflection Brand Question, the Seen→Felt→Proven maturity arc, the Overground/On-the-Ground/Underground campaign framework, the 4-Shot Innovation Offense, and the conviction that taste — not algorithms — will define winners in the AI era.

**Key differentiator from other brand skills**: Alex Copper covers creative strategy. Erica Mallet covers brand magnetism. Oren covers taste development. Hoffman uniquely covers *how all of these operate together inside an institution over decades* — the creative culture, the permission structures, the innovation pipeline, and the emotional architecture that makes products become psychological uniforms.
