# Nicolas Cole — Niche Positioning

## What This Skill Does

Solves positioning blocks by running the specificity ladder down to a Named Person. Every "I don't know which niche" problem is a specificity problem. This skill runs the drill until it becomes uncomfortable — that discomfort is the signal you've hit something real.

Cole's thesis: You don't have a position until you can name a real human being who IS your ideal client. Everything before that is a placeholder.

## When to Load This Skill

- "I don't know which niche to pick"
- "I have lived experience in X but I'm not sure if that's my market"
- "My positioning feels too broad"
- "I have a bias about an industry I know well"
- "I need to figure out who specifically I'm serving"
