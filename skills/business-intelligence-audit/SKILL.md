# Business Intelligence & Deep Analysis Skill

> Extract, analyze, and synthesize any business's online presence to deliver McKinsey-level insights.
This skill enables you to:
1. **Extract** comprehensive data from any business website and online presence
2. **Analyze** using proven McKinsey consulting frameworks (7S, MECE, Pyramid Principle)
3. **Synthesize** into professional audit reports and actionable recommendations
**Use Case:** AI consulting, coaching audits, competitive intelligence, client onboarding
