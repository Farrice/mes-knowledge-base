# Business Intelligence & Deep Analysis Skill

> Extract, analyze, and synthesize any business's online presence to deliver McKinsey-level insights.

## Overview

This skill enables you to:
1. **Extract** comprehensive data from any business website and online presence
2. **Analyze** using proven McKinsey consulting frameworks (7S, MECE, Pyramid Principle)
3. **Synthesize** into professional audit reports and actionable recommendations

**Use Case:** AI consulting, coaching audits, competitive intelligence, client onboarding

---

## When to Use

- Before a sales call with a potential client (know them better than they know themselves)
- Onboarding new coaching/consulting clients
- Competitive analysis for positioning work
- Content research for authority building
- Due diligence on partnerships or opportunities

---

## Workflow

### Quick Audit (30 min)
1. Run **Business Scan** (Prompt 01) on main website
2. Run **Messaging Audit** (Prompt 04) on key pages
3. Run **Gap Identifier** (Prompt 06)
4. Synthesize findings

### Full McKinsey Audit (2-3 hours)
1. **Discovery Phase**
   - Business Scan (01)
   - Competitive Intelligence (02)
   - Customer Intelligence (03)
2. **Analysis Phase**
   - Messaging Audit (04)
   - Digital Presence Analysis (05)
   - Gap Identifier (06)
   - 7S Organizational Scan (07)
3. **Synthesis Phase**
   - SWOT Synthesis (08)
   - Recommendation Engine (09)
   - Executive Report Generator (10)

This skill uses **built-in tools only** (no external APIs):

| Tool | Function |
|------|----------|
| `read_url_content` | Extract text from URLs |
| `browser_subagent` | Handle JS-heavy sites, screenshots |

| `grep_search` | Pattern matching across extracted data |

## Output Quality Standard

A successful audit should:
- ✅ Reveal non-obvious insights the business owner hasn't considered
- ✅ Identify specific gaps and missed opportunities
- ✅ Provide prioritized, actionable recommendations
- ✅ Feel like it came from a $10K consulting engagement
