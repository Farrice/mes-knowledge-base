## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**Pass 1: STRUCTURE**
- Does each scene earn its place?
- Is information revealed at right time?
- Does pacing serve story?

**Pass 2: TENSION**
- Score each scene 1-10 for engagement
- Inject tension where scores are low
- Cut premature resolutions

**Pass 3: CHARACTER**
- Are decisions consistent with character?
- Is each voice distinctive?
- Show-don't-tell check

**Pass 4: SENSORY**
- 3-sense minimum per scene?
- Specific > generic?
- Grounded in body?

**Pass 5: LINE-LEVEL**
- Every word earns its place?
- Rhythm varies?
- Prose serves story?

**Pass 6: MYSTERY AUDIT**
- All loops closed that need closing?
- Remaining ambiguity intentional?
- Reader questions answered?

**Revision notes** per pass with specific change recommendations.
