## INPUT REQUIRED

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **Define the through-line** — What's the spine of the book?
2. **Design the arc** — Beginning state → End state
3. **Create chapter beats** — Each chapter's purpose
4. **Map mysteries** — What opens when, closes when
5. **Plan climaxes** — Emotional peaks and their setup
6. **Design the end** — Where does everything land?

---

## OUTPUT DELIVERABLE

- Core through-line
- Three-act structure
- Chapter-by-chapter breakdown
- Mystery/revelation map
- Emotional architecture

## EXAMPLE OUTPUT

**Context**: Literary thriller about family secrets

### BOOK ARCHITECTURE: "THE LOCKED ROOM"

**CORE THROUGH-LINE**
A woman returns to her childhood home after her mother's death and discovers the family history she was protected from—and the price everyone paid for that protection.

**THREE-ACT STRUCTURE**

**ACT ONE (Chapters 1-8)**: The Return
- Elena returns for mother's funeral
- Discovers locked room in attic
- Mother's lawyer reveals there's an inheritance condition
- Clues suggest family isn't who Elena thought they were
- *FIRST TURNING POINT*: The locked room contains letters from someone who claims to be Elena's real father

**ACT TWO (Chapters 9-20)**: The Unraveling
- Elena investigates her mother's past
- Each discovery recontextualizes her childhood
- Her brother knows more than he admits
- The man in the letters is still alive—and looking for her
- *MIDPOINT*: Elena learns her mother may have committed a crime to protect her
- Her marriage cracks under the weight of secrets
- *SECOND TURNING POINT*: The man appears. He's not who the letters suggested.

**ACT THREE (Chapters 21-26)**: The Truth
- Final revelations about Elena's birth
- Her mother's full sacrifice revealed
- Elena makes her own choice about family, truth, secrets
- *CLIMAX*: Confrontation that reframes everything
- Resolution: Elena decides what to do with the truth

**CHAPTER BEAT BREAKDOWN** (Abbreviated)

| Ch | Title | Purpose | Mystery Status |
|----|-------|---------|----------------|
| 1 | The Funeral | Establish Elena, family dynamics | OPEN: Why was mother secretive? |
| 2 | The House | Elena alone in childhood home | OPEN: The locked room |
| 3-5 | First Discoveries | Letters, photos, inconsistencies | DEEPEN mysteries |
| 8 | The Letter | First turning point | REVEAL: Different father. OPEN: Who is he? |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
| 25 | The Truth | All mysteries close | CLOSE: All |
| 26 | What Remains | Resolution, new normal | Emotional landing |

### MYSTERY/REVELATION MAP

```
Chapter 1:   OPEN: Mother's secrets
Chapter 3:   OPEN: Locked room
Chapter 5:   OPEN: Who is the man in photos?
Chapter 8:   CLOSE: Man is claimed father. OPEN: Is he actually?
Chapter 12:  CLOSE: Real father confirmed. OPEN: Why hidden?
Chapter 18:  CLOSE: Mother's crime revealed. OPEN: Was it justified?
Chapter 24:  CLOSE: Full truth
Chapter 25:  CLOSE: All threads
```

### EMOTIONAL ARCHITECTURE

- **Opening feeling**: Grief, obligation, distance
- **Act One feeling**: Unease, curiosity, dread
- **Midpoint feeling**: Horror, betrayal, confusion
- **Late Act Two**: Desperation, determination
- **Climax feeling**: Catharsis, release
- **Ending feeling**: Peace with complexity
