## Execution

### Phase 1: The 6-Stage Circular Journey Map

Design the complete customer journey with specific transition mechanisms:

#### Stage 1: Stranger → Viewer
- **Transition trigger**: First piece of content they encounter
- **Channels**: Paid ads (→ IG), organic discovery (Reels, YT shorts, SEO), referrals
- **Goal**: Get them to consume ONE full piece of content
- **Assets needed**: Scroll-stopping hooks, targeted ads, SEO content
- **Success metric**: Content view completion rate

#### Stage 2: Viewer → Follower
- **Transition trigger**: They decide your content is worth following
- **Channels**: IG follow, YT subscribe, email opt-in
- **Goal**: Convert passive viewer into active follower
- **Assets needed**: Consistent value delivery, CTA to follow, lead magnets
- **Success metric**: Follow/subscribe rate, email opt-in rate

#### Stage 3: Follower → Lead
- **Transition trigger**: They conclude you can solve their specific problem
- **Channels**: DM, application form, lead magnet download, comment engagement
- **Goal**: Move from passive consumption to active inquiry
- **Assets needed**: Content that addresses their specific pain, stories for nurture, clear "how to work with me" content
- **Success metric**: DM rate, application rate

#### Stage 4: Lead → Client
- **Transition trigger**: All 3 yeses are green — triage setter confirms readiness
- **Channels**: Triage setter qualification → conviction video → sales call
- **Goal**: Close the sale (confirm fit, not sell)
- **Assets needed**: Setter playbook, conviction video, offer document, call script
- **Success metric**: Close rate (target: 80%+)

#### Stage 5: Client → Case
- **Transition trigger**: They achieve the transformation
- **Channels**: Fulfillment delivery, check-ins, milestone tracking
- **Goal**: Deliver exceptional results that create advocates
- **Assets needed**: SOPs, onboarding, accountability systems, check-in protocols
- **Success metric**: Client outcome achievement rate, retention rate

#### Stage 6: Case → Ecosystem Asset (→ loops to Stage 1)
- **Transition trigger**: Success story becomes marketing
- **Channels**: Testimonials, case studies, referrals, co-created content
- **Goal**: Turn their success into YOUR marketing
- **Assets needed**: Testimonial collection workflow, case study template, referral system
- **Success metric**: Referral rate, testimonial count, case study production rate

### Phase 2: Instagram-YouTube Flywheel Blueprint

Design the platform interdependency:

```
PAID ADS → IG (First Impression)
               ↓
         IG Stories (Daily Nurture)     ← Client wins shared here
               ↓
         IG Content (3-Yes Movement)
               ↓
         YouTube (Conviction Bank)      ← Triage setter routes here
               ↓
         DM / Application
               ↓
         Triage Setter
               ↓
         Sales Call (Confirm Fit)
               ↓
         Client → Case → Content → IG (circular)
```

For each node:
1. **Specific content/assets** at this node
2. **Transition mechanism** to the next node
3. **Failure handling** — what happens if they stall?
4. **Re-engagement** — how do you bring them back?

### Phase 3: 3-Yes Asset Mapping

Map EVERY existing and required asset to its YES contribution:

| Asset | Type | Outcome YES | Method YES | Identity YES | Journey Stage |
|-------|------|-------------|------------|-------------|---------------|
| [List each asset] | IG post / YT video / email / etc. | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | Stranger→Viewer / etc. |

Identify:
- **Gaps**: Which yes × which stage has no assets?
- **Overweighted areas**: Where do you have too many assets saying the same thing?
- **Critical missing**: What ONE asset would have the highest impact if created?

### Phase 4: Paid Traffic Integration

Design how paid traffic feeds into (not replaces) the ecosystem:

1. **Traffic destination**: ALWAYS to IG (not landing pages). Build the audience, not the list.
2. **Ad creative**: Should match IG content style (native, not "ad-looking")
3. **Targeting**: ICA psychographics, lookalikes from existing followers/clients
4. **Budget allocation**: Start with $20-50/day, scale based on IG follow rate (not click rate)
5. **Measurement**: Track follower-to-DM conversion, not ad-to-sale conversion. The ecosystem does the selling.

### Phase 5: Team & Operations Layer

For the ecosystem to function, define:

1. **Triage setter role**: Hire/train for qualifying, NOT selling (see workflow 07)
2. **Content production**: Who creates? Who edits? What's the production workflow?
3. **Client fulfillment**: Who delivers? What's the onboarding flow?
4. **Systems**: CRM, scheduling, communication channels, SOPs
