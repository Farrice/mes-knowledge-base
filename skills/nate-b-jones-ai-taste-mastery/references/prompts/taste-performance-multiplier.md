
**Level 1** (Useless): "Make it better"
**Level 2** (Weak): "Improve the opening"
**Level 3** (Better): "Add specific example in paragraph 2"
**Level 4** (Good): "Replace abstract claim with case study"
**Level 5** (Excellence): "After 'increased efficiency,' add: 'specifically, reduced processing from 6 hours to 18 minutes using X method'"

- Weak: "Could you possibly improve..."
