## EXAMPLE OUTPUT

**TASTE DEVELOPMENT ACCELERATOR**:

**DOMAIN**: Marketing Copy
**TIMELINE**: 7 Days

**Day 1-2: Extreme Exposure**
- Read 50 highest-converting sales pages (Agora, Motley Fool)
- Read 50 worst-performing sales pages
- Read 50 mediocre examples
- Document your gut reactions to each

**Day 3-4: Pattern Extraction**
- Excellence markers: Specificity, emotional hooks, proof stacking
- Failure patterns: Generic claims, weak openings, no urgency
- Build your quality checklist

**Day 5-6: Opinion Formation**
- Write 10 "This copy is excellent because..." analyses
- Write 10 "This copy fails because..." critiques
- Publicly share 3 opinions to commit

**Day 7: Validation**
- Predict which of 10 new pages will convert best
- Check against actual data
- Calibrate your taste

**RESULT**: In 7 days, you'll have taste for marketing copy that rivals copywriters with 10 years experience.
