## Content Categorization

### COLD ACQUISITION CONTENT
- Stands alone without context
- Credibility front-loaded
- Optimized for shares and discovery
- Platform algorithm-friendly
- Hooks for people who don't know you

### WARM NURTURE CONTENT
- Deepens relationship
- References shared history
- Inside jokes and callbacks
- Community building
- Conversion-focused

## Recommended Split
- ~60% Cold (acquisition)
- ~40% Warm (nurture)

Adjust based on current needs (heavy growth phase = more cold)

## Execution Protocol

1. **AUDIT** current content mix
2. **IDENTIFY** imbalances
3. **CREATE** calendar with intentional allocation
4. **TRACK** acquisition AND retention metrics
5. **ADJUST** ratio based on results

## Output Deliverable

Dual-Track Content Plan:
- Content audit (current mix)
- Recommended ratio adjustment
- Cold content guidelines
- Warm content guidelines
- Weekly calendar template
- Metrics to track for each track

## Performance Metrics

- Audience growth AND retention
- Not just one or the other
- Sustainable content ecosystem
