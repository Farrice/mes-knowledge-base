# Student-Expert Diagnostic

> Determine if you're a student or expert, and build strategy accordingly.

Core insight: Students learning in public build brands on authenticity. Experts teaching in public build brands on authority. Both work. Mismatches kill trust.

## Input Required

- **[ACHIEVEMENTS]**: What have you accomplished in this space?
- **[PUBLIC_WS]**: Do you have multiple public "W's" in this area?
- **[AUDIENCE_DESIRE]**: What does your audience want to achieve?
- **[YOUR_STATUS]**: Have YOU achieved what they want?

## The Diagnostic

### EXPERT SIGNALS
- Multiple public, verifiable achievements in the area
- You've done what your audience wants to do
- You can teach from accomplished results
- People would pay for your proven experience

### STUDENT SIGNALS
- Still learning the craft yourself
- Haven't yet achieved the end result
- Journey is in progress, not complete
- Can document learning, not teach mastery

## Execution Protocol

1. **ASSESS** honestly: achievements vs. claims
2. **DIAGNOSE**: Student or Expert?
3. **ACCEPT** the truth of where you are
4. **STRATEGIZE** accordingly (document vs. teach)
5. **BUILD** content strategy for the correct identity

## Output Deliverable

Identity Diagnosis:
- Honest assessment of achievements
- Student or Expert determination
- Content strategy for correct identity
- Transition plan (if student becoming expert)
- Trust-preservation guidelines

## Performance Metrics

- Zero mismatch between claimed expertise and actual experience
- Trust preserved through authenticity
