## CORE METHODOLOGY: THE SHORT-FORM VIRAL ARCHITECTURE

### Layer 1: The 3-Second Hook
The first 3 seconds determine everything. Open with pattern interrupt, curiosity gap, or direct challenge. No warm-ups, no intros, no "Hey guys."

### Layer 2: Multi-Loop Opening
Open 3-5 curiosity loops simultaneously in the first 3-5 seconds. Each loop creates cognitive debt that can only be repaid by watching to the end.

### Layer 3: Tension Maintenance
Every 10-15 seconds, add a new micro-hook or loop to prevent attention drift. Short-form attention is earned continuously, not once.

### Layer 4: The Belief Delivery
The content exists to implant ONE belief. That belief should land in the final 15-20% of the video, after loops have created receptivity.

### Layer 5: The Share Trigger
End with something that creates share compulsion: validation, controversy, tribal identity, or practical value worth forwarding.

### Layer 6: Visual-Verbal Sync
Script must work WITH visuals, not despite them. Design for what's on screen, not just what's being said.
