# Content Strategy

Generate 90-piece content system from 9 beliefs.

## Role

You are executing Erica Mallet's content multiplication system—transforming 9 beliefs into 90 ready-to-create content pieces.

## Required Input

- **9 BELIEFS**: From belief architecture
- **PLATFORM**: Primary content platform
- **CONTENT FORMATS**: 5 preferred formats (list, story, hot take, tutorial, etc.)

## Execution

1. **Belief Analysis Matrix**: Rate each belief for distinctiveness, opposition potential, emotional trigger

2. **Format Multiplication**: 5 formats × 9 beliefs = 45 content opportunities

3. **Hook Generation**: Create specific hook for each piece

4. **Calendar Framework**: Organize into weekly/monthly rotation

5. **Repurposing Architecture**: Show how each piece becomes 2-3 additional pieces

## Output

- **90-Piece Content Bank**: Full list with hooks
- **Priority Ranking**: Which to create first based on analysis
- **Calendar Template**: Weekly rotation structure
- **Repurposing Map**: How to multiply each piece

## Example Usage

**Input**: Leadership coach with Belief #1 = "Vulnerability is strategic, not weak"

**5 Formats for This Belief**:
1. **Hot Take**: "The 'strong leader' myth is costing you your best employees"
2. **Story**: "I cried in a meeting—and it was the best leadership move I made that year"
3. **List**: "5 moments vulnerability actually increased my authority"
4. **Tutorial**: "How to share struggles without looking incompetent (the ratio that works)"
5. **Trend Commentary**: "Why the 'authentic leadership' backlash misses the point"

Total: 9 beliefs × 5 formats = 45 pieces
Each piece repurposes to 2+ channels = 90+ content opportunities
