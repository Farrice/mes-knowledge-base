## PHASE 1: DISCOVERY & EXCAVATION

### Pre-Session Client Questionnaire

**Send 3-5 days before first session:**

```
BRAND MAGNETISM DISCOVERY QUESTIONNAIRE

SECTION 1: YOUR JOURNEY
1. Walk me through your career/expertise journey. What roles, experiences, and pivots shaped you?
2. What do people consistently praise you for? What feedback keeps repeating?
3. What do you do better than anyone else you know?
4. What work energizes you vs. drains you?
5. What would you do for free because you love it so much?

SECTION 2: YOUR AUDIENCE
6. Who is your ideal client/customer? Be specific.
7. What problems do they come to you with?
8. What transformation do you create for them?
9. What do they believe before working with you vs. after?
10. Who are they NOT? Who do you actively NOT want to work with?

SECTION 3: YOUR POSITIONING
11. Who do you get compared to? Do you like that comparison?
12. What makes you different from others in your space?
13. What do you wish people understood about your work?
14. What conventional wisdom in your industry do you disagree with?
15. If you had to describe your approach in one sentence, what would it be?

SECTION 4: YOUR VISION
16. What does success look like in 1 year? 3 years?
17. What kind of work do you want to be known for?
18. What would your ideal day look like?
19. What legacy do you want to leave?
20. What would make you feel like you've "made it"?
```

---

### Discovery Session Agenda (90 minutes)

**Part 1: Through-Line Excavation (30 min)**

Questions to ask:
- "What's the thread connecting every role you've had?"
- "What did you love in Job A that you also loved in Job B?"
- "If a stranger watched you work for a week, what patterns would they notice?"
- "What do you do automatically that others have to think about?"

**Part 2: Expertise Mapping (30 min)**

Questions to ask:
- "Walk me through how you actually solve [their core problem]"
- "What questions do you always ask at the start?"
- "Where do clients usually get stuck before they work with you?"
- "What's the biggest mistake people make in your field?"

**Part 3: Enemy Identification (30 min)**

Questions to ask:
- "What advice in your industry makes you angry?"
- "What do competitors do that you refuse to do?"
- "What approach is broken that people keep following?"
- "If you could change one thing about your industry, what would it be?"

## PHASE 2: BELIEF ARCHITECTURE

### Belief Building Session Agenda (90 minutes)

**Part 1: Theme Identification (30 min)**
From Discovery findings, identify 3 core themes that capture their worldview.

Questions:
- "What are the 3 big ideas you want to be known for?"
- "If someone followed you for a year, what 3 topics would they hear most?"
- "What 3 things do you believe that not everyone in your space believes?"

**Part 2: Belief Excavation (45 min)**
For each theme, identify 3 specific beliefs.

Framework for each belief:
- "What do you believe about [theme] that others don't?"
- "If you could make everyone understand one thing about [theme], what?"
- "What's the controversial take you have on [theme]?"

**Part 3: Enemy Refinement (15 min)**
- "Given these beliefs, who/what are you fighting against?"
- "What does the enemy want people to believe?"
- "How does your approach defeat the enemy?"
