# Erica Mallet - Implementation Pathway

## 24-Hour Brand Foundation

**Hour 0-4: Discovery**
- List every skill, experience, interest
- Find connecting thread (through-line)
- Identify 3 themes you could discuss for 5 years

**Hour 4-8: Architecture**
- Extract 9 beliefs (3 per theme)
- Name your enemy
- Define your hero
- Choose 3 tone words

**Hour 8-16: First Content**
- Write one enemy-effect post
- Create one belief-embedded piece
- Run through tone filter

**Hour 16-24: Validation**
- Share with trusted person for clarity check
- Adjust based on feedback
- Document brand architecture

---

## 7-Day Sprint

**Day 1-2**: Complete 24-hour foundation
**Day 3**: Differentiation analysis (15 expectations, violations)
**Day 4**: Content strategy (45 pieces planned from 9 beliefs)
**Day 5**: Psychological loop training (analyze 3 viral videos)
**Day 6**: Create 5 pieces with new architecture
**Day 7**: Publish, measure response, refine

---

## 30-Day Transformation

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Complete belief architecture
- Define enemy and hero
- Establish tone triad
- First 7 pieces published

**Week 2: Differentiation**
- Complete expectation violation analysis
- Refine positioning statement
- Test different angles in content
- Document what resonates

**Week 3: Engagement**
- Master psychological loops
- Deploy enemy effect systematically
- Build connection touchpoints
- Track tribal signals (DMs, comments, shares)

**Week 4: Optimization**
- Audit first 3 weeks
- Double down on winners
- Refine brand architecture based on response
- Establish sustainable rhythm

**Deliverable**: Complete brand system operational. Content feels effortless. Audience responds with "finally someone gets it."
