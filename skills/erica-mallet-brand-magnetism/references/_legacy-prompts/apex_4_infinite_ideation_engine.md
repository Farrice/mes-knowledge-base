### Layer 1: The Belief Expression Matrix

Every belief can be expressed through multiple lenses:

| Expression Type | What It Does | Example Pattern |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **THE STATEMENT** | Direct declaration | "Here's what I believe about X" |
| **THE STORY** | Narrative proof | "The time I learned X" |
| **THE PROOF** | Evidence/data | "Why X is actually true (research)" |
| **THE CONTRAST** | Enemy comparison | "They say Y. Here's why X is better." |
| **THE QUESTION** | Socratic trigger | "What if X isn't what you think?" |
| **THE EXAMPLE** | Specific case | "When Client A discovered X" |
| **THE PERMISSION** | Validation grant | "It's okay to believe X" |
| **THE FRAMEWORK** | Systematic approach | "The 3-step process for X" |
| **THE PREDICTION** | Future implication | "Where X leads in 5 years" |
| **THE CONFESSION** | Vulnerable share | "I didn't always believe X. Here's what changed." |

**One belief × 10 expression types = 10 content pieces**
