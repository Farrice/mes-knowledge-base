## CORE METHODOLOGY: THE STRATEGIC CALENDAR ARCHITECTURE

### Layer 1: Monthly Theme
Each month has a primary theme that creates focus. All content ladders up to this theme, creating cumulative recognition.

### Layer 2: Weekly Focus
Each week within the month has a specific focus area—progressing the monthly theme while providing variety.

### Layer 3: Daily Rhythm
Consistent posting times and content types create audience expectation. Rhythm builds habit.

### Layer 4: Pillar Rotation
Rotate through content pillars systematically to ensure balanced coverage and prevent monotony.

### Layer 5: Depth Variation
Mix content depths: surface pieces (discoverable), medium pieces (engaging), deep pieces (converting). Ratio typically 50/35/15.

### Layer 6: Production Batching
Group similar content types for efficient production. Create all carousels together, all videos together, etc.

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **THEME SELECTION**: Define or confirm the monthly theme based on business goals and audience needs.

2. **WEEKLY BREAKDOWN**: Divide the theme into 4 weekly focus areas that progressively build.

3. **PILLAR MAPPING**: Assign content pillars to specific days/slots to ensure rotation.

4. **CONTENT SLOT CREATION**: Create specific slots with format, pillar, depth, and platform assigned.

5. **ANGLE ASSIGNMENT**: Fill each slot with specific content angles from the content bank.

6. **PRODUCTION BATCHING**: Organize by production type for efficient creation.

7. **FLEXIBILITY BUFFER**: Leave 10-20% slots flexible for reactive content and trending opportunities.

## FLEXIBILITY SLOTS USAGE GUIDE

**Sunday slots are FLEX for:**

1. **Trending Topics**: If a market signal connects to beliefs, create rapid-response content
2. **High-Performer Amplification**: If something from the week performed exceptionally, create a follow-up
3. **Engagement Focus**: Spend time responding to comments, DMs, engaging with others' content
4. **Rest/Buffer**: If production is behind, use as catch-up
5. **Experimentation**: Test a new format or angle

**Rule**: Never fill FLEX slots more than 24 hours in advance. Keep them reactive.
