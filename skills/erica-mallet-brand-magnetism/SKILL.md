# Erica Mallet Brand Magnetism

> Become a category of one using the science and psychology of icons—building brands so magnetic they stop scrollers and create tribal belonging.

## Overview

Erica Mallet has distilled 10+ years of brand building across music, radio, advertising, and content creation into deployable frameworks. Her methodology transforms random content into coherent worldview systems that create obsessive audience connection.

**Core Capabilities:**
- Belief-based brand architecture (3 themes, 9 beliefs)
- Enemy Effect deployment for tribal belonging
- Psychological loop creation (Zeigarnik Effect)
- Differentiation through expectation violation
- Connection economy positioning over attention economy

## Quick Start

**24-Hour Brand Foundation:**
1. Identify your through-line (what connects everything you do)
2. Extract 3 themes and 9 beliefs
3. Name your enemy (what you stand against)
4. Define your hero (who you're becoming)
5. Choose 3 tone words
