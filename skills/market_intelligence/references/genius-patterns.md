## 2. The "Entity Understanding" Protocol (NEW - Critical Fix)
**Origin**: Perplexity Verification Failure (Feb 2026)
**Concept**: Before generating ANY keyword, the system MUST understand what the Niche IS.

### Entity Types
| Entity Type | Example | Correct Keyword Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Product** | "Running Shoes" | "best running shoes", "buy running shoes online" |
| **Service** | "Plumbing" | "plumber near me", "emergency plumber cost" |
| **Demographic** | "First Time Home Buyers" | Keywords for PROGRAMS that SERVE them: "first time home buyer grants", "down payment assistance" |
| **Program** | "CalHFA Loan" | "CalHFA eligibility", "CalHFA vs FHA" |

> **THE FATAL MISTAKE**: Treating a *Demographic* like a *Product*. "Buy First Time Home Buyers" is nonsense.

## 3. The "Shadow Market" Protocol
**Origin**: Samuel Thompson (Product Launch Expert)
**Concept**: Don't just look for "Trends". Look for **"Rigged Slot Machines"**.
- **The Heuristic**: A "Shadow Market" is a niche where customers are *desperate* but serviced by *amateurs*.
- **The Equation**: `Desperation` (Pain) x `Money` (Purchasing Power) / `Competition Quality` (Competency).
- **Signal**: High Search Volume for "Solution" keywords, but top results are ugly, broken, or outdated.

## 4. The "Retrieval Layer" Audit
**Origin**: Nathan Gotch (AI SEO)
**Concept**: In 2026, you don't just rank on Google. You must be "cited" by LLMs (Gemini, ChatGPT).
- **The Metric**: **Citational Authority**.
