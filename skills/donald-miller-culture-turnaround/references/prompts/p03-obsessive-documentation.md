## Execution Protocol

1. **MAP** the Touchpoint Universe - every interaction point, internal and external
2. **DEFINE** Excellence for Each Touchpoint - specific, observable, trainable behaviors
3. **CREATE** Standard Operating Protocols - step-by-step eliminating ambiguity
4. **DESIGN** Accountability Mechanisms - how each standard is measured
5. **BUILD** Training Infrastructure - how new members learn, existing members reminded
6. **PRODUCE** comprehensive operational playbook

---

## Output Deliverable

Operational Excellence Playbook:
- Executive Summary (Philosophy of Documentation)
- Complete Touchpoint Map
- Excellence Standards by Category (specific, measurable)
- Standard Operating Protocols for Major Functions
- Accountability & Measurement Framework
- Training & Onboarding Standards
- Daily/Weekly/Monthly Excellence Rituals
- Quality Control Checkpoints
- Living Document Maintenance Plan

## Success Metric
