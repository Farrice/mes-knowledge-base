## Common Resistance Patterns:
- Identity Threat: Change challenges who they are
- Loss Aversion: Focus on what's lost, not gained
- Competence Fear: Worry they can't perform in new system
- Political Protection: Change threatens power/status
- Nostalgia Anchor: Past is idealized and protected
