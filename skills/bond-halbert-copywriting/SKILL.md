## The 4 Foundation Methodologies

Every piece of copy must embody these:

1. **Market Language Excavation** - Use their exact words, not corporate assumptions
2. **The Pee Test** - If it can be skipped, cut it (400% improvement)
3. **Four Reader Types** - Structure for OCD, skimmers, jumpers, and headline-bullet-PS readers
4. **Objection Pre-Positioning** - Answer questions before they form
