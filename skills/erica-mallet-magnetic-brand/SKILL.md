# Erica Mallet: Magnetic Brand System

> Complete extraction of Erica Mallet's methodology: 44 prompts for brand magnetism, content creation, and cult-like following through belief architecture and entertainment psychology.

## Overview

Erica Mallet has distilled 10+ years of brand building across music, radio, advertising, and content creation into 44 deployable prompts. Her methodology transforms generic creators into magnetic brands that attract cult-like followings through belief architecture, enemy effects, psychological loops, and entertainment-driven content.

**Core Principle**: Connection beats attention. Belief-based brands create belonging that algorithms can't replicate.
