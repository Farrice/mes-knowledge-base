> Produce a complete publishing calendar with content mapped to beliefs, platforms, and goals.

## Required Input

```
[CONTENT_PILLARS]: Your 3-4 pillars (from pillar-architecture)
[PLATFORMS]: Where you publish and how often
[WEEKLY_CAPACITY]: Pieces per platform per week
[LAUNCH_OR_PROMOTION]: Any events, launches, campaigns this month
```

---

## Calendar Architecture

**WEEK THEMES** (Pillar rotation):
- Week 1: Pillar 1 focus
- Week 2: Pillar 2 focus
- Week 3: Pillar 3 focus
- Week 4: Mixed + promotion

**DAILY RHYTHMS** (Platform alignment):
- Monday: Long-form (newsletter, article)
- Tuesday-Thursday: Short-form (posts, reels)
- Friday: Engagement/conversation
- Weekend: Evergreen/scheduled

---

## Execution

### Step 1: Month Strategy
Plan monthly arc:
- What's the big theme?
- Any launches/promotions?
- Key dates to leverage?
- Belief emphasis this month?

### Step 2: Week Assignment
Map pillars to weeks:
- Week 1 focus
- Week 2 focus
- Week 3 focus
- Week 4 synthesis

### Step 3: Platform Allocation
Assign content by platform:
- Platform A: X pieces/week
- Platform B: Y pieces/week
- Cross-posting strategy
- Platform-specific adaptations

### Step 4: Specific Content Assignment
Fill each slot:
- Content type
- Content angle
- Belief connection
- Format specifications

### Step 5: Production Batching
Group for efficient creation:
- Batch similar formats
- Batch by platform
- Batch by energy level required
