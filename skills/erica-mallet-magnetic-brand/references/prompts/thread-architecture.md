## Thread Architecture Types

**FRAMEWORK THREAD**:
1. Bold claim/hook
2. Why this matters (stakes)
3. Context/background
4-8. Steps/elements (one per tweet)
9. Synthesis
10. CTA

**STORY THREAD**:
1. Hook into the drama
2. Setup (who, where, when)
3-7. Rising action (conflict escalates)
8. Climax (turning point)
9. Resolution + insight
10. Universal lesson + CTA

**LISTICLE THREAD**:
1. Hook + list promise
2-9. Individual items (strongest first and last)
10. Summary + CTA

**HOT TAKES THREAD**:
1. Controversial opener
2-8. Individual takes (increasingly bold)
9. Synthesis/underlying principle
10. CTA

## Execution

### Step 1: Hook Engineering
Create unstoppable first tweet:
- Curiosity gap
- Bold claim
- Story hook
- Pattern interrupt
- Number promise

### Step 2: Progressive Revelation
Structure each tweet:
- One idea per tweet
- End on incomplete thought
- Next tweet resolves but opens new
- Maintain momentum

### Step 3: Rhythm Design
Create reading flow:
- Short tweets between long
- Lists break up prose
- Vary sentence rhythm
- Strategic line breaks

### Step 4: Close Engineering
Earn the follow/engagement:
- Summarize value delivered
- Tease future content
- Clear CTA
- Engagement prompt
