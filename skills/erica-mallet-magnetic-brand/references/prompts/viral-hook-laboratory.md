## Execution

### Step 1: Share Trigger Mapping
Select primary trigger:

**IDENTITY AMPLIFICATION**: Content people share to signal who they are
- "This is so me"
- "Tag someone who..."
- Identity validation

**ENEMY ACTIVATION**: Content people share to call out opposition
- "Finally someone said it"
- "This is why I left..."
- Tribal signaling

**UNEXPECTED TRUTH**: Content people share to seem insightful
- "I never thought of it this way"
- "Mind = blown"
- Insight currency

**EMOTIONAL RESONANCE**: Content people share to process feelings
- "This hit different"
- "Watch to the end"
- Catharsis seeking

### Step 2: Hook Engineering
Build hook with:
- Open loop (unanswered question)
- Trigger activation (why they'll stop)
- Pattern interrupt (unexpected element)
- Identity marker (who this is for)

### Step 3: Scroll-Stop Element
Add pattern interrupt:
- Contradiction to expectations
- Specific number or claim
- Emotional word choice
- Visual curiosity (if applicable)

### Step 4: Share Prediction
Assess shareability:
- Why would someone share this?
- What does sharing say about them?
- What conversation does this start?
