## Execution

### Step 1: Belief Activation
Select belief to explore:
- Which belief is most relevant now?
- Which needs more content?
- Which creates strongest reaction?

### Step 2: Expression Selection
Choose angle:
- What's your energy for expressing it?
- What's news/relevant now?
- What format fits the platform?

### Step 3: Format Fit
Match to platform:
- Short-form: Reels, posts
- Medium: Carousels, threads
- Long-form: Newsletters, videos

### Step 4: Emotion Optimization
Layer feeling:
- Primary emotion to create
- Secondary emotion for depth
- Hook emotion (first impression)

### Step 5: Idea Generation
Produce batch:
- Generate 10 ideas minimum
- Score for energy (would you enjoy creating?)
- Flag gems for priority
