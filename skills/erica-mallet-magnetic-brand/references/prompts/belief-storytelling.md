## Execution

### Step 1: Belief-to-Story Fusion
Connect belief to narrative:
- Belief becomes theme, not lesson
- Character embodies belief discovery
- Conflict represents belief violation
- Resolution demonstrates belief truth

### Step 2: Character-Audience Mirroring
Create audience identification:
- Starting point matches audience
- Struggles match audience
- Mistakes match audience
- Transformation inspires audience

### Step 3: Implicit Belief Embedding
Hide belief in narrative:
- Never state belief directly
- Let audience conclude it
- Make it feel like their insight
- "Show don't tell" the belief

### Step 4: Emotional Anchor Points
Design emotional journey:
- Opening hook (curiosity)
- Rising action (tension)
- Crisis point (fear/doubt)
- Turning point (hope)
- Resolution (satisfaction)
- Echo (lasting impression)

### Step 5: Narrative Transportation Depth
Maximize absorption:
- Sensory details
- Internal thought access
- Present-tense moments
- Dialogue when possible
