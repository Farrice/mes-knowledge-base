## Article Architecture

**HOW-TO ARTICLE**:
- Hook + Promise
- Why This Matters
- Prerequisites/Context
- Step-by-Step (numbered)
- Common Mistakes
- Pro Tips
- Summary/Next Steps

**LISTICLE**:
- Hook + List Promise
- Context for list
- Individual items (expanding)
- Synthesis
- Take Action

**ULTIMATE GUIDE**:
- Table of Contents
- What + Why (overview)
- Who This Is For
- Core sections (4-7)
- Advanced topics
- FAQ
- Summary + Resources

**OPINION/HOT TAKE**:
- Bold claim hook
- Context/stakes
- Your argument (3-5 points)
- Counterargument acknowledgment
- Conclusion + implications

**CASE STUDY**:
- Result hook
- Background/context
- Challenge/problem
- Solution/approach
- Results/data
- Lessons learned
- Application for reader

## Execution

### Step 1: Structure Selection
Choose based on topic and goal:
- Teaching: How-To, Guide
- Traffic: Listicle, Guide
- Authority: Opinion, Case Study
- Conversion: Case Study, Guide

### Step 2: Outline Architecture
Build before writing:
- H2s for main sections
- H3s for subsections
- Bullet points for each section
- Key points to cover

### Step 3: Section Writing
Execute each section:
- Hook the section
- Deliver content
- Transition forward
- Maintain belief thread

### Step 4: SEO Integration
Optimize without sacrificing:
- Keyword in title, H1, intro
- Related keywords in H2s
- Natural keyword placement
- Internal linking

### Step 5: Engagement Elements
Break up text:
- Strategic images
- Pull quotes
- Bullet lists
- Callout boxes
- Practical examples
