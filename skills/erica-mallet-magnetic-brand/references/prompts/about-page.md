## About Page Architecture

**ABOVE THE FOLD**:
- Hero statement (what you stand for)
- Visual (you or brand)
- Quick credibility marker

**ORIGIN STORY**:
- Struggle/challenge
- Turning point
- Discovery/insight
- Current mission

**BELIEF DECLARATION**:
- "I believe..."
- "I believe..."
- "I believe..."

**CREDIBILITY SECTION**:
- Experience proof
- Results proof
- Media/recognition
- Testimonials (for YOU, not just results)

**WHO THIS IS FOR**:
- Ideal client description
- "You're in the right place if..."
- "I'm not for everyone..."

**CTA SECTION**:
- What to do next
- How to work together
- Connection opportunity

---

## Execution

### Step 1: Hero Engineering
Create immediate connection:
- What you stand for (1 sentence)
- Who you help (1 sentence)
- Visual that builds trust

### Step 2: Story Architecture
Build connection through narrative:
- Relatable beginning
- Struggle they recognize
- Transformation that inspires
- Mission that invites

### Step 3: Belief Embedding
Declare positions:
- 3 clear belief statements
- Language that resonates or repels
- Tribe-building declarations

### Step 4: Proof Integration
Establish authority:
- Credentials if relevant
- Results and impact
- Social proof
- Media mentions

### Step 5: Conversion Design
Guide next step:
- Clear CTA
- Multiple entry points
- Emotional close

---

**Complete About Page** including:
- Full About page copy (500-1500 words)

- Photo/visual recommendations
- CTA options
- SEO metadata
- Design layout suggestions
