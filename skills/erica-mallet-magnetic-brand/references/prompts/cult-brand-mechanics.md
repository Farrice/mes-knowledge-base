## Cult Brand Elements

**TRIBAL LANGUAGE**:
- Insider vocabulary (15-20 terms)
- Enemy naming convention
- Member naming (what are they called?)
- Transformation vocabulary
- Greeting/closing rituals

**IDENTITY MARKERS**:
- What members say about themselves
- What others say about members
- Visual identifiers (optional)
- Behavioral identifiers
- Values statements

**RITUALS**:
- Daily/weekly practices
- Initiation experiences
- Milestone celebrations
- Group activities
- Content consumption habits

**STATUS ARCHITECTURE**:
- Entry level (new members)
- Core level (engaged members)
- Inner circle (super fans)
- Recognition systems
- Advancement pathways

---

## Execution

### Step 1: Tribal Language Development
Create insider vocabulary:
- 15-20 unique terms
- What the enemy is called
- What members call themselves
- Key concepts with special names

### Step 2: Identity Marker Creation
Design recognition systems:
- "I am a [member name]" statements
- Values declarations
- Behavioral markers

### Step 3: Ritual Engineering
Build recurring practices:
- What members do daily
- Weekly engagement
- Monthly moments
- Annual celebrations

### Step 4: Status System
Create advancement:
- Entry criteria
- Progression levels
- Recognition methods
- Inner circle access

### Step 5: Experience Design
Map the member journey:
- First encounter
- Initiation moment
- Deepening engagement
- Full commitment
