> Architect content strategy for depth over reach, prioritizing connection metrics over vanity metrics.

---

---

## Execution

### Step 1: Belief-Based Pillar Organization
Group beliefs into content pillars:
- Pillar 1: [Theme 1 beliefs]
- Pillar 2: [Theme 2 beliefs]
- Pillar 3: [Theme 3 beliefs]

### Step 2: Depth Stratification
For each pillar, create depth levels:
- Surface: Quick takes, hot takes (attention)
- Medium: Stories, how-tos (engagement)
- Deep: Vulnerable shares, origin stories (connection)

### Step 3: Connection Mechanism Mapping
For each content piece, identify:
- Why would someone DM about this?
- What reply would this prompt?
- Why would someone refer a friend?

### Step 4: AI-Resistance Analysis
Evaluate content for human-only value:
- What in this requires human experience?
- What can't AI replicate?
- How does this build trust over time?

### Step 5: Connection KPIs
Define success metrics:
- DM rate per post
- Reply rate per post
- Referral rate per month
- Testimonial generation rate

---

## Output

**Complete Connection Content System** including:
- 3 Content Pillars with belief mapping
- Depth ladder for each pillar
- 90-day content calendar
- Connection mechanism for each piece
- AI-resistance score for each type
- KPI dashboard template
- Vulnerability roadmap (when to go deep)

---

## Success Metric

- DM/reply rate increases month-over-month
- Customer conversion from followers improves
- Referral rate increases
- Content feels irreplaceable
