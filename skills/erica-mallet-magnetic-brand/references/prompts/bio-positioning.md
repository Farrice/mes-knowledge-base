## Bio Types

**TWITTER/X BIO** (160 chars):
- Role + Belief + Proof

**LINKEDIN HEADLINE** (120 chars):
- What + Who + Result

**LINKEDIN ABOUT** (2600 chars):
- Hook + Story + Beliefs + Proof + CTA

**INSTAGRAM BIO** (150 chars):
- What + Who + Personality + CTA

**SPEAKER BIO** (100-150 words):
- Credibility + Story + Current focus

**PITCH DECK BIO** (50-75 words):
- Authority + Results + Relevance

---

## Execution

### Step 1: Core Message Distillation
Find the essence:
- One sentence of what you do
- One sentence of who you help
- One result you create

### Step 2: Credibility Selection
Choose strongest proof:
- For each context, what matters?
- Match proof to audience
- Lead with most relevant

### Step 3: Belief Integration
Embed positioning:
- Hot take version
- Professional version
- Personality version

### Step 4: Platform Optimization
Adapt to context:
- Character limits
- Audience expectations
- Platform culture
- Emoji appropriateness
