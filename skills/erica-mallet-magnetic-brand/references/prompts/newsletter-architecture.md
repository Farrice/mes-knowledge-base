## Newsletter Architecture

**THE PERSONAL LETTER**:
- Personal opener (like writing to friend)
- Main insight/story
- Application/takeaway
- Brief update/CTA
- Warm sign-off

**THE INSIGHT DROP**:
- Hook (why this matters now)
- Core insight (one big idea)
- Supporting points (2-3)
- Application examples
- Reader CTA

**THE CURATOR**:
- Themed intro
- 5-7 curated links/resources
- Your take on each
- One original insight
- Community prompt

**THE STORY**:
- In media res opening
- Story unfolds
- Insight embedded
- Universal lesson
- Emotional close

## Execution

### Step 1: Architecture Selection
Choose based on purpose:
- Personal Letter: Connection-focused
- Insight Drop: Authority-building
- Curator: Resource value
- Story: Transformation narrative

### Step 2: Opener Engineering
Create must-open experience:
- Subject line promises value
- First line rewards open
- Personal immediately

### Step 3: Body Flow
Maintain reading momentum:
- Short paragraphs
- Strategic white space
- Conversational rhythm
- One call-out/highlight

### Step 4: Close Engineering
End memorably:
- Callback to opener
- Clear takeaway
- CTA (subtle or direct)
- Signature warmth

### Step 5: Conversion Integration
Embed without sleaze:
- Value always first
- CTA serves reader
- Natural mentions
- Optional PS for offers
