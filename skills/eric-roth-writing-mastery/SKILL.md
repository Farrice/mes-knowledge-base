# Eric Roth Writing Mastery

Deploy Oscar-level craft across **every** writing domain. This is the companion skill to `eric-roth-screenwriting-mastery` — same genius, new canvases.

> *"The adventure of creating something new, making something from nothing."* — Eric Roth

| # | Workflow | Domain | Produces | Use When |
