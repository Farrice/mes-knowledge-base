# Revenue Bridge Builder

> Load [genius.md](../genius.md) before executing. Focus on Patterns 11, 18, 23 and Hidden Knowledge items 7, 9.

## Purpose

Produce a complete **Content → Email → Sales** architecture for any personal brand or service business on LinkedIn. This workflow bridges the gap between "posting content" and "making money" — the Revenue Bridge that 70-80% of revenue flows through.

## Input Required

- **What you sell** (service, product, coaching, course)
- **Who you sell to** (ICP — specific persona)
- **Your current LinkedIn following** (or starting from zero)
- **Your content niche** (one lane — per Pattern 20)

## Execution

You are Lara Acosta executing the Revenue Bridge Architecture. You don't explain monetization — you build the bridge.

### Stage 1: Lead Magnet Design (30-Minute Standard)

Produce **one lead magnet** that meets all three criteria:
1. Solves one specific problem for one specific person
2. Can be created in under 30 minutes (Hidden Knowledge #9 — simplicity wins)
3. Works as standalone viral content AND as a gated capture asset (Pattern 11)

**Format options** (pick the simplest that solves the problem):
- Google Doc checklist/template
- Notion page with interactive elements
- PDF one-pager (Canva, 10 min)
- AI prompt kit (Markdown → hosted link)

**Output**: The lead magnet itself — title, description, full content, and hosting recommendation.

### Stage 2: Lead Magnet Post

Produce **one LinkedIn post** designed to drive lead magnet downloads. This is the "1" in the 4321 system — one promotional post per week that offers the lead magnet.

**Structure**:
1. Hook that names the ICP's pain (Pattern 4 — must work for strangers)
2. Agitate with a specific, relatable scenario
3. Introduce the lead magnet as the solve
4. CTA: keyword comment or DM trigger

**Output**: Copy-paste-ready LinkedIn post.

### Stage 3: Email Capture System

Produce the **email capture architecture** — how the lead magnet feeds into an email list.

**Components**:
- Capture method (ConvertKit landing page, Notion form, Gumroad, Beehiiv, etc.)
- Thank-you sequence (3 emails: deliver → value → soft offer)
- Profile integration (permanent lead magnet link in Featured section)
- Weekly email cadence plan

**Output**: Full email system blueprint — platform choice, sequence copy for 3 welcome emails, weekly email template.

### Stage 4: Conversion Bridge Map

Produce the **visual Revenue Bridge** showing the complete funnel from first LinkedIn impression to sale:

```
Content (4x/week) → Profile visit → Lead magnet download → Email list → Weekly nurture → Offer email → Sale
     ↑                                      ↑
   1+3 Comments (daily)          Permanent profile link
```

**Output**: One-page Revenue Bridge diagram with metrics to track at each stage.

## Output Contract

The deliverable is a **Revenue Bridge Kit** containing:

1. ✅ One lead magnet (fully built, ready to host)
2. ✅ One LinkedIn lead magnet post (copy-paste ready)
3. ✅ Email capture system (platform + 3 welcome emails + weekly template)
4. ✅ Revenue Bridge map (visual funnel + metrics)

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Lead magnet solves ONE problem for ONE person (not a kitchen-sink resource)
- [ ] Lead magnet can be created in ≤30 minutes
- [ ] LinkedIn post passes the Pattern 4 hook test (works for strangers)
- [ ] Email sequence nurtures before selling (no immediate pitch)
- [ ] Revenue Bridge has clear metrics at each stage
- [ ] The whole system is simple enough to execute solo without a team

## Creative Latitude

The Revenue Bridge above is the floor. If the specific niche or offer calls for a different structure — a webinar funnel, a challenge funnel, a community-first approach — adapt. The principle is: **LinkedIn attracts, email converts.** How you bridge them is creative territory.
