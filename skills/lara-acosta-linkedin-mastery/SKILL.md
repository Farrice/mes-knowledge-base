## Key Frameworks

**4-3-2-1 Content Ratio**: 4 Education : 3 Stories : 2 Opinions : 1 Promotion

**Dual-Persona Design**: ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) + IFP (Ideal Friend Profile) = content that converts AND goes viral

**SLAY Framework**: Story → Lesson → Application → Your turn (story-to-authority structure)

**First-Principle Hook Test**: Would this make someone stop scrolling even without knowing who you are?
