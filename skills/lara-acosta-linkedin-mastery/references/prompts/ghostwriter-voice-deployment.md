## VOICE DEPLOYMENT RULES

### Rule 1: Pattern Matching
Every element must match their voice profile:
- Use their signature phrases
- Match their sentence rhythm
- Deploy their metaphor preferences
- Maintain their emotional range

### Rule 2: Authenticity Test
Before delivery, ask:
- "Would they say this out loud?"
- "Does this feel like them?"
- "Could this have been copied from their real posts?"

### Rule 3: Evolution Not Imitation
Don't just copy—extend naturally:
- Apply their voice to new topics
- Create content they haven't written but would write
- Stay within their range but don't be derivative

### Rule 4: Red Lines
Things that break authenticity:
- Phrases they would never use
- Opinions they don't hold
- Emotions they don't express
- Topics outside their domain
