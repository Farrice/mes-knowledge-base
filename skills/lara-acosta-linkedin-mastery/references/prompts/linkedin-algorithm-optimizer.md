## ALGORITHM LEVERS

### Lever 1: Timing Optimization
When you post matters.
- Peak times vary by audience
- First hour is critical
- Consistency builds expectation

### Lever 2: Dwell Time
How long people stay on your post.
- Longer posts that hold attention get boosted
- Line breaks create scroll and pause
- Story structure maintains engagement

### Lever 3: Engagement Patterns
Comments > reactions > shares for algorithm.
- First 30-60 minutes engagement signals "good content"
- Comments with substance > emoji reactions
- Your responses to comments boost visibility

### Lever 4: Keyword Optimization
LinkedIn's relevance system.
- Industry keywords in content
- Hashtags (limited, targeted)
- Profile alignment with content

### Lever 5: Format Selection
Different formats get different distribution.
- Text posts: highest initial reach
- Carousels: high save rate
- Video: lower reach, higher retention
- Polls: engagement but less authority

### Lever 6: Native Behavior
Links kill reach.
- No external links in post body
- Links in comments instead
- Native content prioritized

### FIRST-HOUR PROTOCOL

**The Critical 60-Minute Window**

**Minutes 0-15**:
- Stay active on LinkedIn after posting
- Respond to EVERY comment within 10 minutes
- Your response = more visibility

**Minutes 15-30**:
- Go engage on 5-10 ICP posts (authentic comments)
- Your activity signals "active creator" to algorithm
- Don't just like—comment substantively

**Minutes 30-60**:
- Continue responding to any new comments
- Share in relevant groups (if applicable)
- DM 2-3 relevant connections with genuine note

**Pre-Post Priming** (Optional, advanced):
- 30 minutes before posting, engage on 5+ posts
- This "warms up" your presence for the algorithm
- Helps your post get shown to your recent engagement network

**Weekly Format Mix**:
| Format | Frequency | When to Use |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Text-only | 3x week | Opinion, story, frameworks |

- 1200-2000 characters sweetspot

- Use line breaks extensively (1 line per 2-3 sentences)

**Carousel Optimization**:
- 8-12 slides optimal
- First slide = scroll-stopping hook
- Last slide = CTA + summary
- Each slide = one idea

### ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

**Commenting Strategy**:
- Comment on 20-30 posts daily from ICP
- Comments should add value, not just agree
- Pattern: "Great point, AND here's what I'd add..."
- 50-100+ characters per comment

**Reply Strategy**:
- Reply to EVERY comment on your posts
- Ask follow-up questions (extends conversation)
- Thank compliments briefly, engage criticism thoughtfully

**Connection Strategy**:
- 10-15 new connection requests weekly
- Personalized notes for > 500 connection accounts
- Target: Same industry, similar audience

### THINGS TO AVOID

**Reach Killers**:
- ❌ External links in post body (put in comments)
- ❌ Editing within first hour (resets engagement momentum)
- ❌ Posting then going offline
- ❌ Deleting and reposting (looks suspicious)

**Engagement Killers**:
- ❌ Pods and engagement groups (algorithm detects)
- ❌ "Follow for more" CTAs (feels desperate)
- ❌ Buying followers (destroys engagement rate)
- ❌ Generic CTA questions ("Thoughts?")

**Content Killers**:
- ❌ Posts that are clearly AI-generated without voice
- ❌ Controversial for controversy's sake
- ❌ Multi-link posts
- ❌ Tag-baiting (tagging people hoping they share)
