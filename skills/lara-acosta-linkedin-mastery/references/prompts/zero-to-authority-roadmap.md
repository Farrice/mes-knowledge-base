## EXAMPLE OUTPUT

**Context**: Marketing consultant, 0 LinkedIn presence, wants speaking opportunities

**90-DAY AUTHORITY ROADMAP**

---

**VISION**: Unknown → Recognized positioning expert → Speaking opportunities

---

### PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-30)

**Goal**: Complete positioning, optimized presence, 30-post content bank

**Week 1: Positioning Sprint**
- Day 1-2: Complete positioning document (identity, elevator, proof stack)
- Day 3: Define 3 content pillars with 10 topics each
- Day 4-5: Optimize LinkedIn profile (headline, about, banner, featured)
- Day 6-7: Research and join 5 relevant communities/groups

**Week 2: Content Bank Build I**
- Day 8-10: Write 6 educational posts (2 per pillar)
- Day 11-12: Write 4 story posts (personal journey moments)
- Day 13-14: Write 2 opinion posts (industry hot takes)

**Week 3: Content Bank Build II**
- Day 15-17: Write 6 more educational posts
- Day 18-19: Write 4 more story posts (client/work stories)
- Day 20-21: Write 2 more opinion posts + 1 promotional

**Week 4: Launch Prep + First Posts**
- Day 22-23: Create lead magnet (positioning template or checklist)
- Day 24-25: Write 5 more posts (total = 30 in bank)
- Day 26-30: Begin posting daily (using first 5 from bank)

**Phase 1 Metrics**:
- ✓ Complete positioning document
- ✓ Optimized profile
- ✓ 30 posts in content bank
- ✓ 1 lead magnet created
- ✓ First 5 posts live

---

### PHASE 2: MOMENTUM (Days 31-60)

**Goal**: Daily posting rhythm, 500+ followers, first 50 connections, 100+ email list

**Week 5-6: Consistency Muscle**
- Post daily from content bank + create new posts
- 30 minutes daily engagement (comments on ICP content)
- Connect with 5 relevant people daily (personalized note)
- Reply to every comment on your posts

**Week 7-8: Lead Magnet Launch + Community**
- Launch lead magnet with promotional post
- Create 3 additional promotional posts for lead magnet
- Join 5 more communities, become active commenter
- Begin gathering testimonials from past work

**Phase 2 Metrics**:
- ✓ 30+ days consecutive posting
- ✓ 500+ followers (aim for 10-20/day growth)
- ✓ 50+ meaningful connections
- ✓ 100+ email subscribers
- ✓ 5+ testimonials collected

---

### PHASE 3: AUTHORITY (Days 61-90)

**Goal**: Thought leadership content, speaking outreach, first opportunity booked

**Week 9-10: Thought Leadership**
- Write 3 "industry hot take" pieces
- Create 2 prediction/trend posts
- Start a weekly content series
- Publish first long-form article (newsletter or LinkedIn article)

**Week 11-12: Visibility Sprint**
- Pitch 10 podcasts (DM hosts directly)
- Request 5 LinkedIn Lives with aligned creators
- Apply to 3 speaking opportunities (virtual summits)
- Create portfolio page or case study document

**Phase 3 Metrics**:
- ✓ 1000+ followers
- ✓ 300+ email subscribers
- ✓ 1+ podcast/speaking opportunity booked
- ✓ 3+ inbound opportunity conversations
- ✓ Clear offer ready to share

---

### STALL-POINT PROTOCOLS

**If engagement drops at Day 20-30**:
→ Content might be too broad. Narrow to one pillar for a week.
→ Check posting times. Test different slots.
→ Increase comment engagement to 45 min/day.

**If growth stalls at Day 45-50**:
→ Launch or relaunch lead magnet with fresh angle
→ Start collaborative content (tag aligned creators)
→ Increase opinion content ratio

**If no opportunities by Day 75**:
→ Direct outreach to 20 podcasts (volume)
→ Create "signature talk" teaser content
→ Offer free talk to relevant communities
