# Mechanism-First Ad Sprint

Rapid ad creation sprint that starts with a validated mechanism and produces ads for each awareness level using the CASH method. Designed for speed — a focused 45-minute sprint producing 15+ ad concepts.

---

## Skills Used

| Skill | How It's Used |
|-------|--------------|
| `million-dollar-mechanisms` | Mechanism as the strategic anchor |
| `copy-blocks` | CASH Method + Hook Forge for ad generation |
| `unaware-ads` | Awareness-level targeting |

---

## Inputs Required

1. **Validated Mechanism** — Name, type (UMP/UMS), one-sentence description
2. **Product/Offer** — What we're selling
3. **Audience** — Who we're reaching

---

## Sprint Structure (45 min)

### Block 1: Awareness-Level Mapping (10 min)

Map the mechanism through each awareness level:

| Level | What They Know | Mechanism's Role | Entry Angle |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Unaware** | Don't know they have the problem | Mechanism IS the wake-up call | "You have [mechanism] and don't know it" |
| **Problem-Aware** | Know the problem, not the cause | Mechanism explains WHY | "The hidden cause of [problem] is [mechanism]" |
| **Solution-Aware** | Know solutions exist, comparing | Mechanism differentiates | "Unlike other solutions, this targets [mechanism]" |
| **Product-Aware** | Know your product, on the fence | Mechanism removes doubt | "Here's exactly how [product] eliminates [mechanism]" |
| **Most-Aware** | Ready to buy, need a push | Mechanism creates urgency | "[Mechanism] is getting worse — here's why now matters" |

For each awareness level, generate 3 CASH combinations:

### Block 3: Hook Forge Burst (10 min)

For the top 5 CASH combinations, generate 3 hooks each:

| CASH Combo | Hook 1 (Curiosity) | Hook 2 (Contrarian) | Hook 3 (Story) |
|------------|-------------------|---------------------|----------------|
| [combo] | | | |

### Block 4: Ad Script Selection (10 min)

Write 3 complete ad scripts for the strongest combos:
1. **Best Unaware/Problem-Aware Ad** — mechanism introduction
2. **Best Solution-Aware Ad** — mechanism differentiation
3. **Best Product/Most-Aware Ad** — mechanism urgency

Each script: Hook → Body (copy blocks) → CTA with mechanism threaded throughout.

---

## Output

```
Mechanism-First Ad Sprint Results:
├── Awareness-Level Mechanism Map (5 levels × entry angle)
├── CASH Grid (15 combinations across 5 levels)
├── Hook Bank (15+ hooks from top combos)
├── Ad Scripts × 3 (one per awareness tier)
└── Testing Recommendation (which level/combo to test first)
```

---

## Quality Gate

- ☐ Mechanism clearly threaded through EVERY ad (remove mechanism → ad collapses)
- ☐ Each awareness level has distinct messaging (no copy-paste across levels)
- ☐ Scripts use varied copy blocks (not all starting with pain)
- ☐ Testing recommendation prioritizes highest-leverage awareness level
