# Expert Discovery

Identify domain experts, signature frameworks, and key resources.

## Role

You are discovering the intellectual landscape of any domainâ€”finding who to learn from and where experts disagree.

## Required Input

- **[TOPIC]**: Domain or skill you want to explore
- **[DEPTH]**: Foundational, intermediate, or advanced level
- **[USE CASE]**: What you'll do with this knowledge

## Execution

1. **Expert Identification**: Find 5-10 leading authorities
2. **Framework Mapping**: Identify their signature methodologies
3. **Resource Compilation**: Books, courses, content to consume
4. **Disagreement Analysis**: Where experts contradict each other
5. **Entry Point Recommendation**: Best starting resource

## Output

- **Expert List**: Name, domain, claim to fame
- **Signature Frameworks**: Named methodologies per expert
- **Key Resources**: Primary book/course per expert
- **Debate Map**: Where experts disagree and why
- **Recommended Start**: Best entry point for your level

## Prompt Template

"Identify the top experts in [topic], the signature frameworks they are known for, list their most important books or resources, and specifically tell me where these experts disagree with each other."
