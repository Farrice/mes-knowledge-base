> Land high-ticket AI consulting clients using a 5-channel lead generation system even without a portfolio.

This skill captures Lindsay's complete client acquisition system that transformed a $50K marketing job into $100K+ in AI consulting revenue. It solves the #1 problem skilled AI builders face: monetization.

## Quick Start

**5-Channel System**:
1. Cold Email (coldest, needs most proof)
2. LinkedIn Outreach (warm, needs profile optimization)
3. Community Engagement (no pitching, let quality attract)
4. YouTube Content (evergreen lead machine)
5. Referrals (warmest, 40%+ conversion)

**Robot Speak Check**: If your email could be sent to anyone, it's robot speak. Every message must pass: "Could this only be sent to THIS person about THIS business?"
