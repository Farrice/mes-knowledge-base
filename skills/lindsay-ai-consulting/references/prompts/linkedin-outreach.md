## The LinkedIn Framework

### PROFILE OPTIMIZATION
- Headline: Value you provide, not title
- About: Problem you solve, how you're different
- Featured: Proof and thought leadership
- Experience: Results, not responsibilities

### CONNECTION STRATEGY
- Warm connections (mutual connections, engagement)
- Personalized requests (always)
- No pitch in connection request
- 20-50 per week sustainably

### ENGAGEMENT BEFORE OUTREACH
- Comment on their content first
- React to their posts
- Share their content (with value-add)
- Build familiarity before reaching out

### CONVERSATION SEQUENCE
- Connection (no pitch)
- Thanks + genuine question
- Value contribution
- Discovery invitation

## Execution Protocol

1. **OPTIMIZE** profile for conversion
2. **BUILD** target list
3. **ENGAGE** before connecting
4. **CONNECT** with personalization
5. **NURTURE** with value
6. **CONVERT** through conversation
