## The Multi-Channel Framework

### CHANNEL ROLES
- Email: Primary outreach, detailed info
- LinkedIn: Relationship building, social proof
- Twitter/X: Visibility, thought proximity
- Referrals: Highest conversion, lowest volume
- Content: Passive attraction, credibility building

### COORDINATION TIMING
- Day 1: LinkedIn connection request
- Day 2: If accepted, LinkedIn message
- Day 4: Email (reference LinkedIn)
- Day 7: LinkedIn engage with their content
- Day 10: Email follow-up
- Day 14: LinkedIn voice note
- Day 21: Final email with value offer

### CONSISTENT MESSAGING
- Same core value proposition
- Channel-appropriate format
- Progressive disclosure
- Coherent narrative

## Execution Protocol

1. **MAP** channels to prospect segments
2. **DESIGN** coordinated sequences
3. **CREATE** channel-native content
4. **BUILD** orchestration system
5. **TRACK** cross-channel attribution
6. **OPTIMIZE** based on data
