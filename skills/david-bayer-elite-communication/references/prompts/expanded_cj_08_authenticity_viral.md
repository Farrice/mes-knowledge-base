## THE FIVE SHARING EMOTIONS

**Why People Share Content:**

| Emotion | Trigger | Language Pattern | Best For |
|---------|---------|------------------|----------|
| **Recognition** | "This is SO me" | "Anyone else...?" / "That moment when..." | Relatable struggles, daily experiences |
| **Aspiration** | "I want to be like this" | "[Achievement] + How I did it" | Success stories, transformations |
| **Outrage** | "Can you believe this?" | "Unpopular opinion:" / "Hot take:" | Industry criticism, contrarian views |
| **Relief** | "Finally someone said it" | "Nobody talks about..." / "The truth is..." | Taboo topics, unspoken realities |
| **Transformation** | "This changed how I think" | "I used to think... Now I know..." | Reframes, paradigm shifts |

**Emotion Selection Rule**: Choose ONE primary emotion. Content trying to trigger multiple emotions usually triggers none.
