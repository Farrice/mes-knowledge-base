## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### The "Hunting Trap" Diagnosis

Signs you're stuck in hunting mode:
- Every month starts at $0
- Most revenue from new clients
- Little repeat business
- Few referrals coming in
- Constant prospecting pressure

### Compound Mechanisms

**Mechanism 1: Retainer Conversion**
- Design project clients into retainer clients
- Monthly recurring > one-time projects
- Goal: 50%+ of revenue from recurring

**Mechanism 2: Expansion Revenue**
- Every client has more problems you can solve
- Build expansion into delivery (see Audit-to-Implementation Bridge)
- Goal: 30% of revenue from upsells

**Mechanism 3: Referral Flywheel**
- Systematize referral requests (see Referral Trigger System)
- Make one client generate 1-3 introductions
- Goal: 25% of new clients from referrals

**Mechanism 4: Reactivation**
- Past clients are warmer than cold prospects
- Regular touchpoint schedule
- "Here's what's new" quarterly outreach
- Goal: 15% of revenue from reactivated clients

### Compound Metrics

Track monthly:
- % of revenue from repeat clients
- % of clients who expand scope
- Referrals generated per client
- Past client reactivation rate

## EXAMPLE OUTPUT: AI Consulting Compound Architecture

**DIAGNOSIS**: 80% of revenue from hunting (new clients), 20% from repeat. No retainer offers. Referrals are happy accidents.

**COMPOUND DESIGN**:

**Mechanism 1: Retainer Conversion**
*Current*: Project-based only
*New*: Every 6-week implementation ends with retainer offer
*Target*: 40% of implementation clients convert to $3-5K/month retainers
*Revenue Impact*: $15K/month recurring by month 6

**Mechanism 2: Expansion Revenue**
*Current*: Occasional ad-hoc requests
*New*: Week 5 "Future Roadmap" session in every engagement
*Target*: 60% of clients approve additional scope
*Revenue Impact*: +$8K average per client

**Mechanism 3: Referral Flywheel**
*Current*: Occasional, unstructured
*New*: Ask at 3 specific moments, provide templates
*Target*: 1.5 referrals per client
*Revenue Impact*: 8 new clients from referrals annually

**Mechanism 4: Reactivation**
*Current*: None
*New*: Quarterly "What's new in AI" email to all past clients
*Target*: 2 past clients reactivate per quarter
*Revenue Impact*: $48K annually from reactivations

**12-MONTH COMPOUND ROADMAP**:

*Month 1-3*: 
- Launch retainer offer to all new implementation clients

- Start quarterly past-client outreach

*Month 4-6*:
- First retainers active (target: 3)

- Retainer base hitting $15K+/month
