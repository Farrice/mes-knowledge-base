# David Deutsch Genius Patterns

23 virtuoso patterns from the billion-dollar copywriter.

---

## Pattern 1: Boring-to-Brilliant Transformation
**Execute**: Transform dry material by finding hidden emotional drama.
**Success Metric**: Reader feels something within first 50 words.

## Pattern 2: Question-First Intelligence
**Execute**: Ask questions before offering solutions. Questions unlock everything.
**Success Metric**: Generate 10+ insights per conversation through questions.

## Pattern 3: Musical Copy Architecture
**Execute**: Structure copy with theme introduction, development, variation, resolution.
**Success Metric**: Reader experiences unconscious pleasure from structure.

## Pattern 4: Five-Pattern Taxonomy
**Execute**: Generate 5 options for every element (headlines, leads, proof).
**Success Metric**: 5 viable options for any copy element in 10 minutes.

## Pattern 5: Assumption Assassination
**Execute**: List 5 assumptions, test the opposite, find breakthroughs.
**Success Metric**: Identify 3+ testable assumptions before writing.

## Pattern 6: Screenwriter Translation
**Execute**: Show don't tell. Create visual scenes, not explanations.
**Success Metric**: Every key point has visual/sensory component.

## Pattern 7: Loss-Framing Reflex
**Execute**: Reframe gains as what they could lose by not acting.
**Success Metric**: 2.3x response rate improvement.

## Pattern 8: Personification Engine
**Execute**: Make abstracts into characters with agency and drama.
**Success Metric**: Technical concepts become engaging narratives.

## Pattern 9: Salesperson Mining
**Execute**: Interview salespeople for tested copy phrases.
**Success Metric**: Extract 10+ tested phrases before writing.

## Pattern 10: Hidden Weakness Frame
**Execute**: Find one conceptual frame unifying all benefits.
**Success Metric**: Any offer unified under one compelling concept.

## Pattern 11: Sentence Rhythm Mathematics
**Execute**: Vary sentence length 5-25 words, punch vs. flow.
**Success Metric**: No 3 consecutive sentences within 5 words of each other.

## Pattern 12: Cross-Industry Translation
**Execute**: Take patterns from one industry, apply where "unheard of."
**Success Metric**: 2+ cross-industry techniques per project.

## Pattern 13: The "So What?" Ladder
**Execute**: Keep asking "so what?" until you hit emotion.
**Success Metric**: Every feature traced to emotional consequence.

## Pattern 14: Tragedy/Triumph Frame
**Execute**: Every topic prevents tragedy or enables triumph. Find which.
**Success Metric**: Clear emotional stakes in every piece.

## Pattern 15: Friend Conversation Test
**Execute**: Would you say this to a friend? If not, rewrite.
**Success Metric**: Zero marketing-speak detectable.

## Pattern 16: Movie Moment Test
**Execute**: Would this work on screen? Create scenes audiences gasp at.
**Success Metric**: Key points pass movie moment test.

## Pattern 17: Editing Over Writing
**Execute**: Write fast, edit obsessively. "I'm a hell of an editor."
**Success Metric**: More time editing than writing.

## Pattern 18: Non-Threatening Questions
**Execute**: Never tell clients they're wrong—ask questions that lead them.
**Success Metric**: Zero confrontation, maximum influence.

## Pattern 19: AI as Infinite Intern
**Execute**: Use AI accordingly—verify everything, push back constantly.
**Success Metric**: AI output always verified and enhanced.

## Pattern 20: Copy Coach Voice Installation
**Execute**: Install mentor's critical voice in your head permanently.
**Success Metric**: Hear editing voice automatically.

## Pattern 21: The Cleaning Person Test
**Execute**: Show copy to non-experts often closer to market.
**Success Metric**: Non-experts understand and engage.

## Pattern 22: Lottery Psychology
**Execute**: People buy the dream experience, not the probability.
**Success Metric**: Emotional experience over logical claims.

## Pattern 23: Prove Critics Wrong Motivation
**Execute**: People care more about proving doubters wrong than money.
**Success Metric**: Tap into revenge/validation motivation.
