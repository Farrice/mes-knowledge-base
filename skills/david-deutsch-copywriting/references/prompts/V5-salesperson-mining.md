## Input Required

## Execution Protocol

1. **INTERVIEW** 3-5 salespeople
2. **ASK** "What do you say that works?"
3. **ASK** "What objections do you hear most?"
4. **DOCUMENT** proven phrases and responses
5. **EXTRACT** 10+ tested phrases
