## Confidence Sources

### PREPARATION CONFIDENCE
- Know your material deeply
- Anticipate questions
- Practice out loud
- "I've prepared for this"

### EXPERTISE CONFIDENCE
- Own what you know
- Acknowledge what you don't
- Be the curious expert
- "I know this deeply"

### VALUE CONFIDENCE
- Focus on helping
- Detach from outcome
- Serve, don't sell
- "I'm here to help"

### GROWTH CONFIDENCE
- Accept learning curve
- Celebrate small wins
- Track progress
- "I'm getting better"

## Execution Protocol

1. **IDENTIFY** confidence blocks
2. **BUILD** through each source
3. **CREATE** confidence rituals
4. **PRACTICE** until natural
5. **CELEBRATE** wins
6. **ITERATE** continuously
