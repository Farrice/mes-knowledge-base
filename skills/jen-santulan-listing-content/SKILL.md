# Jen Santulan Listing Content Engine

**Domain:** Real estate social media content — Instagram Reels scripts, hooks, captions
**Platform:** Instagram-first (optimized for saves/shares, not just watch time)
**Voice:** Jen Santulan — LA real estate agent, San Fernando Valley specialist

## What This Skill Does

Takes any property address and generates a complete content package:
- 6 scroll-stopping hook packages (8-12 sec spoken, tight, no drag)
- 3 buyer-targeted ad angles for the specific property
- Caption bank (short, story, long-form)
- Shareability assets (proxy gift CTAs, screenshot stats, opinion splits, save hooks)

## What Makes It Different

This prompt was audited and rebuilt by 5 domain experts:
- **Seena Rez** — Hook mechanics, multi-sensory scroll-stop architecture
- **Harry Dry** — Copy evaluation, anti-AI-slop rules, concrete voice modeling
- **Jason Fladlien** — Buyer psychology, identity architecture, status positioning
- **Ward Farnsworth** — Rhetorical devices for spoken delivery, Saxon word dominance
- **Kallaway** — Engagement psychology, dopamine ladder, shareability engineering

## Key Differentiators vs. Generic Real Estate Prompts

1. **Buyer Identity Architecture** — Every hook dissolves a specific limiting belief, not just describes features
2. **Saxon Word Dominance** — Explicit blacklist of AI-sounding Latinate words + Saxon word palette for hooks/closers
3. **Rhetorical Device Menu** — Mandated chiasmus, antithesis, anaphora, or "two languages" in every opening line
4. **Signature Vocab Usage Rules** — Words like "unicorn" and "real talk" have deployment conditions, not just a word list
5. **Shareability Engineering** — Built-in proxy gift CTAs, screenshot frames, opinion splits, save triggers
6. **Quality Gate** — 7-point check including ear test, drag test, and blacklist scan
