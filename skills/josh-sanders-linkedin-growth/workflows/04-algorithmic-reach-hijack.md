## Workflow

### Phase 1: The 30/30/30/10 Portfolio Allocation
Map the target landscape to ensure every minute of engagement is mathematically optimized for reach and conversion.
- **30% Big Creators (Reach)**: Identify 5-10 "Platform Giants" (50k+ followers) who share your broad audience. Objective: "Piggyback" their impressions.
- **30% Industry Peers (Authority)**: Identify 10-15 direct competitors or complementary service providers. Objective: Use "Contrarian Depth" to look like the smartest person in the room.
- **30% ICPs/Potential Clients (Leads)**: Identify 20-30 active "Target Accounts" who post or comment. Objective: Micro-consulting and trust-building.
- **10% Friends/Supporters (Morale)**: Your "Inner Circle." Objective: Signal to the algorithm that your account is "human" and has a loyal base.

### Phase 2: The Gravedigger & Ugly Number Matrix
Before writing, engineer the "Internal Voice" triggers that bypass the reader's marketing filters.
- **Ugly Number Extraction**: Convert generic claims into specific, non-rounded data points (e.g., Change "30% growth" to "31.4% margin expansion").
- **Gravedigger Scripting**: Translate surface-level pains into visceral fears. (e.g., "I want more time" becomes "The quiet terror of your kids calling the nanny 'Mom' because you're stuck in the warehouse until 11 PM.")

### Phase 3: Sniper Comment Engineering
Apply the "Format Arbitrage" and "Line-Paragraph-Line" physics to create comments that outperform the original post.
1. **The Reach Sniper (Top Comment)**: Focus on being the "Top Comment" through early-arrival speed and broad resonance. Use a "Challenger" hook.
2. **The Authority Sniper (Peer Signal)**: Focus on technical depth. Use "Contrarian Depth" to challenge standard practices with a 10x more detailed framework.
3. **The Conversion Sniper (ICP Magnet)**: Focus on solving a specific pain point mentioned in the comments. Offer a "Low-Friction Resource" to trigger de-platforming.

### Phase 4: The 45-Minute Daily Execution Engine
The exact timed routine to trigger the "Velocity" signal and maximize profile-as-landing-page conversion.
- **00:00 - 00:10 (The Radar)**: Scan notifications and saved searches for the 30/30/30/10 list.
- **00:10 - 00:25 (The Warmup)**: Drop 5 "Authority" comments on Peer/Big Creator posts. Focus on **Information Density**.
- **00:25 - 00:30 (The Drop)**: Publish your own "Pre-Validated Outlier" post (referencing your Creator Inspiration Bank).
- **00:30 - 00:45 (The Blitz)**: Respond to all comments on your post + engage with 5 "ICP" target accounts using the **Internal Voice Test**.

### Phase 5: The De-platforming Trigger
When an ICP replies to your comment with high interest, execute the "Value-First" transition:
- **The Script**: "I actually have a [Specific Resource Name] on exactly how we solved that [Ugly Number] dip. Want me to DM it over?"
- **The Goal**: Move the conversation from the public feed to the DM funnel without a "hard sell."
