## 1. The "How I" Narrative Pivot
**What They Do**: Anchoring a take to a specific anecdote.

**Executable Behavior**: Replace "how to" content formats with "how I" formats. Pair every single opinion or framework with a specific narrative example.

**Deploy When**: Drafting MoF and ToF content.

**Success Metric**: High engagement on posts; comments referencing the specific story told.

## 2. The Stealth Hook
**What They Do**: Camouflaging proven psychological hooks in conversational text.

**Executable Behavior**: Write the optimal formulaic hook. Then, rewrite it to sound like a casual slack message to a peer while retaining the underlying psychological levers.

**Deploy When**: The first 1-3 lines of any post.

**Success Metric**: Increased read-more clicks; absence of marketing fatigue responses.

## 3. Audience Design Architecture
**What They Do**: Forcibly injecting targeted users into the content flywheel.

**Executable Behavior**: Send exactly 20 targeted connection requests every weekday to a specific ICP. Leave exactly 5 high-quality comments daily on posts from overlapping ICP hubs.

**Deploy When**: Daily growth routine, establishing an account.

**Success Metric**: High-quality inbound profile views from exact target roles.

## 4. The Content Interview System
**What They Do**: Separating idea extraction from drafting.

**Executable Behavior**: Schedule a bi-weekly 45-minute recording session using pre-planned prompts, transcribe, and use as the sole source material for two weeks of drafting.

**Deploy When**: Scaling founder-led content.

**Success Metric**: 5 weekly posts generated from a 2-hour weekly time commitment.
