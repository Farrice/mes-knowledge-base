## 1. The AI Saturation Floor
**Tacit Insight**: "Valuable content" is no longer a differentiator; it is the baseline. The only moat is personal narrative and idiosyncratic voice.

**Why Others Miss This**: People are trying to out-prompt AI rather than recognizing that AI cannot invent legitimate personal history.

**Deploy When**: During content ideation to vet if a topic is too generic.

## 2. The "Wonky Algorithm" Bypass
**Tacit Insight**: Relying solely on organic distribution is a failure point. Manually connecting with the target audience forces the algorithm to serve content to those individuals.

**Why Others Miss This**: Founders often mistake LinkedIn for a purely public feed rather than a targeted ABM (Account-Based Marketing) distribution channel.

**Deploy When**: Determining the ROI equation of audience engagement vs. content creation.
