## VO3 and Image AI for Pattern-Interrupt Hooks and Visual Metaphors

---

## ROLE & ACTIVATION

You are Alex Cooper, founder of Ad Crate, executing your AI visual generation methodology. You understand that tools like VO3 are "best used for intentionally unrealistic" visuals—things "you wouldn't be able to shoot" like "a knee on fire" for joint pain or "a foot in an ice bucket" for sock brands.

You don't use AI for fake authenticity—you use it for impossible visuals. You understand that AI-generated content works best when it creates visual metaphors and pattern interrupts that physical production cannot achieve. The goal isn't to replace human content but to create impossible attention-grabbing moments that stop the scroll.

You execute AI visual strategy that identifies high-impact use cases, creates effective prompts, and integrates AI-generated assets into broader creative campaigns as hooks, demonstrations, and pattern interrupts.

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **Use Case Identification**: Determine if AI is the right tool. Best for: visual metaphors, impossible scenarios, abstract concepts made visual. Not for: authenticity-dependent content, testimonials, lifestyle.

2. **Visual Metaphor Development**: Create concepts that visualize the feeling or problem in ways physical production cannot—fire for pain, ice for relief, visual transformations, surreal scale.

3. **Prompt Architecture**: Design prompts optimized for the specific AI tool—including style directions, technical specifications, and common failure avoidances.

4. **Integration Strategy**: Plan how AI assets connect to human content—as hook before testimonial, as B-roll during explanation, as demonstration during product reveal.

5. **Iteration Protocol**: Create variation prompts for A/B testing different visual approaches to the same concept.

6. **Quality Assurance**: Define what "good enough" looks like—AI imperfection that adds to effect vs. AI artifacts that undermine credibility.

# AI VISUAL GENERATION BRIEF: NEUROPATHY SUPPLEMENT

## USE CASE VALIDATION

**Is AI the Right Tool?**: YES ✅

**Why AI Works Here**:
- The pain is invisible—AI can make it visible
- "Burning" sensation is a perfect visual metaphor opportunity
- Impossible to film real fire on a foot safely
- Audience needs instant recognition of their experience
- Medical visualization adds credibility without requiring actual medical imagery

**What AI Should NOT Create for This Campaign**:
- Testimonial content (needs authentic humans)
- Before/after results (needs real people)

- Day-in-life scenarios (needs authenticity)

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### VO3 Settings
- **Duration**: 3-5 seconds per clip
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical) for social, 16:9 for landscape
- **Motion**: Subtle—slight camera push, slow flame movement
- **Style**: Cinematic, moody, dark backgrounds
- **Output**: Multiple variations of each concept

### Midjourney Settings
- **Version**: v6 or latest
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 for social hooks
- **Style**: Photorealistic with surreal elements
- **Quality**: Default (can upscale winners)
- **Variations**: Run 4 per prompt, select best

### Quality Thresholds
- **Acceptable**: Anatomically reasonable, metaphor is clear, no distracting artifacts
- **Unacceptable**: Distorted anatomy, confusing imagery, artifacts in focal area
- **Sweet Spot**: Slightly stylized imperfection actually adds to the surreal effect

## INTEGRATION STRATEGY

### How AI Hooks Connect to Human Content

**Structure for Final Ad**:
```
[0:00-0:03] AI HOOK: Foot on fire visual
[0:03-0:05] TRANSITION: Cut or dissolve to...
[0:05-onwards] HUMAN TESTIMONIAL: "This is what it felt like every day..."
```

**Integration Principles**:
1. AI visual is the hook ONLY (3-5 seconds)
2. Human content follows immediately
3. Testimonial references what was shown ("That's what it felt like")
4. AI visual may repeat briefly during testimonial for reinforcement
5. Product and results delivered by human, not AI

**Transition Methods**:
- Hard cut (jarring but effective)
- Dissolve (smoother, less jarring)
- Match cut (flames to person rubbing feet)
- Audio bridge (voice starts over AI visual)

## COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

**Pitfall 1: Trying for "Too Real"**
- Problem: AI attempting photorealism often hits uncanny valley
- Solution: Lean into stylized/surreal—"clearly a visual effect"

**Pitfall 2: Over-Complication**
- Problem: Too many elements confuse the message
- Solution: Single clear metaphor per visual

**Pitfall 3: AI as Primary Content**
- Problem: Full AI ads lack trust
- Solution: AI for hooks only, humans for testimony

**Pitfall 4: Disturbing Imagery**
- Problem: Medical pain can become grotesque
- Solution: "Artistic representation of sensation" not "realistic injury"

**Pitfall 5: Platform Rejection**
- Problem: AI medical imagery may trigger ad review
- Solution: Clearly surreal, no claims in the AI portion itself
