## HIRING CRITERIA AND SCORECARD

### What to Hire For

**Prioritize (in order)**:
1. **Curiosity Level**: Are they already consuming industry content? Do they follow people on Twitter/LinkedIn? Do they have opinions about creative strategy?
2. **Raw Copywriting Ability**: Can they write compellingly? This is hardest to train.
3. **Pattern Recognition**: Can they identify what makes something work?
4. **Self-Direction**: Will they learn without being told to?

**Deprioritize**:
- Years of experience (can be trained)
- Platform certifications (easily acquired)
- Portfolio polish (can develop)
- Education credentials (rarely relevant)

### Screening Criteria

**Resume/Application Screen**:
- Evidence of industry engagement (following, learning, creating)
- Any writing samples (even non-professional)
- Signs of curiosity (side projects, self-teaching)
- NOT: perfect resume, big agency names, marketing degrees

**Application Questions**:
1. "What's the best ad you've seen in the last month and why does it work?"
2. "If you were hiring for your role, what would you look for?"
3. "What are you learning right now that you're excited about?"
4. "Send us a 30-second video explaining why a product you use is great."

## INTERVIEW PROTOCOL

### Stage 1: Screening Call (20 min)

**Purpose**: Validate curiosity and basic fit

**Questions**:
1. "Walk me through how you stay up to date on what's working in paid creative."
   - Looking for: Specific sources, people, habits
   - Red flag: Vague or generic answers

2. "Tell me about an ad you've seen recently that you think is brilliant."
   - Looking for: Specific example, articulate why, understanding of mechanics
   - Red flag: Can't think of one, only describes what it IS not why it WORKS

3. "What's something you taught yourself recently?"
   - Looking for: Self-direction, curiosity, depth
   - Red flag: Nothing comes to mind, all formal training

**Advance if**: Clear industry engagement, can articulate why things work

---

### Stage 2: Take-Home Assignment (2-3 hours)

**Assignment**:
"Here's a brand and their current ads. Create a research brief and 3 new creative concepts."

**Provide**:
- Brand overview
- 5-10 current ads
- Target audience info
- Performance context (optional)

**Evaluate**:
- Research depth and methodology
- Insight quality
- Concept creativity
- Copywriting in concepts
- Strategic thinking

**Scoring**:
| Element | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Research quality | 30% | |
| Concept strength | 30% | |
| Copywriting | 25% | |
| Strategic thinking | 15% | |

**Advance if**: Total score 3.5+, no element below 3

---

### Stage 3: Working Session (45 min)

**Purpose**: See how they think in real-time

**Structure**:
- 10 min: Walk through their take-home, answer questions
- 20 min: Live problem-solving on a new brief
- 15 min: Questions from them

**Live Problem Format**:
"Here's a brand we're working with. They're struggling with [specific challenge]. Walk me through how you'd approach this."

**Evaluate**:
- Thinking process (not just answer)
- Questions they ask
- Comfort with ambiguity
- Collaborative energy

## INCENTIVE STRUCTURE

### "Access, Educate, Incentivize" Framework

**Access**:
- Full visibility into ad account data
- Access to performance metrics
- Ability to see what's winning (and losing)
- Foreplay/motion subscriptions

**Educate**:
- Weekly training time (2 hours minimum)
- External resources budget
- Peer teaching sessions
- CD brainstorms

**Incentivize**:
- Base salary (market rate)
- Performance bonuses tied to outcomes
- Clear progression path
- Recognition system

---

### Performance Bonus Structure

**For Strategists**:

| Achievement | Bonus |
|-------------|-------|
| Ad reaches $10K spend | $100 |
| Ad reaches $25K spend | $250 |
| Ad reaches top 3 in account | $200 |
| Account CAC improves 20%+ | $500 |
| Quarterly hit rate above 15% | $750 |

**For Editors**:

| Achievement | Bonus |
|-------------|-------|
| Video reaches $10K spend | $50 |
| Video reaches $25K spend | $100 |
| Top performer of the week | $75 |

**Implementation Notes**:
- Bonuses paid monthly
- Clear tracking system (who gets credit for what)
- Team celebrations for big wins
- Transparency on who's earning what

---

### Why This Works

"If you say for every ad that spends this arbitrary amount, I'll give you an extra $250, $500‚Äîsuddenly I'm really incentivized to go and do all these things."

The incentive creates alignment:
- Good for team member (earn more)
- Good for agency (better performance)
- Good for clients (better results)
