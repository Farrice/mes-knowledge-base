# Implementation Pathways

## 24-Hour Quickstart (The Research Sprint)

**Hour 0-2: Deep Dive**
- Select one product.
- Find 3 Reddit threads (go 5+ pages deep).
- Mine 20 negative reviews on competitor products ("What did they hate?").
- Document 5 untapped messaging angles.

**Hour 2-6: Asset Audit**
- Collect 30 top-performing ads in your category (Ad Library / Creative Center).
- Categorize them: [Format] + [Hook Type].
- Identify 3 formats you aren't running but should be.

**Hour 6-12: The First Briefs**
- Match your best new Angle to your best new Format.
- Use `creative-brief-engineering` to write a production-ready brief.
- Create a "Context Document" for this product to feed AI later.

**Hour 12-24: AI Setup**
- Build your first Custom GPT: "Brand Voice & Research Analyst."
- Feed it your context document.
- Generate 10 hook variations for your new brief.

---

## 7-Day Sprint (The System Build)

**Days 1-2: Research Foundation**
- Complete full Research Depth Protocol.
- Build library of 100+ reference ads.
- Catalog 20 TikTok Shop creators to study.

**Days 3-4: Production Pipeline**
- Ideas -> Briefs. Produce 5 complete briefs.
- Source talent/creators (using Authority Calibration).
- Set up the "Winner Iteration" workflow.

**Days 5-6: AI Workflow Integration**
- Build 3 specialized GPTs (Script Writer, Forensics, Brief Builder).
- Run your historical data through `performance-forensics` to find patterns.
- Train team (or yourself) on the AI-Human handoff.

**Day 7: Launch & Learn**
- Launch the new creative batch.
- Set up the "Forensics Protocols" for when data comes in.

---

## 30-Day Transformation (Mastery)

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Complete research on all products.
- Systematize the reference library.
- Daily practice: Visual Telegraphing analysis.

**Week 2: Velocity**
- Shift to AI-amplified workflow.
- Target: 5 high-quality briefs per day capability.
- Begin "Winner Stacking" of any early signals.

**Week 3: Contextual Mastery**
- Full custom GPT ecosystem operational.
- Team incentives aligned (if applicable).
- Quality control became "filtering" rather than "editing."

**Week 4: Virtuoso**
- Account-specific playbook finalized.
- Trend-jacking system live (organic -> paid <48 hrs).
- Authority framework deployed across all funnels.
