## Core Principles

1. **Warm Lead Philosophy**: Only pitch to people with stated buying intent (RFPs, job posts, budget announcements)
2. **Flip the Process**: Build solution first → find businesses that need it → show finished product
3. **Tool > Opinion**: Build tools people USE, not content they READ
4. **Recurring Revenue Lock-In**: Low entry ($200) + monthly subscription ($29-99/mo)
5. **Show Don't Pitch**: Lead with live demonstration in first 90 seconds
