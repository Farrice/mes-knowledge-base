# Darrel Wilson - Genius Patterns

## Pattern 1: The Warm Lead Philosophy
**Execute**: Before any outreach, verify prospect has demonstrated active buying behavior—job posts, RFPs, budget announcements, or hiring signals. Never pitch to cold audiences.

**Success Metric**: 80%+ of outreach goes to verified warm prospects with stated budgets.

---

## Pattern 2: The Flip the Process Method
**Execute**: Pre-build 3-5 niche-specific templates/solutions before any prospecting. Use these as "visual pitches" rather than proposals.

**Success Metric**: Closing rate increases 3-5x when showing finished products vs. pitching concepts.

---

## Pattern 3: Tool Over Opinion Positioning
**Execute**: For any affiliate niche, identify the ACTION users want to take and build a tool that facilitates that action—with affiliate links embedded in the natural flow.

**Success Metric**: Tool provides genuine utility independent of affiliate component.

---

## Pattern 4: The Recurring Revenue Lock-In
**Execute**: Design all service offerings with low-friction entry point ($200) + ongoing monthly component (hosting, maintenance, updates).

**Success Metric**: 70%+ of clients on recurring plans within 90 days.

---

## Pattern 5: The Ugly Website Arbitrage
**Execute**: Use structured evaluation criteria (mobile responsiveness, load speed, design age) to identify prospects via Google Maps, then create personalized mockups with their existing branding.

**Success Metric**: 50+ qualified prospects identified per hour of scanning.

---

## Pattern 6: The Proof-First Demonstration
**Execute**: Lead every sales conversation with a live demonstration of the finished product in action. "Let me show you" precedes "here's how."

**Success Metric**: Prospects see working proof within first 90 seconds.

---

## Pattern 7: The Niche Template Arsenal
**Execute**: Build one high-quality template for each of 5-10 high-volume niches (dentists, gyms, restaurants, lawyers). Customize only branding layer for clients.

**Success Metric**: Client delivery time under 48 hours.

---

## Pattern 8: The AI Efficiency Multiplier
**Execute**: For every manual task in a workflow, ask: "Can AI handle this repetitive element?" Focus human attention on strategy and relationships.

**Success Metric**: 80%+ of repetitive tasks automated.

---

## Pattern 9: The RFP Goldmine Recognition
**Execute**: Build automated scraping for RFP boards, government contract sites, and institutional procurement pages. Filter by service category and budget range.

**Success Metric**: 10+ qualified RFP opportunities surfaced weekly.

---

## Pattern 10: The Value Ladder Pricing
**Execute**: Structure offerings at 3-4 price points: free/low entry (builds audience), mid-tier (core revenue), high-ticket (profit maximizer).

**Success Metric**: Revenue distributed across all tiers, 60%+ from mid-tier and above.
