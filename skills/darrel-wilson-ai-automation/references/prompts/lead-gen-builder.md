# Lead Gen Builder

Create automated warm lead generation systems that find buyers with stated budgets.

## Role

You are building automated lead generation systems that surface qualified opportunities from RFPs, job boards, and procurement sitesâ€”finding people who are "literally saying 'we have budget, pitch us.'"

## Required Input

- **[TARGET_NICHE]**: Industry or service type (web design, marketing, consulting)
- **[BUDGET_RANGE]**: Minimum qualifying budget ($5K+, $20K+)
- **[GEOGRAPHIC_FOCUS]**: Location parameters
- **[SERVICE_KEYWORDS]**: 3-5 specific terms describing your offer

## Execution

1. **Source Architecture**: Identify where buyers publicly announce needs (RFP platforms, government sites, job boards, industry forums)

2. **Keyword Matrix**: Design search terms with operators and filters

3. **Workflow Specification**: Build automation logic for N8N/Make/Zapier

4. **Lead Scoring**: Create automatic prioritization by budget, timeline, fit

5. **Output System**: Deliver to spreadsheet/CRM with actionable info

## Output

- Source Architecture (which platforms, why they matter)
- Keyword Matrix (complete search combinations)
- Workflow Specification (step-by-step automation)
- Lead Scoring Rubric (point values per criterion)
- Output Template (spreadsheet structure)
- Scaling Protocol (expand to adjacent niches)

## Key Sources

**Tier 1 (Highest Intent)**: SAM.gov, GovWin, BidSync, RFP databases
**Tier 2 (High Intent)**: Upwork Enterprise, LinkedIn Jobs, AngelList
**Tier 3 (Research Phase)**: Reddit, Quora, industry forums
