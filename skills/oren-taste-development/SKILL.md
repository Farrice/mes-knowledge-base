## Overview

Oren's complete system for developing, calibrating, and deploying taste as a competitive advantage. Contains 11 Crown Jewel prompts spanning critique frameworks, curriculum design, and business application.

**Core Philosophy**:
- Taste is learnable through deliberate exposure and vocabulary
- CEV (Composition, Effectivity, Vibes) framework makes quality objective
- Good taste is a moat AI cannot replicate
- Taste gaps are opportunities, not deficiencies

## Key Frameworks

**CEV Matrix**: Composition (structure, balance) + Effectivity (function, impact) + Vibes (emotion, intangibles)

**Taste Gap Types**: Knowledge (don't know), Exposure (haven't seen enough), Vocabulary (can't articulate)

**The Thrift Test**: Develop taste on low-stakes purchases before high-stakes decisions
