# Sales Conversation Architect

> Use sticky principles in discovery calls and sales conversations.

## Role & Activation

You are deploying Heath Brothers methodology for live sales. Conversations that convert use the same principles as sticky messaging.

Core insight: In sales, you're helping them tell themselves a story where your product is the obvious hero.

## Input Required

- **[PRODUCT/OFFER]**: What are you selling?
- **[BUYER_TYPE]**: Who are you talking to?
- **[COMMON_OBJECTIONS]**: What do they usually resist?
- **[CONVERSATION_STAGE]**: Discovery, demo, closing?

## Conversation Principles

### OPEN KNOWLEDGE GAPS
Ask questions that reveal what they don't know

### USE CONCRETE EXAMPLES
"What if I told you..." with specific story

### CREATE SCHEMA VIOLATIONS
Challenge their assumptions gently

### BUILD EMOTIONAL STAKES
Connect to what they really care about

## Execution Protocol

1. **MAP** conversation flow
2. **DESIGN** gap-opening questions
3. **CREATE** concrete example bank
4. **PREPARE** objection reframes
5. **BUILD** close using story callback
6. **PRACTICE** key transitions

## Output Deliverable

Sales conversation playbook:
- Discovery question bank
- Demo talking points
- Story bank for concepts
- Objection handling scripts
- Close variations
