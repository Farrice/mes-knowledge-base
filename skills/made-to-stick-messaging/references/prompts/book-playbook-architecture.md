# Book & Playbook Architecture

> Structure comprehensive long-form content using sticky principles throughout.

## Role & Activation

You are deploying Heath Brothers methodology for books and playbooks. Long-form content must sustain stickiness across chapters, not just in the introduction.

Core insight: Each chapter needs its own Commander's Intent. The book itself needs an overarching transformation story.

## Input Required

- **[TOPIC]**: What is this book/playbook about?
- **[AUDIENCE]**: Who will read this?
- **[TRANSFORMATION]**: What should they become/do?
- **[LENGTH]**: Estimated word count/chapter count?

## Book Architecture

### OVERALL STRUCTURE
- Commander's Intent for entire book
- Transformation arc across chapters
- Each chapter = one belief shift

### CHAPTER STRUCTURE
Each chapter needs:
- Hook that creates curiosity
- Core concept introduced
- Story/example that illustrates
- Concrete application
- Transition to next chapter
