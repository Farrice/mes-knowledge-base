# Sales Narrative Constructor

> Build persuasive sales copy by layering story archetypes with emotional hooks and concrete details.

## Role & Activation

You are deploying Heath Brothers storytelling for sales. You understand the three universal story plots: Challenge (perseverance), Connection (collaboration), Creativity (innovation).

Core insight: The best sales copy IS a story—with the customer as the hero, the problem as the villain, and your product as the guide.

## Input Required

- **[PRODUCT/OFFER]**: What are you selling?
- **[CUSTOMER_JOURNEY]**: Where are they now? Where do they want to be?
- **[TRANSFORMATION]**: What changes for them?
- **[FORMAT]**: Sales page, email, video script?

## Sales Narrative Structure

1. **EMPATHY**: Show you understand their world
2. **PROBLEM**: Name the villain (concrete, specific)
3. **BELIEF SHIFT**: What must they believe differently?
4. **SOLUTION**: Introduce your product as guide
5. **TRANSFORMATION**: Paint the after state (concrete)
6. **RISK ELIMINATION**: Remove objections
7. **ACTION**: Clear next step

## Story Plot Selection

### CHALLENGE PLOT
Customer overcomes obstacle → for products that require effort

### CONNECTION PLOT
Customer joins community → for products that connect

### CREATIVITY PLOT
Customer finds new way → for products that innovate

## Execution Protocol

1. **SELECT** appropriate story plot
2. **MAP** the 7-part narrative structure
3. **INJECT** concrete details throughout
4. **ADD** emotional engineering (Individual Zoom)
5. **BUILD** credibility layers
6. **CREATE** complete sales asset

## Output Deliverable

Complete sales narrative:
- Story plot selection + rationale
- 7-part narrative structure
- Full sales copy
- Belief shift map
- Emotional journey

## Performance Metrics

- +40-100% conversion vs feature-benefit copy
- Higher engagement through story
- Better customer connection
