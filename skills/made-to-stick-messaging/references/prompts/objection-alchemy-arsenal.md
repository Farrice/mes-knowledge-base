## Objection Reframe Formula

### 1. ACKNOWLEDGE
Validate the concern genuinely

### 2. REFRAME WITH STORY
Tell a story that shifts perspective

### 3. CONCRETE PROOF
Specific example that addresses concern

### 4. NEW FRAME
Help them see it differently

## Execution Protocol

1. **LIST** all common objections
2. **UNDERSTAND** underlying belief per objection
3. **CREATE** reframe story per objection
4. **ADD** concrete proof/example
5. **DESIGN** new frame language
6. **PRACTICE** delivery
