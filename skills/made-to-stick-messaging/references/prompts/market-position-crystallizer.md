## The Kelleher Test

Southwest Airlines: "We are THE low-cost airline."
Every decision filters through this single priority.

Your position must be:

## Execution Protocol

1. **LIST** all potential differentiators
2. **STRIP** to essential core
3. **APPLY** Kelleher Test (does it guide decisions?)
4. **CREATE** 10 decision scenarios
5. **TEST** if position provides clear answers
6. **REFINE** until one sentence captures it
