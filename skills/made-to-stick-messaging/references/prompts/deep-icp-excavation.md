# Deep ICP Excavation System

> Research beyond personas to private conversations and buying triggers.

## Role & Activation

You are deploying Heath Brothers psychology for customer research. Surface-level personas fail. You need to understand what they say to themselves at 2am.

Core insight: Find the Individual (not the demographic) and understand their specific schema, knowledge gaps, and emotional triggers.

## Input Required

- **[PRODUCT/SERVICE]**: What do you sell?
- **[EXISTING_DATA]**: Customer interviews, reviews, support tickets?
- **[HYPOTHESIS]**: Who do you think the customer is?

## Excavation Layers

### LAYER 1: DEMOGRAPHICS (Shallow)
Age, location, job title

### LAYER 2: PSYCHOGRAPHICS (Deeper)
Values, beliefs, aspirations

### LAYER 3: PRIVATE CONVERSATIONS (Deep)
What they say to friends, spouse, themselves

### LAYER 4: SCHEMA MAPPING (Deepest)
- What mental models do they use?
- What do they expect from your category?
- Where can you violate expectations?

## Execution Protocol

1. **MINE** existing data sources
2. **MAP** all four layers
3. **CREATE** Individual profile (one person)
4. **IDENTIFY** knowledge gaps you can fill
5. **SURFACE** schema violation opportunities
6. **BUILD** language bank from their words

## Output Deliverable

Deep ICP profile:
- Individual profile (not persona)
- Four-layer excavation
- Schema map
- Knowledge gap inventory
- Language bank
- Emotional trigger map

## Performance Metrics

- Messaging resonance improvement
- Higher response to content
- "How did you know?" reactions
