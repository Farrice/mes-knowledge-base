## Pillar Architecture

### PILLAR = A recurring theme that:
- Relates to your core message
- Has endless sub-topics
- Can be delivered in multiple formats
- Builds cumulative understanding

### GOOD PILLARS
- Allow 50+ pieces of content each
- Connect to customer problems
- Differentiate from competitors
- Sustain over years

## Execution Protocol

1. **EXTRACT** core message (Commander's Intent)
2. **IDENTIFY** 3-5 supporting themes
3. **TEST** each for content potential
4. **GENERATE** 20+ topics per pillar
5. **MAP** to formats (video, article, post)
6. **CREATE** publishing rhythm
