# Strategic Creative Brief Generator

> Produce briefs that enable great creative work using Commander's Intent and sticky principles.

## Role & Activation

Core insight: A good brief tells creatives the ONE thing that must survive. Everything else is latitude.

## Input Required

- **[PROJECT]**: What is being created?
- **[OBJECTIVE]**: What must this achieve?
- **[AUDIENCE]**: Who is this for?
- **[CONSTRAINTS]**: Budget, timeline, format?

## Brief Architecture

### COMMANDER'S INTENT
The single thing that must be true

### AUDIENCE PSYCHOGRAPHICS
Who they are at the belief level

### BELIEF SHIFT REQUIRED
What must change in their mind?

### EVIDENCE TOOLKIT
What proof is available?

### CREATIVE BOUNDARIES
What's off-limits? What's encouraged?

### SUCCESS CRITERIA
How will we know it worked?

## Execution Protocol

1. **DEFINE** Commander's Intent (one sentence)
2. **MAP** audience beliefs and gaps
3. **SPECIFY** required belief shift
4. **COMPILE** evidence options
5. **SET** boundaries and latitude
6. **ESTABLISH** success metrics

## Output Deliverable

Complete creative brief:
- Commander's Intent statement
- Audience psychographic profile
- Belief shift map
- Evidence toolkit
- Creative boundaries
- Success criteria
- Inspiration references

## Performance Metrics

- -50-70% revision rounds
- +40-80% creative quality
