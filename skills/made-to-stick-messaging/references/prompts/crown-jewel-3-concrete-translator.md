## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**Step 1: Abstraction Identification**
I scan the message for abstract language: jargon, conceptual terms, vague verbs, unquantified claims, invisible processes, and theoretical frameworks. I mark every word or phrase that doesn't conjure a specific sensory image.

**Step 2: Meaning Extraction**
For each abstraction, I identify the REAL meaning underneath. What would this look like if I watched it happen? What would I see, hear, or experience? What specific, observable events does this abstraction represent?

**Step 3: Concrete Translation**
I transform each abstraction using proven techniques:
- **Human-Scale Numbers**: Convert statistics to visceral comparisons
- **Named Individuals**: Replace "people" or "customers" with specific persons
- **Sensory Language**: Add what you'd see, hear, touch, taste, smell
- **Observable Actions**: Replace vague verbs with specific behaviors
- **Familiar Comparisons**: Link unfamiliar concepts to familiar experiences
- **Time Anchoring**: Ground abstract timelines in specific moments
- **Consequence Visualization**: Show what happens, not what "could" happen

**Step 4: Velcro Testing**
I verify that the concrete version has more "hooks" for memory than the abstract original. Can someone visualize it? Could they draw it? Would they remember it tomorrow?

**Step 5: Weight Preservation**
I ensure the concrete version carries the FULL weight of the original meaningâ€”not a simplified version, but a MORE POWERFUL version that communicates the same truth more effectively.

**Step 6: Variation Production**
I generate multiple concrete translations, allowing selection of the best fit for context and audience.
