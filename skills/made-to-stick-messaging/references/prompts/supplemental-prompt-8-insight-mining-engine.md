## CORE METHODOLOGY

### The Insight Mining Formula

**STICKY INSIGHT = Raw Data + Human-Scale Translation + Emotional Comparison + Visceral Framing**

### The Three Insight Alchemy Processes

**PROCESS 1: TRANSLATION**
*Convert abstract numbers into human experience*

Raw: "Americans waste 40% of food produced"
Translated: "Every family throws away $2,200 of groceries per year"
Human-Scale: "That's 20 pounds of food per person per month—a full grocery bag straight into the trash"

**PROCESS 2: COMPARISON**
*Create shocking contrasts that reveal truth*

Raw: "A large soda has 44g of sugar"
Compared: "That's 11 sugar cubes dissolved in your drink"
Shocking: "Drinking one large soda daily for a year means eating 25 pounds of pure sugar"

**PROCESS 3: VISUALIZATION**
*Make abstract concepts physically imaginable*

Raw: "The national debt is $33 trillion"
Visualized: "If you stacked $33 trillion in $100 bills, it would reach 22,000 miles—enough to wrap around Earth almost once"
Visceral: "Every American's share is $100,000—including your newborn"

### The Insight Quality Hierarchy

**LEVEL 1: INFORMATIVE** (Weak)
"Sales increased 23% last quarter"
→ Data stated, no transformation

**LEVEL 2: TRANSLATED** (Better)
"We added $1.2M in revenue—enough to hire 12 new team members"
→ Numbers given meaning

**LEVEL 3: COMPARATIVE** (Strong)
"We grew more in one quarter than our main competitor grew all year"
→ Context creates impact

**LEVEL 4: VISCERAL** (Sticky)
"Every day last quarter, we added revenue equivalent to a new customer walking in every 7 minutes, 24/7, for 90 days straight"
→ Physically imaginable, emotionally resonant

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**Step 1: Data Audit**
I review all available data looking for:
- Surprising numbers (counterintuitive findings)
- Extreme numbers (largest, smallest, fastest)
- Trend numbers (change over time)
- Comparison numbers (versus benchmark, competitor, expectation)
- Human-impact numbers (affects people directly)

**Step 2: Insight Candidate Identification**
I identify the 3-5 data points most likely to become sticky insights:
- Which number would make someone stop scrolling?
- Which number challenges a belief?
- Which number has emotional weight?

**Step 3: Translation Development**
For each candidate, I develop human-scale translations:
- What does this number mean in daily life terms?
- What would this look like for one person?
- How does this translate to time, money, or physical space?

**Step 4: Comparison Engineering**
For each candidate, I find the killer comparison:
- What familiar thing creates shocking contrast?
- What unexpected combination equals this number?
- What "this vs. that" makes the point undeniable?

**Step 5: Visualization Creation**
For each candidate, I create physical/visual framings:
- What would this look like if you could see it?
- What physical metaphor captures this?
- What scenario makes this viscerally real?

**Step 6: Insight Selection**
I select the top 1-3 insights based on:
- Stickiness (memorability)
- Relevance (to audience and purpose)
- Shareability (would they tell someone?)
- Action potential (does it drive desired behavior?)

**Step 7: Insight Packaging**
I package each insight for deployment:
- Headline version (one sentence)
- Explanation version (with context)
- Visual version (for graphics/slides)
- Speaking version (for presentations)
