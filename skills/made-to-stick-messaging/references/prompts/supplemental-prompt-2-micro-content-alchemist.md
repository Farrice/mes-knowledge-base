## CORE METHODOLOGY

### The Micro-Content Formula

**TRANSFORMATIONAL MICRO-CONTENT = Concentrated Insight + Instant Recognition + Memorable Phrasing + Emotional Resonance**

### The Five Micro-Content Architectures

**ARCHITECTURE 1: THE REFRAME**
*Pattern*: Take something familiar, show it differently
*Structure*: Common belief â†’ "But actually" â†’ New frame
*Example*: "Networking isn't about meeting people. It's about being remembered by people. Focus on being memorable to 10 people, not known by 100."
*Best for*: Shifting perspectives, challenging assumptions

**ARCHITECTURE 2: THE CONTRAST**
*Pattern*: Juxtapose two things to reveal truth
*Structure*: Thing A does X. Thing B does Y. The difference is everything.
*Example*: "Average writers write to express themselves. Great writers write to change their readers. One is therapy. One is craft."
*Best for*: Distinctions, elevating standards, "aha" moments

**ARCHITECTURE 3: THE SPECIFICITY BOMB**
*Pattern*: One hyper-specific detail that carries universal weight
*Structure*: Specific scene/detail â†’ Universal insight
*Example*: "The moment I knew I'd become my father: I caught myself saying 'we have food at home.' It wasn't the wordsâ€”it was the tone."
*Best for*: Relatability, emotional connection, story compression

**ARCHITECTURE 4: THE COUNTERINTUITIVE TRUTH**
*Pattern*: Lead with unexpected claim, support briefly
*Structure*: Surprising claim â†’ Brief evidence â†’ Landing
*Example*: "The best time to ask for a raise is right after you've made a mistake and fixed it. You're top of mind, you've shown accountability, and you've demonstrated value. Nobody asks then. That's why it works."
*Best for*: Getting attention, establishing authority, shareability

**ARCHITECTURE 5: THE QUESTION FLIP**
*Pattern*: Reframe a common question to reveal better question
*Structure*: People ask X. But the real question is Y.
*Example*: "Stop asking 'how do I find my passion?' Start asking 'what do I do that makes time disappear?' Passion isn't found. It's noticed."
*Best for*: Guidance, coaching, depth in small space
