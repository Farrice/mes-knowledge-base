# Ward Farnsworth — Methodology & Implementation

The core methodology for applying Ward Farnsworth's rhetorical mastery to your writing process.

## The Rhetorical Overlay Pipeline

You don't start by writing in Chiasmus or Epistrophe. You write the idea first, then run it through the Rhetorical Engine.

### 1. The Diagnostic Pass
Write the draft focusing purely on conciseness and clarity. Get the architecture of the argument onto the page without worrying about rhythm or poetry. 

### 2. The Contrast Injection (The Chord Change)
Locate the core thesis, the punchline, or the final point of the section.
- **Step A**: Rewrite the sentences leading up to the point to be slightly more Latinate/conceptual (multi-syllable, abstract words).
- **Step B**: Rewrite the final sentence or clause to be purely Saxon (short, stony, one-syllable, Germanic words with hard consonants).

### 3. The Pattern Assignment
Review the argumentative posture of the piece and apply the correct structural pattern:
- **If correcting a misconception**: Apply **Chiasmus** (ABBA reversal).
- **If building momentum to a crescendo**: Apply **Anaphora** (Start-of-sentence repetition).
- **If driving an inescapable conclusion**: Apply **Epistrophe** (End-of-sentence repetition).
- **If appealing to logic and emotion**: Apply **"Two Languages"** (Say it conceptually, then repeat it viscerally).

### 4. The Ear Check
Read the final output aloud to detect the "chord changes." If the rhythm is monotonous, adjust the phrasing so the sentence lengths and word origins contrast with each other.

## Implementation Pathway

### Phase 1: 24-Hour Quickstart
- Apply the "Saxon Punch" to the final line of your next 3 social media posts or email newsletters. Make sure the sentence leading up to it is sufficiently complex to provide the contrast.
- Read one piece of standard copywriting and manually rewrite the headline using a Chiasmus.

### Phase 2: 7-Day Sprint
- Integrate the Farnsworth Prompts into your outline-to-draft workflow.
- Rewrite the core value proposition of your current primary offer using a Chiasmus to position it perfectly against the "enemy" or alternative solutions.
- Generate 5 hooks using the Anaphora pattern to build intense tension.

### Phase 3: 30-Day System Integration
- Embed the Farnsworth Rhetorical Engine into your central copywriting autonomous agent.
- Make the "Ear Check" a mandatory validation step in all final content QA protocols. All final outbound assets must contain at least one deliberate rhetorical chord change.
