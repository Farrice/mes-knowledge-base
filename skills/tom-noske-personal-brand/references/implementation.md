## 24-Hour Quickstart

**Hour 0-2**: Archetype Diagnosis
- Watch 5 of your content pieces as a stranger
- Identify: "Valuable & Boring" or "Addictive & Useless"?
- Write paragraph describing archetype trap
- Identify 3 behaviors to change

**Hour 2-4**: Mission Crystallization
- Complete five questions framework
- Draft "Help Younger [Name]" mission statement
- Draft business mission (ICP + transformation)
- Test: Can you make content about 20 different topics?

**Hour 4-8**: Origin Story Excavation
- Write WHY your mission matters
- Identify 3-5 key struggles that shaped you
- Create iceberg documentâ€”mission above, origin below
- Select one vulnerability to share next

**Hour 8-24**: Content + Product
- Audit 10 content pieces against 90/10 rule
- Add 5 "90%" content ideas that excite you
- Draft transformation blueprint
- Audit first 3 of seven ingredients

## 7-Day Sprint

**Day 1-2**: Foundation Lock
- Finalize diagnosis and correction plan
- Complete both mission statements
- Record origin story in written + video
- Publish first vulnerability-forward content

**Day 3-4**: Trust System Build
- Implement 90/10 calendar for 30 days
- Create vulnerability deployment schedule
- Record first unscripted content
- Establish earnings-per-follower baseline

**Day 5-6**: Offer Architecture
- Complete seven-ingredient audit
- Fill gaps identified
- Design enrollment for qualification, not persuasion
- Create results collection system

**Day 7**: Integration
- Review all systems
- Publish "magnetic blend" content
- Set 30-day revenue-per-follower goal
