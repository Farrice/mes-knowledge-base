# Tom Noske Genius Patterns

12 virtuoso patterns for magnetic personal brand monetization.

---

## Pattern 1: Archetype Diagnostic
**Execute**: Categorize any creator into failure modes (Valuable & Boring or Addictive & Useless). Prescription is fusion, never choosing one.
**Success Metric**: Identify archetype within 30 seconds, articulate correction needed.

## Pattern 2: Three Buying Motivations Hierarchy
**Execute**: Frame all through WHY people buy—outcomes (lowest), brands (medium), people (highest). Engineer content to ascend hierarchy.
**Success Metric**: Audience says they buy because of YOU, not just the transformation.

## Pattern 3: Iceberg Revelation Strategy
**Execute**: Mission visible above water, origin story beneath. Systematically reveal depth to increase credibility.
**Success Metric**: Audience articulates not just WHAT you do but WHY.

## Pattern 4: 90/10 Content Liberation Formula
**Execute**: 10% strategically planned for conversion, 90% intuitive passion-driven. Only filter through mission statement.
**Success Metric**: Content feels energizing, engagement on "random" matches strategic.

## Pattern 5: Broad Mission / Niche Business
**Execute**: Broad content mission ("help younger me") + hyper-specific business mission (specific ICP + transformation).
**Success Metric**: Make content about anything, business laser-focused.

## Pattern 6: Anti-Teleprompter Authenticity
**Execute**: Speak without scripts, include stumbles, go "off-script" to signal genuine expertise.
**Success Metric**: Audience says it feels like a conversation.

## Pattern 7: Vulnerability-Trust Accelerator
**Execute**: Share struggles (bankruptcy, hardship) at strategic moments to accelerate relationship depth.
**Success Metric**: Audience shares their struggles in comments/DMs.

## Pattern 8: Product-First Content Feedback Loop
**Execute**: Customer questions = content source. Record coaching calls, repurpose as content.
**Success Metric**: Content mirrors actual customer language and concerns.

## Pattern 9: Pre-Sold Pipeline Philosophy
**Execute**: Build brand so sales require zero persuasion. Replace convincing with qualification.
**Success Metric**: Sales conversations focus on "is this right for you?"

## Pattern 10: Horsepower Hierarchy
**Execute**: Evaluate content through "earning potential per follower"—brain rot (low), escapism (medium), education (high).
**Success Metric**: Revenue per follower exceeds $2/year.

## Pattern 11: Results-Reputation Flywheel
**Execute**: Customer results = primary marketing asset. Document obsessively, feature prominently.
**Success Metric**: Can cite specific, quantified customer results for any claim.

## Pattern 12: Live Delivery Iteration Engine
**Execute**: Deliver live, not pre-recorded. Forces continuous improvement with each cohort.
**Success Metric**: Current version unrecognizable from v1.0.
