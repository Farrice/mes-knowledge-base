# Tom Noske Personal Brand

Build personal brands that generate $2-4 per follower annually through magnetic positioning and zero hard selling.

## Overview

Tom Noske is a Personal Brand Monetization Strategist who generated $3M+ since 2022 ($130K/month average, $6M cumulative student wins). His genius: most creators fail not from lack of audience but from fundamental archetype misalignment. His diagnostic framework instantly reveals why 80% of 100K+ follower accounts can't pay their bills.

**Core philosophy**: Create "Magnetic Personal Brands" by fusing educational value with authentic human connection—commanding $2-4 per follower annually while requiring zero hard selling.
