## Overview

Jonathan Franzen's methods operate on **compression logic** rather than amplification. The smaller and more specific the problem, the more powerful. The less you explain, the more the reader imagines. This skill captures his literary genius AND translates it for marketing/business applications.

## Core Principle

**Compression > Amplification**: Most writing (and marketing) operates on amplification—make everything bigger, bolder, louder. Franzen operates on compression—the smaller and more specific the insight, the more powerful. 95% of marketers have never encountered these frameworks.
