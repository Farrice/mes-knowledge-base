# LinkedIn Comedy Engine Workflow

Produce LinkedIn content that combines comedy intelligence with platform-specific optimization. This is the full LinkedIn system — not just "be funny on LinkedIn," but an engineered approach to content that gets reach AND resonance.

## When To Use
- Creating LinkedIn posts as Fresh
- Testing comedy-enhanced LinkedIn content strategies
- Building a content bank of comedy-intelligent posts

## Steps

### 1. Load Full Stack
// turbo
Read these files in order:
1. `skills/robert-mack-comedy-writing/genius.md` — comedy architecture
2. `skills/fresh-voice-system/SKILL.md` — Fresh's voice DNA
3. `skills/robert-mack-comedy-writing/references/prompts/linkedin-comedy-intelligence.md` — the LinkedIn Comedy Intelligence Engine
4. `skills/robert-mack-comedy-writing/references/voice-layer/fresh_ghostwriting_deployment_system.md` — content calendar and structural templates

### 2. Gather Input
- **Topic or content idea**: What insight is being shared?
- **Content pillar** (if applicable): Which of Fresh's 5 pillars does this fall under?
- **Post type**: Audit Post, Contrarian Reframe, Psychology Post, Year-by-Year, or Fake Survey?
- **Goal**: Engagement? Authority? Lead generation?

### 3. Execute the LinkedIn Comedy Intelligence Engine
Follow the methodology in the LinkedIn Comedy Intelligence prompt:
1. Identify core truth
2. Build recognition ladder
3. Apply comedy devices
4. Structure for LinkedIn algorithm (scroll-stopping hook, line breaks, engagement close)
5. Run quality checks

### 4. Quality Gate
Run all 5 checks from the `/comedy` workflow quality gate, PLUS:
- **Algorithm Check**: Does the hook create a Pattern Break that stops the scroll?
- **Engagement Architecture**: Does the close invite response without being a cheesy CTA?

### 5. Deliver
Output the finished post with comedy devices annotated.
