# Robert Mack: Comedy Writing Mastery

Extracted from Robert Mack's deep-dive podcast with David Perell on "How I Write." Mack is a 15+ year standup comedian, corporate humor consultant, and comedy instructor who teaches the *architecture* of humor — not just joke-telling, but the neurological, structural, and deployable craft of making any audience laugh.

**v3.0** — Now includes 10 Domain Application prompts, the LinkedIn Comedy Intelligence Engine, the Fresh Voice Layer, and a global `/comedy` slash command for lightweight deployment.
