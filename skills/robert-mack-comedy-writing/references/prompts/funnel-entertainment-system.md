## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Brunson Funnel Architecture

1. **Value Ladder Mapping**: What's the journey from free to paid?
   - Lead magnet (free value)
   - Tripwire/low-ticket (first purchase)
   - Core offer (main revenue)
   - Upsells/premium (maximum value)

2. **Hook-Story-Offer Structure**: Each step follows:
   - **Hook**: Pattern interrupt that earns attention
   - **Story**: Narrative that builds belief and desire
   - **Offer**: Clear ask with value articulation

3. **Belief Building**: What beliefs must shift?
   - Vehicle belief: "This approach can work"
   - Internal belief: "I can do this"
   - External belief: "This will work for MY situation"

4. **Urgency Engineering**: Why act now?
   - Real scarcity (not fake)
   - Timely relevance
   - Cost of delay

5. **Objection Mapping**: What stops them at each stage?
   - Lead magnet: "Is this worth my email?"
   - Tripwire: "Is this worth $7-47?"
   - Core offer: "Is this worth $X?"
   - Each stage needs objection handling

### Phase 2: Mac Entertainment Integration

6. **Pattern Interrupt at Every Stage**: No boring openings
   - Opt-in page: Not "Get my free guide"
   - Email subject lines: Not "Here's your download"
   - Sales page: Not "Welcome to..."
   - Each touchpoint earns continued attention

7. **Story Entertainment**: Make narratives enjoyable
   - Vulnerability + humor (relatable struggles)
   - Specific details that create vivid pictures
   - Self-deprecation that builds trust
   - Unexpected turns that maintain interest

8. **Objection Comedy**: Handle concerns through humor
   - Name the objection before they think it
   - Use humor to reframe
   - Self-aware acknowledgment of sales context

9. **Personality Consistency**: Same voice throughout
   - Opt-in page voice = email voice = sales page voice
   - Character development across the funnel
   - Running gags/callbacks between steps

10. **Entertainment Urgency**: Create FOMO without desperation
    - Confident, not pushy
    - Humor softens hard closes
    - Real reasons framed engagingly

### Phase 3: Funnel Stage Optimization

11. **Opt-in Page Entertainment**:
    - Headline that surprises
    - Bullets that intrigue (not just inform)
    - CTA that feels fun, not transactional

12. **Welcome/Delivery Email Entertainment**:
    - Subject line worth opening
    - Personality established immediately
    - Resource delivered WITH voice, not just link

13. **Nurture Sequence Entertainment**:
    - Each email has standalone value
    - Story-driven rather than pitch-driven
    - Builds relationship, not just familiarity

14. **Sales Page Entertainment**:
    - Opening that hooks
    - Testimonials presented with personality
    - Objections handled with humor
    - CTA that doesn't feel like begging

15. **Post-Purchase Entertainment**:
    - Confirmation that celebrates
    - Onboarding that delights
    - Ascension offers that feel natural, not pushy
