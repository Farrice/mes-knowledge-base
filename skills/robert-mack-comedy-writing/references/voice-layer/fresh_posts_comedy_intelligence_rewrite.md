## ALGORITHM INTELLIGENCE (2026)

Before we rewrite, here's what the research says:

| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Dwell time > likes** | Long reads beat quick reactions. Every line must pull to the next. |
| **Comments 15x more valuable than likes** | Posts need genuine questions, debate triggers |
| **First 60-90 min critical** | "Golden hour" determines distribution |
| **Niche > broad** | "Coaches at $30-50K/month" beats "entrepreneurs" |
| **Failures > success stories** | "How I lost $15K" gets 6x the comments of "How I landed 10 clients" |
| **Saves/shares matter** | Frameworks and "reference content" get boosted |
| **Engagement bait penalized** | "Comment YES if you agree" is detected and suppressed |
| **700-1,300 words optimal** | But TIGHT—no filler |
| **Document posts (carousels) crush** | 6.6% engagement vs ~2% for text |
| **Mobile-first** | 7 seconds to hook. 72% on mobile. |

**Translation**: Posts need to be specific enough to niche-target, hooky enough to stop scrolls, long enough to build dwell time, tight enough to hold attention, and emotionally resonant enough to trigger meaningful comments and saves.
