# Luke Iha: Unaware Ads — Genius Context

## Core Philosophy
Most advertisers fight over problem-aware and solution-aware audiences — expensive, crowded, diminishing returns. The unaware audience is the blue ocean: untouched, cheaper to reach, and more loyal once converted. But writing for unaware audiences requires a fundamentally different architecture. You can't lead with the problem (they don't know they have one). You can't lead with the solution (irrelevant to them). You must lead with **curiosity** and **worldview validation** — then systematically shift their beliefs until your product feels inevitable.

## Genius Patterns

### 1. Curiosity × Relevance Architecture
**What They Do**: Every hook is evaluated on two axes — curiosity (novelty) and relevance (personal stakes). Too much curiosity = clickbait. Too much relevance = boring. Both must score 7+.
**Executable Behavior**: Score every hook 1-10 on both axes. Only deploy hooks at 7+ on BOTH. Low curiosity? Add paradox or contradiction. Low relevance? Add personal stakes.
**Deploy When**: Ad hooks, email subjects, YouTube titles, social openers.
**Success Metric**: CTR above 2% WITH conversion above 1%.

### 2. Worldview Porn
**What They Do**: Articulate beliefs the audience already holds but hasn't seen expressed. Creates instant "god, YES" identity resonance that bypasses all skepticism.
**Executable Behavior**: Research tribal beliefs. Frame as declarations: "Everyone knows [tribal belief] but nobody's willing to say it." The audience feels *seen*, not sold to.
**Deploy When**: Top-of-funnel ads for unaware audiences, brand manifestos, community building.
**Success Metric**: High share rates and "I've been saying this for years!" comments.

### 3. The Open Loop Engine
**What They Do**: Never resolve curiosity until after the mechanism reveal. Each section opens a new question while partially answering the previous one.
**Executable Behavior**: After hook, open loop about mechanism. Deliver hook payoff but open NEW loop. Deliver mechanism, open solution loop. Product resolves ALL loops.
**Deploy When**: Long-form ads, VSLs, webinar scripts, email sequences.
**Success Metric**: Read-through rate above 60% (vs. typical 20-30%).

### 4. Organic Hook Theft
**What They Do**: Systematically steal hooks validated by organic platforms — YouTube titles with millions of views, viral tweets, Reddit posts with thousands of upvotes.
**Executable Behavior**: Search YouTube for "[topic] + surprising/secret/hidden." Note titles with 500K+ views. Scan Reddit top posts. Collect viral tweets. Adapt hook structure, swap subject.
**Deploy When**: Ad creation, content ideation, email subjects.
**Success Metric**: Hook bank of 50+ validated hooks per niche.

### 5. The Paradox Hook
**What They Do**: "X everyone does is actually killing Y." Contradicts common belief to create irresistible cognitive dissonance.
**Executable Behavior**: Identify most common advice in market. Invert it. Support with mechanism that explains the paradox.
**Deploy When**: Unaware and problem-aware ads. Contrarian content. Category creation.
**Success Metric**: CTR 2-4x higher than direct benefit hooks.

### 6. The Conspiracy Frame
**What They Do**: "They don't want you to know X." Creates enemy, secret, and hero (the reader as insider).
**Executable Behavior**: Identify market's establishment. Frame conventional wisdom as ignorant or misleading. Present mechanism as suppressed alternative. Tone: rebellious insider, not tinfoil.
**Deploy When**: Health, finance, education, self-improvement markets.
**Success Metric**: Strong emotional engagement and community formation.

### 7. The Lost Wisdom Hook
**What They Do**: "Ancient/forgotten technique that X." Taps nostalgia and anti-modern sentiment.
**Executable Behavior**: Research historical practices. Find genuine efficacy abandoned by mainstream. Frame as rediscovered. Stack with modern validation.
**Deploy When**: Markets with historical depth (health, productivity, relationships).
**Success Metric**: Curiosity + authority in one hook.

### 8. Vectors of Winning Angles
**What They Do**: Generate unlimited hooks by running any topic through three axes: **Time** (past/present/future), **Behavior** (do/don't do), **Object** (unexpected tools/ingredients).
**Executable Behavior**: Fill the matrix with 3-5 hooks per axis. Select best 5 for testing.
**Deploy When**: Ad creative sprints, content ideation, hook bank expansion.
**Success Metric**: 20+ unique hooks generated per session.

## Hidden Knowledge

- **Unaware Ads = 10x Value**: Untouched audiences cost less (no competitive bidding), are more grateful, and have no competing solution to compare against.
- **The Ad Structure Isn't Linear**: Hook → **Payoff** → Mechanism → Solution → Product → Reasons to Buy. Payoff comes BEFORE mechanism to maintain engagement.
- **Transformation > Information**: The ad's job is worldview transformation. By the time they see the product, their beliefs have already shifted.
- **Hook Banks Are Gold Mines**: Systematic hook collection compounds over time. While competitors stare at blank pages, you select from proven templates.
