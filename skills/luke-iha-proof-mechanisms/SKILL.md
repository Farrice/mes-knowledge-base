# Luke Iha: Proof Mechanisms in Copywriting

Luke Iha is a world-class copywriter who has generated over $100 million for his clients primarily through Video Sales Letters (VSLs). He believes that proof is infinitely more important than the promise, and emphasizes that true conversion happens when a prospect feels entirely safe buying from you.
