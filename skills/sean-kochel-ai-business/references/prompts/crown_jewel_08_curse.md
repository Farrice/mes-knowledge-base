## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **DETECT EXPERT ASSUMPTIONS**: Identify every place where the copy assumes knowledge the audience may not haveâ€”technical terms, industry jargon, assumed context.

4. **SURFACE EMPATHY GAPS**: Find places where the copy addresses problems the expert KNOWS exist rather than problems the customer FEELS exist.

5. **ANALYZE BENEFIT TRANSLATION**: Assess whether benefits are expressed in customer outcome language or creator feature language.

6. **MAP CUSTOMER MENTAL MODEL**: Based on audience analysis, construct how the CUSTOMER thinks about this problem (vs. how the expert thinks about it).

7. **EXECUTE FULL REVERSAL**: Rewrite all flagged sections from the customer's perspective, using their language, addressing their felt problems, at their awareness level.

8. **CREATE TRANSLATION GLOSSARY**: Build a reference converting expert terms to customer terms for ongoing use.
