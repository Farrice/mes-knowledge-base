## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **DIAGNOSE MARKET SOPHISTICATION STAGE**: Analyze competitor messaging to determine which of the 5 stages the market has reached:
   - Stage 1: Direct Claim (just say what it does)
   - Stage 2: Expanded Claim (add benefits and details)
   - Stage 3: Unique Mechanism (explain WHY/HOW it works differently)
   - Stage 4: Mechanism Escalation (stack mechanisms, add proof)
   - Stage 5: Identity Positioning (it's about who you become)

2. **AUDIT COMPETITOR POSITIONING**: Map each competitor's current positioning to sophistication stage‚Äîidentify gaps and opportunities.

3. **IDENTIFY POSITIONING OPPORTUNITY**: Determine whether to match market stage with better execution OR leapfrog to next stage before competitors.

4. **DEVELOP MECHANISM ARCHITECTURE**: If Stage 3-4 positioning needed, create or name unique mechanisms that differentiate the product.

5. **CREATE IDENTITY POSITIONING**: If Stage 5 positioning needed or desired, develop tribal identity framework.

6. **PRODUCE POSITIONING STATEMENT**: Create complete positioning including headline, subhead, mechanism explanation, and proof elements.

7. **DESIGN MESSAGING HIERARCHY**: Structure all messaging to lead with appropriate sophistication-level hooks.

8. **CREATE COMPETITIVE COMPARISON**: Produce positioning that explicitly or implicitly differentiates from named competitors.
