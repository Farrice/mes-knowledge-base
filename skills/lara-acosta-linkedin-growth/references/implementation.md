# Lara Acosta — Implementation Pathway

The optimal sequence for deploying this expertise into an active LinkedIn strategy. 

## The 30-Minute LinkedIn Execution Suite
1. **Identify the Core Arbitrage**: Select a trending topic (Strategic Arbitrage) or a specific, high-value client result (Monetizable Expertise).
2. **Draft the 8-Word Hook**: Write the primary hook. Hard cut. Write the 8-word rehook. Hard cut.
3. **Format via SLAY**: Build out the body using the Story -> Lesson -> Actionable -> You framework.
4. **Apply F-Shape Spacing**: Break all paragraphs down into maximum 2-sentence blocks. Add visual breathing room.
5. **Live Deployment**: Publish natively (no schedulers). Remain on platform for 30 minutes to sprint-engage with initial comments.

## 30-Day Sprint Plan

**Week 1: The Humble Brag Activation**
- Purpose: Prime the algorithm with strong organic reach using high-engagement milestone formats.
- Action: Deploy 2x posts using the `humble-brag-architect.md` prompt, focusing on a recent win or a personal origin story related to the business. 
- Goal: Secure 50+ comments per post through aggressive 30-minute sprint engagement.

**Week 2-3: The SLAY Education Cadence**
- Purpose: Shift from TAM engagement to authority building and lead qualification. 
- Action: Deploy 3x posts per week using `slay-post-generator.md`. Focus deeply on Monetizable Expertise—giving away the exact step-by-step blueprints for how you deliver client results.
- Goal: Move readers from "liking" your story to saving and implementing your advice. 

**Week 4: The 4-3-2-1 Matrix Integration**
- Purpose: Lock into the sustainable weekly architecture.
- Action: Publish 4 times this week: 
  1. Broad TAM Post (`humble-brag-architect.md` or trend-jacking)
  2. Growth Niche Post (`slay-post-generator.md`)
  3. Narrow Growth Niche Post (`slay-post-generator.md` on an advanced execution element)
  4. Direct Sales Post (blunt CTA relying on the accumulated goodwill of the week).
- Goal: Systematized inbound volume while maintaining algorithmic favor.
