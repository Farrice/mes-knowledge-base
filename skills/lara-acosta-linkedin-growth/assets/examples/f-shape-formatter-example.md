# Example: F-Shape Formatter

**Context**: 
- Raw Copy: "Today I'm going to share 3 ways to improve your SEO. A lot of people struggle with getting backlinks, but it's important. First, you should write guest posts. Second, you can do broken link building. Third, make sure your on-page SEO is good. This will help you rank higher on Google if you stay consistent with it over a few months."

**THE DELIVERABLE:**

SEO feels impossible for most founders.

But I crack it in 3 steps.

For years, I completely struggled with getting backlinks.
I thought it was a dark art.
I thought you needed a massive PR budget to rank.

But then I built a repeatable system.
Here is exactly how I rank on page one:

1. Guest Post Leverage: I stopped pitching strangers and started offering free value to adjacent niches.
2. Target Broken Links: I run audits on competitors, find their dead links, and offer my active links to the host.
3. On-Page Obsession: Backlinks don't matter if your site structure is a mess. I fix H1s before I ever send an email.

Consistency is the only algorithm that matters.
If you do this for 90 days, your calendar will fill up.

If you had to pick one, do you prioritize backlinks or on-page content?
