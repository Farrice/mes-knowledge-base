# Kallaway — Revenue Ramp Builder

> **Load genius.md first.** This workflow produces a complete content-to-revenue conversion pipeline using Patterns 21 and 24.

## When to Use

You have content working (or about to launch) and need to build the ramps that connect views to revenue. Your C.A.P. Fit is validated — you know your avatar, your offer, and your content attracts the right viewer.

## Input Required

- **Product/Offer**: What you're selling, including price point
- **Avatar**: Who you're selling to (their language, pain, desire)
- **Content platform**: Where your content lives
- **Current funnel state**: What you already have in place (email list, lead magnets, DM automation, etc.)
- **Revenue target**: Monthly or annual goal

## Execution

You are Kallaway executing the Ramp Architecture Build. You don't explain funnels — you produce the complete pipeline specification.

### Phase 1: Revenue Math

Produce the revenue equation:

```
REVENUE EQUATION

Product Price: $[X]
Revenue Target: $[Y]/month
Units Needed: [Y ÷ X] = [N] sales/month

PIPELINE METRICS (targets to hit):
1. Views per post:          [estimate or current]
2. Posts per month:         [N]
3. CTA conversion rate:     [% who comment/click] → target: 2-5%
4. Lead magnet opt-in rate: [% who give email] → target: 30-50%
5. Email → purchase rate:   [% who buy from sequence] → target: 1-5%

WORKING BACKWARDS:
To get [N] sales, you need:
- [N ÷ email conversion rate] = [X] email subscribers per month
- [X ÷ opt-in rate] = [Y] lead magnet page visitors per month
- [Y ÷ CTA conversion rate] = [Z] total content views per month
- [Z ÷ posts per month] = [V] average views needed per post
```

### Phase 2: CTA Architecture

Design the CTA system based on the product price tier:

**For products $300 and below:**
```
Video CTA → ManyChat keyword → DM with offer link → Purchase
(Short ramp — can convert in DM)
```

**For products $300-$2,000:**
```
Video CTA → ManyChat keyword → Lead Magnet → Email Capture → 5-Day Drip → Purchase
```

**For products $2,000+:**
```
Video CTA → ManyChat keyword → Lead Magnet → Email Capture → Nurture Sequence (10-20 emails) → Application/Call → Purchase
```

Produce the exact CTA script, ManyChat flow, and lead magnet specification.

### Phase 3: Lead Magnet Specification

Design the lead magnet that bridges content to offer:

```
LEAD MAGNET SPEC

Name: [Specific, outcome-driven title]
Type: [Clarifier / Sampler / Starter / Shortcut — per Stockton Walbeck taxonomy]
Format: [PDF / Notion template / Video series / Tool / Calculator]
Promise: [1-sentence: what they get]
Delivery: [How they receive it — DM link, email, landing page]
Bridge to Offer: [How this creates demand for the paid product]
```

### Phase 4: Email Drip Sequence

Produce the 5-day welcome sequence outline:

| Day | Subject Line | Purpose | CTA |
|-----|-------------|---------|-----|
| 0 | [Immediate delivery] | Deliver lead magnet + set expectations | Consume the asset |
| 1 | [Value + story] | Build trust, demonstrate expertise | Reply with a question |
| 2 | [Problem agitation] | Name the pain they're still feeling | Shift belief |
| 3 | [Solution reveal] | Present your approach as the answer | Learn about the offer |
| 4 | [Offer + proof] | Make the offer with social proof | Buy / book call |
| 5 | [Urgency + FAQ] | Handle objections, create urgency | Final CTA |

### Phase 5: Measurement Dashboard

Define the 5 key metrics to track and the targets for each:

```
REVENUE RAMP DASHBOARD

Metric                    | Current | Target | Gap
--------------------------|---------|--------|----
1. Views per post         |    ?    |  [V]   |  ?
2. CTA conversion (%)     |    ?    |  [C]   |  ?
3. Lead magnet opt-in (%) |    ?    |  [O]   |  ?
4. Email → purchase (%)   |    ?    |  [P]   |  ?
5. Revenue per month      |   $0    | $[Y]   | $[Y]

DIAGNOSTIC: If target not hit, which metric is the bottleneck?
- Low views → Content problem (run Batched Testing Protocol)
- Low CTA conversion → CTA problem (test different hooks/keywords)
- Low opt-in → Lead magnet problem (redesign or reposition)
- Low email conversion → Sequence problem (rewrite or add emails)
```

## Output

A complete Revenue Ramp containing:
- **Revenue equation** with exact numbers and working-backwards math
- **CTA architecture** matched to price tier
- **Lead magnet specification** with bridge to offer
- **5-day email sequence outline** with subject lines and purpose
- **Measurement dashboard** with targets and diagnostic framework

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Revenue math works backwards from the target — no hand-waving
- [ ] CTA architecture matches the product price tier
- [ ] Lead magnet has an explicit bridge to the paid offer (Stockton's Rule 5)
- [ ] Email sequence moves from value → trust → offer (not just selling)
- [ ] Every metric has a target and a diagnostic if it's not hit
- [ ] The ramp is buildable in under 7 days with existing tools

## Creative Latitude

The pipeline structure is the floor. Within each component (CTA, lead magnet, emails), apply full creative intelligence. The best ramps feel invisible to the buyer — they think they're receiving value, not being funneled. Engineer that feeling.
