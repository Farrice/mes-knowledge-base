## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Trust Requirement Mapping

1. **CALCULATE TRUST THRESHOLD**:

   ```
   TRUST THRESHOLD BY PRICE POINT:
   
   Free Lead Magnet: ~5 content minutes
   └── Required: Basic credibility, relevance proof
   
   Low-Ticket ($27-97): ~15-30 content minutes
   └── Required: Expertise proof, problem understanding, some transformation evidence
   
   Mid-Ticket ($297-997): ~60-120 content minutes
   └── Required: Deep expertise, multiple proof points, methodology clarity
   
   High-Ticket ($2K-10K): ~200-400 content minutes
   └── Required: Comprehensive expertise, significant proof, relationship familiarity
   
   Premium ($10K+): ~500+ content minutes
   └── Required: Deep relationship, extensive proof, high comfort level
   ```

2. **IDENTIFY TRUST COMPONENTS**:

   ```
   COMPETENCE TRUST:
   └── Do they believe you CAN help them?
   └── Evidence: Results, credentials, demonstrations
   
   CHARACTER TRUST:
   └── Do they believe you WILL help them (not exploit them)?
   └── Evidence: Vulnerability, values alignment, fair dealing
   
   CARING TRUST:
   └── Do they believe you understand and care about THEIR specific situation?
   └── Evidence: Specificity, personalization, empathy demonstration
   
   CONSISTENCY TRUST:
   └── Do they believe you'll deliver reliably over time?
   └── Evidence: Track record, testimonials, presence stability
   ```

3. **MAP CURRENT TRUST GAPS**:
   
   For each conversion goal, identify:
   - Which trust component is weakest?
   - What specific doubt remains?
   - What evidence would resolve it?

### Phase 2: Trust Density Engineering

4. **DESIGN HIGH-DENSITY CONTENT**:

   **Low-Density Content (Avoid/Minimize):**
   - Generic tips that anyone could share
   - Surface-level takes without depth
   - Entertainment without expertise demonstration
   - Content that doesn't showcase unique methodology

   **High-Density Content (Prioritize):**
   - Specific methodology reveals with examples
   - Case studies with detailed process breakdowns
   - Contrarian insights that demonstrate deep expertise
   - Vulnerable shares that build character trust
   - Direct problem-solving that shows caring trust

5. **CALCULATE CONTENT TRUST VALUE**:

   ```
   TRUST VALUE = Specificity × Proof × Relevance × Uniqueness
   
   Where:
   - Specificity: How concrete vs. generic (1-5)
   - Proof: How much evidence provided (1-5)
   - Relevance: How directly applicable to their situation (1-5)
   - Uniqueness: How differentiated from what they've heard (1-5)
   
   Score 15+: High-density content
   Score 10-14: Medium-density content
   Score <10: Low-density content (don't publish)
   ```

6. **CREATE TRUST CONCENTRATION SEQUENCES**:

   Design content series that build trust components systematically:

   **Competence Concentration Sequence:**
   - Video 1: Framework overview (establishes methodology)
   - Video 2: Deep-dive into one element (proves depth)
   - Video 3: Case study applying framework (proves results)
   - Video 4: Common mistakes (proves experience)
   - Video 5: Advanced applications (proves mastery)

   **Character Concentration Sequence:**
   - Share 1: Origin story with failures (vulnerability)
   - Share 2: Why you do this work (values)
   - Share 3: Client you turned away (integrity)
   - Share 4: Something you changed your mind about (growth)

### Phase 3: Trust Architecture Design

7. **BUILD TRUST PATHWAYS**:

   **For Low-Ticket Conversions ($27-97):**
   ```
   Discovery content (1-2 pieces) → Lead magnet → Email sequence (3-5 emails) → Offer
   Trust minutes required: ~15-30
   Timeline: 3-7 days
   ```

   **For Mid-Ticket Conversions ($297-997):**
   ```
   Pillar content (3-5 pieces) → Deep-dive series → Case studies → Offer
   Trust minutes required: ~60-120
   Timeline: 2-4 weeks
   ```

   **For High-Ticket Conversions ($2K+):**
   ```
   Comprehensive content library → Personal connection point → Application process
   Trust minutes required: ~200-400
   Timeline: 1-3 months
   ```

8. **DESIGN TRUST ACCELERATION TACTICS**:

   **Borrowed Trust:**
   - Guest appearances on trusted platforms
   - Testimonials from recognized names
   - Association with credible institutions
   - Co-creation with established experts

   **Compressed Trust:**
   - Long-form content (one 45-min video = 45 minutes of concentrated attention)
   - Binge-able series (accumulated trust in single session)
   - Intensive experiences (workshops, challenges)

   **Proof Stacking:**
   - Multiple case studies (variety of situations)
   - Quantified results (specific numbers)
   - Before/after demonstrations (visual proof)
   - Process transparency (methodology evidence)

### Phase 4: Trust Measurement

9. **TRACK TRUST INDICATORS**:

   **Content Engagement Signals:**
   - Time spent (actual trust minutes accumulated)
   - Return visits (relationship building)
   - Content depth consumption (watched to end, read full post)
   - Sharing behavior (trust endorsement to others)

   **Conversion Readiness Signals:**
   - Email engagement patterns
   - Multiple content consumption
   - Questions about logistics (price, timing) vs. value
   - Self-qualification statements

10. **OPTIMIZE TRUST-TO-CONVERSION RATIO**:

    Track: Trust minutes accumulated before conversion
    Goal: Reduce while maintaining conversion rate
    Method: Increase trust density, not trust volume
