## COMMENT PSYCHOLOGY

### Why Platforms Love Comments

1. **Time on platform**: Comments = users writing (sticky engagement)
2. **Discussion quality**: Long comments > emoji reactions
3. **Reply chains**: Back-and-forth = community signal
4. **Sentiment**: Positive comments > negative (mostly)

## COMMENT GENERATION TACTICS

### Tactic 1: The Direct Question

End content with a specific question that's easy to answer:
- ❌ "What do you think?"
- ✅ "What's your #1 struggle with [specific thing]?"
- ✅ "Have you ever experienced [specific situation]?"

### Tactic 2: The Controversial Take

Make a statement that creates "I have to respond" energy:
- ❌ "This is one approach..."
- ✅ "This is the ONLY approach that actually works..."
- ✅ "If you're doing [common thing], you're wasting your time..."

### Tactic 3: The Fill-in-the-Blank

Create templates people want to complete:
- "I used to [blank], but now I [blank]"
- "My biggest [topic] mistake was [blank]"
- "One thing I wish I knew about [topic]: [blank]"

### Tactic 4: The This-or-That

Binary choices drive responses:
- "Are you a [type A] or [type B]?"
- "[Option 1] or [option 2]—which do you prefer?"
- "Would you rather [A] or [B]?"

### Tactic 5: The Story Prompt

Invite personal stories:
- "Tell me about your experience with [topic]..."
- "When did you first realize [insight]?"
- "What's the craziest [topic] story you have?"

## REPLY STRATEGY

### The Reply Hierarchy

| Comment Type | Reply Strategy |
|--------------|----------------|
| **High-intent question** | Detailed answer + offer to DM |
| **Thoughtful response** | Acknowledge + add value + question back |
| **Simple positive** | Thanks + question to deepen |
| **Story share** | Relate + ask follow-up |
| **Negative/critical** | Address if valid, ignore if troll |

### Reply Formulas

**For questions**:
"Great question—[brief answer]. [Follow-up question back]"

**For stories**:
"That's exactly it. What happened after [detail]?"

**For positive**:
"Thanks! What specifically resonated?"
