## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Trust Economics Understanding

1. **MAP THE TRUST EQUATION**:

   ```
   TRUST = (Credibility × Reliability × Intimacy) ÷ Self-Interest Perception
   
   Where:
   - Credibility: Do they believe you know what you're talking about?
   - Reliability: Do they believe you'll deliver on promises?
   - Intimacy: Do they feel you understand THEM specifically?
   - Self-Interest: Do they think you're just trying to sell them?
   ```

2. **IDENTIFY TRUST DEPOSITS VS WITHDRAWALS**:

   **Trust Deposits (+):**
   - Specific proof (numbers, screenshots, receipts)
   - Admitting weaknesses or failures
   - Teaching without asking for anything
   - Demonstrating deep understanding of their situation
   - Providing unexpected value
   - Being specific rather than vague
   - Showing work/process, not just results
   - Third-party validation (testimonials, press, credentials)
   - Consistency over time

   **Trust Withdrawals (-):**
   - Vague claims without proof
   - Only showing wins, never losses
   - Premature selling before value delivered
   - Generic advice that could apply to anyone
   - Overpromising results
   - Inconsistency between platforms/messages
   - Obvious self-interest without value exchange
   - Pressure tactics or false urgency

3. **CALCULATE TRUST THRESHOLD BY CONVERSION**:

   | Conversion Type | Trust Threshold | Content Minutes Typically Required |
   |-----------------|-----------------|-----------------------------------|
   | Email signup | Low (20%) | 3-5 minutes |
   | Free consultation | Medium (40%) | 15-30 minutes |
   | Low-ticket ($50-200) | Medium (50%) | 30-60 minutes |
   | Mid-ticket ($500-2K) | High (70%) | 60-120 minutes |
   | High-ticket ($5K+) | Very High (85%) | 120-300+ minutes |
   | Retainer/ongoing | Maximum (95%) | 300+ minutes + social proof |

   - Understanding of their specific context

### Phase 3: Trust Sequence Design

6. **DESIGN TRUST ACCUMULATION PATHWAY**:

   **For Cold Audiences:**
   ```
   Stage 1: PATTERN INTERRUPT (0-30 sec)
   └── Hook that signals "this is different"
   └── Trust element: Unexpected honesty or contrarian view
   
   Stage 2: CREDIBILITY ESTABLISHMENT (30 sec - 2 min)
   └── Why should they listen to you?
   └── Trust element: Brief, specific proof of expertise
   
   Stage 3: INTIMACY BUILDING (2-5 min)
   └── Demonstrate understanding of THEIR situation
   └── Trust element: Private conversation language
   
   Stage 4: VALUE DELIVERY (5-15 min)
   └── Give them something useful
   └── Trust element: Teaching without selling
   
   Stage 5: SOCIAL PROOF (layered throughout)
   └── Evidence others have benefited
   └── Trust element: Testimonials, results, case studies
   
   Stage 6: BRIDGE TO CONVERSION
   └── Connect value to offer
   └── Trust element: Logical next step, not pressure
   ```

7. **CALCULATE TRUST DENSITY PER MINUTE**:

   ```
   Trust Density = Trust Deposits / Content Minutes
   
   Target Density by Format:
   - Short-form: 3+ deposits per minute
   - YouTube: 1-2 deposits per minute
   - Podcast: 0.5-1 deposits per minute
   - Written: 1-2 deposits per paragraph
   ```

8. **OPTIMIZE FOR TRUST ACCELERATION**:

   **Trust Accelerators:**
   - Specificity (numbers, names, dates)
   - Vulnerability (admitted failures)
   - Proof stacking (multiple evidence types)
   - Pattern breaking (saying what others won't)
   - Overfulfilling expectations

   **Trust Velocity Killers:**
   - Vague language
   - Premature asks
   - Generic advice
   - Self-congratulation
   - Inconsistent messaging
