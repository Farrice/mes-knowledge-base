### DM CONVERSATION FRAMEWORK

**Opening** (After they DM or you reach out):
"Hey [Name]! Saw your comment about [their pain]. What's going on with [their situation]?"

**Discovery** (Understanding their situation):
- "What have you tried so far?"
- "How long has this been a challenge?"
- "What's your goal - what would success look like?"

**Transition** (Moving toward solution):
- "Based on what you've shared, I think the main issue is [diagnosis]"
- "Have you seen my [lead magnet]? It addresses exactly this part"
- For high-fit: "I have a complete system on this - would you want me to share what's in it?"

**Close** (To email or offer):
- To email: "I can send you the templates + a video walkthrough. What's your email?"
- To offer: "The full system is in my course. Want me to send you the details?"
