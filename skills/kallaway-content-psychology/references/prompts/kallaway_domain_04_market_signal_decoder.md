## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Signal Source Mapping

1. **ESTABLISH SIGNAL INFRASTRUCTURE**:

   **Search Intent Signals:**
   - Google Trends (relative search volume changes)
   - Google Search Console (what's driving traffic NOW)
   - Answer the Public (question-based queries)
   - Ahrefs/SEMrush (keyword difficulty + volume trends)
   - YouTube search suggestions (video-specific intent)
   - TikTok search (emerging Gen-Z/millennial intent)

   **Social Conversation Signals:**
   - Twitter/X trending topics + hashtag velocity
   - Reddit rising posts in niche subreddits
   - LinkedIn trending articles (B2B signals)
   - Instagram Explore patterns
   - TikTok For You Page themes

   **Buyer Intent Signals:**
   - "Best [product] for [use case]" search volume
   - "Alternative to [competitor]" queries
   - Review searches for specific products
   - Comparison queries ("[A] vs [B]")
   - Price-related searches ("[product] cost/pricing")

   **Cultural Momentum Signals:**
   - News cycle topics affecting your niche
   - Celebrity/influencer mentions
   - Meme templates being adapted
   - Controversy/debate emergence
   - Seasonal/event-driven spikes

### Phase 2: Signal Analysis

2. **DECODE SEARCH INTENT TYPES**:

   **Informational Intent** (Learning):
   - Signals: "what is," "how to," "why does"
   - Content opportunity: Educational content, guides
   - Monetization path: Trust building → eventual conversion
   - Example: "what is content marketing" → beginner guide

   **Navigational Intent** (Finding):
   - Signals: Brand names, specific product searches
   - Content opportunity: Comparison, review content
   - Monetization path: Affiliate, alternative positioning
   - Example: "HubSpot alternatives" → your solution

   **Commercial Intent** (Evaluating):
   - Signals: "best," "top," "review," "vs"
   - Content opportunity: Comparison content, listicles
   - Monetization path: Direct conversion, affiliate
   - Example: "best email marketing software 2024" → recommendation

   **Transactional Intent** (Buying):
   - Signals: "buy," "price," "discount," "coupon"
   - Content opportunity: Product pages, offers
   - Monetization path: Immediate conversion
   - Example: "ConvertKit pricing" → offer page

3. **MAP TREND LIFECYCLE POSITION**:

   ```
   EMERGENCE (Opportunity: First-mover)
   └── Signal: <1,000 searches but 200%+ growth
   └── Strategy: Define the conversation, create foundational content
   └── Risk: Trend may not materialize
   
   GROWTH (Opportunity: Scale capture)
   └── Signal: Rapid search growth, social mentions increasing
   └── Strategy: Multiple angles, comprehensive coverage
   └── Risk: Competition entering
   
   PEAK (Opportunity: Maximum reach)
   └── Signal: Highest search volume, mainstream conversation
   └── Strategy: Differentiated angles only, contrarian takes
   └── Risk: Saturation, declining novelty
   
   DECLINE (Opportunity: Evergreen conversion)
   └── Signal: Search volume dropping, conversation moving on
   └── Strategy: Evergreen optimization, long-tail capture
   └── Risk: Diminishing returns on new content
   ```

4. **IDENTIFY SIGNAL INTERSECTIONS**:
   
   **High-Value Intersections:**
   - Rising search + social conversation = Emerging trend
   - Commercial intent + low competition = Conversion opportunity
   - News cycle + your expertise = Authority positioning
   - Controversy + your angle = Attention capture

### Phase 3: Content Opportunity Mapping

5. **GENERATE SIGNAL-DRIVEN CONTENT IDEAS**:

   **For Each Signal Identified:**
   - What's the underlying need driving this signal?
   - What content would perfectly satisfy that need?
   - What angle hasn't been covered yet?
   - How does this connect to your monetization?
   - What's the timing window?

6. **PRIORITIZE BY SIGNAL STRENGTH**:

   | Factor | Weight | Score |
   |--------|--------|-------|
   | Search volume trend | 25% | Rising/Stable/Falling |
   | Competition level | 20% | Low/Medium/High |
   | Intent-offer alignment | 25% | Direct/Adjacent/Distant |
   | Timing window | 15% | Urgent/This week/This month |
   | Content effort required | 15% | Low/Medium/High |

7. **CREATE CONTENT BRIEFS FROM SIGNALS**:

   **For Each Priority Topic:**
   - Primary keyword + search intent
   - Secondary keywords to capture
   - Angle that differentiates
   - Trend position timing
   - Monetization integration
   - Competitive gap to exploit
