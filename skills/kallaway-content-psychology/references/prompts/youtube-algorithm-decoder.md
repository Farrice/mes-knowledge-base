## THUMBNAIL-TITLE ENGINEERING

**Thumbnail Formula**:
1. Pop at small size (mobile viewing)
2. Create curiosity gap with title
3. Show emotion (faces with clear expressions)
4. Use contrast
5. Limit text (3 words maximum)

**Title Formula**:
```
[SEARCH KEYWORD] + [CURIOSITY HOOK] + [SPECIFICITY]

Examples:
"How to Edit Videos FASTER (The Method Pros Don't Share)"
"I Tried the 5AM Routine for 30 Days. Here's What Happened."
```

**Title CTR Boosters**:
- Numbers: +15-25% CTR
- Parentheticals: +10-20% CTR
- "I" Statements: +10-15% CTR
- Negative Framing: +10-20% CTR
