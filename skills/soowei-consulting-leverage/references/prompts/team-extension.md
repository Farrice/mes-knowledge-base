## Team Extension Options

### VIRTUAL SUPPORT
- Admin tasks
- Research
- Documentation
- Low cost, high time savings

### ASSOCIATE CONSULTANTS
- Client-facing delivery
- Specialized expertise
- Subcontractor model
- Medium cost, capacity multiplication

### STRATEGIC PARTNERS
- Complementary services
- Referral relationships
- White-label arrangements
- Variable, expands capability

### FRACTIONAL RESOURCES
- Finance, operations, marketing
- Part-time senior talent
- Strategic capabilities
- Premium cost, high impact

## Execution Protocol

1. **AUDIT** current constraints
2. **IDENTIFY** delegation candidates
3. **SELECT** team extension model
4. **DESIGN** working arrangements
5. **CREATE** quality assurance systems
6. **BUILD** gradually

## Output Deliverable

A complete **Team Extension Plan** containing:
- Constraint analysis
- Delegation priority list
- Team model recommendation
- Arrangement structures
- Quality systems
- Financial projections
