## The Qualification Matrix

### DIMENSION 1: FIT
- Problem matches your expertise
- Industry/stage you understand
- Working style compatibility
- Values alignment

### DIMENSION 2: READINESS
- Has budget allocated
- Has authority to decide
- Has urgency to act
- Has resources to execute

### DIMENSION 3: QUALITY
- Respectful of expertise
- Communicates clearly
- Pays on time
- Long-term potential

### DIMENSION 4: VALUE
- Project size worth pursuing
- Strategic importance
- Referral/case study potential
- Learning opportunity

## Execution Protocol

1. **DEFINE** ideal client profile
2. **IDENTIFY** qualification questions
3. **CREATE** intake process
4. **DESIGN** scoring system
5. **BUILD** conversation guides
6. **ESTABLISH** walkaway criteria

## Output Deliverable

A complete **Qualification System** containing:
- Ideal client profile
- Red flag checklist
- Qualification questions (10-15)
- Scoring matrix
- Intake process design
- Decline scripts (graceful)
