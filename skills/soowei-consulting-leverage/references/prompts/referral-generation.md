## The Referral System

### EARN THE RIGHT
- Exceed expectations
- Create referral-worthy moments
- Document wins for sharing

### ASK STRATEGICALLY
- Timing (peak satisfaction)
- Language (make it easy)
- Specificity (who exactly)

### MAKE IT EASY
- Referral language for them
- Materials they can share
- No friction handoffs

### RECOGNIZE AND REWARD
- Thank immediately
- Update on outcomes
- Consider incentives

1. **AUDIT** current referral sources

5. **DESIGN** recognition system
6. **TRACK** referral pipeline
