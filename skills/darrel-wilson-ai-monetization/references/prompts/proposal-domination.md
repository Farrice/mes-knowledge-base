## Input Required

- **[OPPORTUNITY]**: The RFP or contract opportunity
- **[REQUIREMENTS]**: Stated criteria and deliverables
- **[COMPETITION]**: Who else is likely bidding
- **[YOUR_STRENGTHS]**: Unique advantages you bring

---

## Execution Protocol

1. **DECODE** what the buyer actually wants beyond stated requirements
2. **STRUCTURE** proposal around their decision criteria
3. **DIFFERENTIATE** from likely competition
4. **DEMONSTRATE** proof of capability
5. **PRICE** strategically based on competition and value

---

## Output Deliverable

Complete winning proposal:
- Executive summary hitting key decision factors
- Approach section demonstrating understanding
- Proof elements and case studies
- Pricing with value justification
- Follow-up strategy post-submission
