## Execution

1. **Audio-first design**: What would this be if purely audio?

3. **Visual supplement**: What visuals support (not replace) the audio?

4. **Replayability engineering**: How does this compound with repeated consumption?

## Output

- Audio strategy (voice, music, pacing)
- Emotional sound design
- Visual support layer
- Replayability mechanics
- Platform-specific adaptations

## Key Principle

"The ears never turn off. Design for the sense that cannot be avoided."
