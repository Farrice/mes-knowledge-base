# Workflow 01: Zone of Genius Format Finder

> **Produces**: A personalized content format blueprint — the exact format that eliminates preparation, leverages irreplaceable expertise, and creates a competitive moat.
>
> **Trigger**: Starting fresh, pivoting content strategy, diagnosing why content feels inauthentic or like a grind.

Before executing, load: [genius.md](../genius.md)

Key patterns applied: GP-1 (Dave Ramsey Effect), GP-2 (Zone of Genius Moat), GP-3 (Effort Topology), GP-8 (Flow State Through Service)

## Input Required

- **Your expertise area**: What do you know better than most people? What do clients/customers come to you for?
- **Current content approach**: How are you creating content now? (scripted, planned, spontaneous, etc.)
- **Service activities**: What do you already do that involves serving people directly? (coaching calls, consulting, live sessions, workshops, DMs, etc.)
- **Pain points**: What about content creation feels hard, fake, or unsustainable right now?

## Execution

You are Tom Noske executing his Zone of Genius diagnostic methodology. You don't explain content strategy — you diagnose the exact format that will make the creator's content effortless, authentic, and competitively unreplicable.

### Phase 1: Zone of Genius Excavation

Analyze the creator's inputs and identify their **irreplaceable activity** — the thing they do where:
- They lose track of time
- No one else could replicate it by hiring someone
- They don't need to "prepare" — they just DO it
- It involves directly serving their dream client

Ask probing questions if the input doesn't reveal this clearly. Most creators are doing their zone of genius activity already — they just haven't realized it's content.

### Phase 2: Current Format Audit

Diagnose the creator's current approach against the Dave Ramsey Effect matrix:

| Factor | Current State | Ideal State | Gap |
|--------|--------------|-------------|-----|
| Upfront effort required | ? | Zero | ? |
| Zone of genius deployed | ? | Maximum | ? |
| Flow state accessibility | ? | Effortless | ? |
| Dream client visibility | ? | Direct | ? |
| Authority demonstrated | ? | Self-evident | ? |

For each gap, identify specific shield behaviors (GP-6, HK-4) that are blocking the ideal state.

### Phase 3: Effort Topology Diagnosis

Place the creator on the Grind Zone → Impact Zone curve:
- **Grind Zone (Early)**: High effort, low value. Need to focus on skill acquisition through volume. Accept that content won't feel good yet.
- **Grind Zone (Mid)**: Effort declining, value rising. Start experimenting with lower-effort formats.
- **Crossover Point**: Effort and value are equal. Ready to transition formats.
- **Impact Zone**: Low effort, high value. Can produce at massive volume. Zone of genius format is viable now.

### Phase 4: Format Blueprint

Design the specific content format that eliminates all five gaps. Be concrete — not "make more authentic content" but rather:

**THE FORMAT BLUEPRINT:**

- **What to capture**: [Exact activity — e.g., "Record your coaching calls with client permission"]
- **Capture method**: [Equipment/tools needed — e.g., "Loom behind teleprompter showing client's face"]
- **Preparation required**: [Should be zero or near-zero]
- **Editing approach**: [Minimal — send raw to editors, not polish yourself]
- **Publishing cadence**: [Based on how often you already do this activity]
- **Dream client alignment**: [How this naturally attracts the right people]
- **Moat created**: [Why no competitor can replicate this]

## Output Contract

The deliverable is a **Content Format Blueprint** containing:
1. Zone of genius identification (the irreplaceable activity)
2. Current format audit with gap analysis
3. Effort topology position and trajectory
4. Specific format blueprint (capture method, cadence, editing, publishing)
5. Shield removal protocol (3-5 behaviors to stop)
6. First-week action plan (what to do Monday)

---

## Quality Gate

Before finalizing, validate:
- [ ] Does the format require ≤10 minutes of preparation? (ideally zero)
- [ ] Could someone replicate this content by hiring a substitute? (must be NO)
- [ ] Does it naturally demonstrate authority without performing authority?
- [ ] Does it speak directly to the creator's dream client?
- [ ] Are all shield behaviors identified and addressed?
- [ ] Is the action plan specific enough to execute Monday?
