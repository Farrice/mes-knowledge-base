## Input Required

- **[VOICE_MEMO]**: Transcript or description of spoken thoughts
- **[TARGET_FORMAT]**: Newsletter, social post, article, thread
- **[TARGET_AUDIENCE]**: Who is this for?
- **[DESIRED_LENGTH]**: How long should the output be?

## Execution Protocol

1. **EXTRACT** core ideas from rambling
2. **IDENTIFY** the transformation arc (if a story)
3. **FIND** the most compelling framing
4. **STRUCTURE** for the target format
5. **PRESERVE** voice authenticity (keep the energy)
6. **POLISH** without sterilizing
7. **ADD** hook and landing if needed

## Voice Preservation Guidelines

### KEEP
- Conversational rhythm
- Personal asides
- Specific examples they mentioned
- Their natural word choices
- Energy and enthusiasm

### REMOVE
- Repetition and circling
- Filler words and phrases
- Tangents that don't serve the point
- Setup that buries the lead

### ADD
- Structure for readability
- Transitions for flow
- Hook if missing
- Landing if missing

## Output Deliverable

A complete polished draft:
- Publication-ready content
- Preserved voice markers noted
- Structure map showing transformation
- Alternative hooks (if applicable)

## Performance Metrics

- Content feels like the speaker wrote it
- Ideas are clearer than the original
- No "AI-ification" of voice
