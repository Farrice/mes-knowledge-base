# Nick Saraev: Bottleneck Thinking

> Every business is a pipeline. Output = speed at the narrowest point. Find it. Widen it. Repeat.

Nick Saraev applies fluid dynamics and Goldratt's Theory of Constraints to business growth. His core insight: the only work that matters is identifying and widening your bottleneck. Everything else is a strategic error. He scaled an automation agency to $72K/mo using this flywheel, compressing years-long growth cycles into months through faster feedback loops.
