# Luke Iha — Million-Dollar Mechanisms

The unique mechanism engineering system. How to find, name, validate, and sell the "reason it works" that makes every other element of copy more believable.

## What This Skill Does

A promise without a mechanism is just another claim. This skill turns products into *discoveries* by engineering the "why behind the what" — the mechanism that transforms "lose 20 pounds" into "lose 20 pounds by neutralizing the cortisol switch in your gut."

## Core Capabilities

1. **UMP vs UMS Strategic Selection** — Choose problem mechanism or solution mechanism based on market sophistication
2. **SIN Framework** — Simple, Intuitive, New filter for mechanism viability
3. **Universal Mechanism Matrix** — 3×4 brainstorming grid for systematic mechanism generation
4. **Characterization (Naming)** — The name IS the mechanism in the reader's mind
5. **Little Big Idea Discovery** — Product truth × audience desire intersection
6. **Mechanism Validation Triangle** — Story + hook + visual metaphor quality gate
7. **Pain Matrix Integration** — 10-dimension pain mapping with causal pain as entry point

## When to Use

- Before writing ANY copy that needs a "reason to believe"
- When an offer's promise feels generic or unbelievable
- When differentiating in a crowded market
- When developing a new product angle or repositioning
