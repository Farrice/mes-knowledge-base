### HOOK FORMULAS FOR SAVE-WORTHY CONTENT

**Formula 1: The Checklist Hook**
"[X] things to [do/check/know] before you [high-stakes action]"
- Example: "7 things to check before you launch your business"

**Formula 2: The Framework Hook**
"The [name] framework: How to [desirable outcome] in [timeframe/steps]"
- Example: "The SCORE framework: How to close any sales call in 5 steps"

**Formula 3: The Ultimate Guide Hook**
"The complete guide to [topic] (save this)"
- Example: "The complete guide to pricing your services"

**Formula 4: The Mistake-Avoidance Hook**
"[X] mistakes that [negative outcome] (and what to do instead)"
- Example: "6 mistakes that kill your launch (and what to do instead)"

**Formula 5: The Template Hook**
"The exact [template/script/system] I use for [desirable outcome]"
- Example: "The exact email template that books me 10 calls/week"

---

### CAROUSEL STRUCTURE FOR SAVES

**Slide 1: HOOK**
- Use one of the formulas above
- Include visual "save this" cue (bookmark icon, save icon)
- Bold, clear premise

**Slides 2-8: VALUE DELIVERY**
- One clear point per slide
- Numbered or labeled for reference
- Visual hierarchy (headline, explanation, example)
- Dense but readable

**Final Slide: CTA + SUMMARY**
- "Save this for when you [relevant situation]"
- Recap the key points (1-2 sentences)
- Follow prompt

**Optimal Slide Count**: 7-10 slides
- Too few = not comprehensive enough to save
- Too many = overwhelming, won't finish

### FAILURE MODES TO AVOID

**Failure 1: Vague Value**
- Bad: "Some thoughts on pricing"
- Good: "7 principles that changed how I price"
- Fix: Be specific and comprehensive

**Failure 2: Too Dense**
- Bad: Walls of text per slide
- Good: One clear point per slide
- Fix: Split content across more slides

**Failure 3: No Clear Reference Value**
- Bad: Opinion-based content (interesting but not reference-worthy)
- Good: Framework/system content (worth coming back to)
- Fix: Structure content as reusable reference

**Failure 4: Forgetting the CTA**
- Bad: Ending without save prompt
- Good: Explicit "Save this for [situation]"
- Fix: Always include save CTA with context
