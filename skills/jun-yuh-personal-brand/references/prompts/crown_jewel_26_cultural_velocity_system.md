## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Trend Classification

**Trend Categories**:

| Type | Definition | Velocity | Content Window |
|------|------------|----------|----------------|
| **Breaking News** | Major events, unexpected happenings | Highest | 2-24 hours |
| **Viral Content** | Trending videos, memes, formats | High | 24-72 hours |
| **Cultural Conversations** | Ongoing debates, movements | Medium | 3-14 days |
| **Seasonal/Predictable** | Holidays, annual events | Lower | 1-4 weeks |
| **Platform Trends** | Audio trends, format trends | Medium | 3-10 days |

### Phase 2: Relevance Mapping

**The Authentic Connection Test**:
1. Does this topic genuinely relate to my expertise?
2. Can I add unique value to this conversation?
3. Would my audience expect me to comment on this?
4. Can I make this about THEM, not just the trend?

**Connection Types**:
- **Direct**: Topic is in your wheelhouse (finance creator + economic news)
- **Adjacent**: Topic relates to your expertise tangentially (business creator + celebrity business failure)
- **Metaphor**: Topic illustrates a principle you teach (any creator + cultural moment as case study)
- **Contrarian**: Your perspective challenges the mainstream narrative

### Phase 3: Content Angle Development

**Angle Formulas**:

1. **The Hot Take**: "Here's what everyone's missing about [trend]"
2. **The Explainer**: "Why [trend] is happening, explained simply"
3. **The Lesson**: "What [trend] teaches us about [your expertise]"
4. **The Personal**: "My experience with [trend] / What I'm doing about [trend]"
5. **The Prediction**: "What happens next with [trend]"
6. **The Contrarian**: "Unpopular opinion about [trend]"
7. **The How-To**: "How to [benefit] from [trend]"
8. **The Story**: "[Trend] reminds me of when I [personal story]"

### Phase 4: Velocity Optimization

**Speed-to-Post Factors**:
- Breaking news: Hours matter, not perfection
- Viral content: Days matter, quality helps
- Cultural conversations: Quality beats speed
- Seasonal: Preparation enables quality

**Rapid Creation Protocol**:
1. Identify angle in <5 minutes
2. Script/outline in <15 minutes
3. Create in <30 minutes
4. Post (don't perfectify)

### Phase 5: Content Package Production

Produce complete trend-aligned content:
- Hook optimized for trend discovery
- Content body connecting trend to value
- CTA leveraging trend interest
- Hashtag/keyword strategy for discoverability
- Cross-platform adaptation notes
