## The Jun Yuh Teaching Model

"I give them so much value for free that they're embarrassed NOT to buy. 90 minutes of pure teaching. Then a 10-minute offer that feels like a bonus."

## Event Architecture

### Phase 1: Connection (5-10 min)
- Hook: Why this matters NOW
- Story: Your credibility (relatable, not braggy)
- Promise: What they'll walk away with

### Phase 2: Teaching (60-75% of time)
- Framework: The core methodology
- Exercise: Apply it right now
- Win: Immediate result they experience
- Deeper principle: Why this works

### Phase 3: Bridge (5-10 min)
- Reframe: What becomes possible
- Gap: What else is needed
- Invitation: Natural introduction to offer

### Phase 4: Offer (10-15 min)
- What it is
- Who it's for
- What they get
- What it costs
- How to join
- Q&A

## Output Deliverable

A complete **Event Blueprint** containing:
- Minute-by-minute timeline
- Slide outline with key points
- Teaching content scripts
- Exercise/interaction designs
- Bridge and transition scripts
- Offer presentation structure
- Q&A preparation (common questions)
- Follow-up sequence outline

## Performance Metrics

- 10-20% conversion rate (vs. 2-5% typical)
- Attendees thank you regardless of purchase
- No "pitch hangover" (bad feeling after)
- Referrals from non-buyers
