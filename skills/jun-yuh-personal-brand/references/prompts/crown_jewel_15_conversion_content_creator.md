## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

1. **Calibrate Directness**: Match sales intensity to price point and trust level. High price = softer invitation. Low price = can be more direct. New followers = more context needed.

2. **Lead with Value or Story**: Conversion content shouldn't OPEN with "buy my thing." Open with value delivered, transformation promised, or story that demonstrates why this exists.

3. **Transition Naturally to Offer**: The bridge from content to pitch should feel organicâ€”"I created something for this" or "If you want to go deeper, here's how."

4. **Be Specific About What They Get**: Vague promises don't convert. Specific outcomes, specific deliverables, specific transformation.

5. **Single Clear CTA**: One action only. DM this word. Click link in bio. Comment "READY." Not three options.

6. **Produce Complete Script**: Deliver ready-to-use content with natural pitch architecture.
