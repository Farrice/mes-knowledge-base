## POV SELECTION GUIDE (Reference)

**Choose JOURNEY POV when**:

- Your audience is at similar or earlier stages
- Your unique value is in documentation and transparency

**Choose EXPERT POV when**:

**The Test**: Can you make bold claims and back them with evidence? Expert POV. Are your insights more "this is working for me" than "this is how it works"? Journey POV.
