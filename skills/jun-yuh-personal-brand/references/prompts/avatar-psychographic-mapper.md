# Avatar Psychographic Deep Mapper

> Produce deep audience profiles that go beyond demographics into fears, desires, and internal dialogue.

## The Psychographic Layers

### LAYER 1: Surface (Demographics)
- Age, location, occupation, income
- Family status, education level
- What they DO (observable behavior)

### LAYER 2: Desires (What They Want)
- Stated goals and aspirations
- Success markers they chase
- How they want to be perceived

### LAYER 3: Fears (What Keeps Them Up)
- Failures they're afraid of
- Judgments they fear from others
- The scenario that haunts them

### LAYER 4: Internal Dialogue (What They Tell Themselves)
- Private doubts (imposter syndrome)
- Secret hopes (unspoken desires)
- The question they can't answer

### LAYER 5: Content Consumption
- Who they follow (and why)
- What content they consume but don't share
- Where they lurk vs. participate

## Execution Protocol

1. **MAP** demographics (the foundation)
2. **EXTRACT** desires (stated + unstated)
3. **IDENTIFY** fears (surface + deep)
4. **WRITE** internal dialogue (their actual words)
5. **RESEARCH** content consumption patterns
6. **CREATE** the "Day in the Life" narrative
7. **DEVELOP** messaging implications (how to reach them)

## Output Deliverable

A complete **Avatar Profile** containing:
- Demographics summary
- Desires map (stated + secret)
- Fear inventory (surface + deep)
- Internal dialogue script (50+ phrases)
- Content consumption profile
- "Day in the Life" narrative
- Messaging framework (how to speak to them)
- Content triggers (what makes them engage)

## Example Usage

**Segment**: Freelancers who haven't broken $5K/month

**Internal Dialogue Extract**:
- "Am I actually good at this, or just lucky?"
- "Why can everyone else charge more?"
- "If I raise my rates, I'll lose all my clients"
- "Maybe I'm not cut out for this"
- "I work harder than employed people and make less"
- "At what point do I just go back to a job?"

**Fear Deep Layer**:
"What if I tell everyone I'm a freelancer and then have to admit I failed and went back to corporate?"

**Messaging Implication**:
Don't promise more money. Promise permission to charge what they're worth without fear.
