> Produce authentic "walls down" content that builds deep connection without requiring transformation arcs.

## Nurture Content vs. Attract Content

| Nurture | Attract |
|---------|---------|
| Current struggle | Completed transformation |
| Process, not result | Result, not process |
| Vulnerability | Authority |
| "I'm figuring it out" | "Here's what I learned" |
| Builds connection | Builds audience |

## Nurture Content Types

### 1. THE CURRENT STRUGGLE
Share what you're wrestling with NOW (no resolution needed)

### 2. THE BEHIND-THE-SCENES
Show the messy reality behind the polished content

### 3. THE HONEST ADMISSION
Acknowledge something you got wrong or are still learning

### 4. THE PERSONAL MOMENT
Share something unrelated to business (human connection)

### 5. THE VALUES STAND
Express a belief, even if unpopular

## Execution Protocol

1. **IDENTIFY** authentic material (what's real right now)
2. **CALIBRATE** vulnerability depth (appropriate for platform)
3. **WRITE** script without hook mechanics (conversational)
4. **CHECK** for over-polishing (keep the rough edges)
5. **CONNECT** to brand without being salesy
6. **ADD** invitation for connection (not conversion)

## Output Deliverable

A **Nurture Content Script** containing:
- Complete script (text overlay or voiceover)
- Vulnerability calibration check
- Filming notes (casual, authentic feel)
- Caption that continues the conversation
- No CTA beyond "let me know if you relate"
