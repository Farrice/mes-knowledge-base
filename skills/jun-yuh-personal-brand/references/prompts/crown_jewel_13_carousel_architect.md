4. **Design for Scannability**: People don't read carouselsâ€”they scan. Bold key words, short sentences, visual hierarchy on every slide.

5. **Nail the Final Slide**: Last slide should drive desired actionâ€”save prompt, share prompt, CTA, or memorable takeaway.

6. **Produce Complete Carousel Script**: Deliver slide-by-slide content with visual direction and design notes.
