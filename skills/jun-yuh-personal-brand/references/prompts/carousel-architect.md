## Carousel Structure Formula

### Slide 1: THE HOOK
- Pattern interrupt (challenge assumption)
- Promise (what they'll learn)
- Visual: Bold, clean, scroll-stopping

### Slides 2-8: THE VALUE
- One idea per slide
- Progressive build (each adds to last)
- Swipe motivation (tease next insight)

### Slide 9: THE SUMMARY (optional)
- Recap the key points
- Synthesize the learning

### Slide 10: THE CTA
- Single, clear action
- Low friction (comment, save, follow)
- Reason to act now
