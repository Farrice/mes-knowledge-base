## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: System Architecture

**The 5 Pillars of a Creator Operating System**:

1. **IDEATION SYSTEM**: Where ideas come from, how they're captured
2. **CREATION SYSTEM**: How content gets made (batching, tools, workflows)
3. **PUBLISHING SYSTEM**: How content gets posted (scheduling, platforms)
4. **ENGAGEMENT SYSTEM**: How you interact with audience (time-boxed)
5. **ITERATION SYSTEM**: How you learn and improve (tracking, analysis)

Each pillar needs:
- Clear process
- Time allocation
- Tools/systems
- Failure prevention

### Phase 2: Time Budget Optimization

**Time Allocation by Pillar** (typical ratio):

| Pillar | % of Time | Example (10 hrs/week) |
|--------|-----------|----------------------|
| Ideation | 10% | 1 hour |
| Creation | 50% | 5 hours |
| Publishing | 10% | 1 hour |
| Engagement | 20% | 2 hours |
| Iteration | 10% | 1 hour |

**Key Insight**: Most creators over-allocate to creation and under-allocate to ideation and iteration. This creates random quality.

### Phase 3: Batching Architecture

**The Weekly Batch Model**:
- **Day 1**: Ideation + Scripting (all week's content)
- **Day 2**: Creation/Filming (all at once)
- **Day 3**: Editing + Scheduling
- **Days 4-7**: Posting (automated) + Engagement + Iteration

**Why This Works**:
- Context-switching kills creativity
- Batching creates flow state
- Separation allows quality control
- Pre-scheduling reduces daily decisions

### Phase 4: Process Standardization

**For Each Content Type, Define**:
- Exact workflow steps
- Time per step
- Tools needed
- Quality checklist
- Common mistakes to avoid

### Phase 5: Failure Prevention

**Build Systems For**:
- Idea drought (emergency idea bank)
- Missed days (buffer content)
- Low energy periods (minimum viable content)
- Life interruptions (pause protocols)

### PILLAR 2: CREATION SYSTEM

**Sunday Batch Session (2 hours)**

**Setup (5 min)**:
- Natural light (face window) or lamp on
- Phone propped at eye level
- AirPods in
- Water nearby
- "Do Not Disturb" on

**Scripting (30 min)**:
For each of 5 pieces, write:
- Hook (first 2 sentences)
- 3 key points
- Closing line

**DON'T write word-for-word script**. Bullet points = more natural delivery.

**Filming (45 min)**:
- Film ALL 5 talking heads back-to-back
- 2-3 takes max per video
- Don't review between takes (wastes time)
- If you stumble badly, just restart that section
- Energy 20% higher than feels natural

**Editing (45 min)**:
- CapCut on phone (free, fast)
- Per video (9 min each):
  - Trim start/end (1 min)
  - Cut mistakes (2 min)
  - Add captions (auto-generate) (3 min)
  - Add hook text if needed (2 min)
  - Export (1 min)

**Output**: 5 ready-to-post pieces in 2 hours
