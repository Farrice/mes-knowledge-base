## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

**Universal Algorithm Truths** (all platforms):

1. **Retention is King**: How long do people stay? Watch time, scroll stop, session time.
2. **Engagement Velocity**: How fast do early viewers engage? First-hour performance.
3. **Share Signal**: Does content get shared? Strongest distribution signal.
4. **Save/Bookmark Signal**: Does content get saved? Indicates high value.
5. **Completion Rate**: Do people finish? Indicates quality.
6. **Negative Signals**: Do people scroll past quickly? Skip? Report? Hide?

**Platform-Specific Weights**:
- Different platforms weight these signals differently
- Understanding the weights = understanding the algorithm

### Phase 2: Distribution Mechanics

**The Distribution Funnel**:
```
CONTENT POSTED
    ↓
INITIAL TEST (small audience sample)
    ↓
EARLY SIGNALS (engagement velocity, retention)
    ↓
EXPAND OR SUPPRESS (algorithm decision)
    ↓
WIDER DISTRIBUTION (if signals positive)
    ↓
VIRAL POOL (if signals exceptional)
```

**Key Decision Points**:
- Test audience: First 100-500 viewers (followers or explore sample)
- Decision threshold: Usually within 1-2 hours
- Expansion waves: Progressively larger audiences if signals hold

### HOW INSTAGRAM'S ALGORITHM ACTUALLY WORKS

**The Core Truth**: Instagram's algorithm optimizes for **session time**. It wants users to stay on the app longer. Content that keeps people watching and scrolling gets distributed. Content that makes people leave gets suppressed.

**The Distribution Mechanics**:

```
YOU POST A REEL
       ↓
PHASE 1: Shown to 5-10% of your followers
       ↓
SIGNAL CHECK: Watch time, likes, comments, shares, saves
       ↓
IF POSITIVE: Released to "Suggested Reels" (Explore/Discover)
       ↓
PHASE 2: Tested on small non-follower sample
       ↓
SIGNAL CHECK: Same metrics, compared to similar content
       ↓
IF POSITIVE: Expanded to larger audience
       ↓
CONTINUE: Until signals drop below threshold
```

**Key Insight**: Your followers are the TEST AUDIENCE. Their response determines if non-followers ever see your content.

---

### SIGNAL HIERARCHY (What Instagram Measures)

**Tier 1 - Primary Signals (70% of algorithm weight)**:

| Signal | What It Measures | How to Optimize |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Watch Time** | Do people watch most of the video? | Strong hook, good pacing, appropriate length |
| **Rewatch Rate** | Do people watch again? | Value density, satisfying content |
| **Completion Rate** | Do people finish? | Optimal length, no dead spots |

**Tier 2 - Engagement Signals (20% of weight)**:

| Signal | What It Measures | How to Optimize |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Shares** | Is this worth sharing? | Relatable, valuable, or entertaining |
| **Saves** | Is this worth saving? | Useful, educational, reference-worthy |
| **Comments** | Does this spark response? | Opinion-provoking, question-asking |
| **Likes** | General approval | Good content (least weighted) |

**Tier 3 - Supporting Signals (10% of weight)**:

| Signal | What It Measures | How to Optimize |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Profile Visits** | Curiosity about creator | Strong branding, curiosity hooks |
| **Follow from Reel** | Audience-building power | Clear value proposition |
| **Engagement Velocity** | How fast do interactions come? | Engaged existing audience |

---

### DISTRIBUTION TRIGGERS (What Gets Content Pushed)

**Trigger 1: High First-Second Retention**
- If people watch past 3 seconds, algorithm sees "good hook"
- Optimize: Open with pattern interrupt, curiosity, or immediate value
- **Target**: 70%+ retention at 3-second mark

**Trigger 2: High Completion Rate**
- If people finish, algorithm sees "quality content"
- Optimize: Right length for content (don't pad, don't rush)
- **Target**: 50%+ watch full video

**Trigger 3: Shares (Strongest Signal)**
- Shares = "this is worth spreading"
- Optimize: Create "I need to send this to someone" moments
- Types that get shared: Relatable, useful, entertaining, validating

**Trigger 4: Saves**
- Saves = "I'll want this later"
- Optimize: Tutorials, lists, frameworks, templates
- Explicitly ask: "Save this for later"

**Trigger 5: Comments (Especially Quick Comments)**
- Fast comments = "this sparked something"
- Optimize: End with question, opinion request, or controversial take

---

### DISTRIBUTION KILLERS (What Suppresses Reach)

**Killer 1: Quick Scroll-Past**
- If people scroll past in <2 seconds = strong negative signal
- Cause: Weak hook, unclear premise, low energy
- Fix: Obsess over first 2 seconds

**Killer 2: Drop-Off Before 50%**
- Signals content quality issue
- Cause: Too long, boring middle, poor pacing
- Fix: Tighter editing, remove dead space

**Killer 3: Low Engagement Rate**
- Few likes/comments relative to views
- Cause: Content is "meh," not strongly positive or negative
- Fix: Take stronger positions, create reaction

**Killer 4: "Not Interested" / Hide / Unfollow**
- Explicit negative feedback = heavy suppression
- Cause: Wrong audience, offensive content, spam signals
- Fix: Stay on-topic, serve your audience

**Killer 5: Engagement Pod / Fake Engagement**
- Algorithm detects unnatural patterns
- Cause: Groups inflating engagement artificially
- Fix: Don't use pods, grow organically

### OPTIMAL CONTENT ENGINEERING

**Ideal Reel Structure**:
```
[0-1 sec]: HOOK - Stop the scroll (visual + text)
[1-3 sec]: PROMISE - What will they learn/feel?
[3-30 sec]: DELIVER - The core content
[Last 5 sec]: CLOSE - CTA or memorable ending
```

**Optimal Length by Content Type**:

| Content Type | Optimal Length | Why |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| Quick tips | 15-30 sec | Dense value, high completion |
| Educational | 45-60 sec | Enough depth, still finishable |
| Storytelling | 60-90 sec | Need arc, but don't drag |
| Transformation | 45-60 sec | Emotional journey compressed |

**Key Insight**: Shorter is usually better. A 30-second video with 70% completion beats a 90-second video with 30% completion.

**Format Preferences (Current)**:
- Talking head: Still strong, especially with text overlays
- B-roll stories: Performing well for transformation content
- Carousel: Excellent for saves and educational content
- Static images with text: Lower reach than video

### TIMING OPTIMIZATION

**When to Post (General)**:
- Weekdays: 7-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm (your audience's timezone)
- Weekends: 10am-2pm
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday typically highest engagement

**The Real Truth**: Timing matters less than content quality. A great post at a bad time beats a mediocre post at a perfect time.

**Consistency > Timing**: Posting daily at random times beats posting twice weekly at "optimal" times.

### COMMON MYTHS DEBUNKED

**Myth 1: "The algorithm hates me"**
Reality: The algorithm is neutral. If content underperforms, it's because early signals were weak.

**Myth 2: "Hashtags are crucial"**
Reality: Hashtags have minimal impact. Focus on content quality first.

**Myth 3: "Posting time is everything"**
Reality: Timing is ~5-10% of the equation. Hook and content are 80%+.

**Myth 4: "You need to respond to comments immediately"**
Reality: Responding helps, but quality of content matters more than response speed.

**Myth 5: "The algorithm suppresses links"**
Reality: Feed posts with links get slightly less reach. Stories and bio links are fine.
