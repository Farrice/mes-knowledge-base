## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Buyer Awareness Stage Mapping

**Eugene Schwartz's 5 Awareness Levels + Jun's 4 Missions**:

| Awareness Level | Buyer State | Primary Mission | Content Goal |
|-----------------|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Unaware** | Don't know they have a problem | ATTRACT | Create problem awareness |
| **Problem Aware** | Know the problem, not solutions | ATTRACT + NURTURE | Validate pain, build connection |
| **Solution Aware** | Know solutions exist, not yours | POSITION | Establish your methodology |
| **Product Aware** | Know your offer, evaluating | POSITION + CONVERT | Overcome objections, build urgency |
| **Most Aware** | Ready to buy, need final push | CONVERT | Remove friction, invite action |

### Phase 2: Mission-Specific Content Architecture

**ATTRACT Content (Top of Funnel)**
- **Purpose**: Reach new audiences, create awareness, drive discovery
- **Psychology**: Pattern interrupt, curiosity, relatability, "this is exactly my problem"
- **Formats**: Viral formats, broad hooks, story-led content, relatable struggles
- **CTA**: Follow, save, share (NOT buy)
- **Metrics**: Reach, new followers, shares

**NURTURE Content (Relationship Building)**
- **Purpose**: Build trust, show humanity, create emotional connection
- **Psychology**: Vulnerability, relatability, "they're just like me"
- **Formats**: Behind-the-scenes, current struggles, personal stories, values
- **CTA**: Engage, comment, reply (conversation starters)
- **Metrics**: Comments, DMs, reply rate

**POSITION Content (Authority Building)**
- **Purpose**: Establish expertise, differentiate from alternatives, build credibility
- **Psychology**: "They know what they're talking about," social proof, methodology
- **Formats**: Expert tips, frameworks, client results, contrarian takes, how-to
- **CTA**: Save, share with someone who needs this
- **Metrics**: Saves, profile visits, qualified DMs

**CONVERT Content (Action Driving)**
- **Purpose**: Move ready buyers to action, overcome final objections
- **Psychology**: Urgency, clarity, permission, "this is for me"
- **Formats**: Offer announcements, testimonials, objection handling, deadlines
- **CTA**: DM word, click link, apply, buy
- **Metrics**: Link clicks, DMs, applications, sales

### Phase 3: Content Ratio Optimization

**Jun's 3-1-2-1 Formula Applied**:
- 3 ATTRACT (reach, growth)
- 1 NURTURE (connection)
- 2 POSITION (authority)
- 1 CONVERT (sales)

**Total**: 7 pieces per week

**Ratio Adjustments by Business Stage**:
| Stage | Attract | Nurture | Position | Convert |
|-------|---------|---------|----------|---------|
| Building audience | 4 | 1 | 2 | 0 |
| Growing + monetizing | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 |
| Established + launching | 2 | 1 | 2 | 2 |
| Evergreen funnel | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 |

### Phase 4: Platform-Specific Deployment

Different platforms favor different missions:
- **Instagram/TikTok**: Strong for Attract + Nurture
- **LinkedIn**: Strong for Position + Convert
- **YouTube**: Strong for Position (long-form authority)
- **Email**: Strong for Nurture + Convert
- **Podcast**: Strong for Position + Nurture

### MISSION BREAKDOWN: Your Specific Content

#### ATTRACT CONTENT (3/week Instagram)
**Purpose**: Reach corporate professionals scrolling, create "that's me" moment

**Content Ideas**:
1. "The Sunday Scaries aren't about Monday. They're about the next 30 years of Mondays."
   - Format: Silent film transformation or talking head
   - Hook type: Pain amplification + time projection
   - Audience: Unaware/Problem aware

2. "I used to write 50+ emails a day at my corporate job. Now I get paid $500 to write one."
   - Format: Talking head, story-led
   - Hook type: Contrast + specific number
   - Audience: Problem aware

3. "What most people don't realize about 'stable' jobs (from someone who left one)"
   - Format: Carousel or talking head
   - Hook type: Pattern interrupt + authority
   - Audience: Problem aware

4. "The hidden skill every corporate employee has that's worth $100/hour"
   - Format: Educational talking head
   - Hook type: Curiosity + specific value
   - Audience: Problem → Solution aware

5. "POV: You check your email and see a $2,000 payment for 8 hours of work"
   - Format: Silent film transformation
   - Hook type: Desire projection
   - Audience: Solution aware

**CTA Strategy**: "Follow for more" or "Save this"—NO selling yet

---

#### NURTURE CONTENT (1/week Instagram)
**Purpose**: Show humanity, build emotional connection, bring walls down

**Content Ideas**:
1. "I still have imposter syndrome. Here's what I don't tell you on the 'success' posts."
   - Format: Talking head, vulnerable tone
   - No resolution required—just honesty
   - CTA: "Anyone else?" (conversation starter)

2. "The scariest part of leaving my job wasn't the money. It was this..."
   - Format: Talking head or text post
   - Share the EMOTIONAL journey, not just tactical
   - CTA: "What's holding you back?" (engagement)

3. "Today I got rejected by a client I really wanted. Sharing because I want you to see the real version."
   - Format: Story-style or talking head
   - Current struggle, not past
   - CTA: "How do you handle rejection?" (community)

4. "A reminder that I had 0 clients 3 years ago. The comparison game is lying to you."
   - Format: Carousel with timeline or talking head
   - Encouragement through transparency
   - CTA: "Screenshot this for hard days"

**Key Principle**: NO LESSON REQUIRED. Nurture content connects, not teaches.

---

#### POSITION CONTENT (2/week Instagram)
**Purpose**: Establish expertise, differentiate methodology, build "they know their stuff" feeling

**Content Ideas**:
1. "The 5 types of freelance writing (and which one pays best for your background)"
   - Format: Carousel with breakdown
   - Expert POV—you've seen the landscape
   - CTA: "Save this" + "Which type fits you?"

2. "I've made $500K+ from freelance writing. Here are the 3 things that actually mattered."
   - Format: Talking head, authority tone
   - Results-backed credibility
   - CTA: "Which one surprises you?"

3. "Why most freelance writing advice is wrong for corporate escapees"
   - Format: Talking head, contrarian
   - Differentiation from other teachers
   - CTA: "Agree or disagree?"

4. "Client testimonial: [Student] went from $0 to $4K/month in 90 days. Here's what she did."
   - Format: Carousel with her story + quotes
   - Social proof, methodology validation
   - CTA: "DM me 'STORY' for her full breakdown"

5. "The exact email template that's landed me $50K+ in clients"
   - Format: Carousel with template
   - High-value free content that positions expertise
   - CTA: "Save + try this week"

**Key Principle**: Position content should make them think "I want to learn from THIS person specifically."

---

#### CONVERT CONTENT (1/week Instagram)
**Purpose**: Move ready buyers to action, clear path to purchase

**Content Ideas**:
1. "If you've been watching for a while and wondering 'is this for me?'—let me answer that."
   - Format: Talking head, direct address
   - Objection handling woven in
   - CTA: "DM me 'READY' if you want to talk"

2. "My free workshop is happening [date]. Here's exactly what I'm teaching (and who should attend)."
   - Format: Talking head or carousel
   - Clear value promise, qualification
   - CTA: "Link in bio to save your spot"

3. "What [Student] said after going from corporate to $8K months in 6 months"
   - Format: Testimonial feature
   - Proof of transformation
   - CTA: "DM 'HOW' if you want the same"

4. "I'm opening enrollment next week. Here's what to expect (no pressure, just info)."
   - Format: Talking head, soft sell
   - Expectation setting, objection preemption
   - CTA: "Get on the waitlist, link in bio"

**Key Principle**: Convert content only works because ATTRACT + NURTURE + POSITION came first. Never lead with this.

### GAP ANALYSIS: Your Current Content

**Current State**: "Mostly educational tips, very little personal content, almost no direct selling"

**Diagnosis**:
- Over-indexed on POSITION (educational = authority building)
- Under-indexed on NURTURE (personal content = connection)
- Under-indexed on CONVERT (no selling = no sales)
- ATTRACT unclear (may be too niche, not wide enough)

**Prescription**:
1. Immediately add 1 NURTURE post per week (vulnerable, personal)
2. Add 1 CONVERT post per week (soft sell, permission-based)
3. Widen ATTRACT hooks (speak to emotion, not just tactics)
4. Trust the system—CONVERT works when other missions have built trust
