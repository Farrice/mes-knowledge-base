## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Structural Deconstruction

**Hook Architecture Analysis** (0-3 seconds):
- Visual hook: What appears first? Why does it stop scrolling?
- Text hook: First words/line—what psychological trigger?
- Audio hook: What sound/music establishes tone?
- Combined effect: How do these three work together?

**Story Arc Mapping**:
- Divide content into phases (beginning, middle, end)
- Identify emotional trajectory (where do feelings peak?)
- Map tension/release pattern
- Note pacing rhythm (scene lengths, transitions)

**Technical Measurement**:
- Total duration
- Number of scenes/cuts
- Text words per frame
- Text display duration
- Music timing (builds, drops)
- Visual style (static, dynamic, B-roll types)

### Phase 2: Psychological Trigger Identification

**The 12 Viral Psychology Triggers**:

1. **Curiosity Gap**: Information asymmetry that demands resolution
2. **Pattern Interrupt**: Violated expectations create attention
3. **Identity Resonance**: "This is exactly me" recognition
4. **Social Proof**: Others validate the message
5. **Emotional Intensity**: Strong feelings = strong sharing
6. **Novelty**: New information or perspective
7. **Utility Value**: "I need to save this"
8. **Tribal Signaling**: Share to show who I am
9. **Controversy**: Polarization drives engagement
10. **Aspiration**: This is who I want to become
11. **Nostalgia**: Emotional connection to past
12. **Relatability**: Shared experience = connection

### Phase 3: Format Formula Extraction

**Isolate the Replicable Structure**:
- What can be copied exactly?
- What needs to be adapted for different content?
- What's specific to this creator vs. universally applicable?

**Create the Template**:
```
[SECOND 0-X]: Hook type + visual + text structure
[SECOND X-Y]: Setup/context structure
[SECOND Y-Z]: Core content delivery method
[SECOND Z-END]: Resolution + CTA structure
```

### Phase 4: Niche Adaptation Blueprint

**Transfer Protocol**:
1. Keep: Format, structure, pacing, emotional arc
2. Change: Topic, specific details, your voice
3. Enhance: What could be improved for your audience?

## EXAMPLE OUTPUT 2: Rapid Format Analysis (Talking Head Hook Pattern)

**Content Description**: "Creator opens mid-sentence: '—and that's exactly why your morning routine isn't working.' Pauses, says 'Let me explain.' Gives 3 reasons in rapid succession with text overlays. Ends with 'Do this instead' and one specific tip."

---

**QUICK DECONSTRUCTION**:

**Format Name**: Mid-Sentence Authority Hook

**Hook Mechanism**: 
- Starts mid-thought (implies you arrived at important part)
- Challenges current behavior ("YOUR routine isn't working")
- Creates curiosity gap (why isn't it working?)

**Structure**:
```
[0-3 sec]: Mid-sentence hook + challenge
[3-5 sec]: "Let me explain" reset
[5-30 sec]: 3 reasons with text overlays
[30-40 sec]: "Do this instead" + single tip
[40-45 sec]: Quick close
```

**Replication Formula**:
"—and that's [exactly why/the real reason] [your X isn't working/you're stuck at Y]"

Pause. "Let me explain."

Deliver 3 points with text overlay reinforcement.

"Do this instead:" + one actionable tip.

**Adaptation Examples**:
- Business: "—and that's exactly why your content isn't getting reach"
- Health: "—and that's why you're tired by 2pm every day"
- Finance: "—and that's why your budget never works"
