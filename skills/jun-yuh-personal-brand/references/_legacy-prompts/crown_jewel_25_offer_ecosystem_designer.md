## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: Value Stack Architecture

**Jun's Value Stack Model**:

| Tier | Price Range | Purpose | Commitment Level |
|------|-------------|---------|------------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Lead generation, trust building | Email/follow |
| **Low Ticket** | $7-$197 | First purchase, segment buyers | Low risk trial |
| **Mid Ticket** | $497-$2,997 | Core transformation offer | Significant commitment |
| **High Ticket** | $3,000-$25,000+ | Premium, done-with-you/for-you | Full commitment |
| **Continuity** | $29-$497/month | Ongoing relationship, retention | Recurring |

**Design Principle**: Each tier should naturally lead to the next. Free → Low ticket is easiest. Low ticket buyer → Mid ticket is highest-leverage conversion.

### Phase 2: Offer-Market Fit Analysis

**For Each Tier, Answer**:
1. What specific transformation does this offer provide?
2. Who specifically is this for (within your audience)?
3. What format delivers this best (digital, live, 1:1)?
4. What's the time investment for you?
5. What's the profit margin?

### Phase 3: Content-to-Offer Pathway Design

**Map Content Missions to Offer Tiers**:

| Content Mission | Primary Offer Connection |
|-----------------|-------------------------|
| ATTRACT | → Free offers (lead magnets) |
| NURTURE | → Community, brand affinity |
| POSITION | → Mid/High ticket (authority builds) |
| CONVERT | → Direct offer promotion |

**Pathway Architecture**:
```
Content → Free → Email → Low Ticket → Mid Ticket → High Ticket
    ↓                                       ↓
    └─────────── Continuity ←──────────────┘
```

### Phase 4: Revenue Model Projection

**Calculate Per Tier**:
- Volume capacity (how many can you serve?)
- Conversion rates (realistic estimates)
- Revenue per tier
- Time investment per tier
- Profit margin per tier

### Phase 5: Implementation Roadmap

**Sequencing Matters**:
- Start with what you can deliver NOW
- Build complexity over time
- Don't launch everything at once
