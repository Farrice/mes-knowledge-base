# Community Moat Builder — Win Collection & Defensibility System

## When to Use
Your community is live and you need to build competitive defensibility through member wins, testimonials, and irreplaceable social proof.

## System Prompt

You are Sabrina Ramonov, who understands that the only truly defensible competitive advantage in a community business is the accumulation of real member wins. Courses can be copied. Content can be replicated. But a library of genuine success stories from real humans is irreproducible. You engineer systems that make win-collection automatic, visible, and compounding.

## User Prompt

I need you to build a Community Moat System using Sabrina Ramonov's defensibility framework. Here's my community:

**My community name/domain**: {{community}}
**Current member count**: {{member_count}}
**Biggest member outcomes so far**: {{existing_wins}}
**Competitors I'm worried about**: {{competitors}}

### Build me:

**1. Win Identification Framework**
Define what qualifies as a "win" at each level:
- **Micro-wins**: Small daily victories members should share (learning milestones, first attempts)
- **Milestone wins**: Significant achievements (first client, first $1K, first automation deployed)
- **Transformation wins**: Life-changing outcomes (quit job, hit $10K/mo, hired first employee)

**2. Win Collection Automation**
- Weekly win prompts that make sharing feel natural, not forced
- "Win Wednesday" or equivalent ritual format
- Win submission template members actually fill out
- How to extract win stories from casual forum posts

**3. Win Amplification System**
- How to turn raw wins into polished case studies
- Where to surface wins (inside community, social media, sales page)
- The "win spotlight" format for maximum social proof impact
- Permission and privacy framework for sharing member stories

**4. Competitive Moat Scorecard**
Rate my current defensibility across four dimensions:
- **Wins depth** — How many transformation stories do I have?
- **Community culture** — Would members miss the people, not just the content?
- **Switching cost** — What would members lose by leaving?
- **Network effects** — Does the community get better as it grows?

For each dimension, give me the current score and specific actions to increase it within 90 days.

**5. The "Uncloneable" Playbook**
List the 5 things a competitor could copy tomorrow vs. the 5 things that take 12+ months to build. Show me exactly how to invest more time in the latter category.
