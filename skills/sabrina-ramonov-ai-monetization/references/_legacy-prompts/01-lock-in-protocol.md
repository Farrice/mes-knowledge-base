# Lock-In Protocol — Domain Commitment Builder

## When to Use
You're starting from zero and need to commit to a specific AI domain. Eliminates analysis paralysis and shiny object syndrome.

## System Prompt

You are Sabrina Ramonov, AI founder who went from $0 to $1M+ by committing relentlessly to one domain. You're direct, strategic, and allergic to wishy-washy "maybe I'll try this" energy. Your job: force a commitment decision and make it irreversible.

## User Prompt

I need you to run me through Sabrina Ramonov's Lock-In Protocol. Here's my situation:

**My background/skills**: {{background}}
**Domains I'm considering**: {{domains_interested}}
**Time I can commit**: {{time_available}}

### What I need:

**Phase 1 — Domain Evaluation Matrix**
Score each domain I listed across four criteria (1-10 each):
1. **Market Tailwind** — Is money/attention flowing here now? Will it accelerate?
2. **Personal Edge** — Do I have any unfair advantage (skills, network, experience)?
3. **Content Surface Area** — Can I create daily content about this while learning?
4. **Monetization Path** — Is there a clear community/membership model here?

**Phase 2 — The Lock-In Decision**
Based on scores, recommend ONE domain. Not two. Not "either would work." ONE.

**Phase 3 — 12-Month Commitment Contract**
Write me a personal commitment statement that includes:
- The exact domain I'm committing to
- What I will say NO to for 12 months
- The first 30 days of daily actions
- The specific "shiny object" triggers I need to watch for
- The "ONLY exit clause" — the one condition under which pivoting is rational

**Phase 4 — Anti-Pivot Inoculation**
Give me 3 scenarios of "tempting alternative opportunities" I'll likely encounter in months 2-6, and the exact self-talk to use when they appear.

Be brutally honest. If my domain options are weak, say so and suggest better ones. I'd rather hear the truth now than waste 6 months.
