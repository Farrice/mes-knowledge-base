# ThriveCart Implementation Pathway

---

## 24-Hour Quickstart

**Hour 0-4**: Weekend Product Concept
- Identify your "obvious" expertise
- Draft transformation promise
- Define target customer specifically

**Hour 4-8**: Validation Check
- Research 3-5 competitors
- Confirm demand exists
- Identify your unique angle

**Hour 8-16**: MVP Creation
- Create minimum viable product
- Set up basic checkout
- Price at $17-47 entry point

**Hour 16-24**: Ship It
- Launch to any audience you have
- Document everything for iteration
- Commit publicly to 7 iterations

---

## 7-Day Sprint

**Day 1-2**: Product Shipped
- Complete Weekend Product
- Basic order bump added
- First sales or feedback collected

**Day 3-4**: Feedback Integration
- Collect iteration 1 feedback
- Document improvements needed
- Plan iteration 2

**Day 5-6**: Revenue Expansion
- Add upsell/downsell
- Implement improvements
- Launch iteration 2

**Day 7**: Three Levers Audit
- Diagnose primary bottleneck
- Create focused improvement plan
- Set 30-day targets

---

## 30-Day Transformation

**Week 1**: Get Going complete (iterations 1-2)
**Week 2**: Order bump + upsell live (iterations 3-4)
**Week 3**: Product ecosystem mapped (iterations 5-6)
**Week 4**: Iteration 7 + Three Levers optimization

**Day 30 Benchmark**:
- 7 iterations complete
- Order bump + upsell active
- Clear bottleneck identified
- Revenue per customer improving
