# Joanna Wiebe — Persuasion Mastery

Joanna Wiebe is the founder of Copyhackers and the architect of the **5-Level Persuasion Hierarchy** and the **5 Authority Craft Mechanics**. This skill covers the full stack of persuasive writing — from copy structure (how to build a persuasive piece) down to sentence-level execution (how to make each line land with weight and authority).

## Available Workflows

| # | Workflow | Produces | Use When |
|---|---------|----------|----------|
| persuasion | [Persuasion Audit & Identity Mining](workflows/persuasion-audit-and-identity-mining.md) | Copy Performance Audit & Identity Lexicon | You need to diagnose why current copy isn't converting and extract the specific language your audience uses to describe themselves. |
| behavioral | [Behavioral Friction & Bias Optimization](workflows/behavioral-friction-and-bias-optimization.md) | Frictionless Conversion Map | You have a draft that feels clunky or lacks psychological punch, and you need to remove cognitive load while adding behavioral triggers. |
| level | [Level 5 Narrative & Identity Engine](workflows/level-5-narrative-and-identity-engine.md) | Invisible Persuasion Campaign | You are writing high-stakes sales pages or emails where the reader must feel like the hero of their own story rather than a sales target. |
| authority | [Authority Craft Engine](workflows/authority-craft-engine.md) | Authority-Refined Writing | You have a draft that needs to SOUND authoritative — catchy phrasing, strategic revelation order, anti-hype, preemptive disqualification, and cognitive pacing at the sentence level. |
