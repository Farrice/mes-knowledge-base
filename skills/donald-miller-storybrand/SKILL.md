# Donald Miller — StoryBrand Mastery

The StoryBrand framework weaponizes the survival instinct of the human brain. The brain daydreams 30% of waking hours and only stops when it detects survival relevance. Story is the single format that can commandeer 100% of human attention. Every piece of business communication is a story invitation — the customer is the hero, the brand is the guide, and clarity beats cleverness at every level.

This skill deploys Miller's complete messaging operating system: the SB7 BrandScript, the 7-element AI copy formula, the one-liner, the Marketing Made Simple funnel, and the survival-brain engagement mechanism. It stacks with Harry Dry (concrete execution), Joanna Wiebe (persuasion hierarchy), Jeremy Miner (identity shift), Cardinal Mason (AI scale), and Made to Stick (stickiness).
