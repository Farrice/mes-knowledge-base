# Steven Pressfield — Narrative Mastery

Steven Pressfield is the author of *The War of Art*, *Gates of Fire*, *The Legend of Bagger Vance*, and *The Arcadian*. He's spent decades studying what great stories have in common — from Homer to Spielberg, from the Bible to Mean Girls. His core genius: stories aren't held together by formulas but by **gravitational forces** — the physics of how human psychology processes change, identity, sacrifice, and mystery. These forces are **domain-agnostic**: they work identically in a 3,000-year-old myth, a Hollywood screenplay, a sales page, a LinkedIn post, or a brand manifesto.

His second genius — equally famous — is the **War of Art**: the diagnostic framework for creative Resistance. Why talented people fail to produce. The Amateur vs. Professional identity shift. Turning Pro. Territory vs. Hierarchy. These principles apply to every creator, founder, and professional who knows what they should be doing but can't seem to do it.

This skill deploys Pressfield's narrative physics and Resistance warfare across 15 workflows covering narrative architecture, character design, sales, hooks, quality control, brand narrative, tension engineering, creative blocks, social media, email sequences, screenwriting, serialized content, manifestos, narrative repair, and cross-domain transfer.
