# Workflow 14 — Narrative Diagnostic & Rescue

> **What this is**: The "doctor" workflow. Bring in ANY content that "falls flat" — a blog post that doesn't engage, a sales page that doesn't convert, a brand story that feels generic, a video script that loses people — and get a specific structural diagnosis of which narrative physics principles are missing, broken, or misapplied. Different from Workflow 05 (Five Aims Quality Gate) because this diagnoses *structural narrative failures*, not quality polish.

---

## What This Produces

1. **Diagnostic Report** — Exact identification of which narrative physics are missing/broken
2. **Gravity Map** — Visual mapping of which forces are present, weak, and absent
3. **Prescription** — Specific structural repairs ranked by impact
4. **Before/After Demonstration** — Rewritten sections showing the fix in action
5. **Prevention Protocol** — What to do differently next time

---

## Input Required

- **The content** — Full text of the piece that's underperforming
- **Context** — What is this? (Post, email, sales page, script, brand story, pitch, etc.)
- **Symptom** — What's wrong? (No engagement, doesn't convert, feels flat, loses people, "something's off")
- **Intent** — What was this supposed to DO? (Sell, teach, inspire, persuade, entertain)
- **Audience** — Who was this for?

---

## Load Context

Read [genius.md](../genius.md) before execution. This workflow uses ALL 18 patterns + hidden knowledge as a diagnostic checklist. Key diagnostic lenses:
- **Structural Forces (GP1-4)** — Is the architecture sound?
- **Character Forces (GP5-10)** — Is there a protagonist? Do they transform?
- **Tension Forces (GP11-16)** — Is there real conflict? Does the villain own Act 2?
- **Mystery Forces (GP17-18)** — Does a question remain open?
- **Hidden Knowledge (HK1-6)** — Are the tacit principles violated?
- **Resistance Patterns (R1-R4)** — Was this content created from amateur or professional orientation?

---

## Execution

### Phase 1 — Symptom Assessment

Before touching the content, understand the presenting symptoms:

| Symptom | Likely Structural Cause |
|---------|----------------------|
| "Falls flat" / boring | Missing curse (GP5) or no transformation (GP6) |
| "Too long / loses people" | No inciting incident future-flash (GP2) or weak mystery threads (GP17) |
| "Doesn't convert" | No All Is Lost → Epiphany (GP13) or no sacrifice (GP7) |
| "Feels generic" | No genre spin (GP4) or no identity revelation (GP9) |
| "Something's off but I can't say what" | Stanislawski Triad unanswered (GP10) |
| "Reads like everyone else" | Hierarchy orientation, not territory (R4) |
| "Starts strong, fades" | Villain doesn't own Act 2 (GP12) |
| "Nice but forgettable" | No Michael Corleone Moment (GP11) — no irreversible commitment |
| "Checks all the boxes but doesn't move me" | Missing beauty (HK3) or spell-casting rhythm (HK4) |

### Phase 2 — Structural X-Ray

Go through the content with the full 18-pattern diagnostic checklist:

```
STRUCTURAL FORCES
[ ] GP1: Three-Act Gravitational Field — Hook → Escalation → Acceleration present?
[ ] GP2: Future-Flash — Does the opening let the audience glimpse the climax?
[ ] GP3: Start at the End — Was the ending designed first? Does everything serve it?
[ ] GP4: Genre + Spin — Is there a recognizable genre with a fresh twist?

CHARACTER / PROTAGONIST FORCES
[ ] GP5: The Curse — Is there a limitation that creates empathy?
[ ] GP6: Hero Changes — Is there genuine transformation (Point A → Point Z)?
[ ] GP7: Self-Sacrifice — Does transformation cost something real?
[ ] GP8: Extraordinary World Shift — Is there a threshold crossing?
[ ] GP9: Identity Revelation — Is there a "true self" moment?
[ ] GP10: Stanislawski Triad — Who am I? Why here? What do I want?

TENSION ARCHITECTURE
[ ] GP11: Michael Corleone Moment — Is there a point of no return?
[ ] GP12: Villain Owns Act 2 — Do obstacles dominate the middle?
[ ] GP13: All Is Lost → Epiphany — Maximum darkness → transcendent reframe?
[ ] GP14: Turn In Badge — Institutional rejection → conviction?
[ ] GP15: Quiet Solo Moment — Private vulnerability before action?
[ ] GP16: Hero at Mercy of Villain — Philosophical confrontation?

MYSTERY & MEANING
[ ] GP17: The Female/Mystery — Unsolvable question at the center?
[ ] GP18: The Child/Divine — Element of innocent clarity?
```

For each item, mark:
- ✅ **Present and strong**
- ⚠️ **Present but weak** — Exists but doesn't pull its weight
- ❌ **Missing** — Not deployed at all
- 🔄 **Misapplied** — Attempted but executed incorrectly

### Phase 3 — Hidden Knowledge Audit

Check the tacit violations:

- [ ] **HK1** — Is there a story behind the story, or does it resolve too neatly?
- [ ] **HK2** — Are stakes earned through specificity, or inflated through scale?
- [ ] **HK3** — Is it beautiful? (Not polished — genuinely beautiful in craft)
- [ ] **HK4** — Does the prose rhythm match the intended mood?
- [ ] **HK5** — Does it plug into gossip-pattern neural pathways?
- [ ] **HK6** — Is sacrifice real, or does it promise "you can have it all"?

### Phase 4 — Orientation Diagnosis

Sometimes the content is structurally correct but created from the wrong orientation:

- **Amateur markers**: Written for approval, avoids risk, sounds like everyone else, hedges convictions
- **Professional markers**: Written from territory, takes a position, has personal cost, sounds like no one else
- **Hierarchy orientation**: Optimized for algorithm/metrics, not for the work itself
- **Territory orientation**: Would exist even without an audience

If the orientation is wrong, no structural fix will save it. The content needs to be reconceived from territory.

### Phase 5 — Gravity Map

Visualize the forces:

```
NARRATIVE GRAVITY MAP — [Content Title]

                    STRONG          WEAK          MISSING
STRUCTURAL       │ ✅ GP1          │ ⚠️ GP2       │ ❌ GP3, GP4
CHARACTER        │ ✅ GP5, GP10    │              │ ❌ GP6, GP7, GP8, GP9
TENSION          │                │ ⚠️ GP12      │ ❌ GP11, GP13-16
MYSTERY          │                │ ⚠️ GP17      │ ❌ GP18
HIDDEN KNOWLEDGE │ ✅ HK2          │              │ ❌ HK1, HK3-6

DIAGNOSIS: This content has empathy (GP5) but no transformation (GP6),
tension set up but never resolved (GP12 weak), and no mystery architecture.
The audience connects initially but has no reason to stay.
```

### Phase 6 — Prescription (Ranked by Impact)

Rank the repairs by highest-impact-first:

```
PRESCRIPTION — [Content Title]

CRITICAL (Fix these first — they're load-bearing)
1. [Specific fix] — Add transformation arc (GP6): the protagonist is static.
   Currently: "We struggled, then we figured it out."
   Fix: Show what was GIVEN UP. What identity was shed. What can't be taken back.

2. [Specific fix] — Add mystery thread (GP17): the piece closes completely.
   Currently: Problem → Solution → Done.
   Fix: End by opening a bigger question than the one you answered.

HIGH IMPACT (Fix these next — they amplify everything)
3. [Specific fix]...

POLISH (Fix last — they improve but don't transform)
4-6. [Specific fixes]...
```

### Phase 7 — Before/After Demonstration

For the top 2-3 prescriptions, show the fix in action:

**BEFORE** (current text):
> [Exact excerpt from the broken content]

**AFTER** (with narrative physics applied):
> [Rewritten version demonstrating the fix]

**WHAT CHANGED**: [1-2 sentence explanation of which force was activated]

---

## Output Contract

Deliver:
1. ✅ Symptom Assessment (presenting problem mapped to structural causes)
2. ✅ Full 18-Pattern Diagnostic Checklist (present/weak/missing/misapplied)
3. ✅ Hidden Knowledge Audit
4. ✅ Orientation Diagnosis (amateur/professional, hierarchy/territory)
5. ✅ Gravity Map (visual force mapping)
6. ✅ Ranked Prescription (critical → high → polish)
7. ✅ Before/After Demonstrations (top fixes in action)
8. ✅ Prevention Protocol (what to do differently next time)

---

## Quality Gate

The diagnostic itself must pass the Five Aims:
- [ ] Is the diagnosis SPECIFIC to this content, not generic writing advice?
- [ ] Are the prescriptions ACTIONABLE, not abstract?
- [ ] Does the before/after demonstration PROVE the physics work?
- [ ] Would a different piece of content get a fundamentally different diagnosis?
- [ ] Is the report itself beautifully organized and clear?
