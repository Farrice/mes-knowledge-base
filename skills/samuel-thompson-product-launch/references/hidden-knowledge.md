# Samuel Thompson - Hidden Knowledge

10 tacit expertise points revealed—things he does unconsciously that separate masters from beginners.

## 1. The $289 Budget Is Strategic
He doesn't spend $289 because he's broke—he spends $289 because it forces radical prioritization. Every dollar must prove itself.

**Deploy**: Set artificial budget constraints even with more capital. Ask "Would I spend this if I only had $300 total?"

---

## 2. Divorce Attorneys Are Competition Signals
When attorneys dominate advertising in a niche, it signals: (1) audience has money, (2) pain is severe, (3) room for lower-cost alternatives.

**Deploy**: Search Facebook Ads Library. If professionals advertise heavily, there's a gap for info products at lower prices.

---

## 3. The "Lakers Game" Quality Bar
First version built while watching basketball. If you can't build it distracted, it's too complex for validation.

**Deploy**: Set 60-90 minute timer. Build whatever you can while doing something else. That's V1.

---

## 4. "It's Just Sugar" Philosophy
The hummingbird food revelation (just sugar) exposes: most products are commodities dressed in branding. The $30M Amazon seller proved market exists—your job is better marketing.

**Deploy**: Ask "What's the commodity version? Who's selling it poorly?" That's your opportunity.

---

## 5. 300-Books Commitment Test
Ordering 300 physical copies before proving demand. He treats physical product as marketing asset, not inventory liability.

**Deploy**: Order minimum physical inventory after first digital sales. Tangible product becomes content that increases perceived value.

---

## 6. Theme Templates As Conversion Insurance
Elixir and Solo Drop are "conversion optimized"—thousands of stores already A/B tested element placement. He's inheriting proven patterns.

**Deploy**: Never design from scratch. Find what's converting, buy template, change content.

---

## 7. Break-Even Funnel Is The Goal
Target isn't profit on first purchase—it's break even. Free customer acquisition means all profit comes from backend.

**Deploy**: Calculate acceptable CAC as front-end price minus product cost. If you acquire customers free, everything else is margin.

---

## 8. Fiverr As Single Leverage Point
Uses Fiverr for the 1-2 specialized tasks worth outsourcing while keeping everything else in-house with AI.

**Deploy**: Identify your single biggest skill gap. Outsource that one thing. Do everything else yourself.

---

## 9. Placeholder Social Proof
Starts with placeholder testimonials replaced with real ones. Realistic acknowledgment that conversion requires proof, proof requires sales—chicken-egg problem.

**Deploy**: Design testimonial sections from Day 1. Use AI placeholders representing realistic outcomes. Replace as real ones come in.

---

## 10. Seasonal Pricing = Perpetual Testing
"New Year Sale," "St. Patrick's Day Sale"—not gimmicks, but continuous price elasticity tests disguised as promotions.

**Deploy**: Create 12-month promotional calendar. Each "sale" is a price point test. Track which price-conversion combinations yield highest revenue per visitor.
