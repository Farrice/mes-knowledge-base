# Nicolas Cole Sentence Craft

Transform any writing into tighter, more powerful prose through systematic application of Nicolas Cole's sentence-level mastery techniques.

## Overview

Nicolas Cole is a professional writer with 10 books, who has trained tens of thousands of writers. His genius operates at the **atomic level of writing**—the sentence itself. While others focus on ideas and structure, Cole treats each sentence as an engineered unit with specific mechanical properties that can be optimized.

**Core philosophy**: The best version of any sentence is the shortest version that preserves full meaning, ends on a power word, and uses vocabulary calibrated to audience.

## Quick Start (24-Hour Implementation)

**Hour 0-4**: Apply Sentence Power Optimizer to 10 sentences. Learn terminal word placement.

**Hour 4-8**: Run Three-Pass Compression on one full piece. Target 30%+ reduction.

**Hour 8-12**: Use Redundancy Elimination Scanner on recent content. See how often you repeated yourself.

**Hour 12-24**: Apply Two-Comma Discipline and Adverb Eliminator as final polish. Notice the difference.
