## 1. The Dual-Sided Monetization Model
**What They Do**: They don't view "non-buyer" audience members as a waste, but as a captive customer base for a digital product.

**Executable Behavior**: Build a core high-ticket service for the top 1%. Build an affordable digital product (or community) for the 99% of unqualified leads attracted by your content.

**Deploy When**: Building an audience on social media where the majority of followers will never afford your premium B2B services.

**Success Metric**: Generating significant standalone revenue from a digital product while simultaneously closing enterprise clients from the exact same content.

## 2. Positioning Storytelling
**What They Do**: They filter all personal stories through a "Does this make me look like the best in the world?" lens. No victim stories.

**Executable Behavior**: Share stories about giving equity to your team, intense 12-hour work sprints for massive launches, and high-level problem-solving. Show you operate at an elite level.

**Deploy When**: Writing narrative content meant to build trust and authority (1-2x per week).

**Success Metric**: Potential clients read the story and think, "I want to work with a winner like this."

## 3. The "Hijack" Authority Post
**What They Do**: They don't wait for permission or client logos to demonstrate expertise. They audit massive brands publicly.

**Executable Behavior**: Reverse engineer a famous brand's funnel/emails (e.g., Gymshark, Whoop). Provide an in-depth, expert breakdown and offer your own insights and corrections.

**Deploy When**: Creating educational content to serve as a "public sales call."

**Success Metric**: Readers assume you work with top-tier brands and recognize your detailed expertise.

## 4. The Problem-Specific Lead Magnet
**What They Do**: They avoid massive "swipe files" or generic newsletters because they provide no transformation.

**Executable Behavior**: Create a lead magnet that answers: "If someone paid you $10,000 right now, what is the ONE immediate transformation you could give them?"

**Deploy When**: Creating a top-of-funnel asset to capture emails for a backend sales sequence.

**Success Metric**: High conversion rate from free lead magnet consumer to booked strategy call or digital product buyer.
