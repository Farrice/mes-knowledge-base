# Output Templates

## Consumer Posture Profile Template

```markdown
# [BRAND NAME] CONSUMER POSTURE PROFILE

## The Individual

[Vivid description of ONE specific person. Name them. Describe their existence with precise emotional texture. Include age only as a reference point that "means nothing"—what matters is who they ARE.]

## Occupation in Your World

[Their role in the brand ecosystem. What makes the brand world tick through their presence. The energy they bring. Use a defining phrase like "The [Role] Who [Characteristic]"]

## Activity in Your World

[Specific rituals and routines. How they spend time, money, attention. Identity-reinforcing behaviors. What you'd observe them doing. Include the ABSENCE of activity where relevant—what they don't do and why that matters.]

## Thought Process in Your World

[Internal logic and justification. What feels aligned. What feels inauthentic. Why this brand over alternatives. What they're refusing. Quote their internal monologue.]

## The Posture Synthesis

[How they "sit" in relation to your brand—their orientation, tension, presence. One paragraph that unifies the three dimensions into a coherent portrait of their relationship with the brand.]

## Strategic Implications

**Content strategy**: [Specific guidance]

**Brand voice**: [Specific guidance]

**Product decisions**: [Specific guidance]

**Experience design**: [Specific guidance]

**What NOT to do**: [Critical anti-patterns]

## The Prediction Test

1. **[Hypothetical brand decision 1]**: [Predicted response]

2. **[Hypothetical brand decision 2]**: [Predicted response]

3. **[Hypothetical brand decision 3]**: [Predicted response]

4. **[Hypothetical brand decision 4]**: [Predicted response]

5. **[Hypothetical brand decision 5]**: [Predicted response]
```

## Strategic Brief Template (Condensed Format)

For quick reference after the full profile exists:

```markdown
# [BRAND] Consumer Posture Brief

**The Individual**: [One sentence capturing essence]

**Their Posture**: [How they sit in relation to brand—one sentence]

**Core Need**: [What the brand provides that they can't get elsewhere]

**Refusals**: [Top 3 things they reject]

**Strategic Imperatives**:
1. [Do this]
2. [Do this]
3. [Never do this]
```

## Word Count Guidelines

| Deliverable | Target Length |
|-------------|---------------|
| Full Consumer Posture Profile | 1500-2000 words |
| Strategic Brief | 150-250 words |
| Individual section | 150-300 words |
| Prediction Test (each) | 30-50 words |
