# Jonathan Courtney — CEO Marketing Strategy

Jonathan Courtney is the CEO of AJ & Smart, a multi-seven-figure entrepreneur with 14 years of direct response marketing experience. His core framework — the **Promoter Blueprint** — diagnoses the #1 failure mode of AI-enabled solopreneurs: building without promoting. He provides a complete marketing system that positions AI tools as support infrastructure for the CEO's real job: generating traffic, warming audiences, and running selling events.
