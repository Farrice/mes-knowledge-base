# Implementation Pathway: Omar Eddaoudi (Module 1: Luxury Branding)

## 24-Hour Quickstart
Audit your current "Hero" offer. Remove all "Value/Discount" language. Increase the price by 20% and replace the feature list with a single, high-stakes story of the product in use by an authority figure.

## 7-Day Sprint
Implement "Controlled Distance." Change your lead capture from a "Sign Up" to an "Application." Add a 48-hour "Review Period" before they can buy. Use "Cold" visual assets (high contrast, low saturation).

## 30-Day Integration
Build the "Identity Mirror." Partner with or feature an individual who embodies the "Extreme Version" of your customer's ambition. Shift all marketing from "What this does for you" to "What this says about you."
