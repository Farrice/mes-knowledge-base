# Implementation Strategy: Tyler Denk

## Phase 1: 24-Hour Quickstart (Intelligence Capture)
1. **The Survey Map**: Map out the exact 3 data points your ideal B2B sponsor wishes they knew about your audience (e.g., job title, company size, tech stack).
2. **The Interception**: Create a Typeform or Tally survey and embed it as the *immediate* next step after a user confirms their email subscription.
3. **The Automated Nudge**: Set up a 3-day automated email that resends the survey link to anyone who didn't fill it out on day one.

## Phase 2: 7-Day Sprint (Zero-Cost Referral Architecture)
1. **Asset Audit**: Inventory all digital products, exclusive templates, or private community access you currently possess.
2. **The 3-Tier Design**:
   - Tier 1 (1-3 referrals): High utility (A template or guide).
   - Tier 2 (5-10 referrals): Status/Access (Database or archive access).
   - Tier 3 (25+ referrals): Exclusivity (1-on-1 call or inner-circle access).
3. **Implementation**: Build this structure into your newsletter platform (like Beehiiv) and add the promotional copy to the footer of every issue.

## Phase 3: 30-Day Integration (The BDE Growth Engine)
1. **The Tease-Drop-Trap Cadence**: Transition from simply "publishing" to "launching" content.
2. **Execution**: For your next major piece of content, execute the 3-part sequence: A 24-hour tease on social media, a 30% value drop on launch day, and a hard subscription gate for the remaining 70%.
3. **Founder Intimacy Delegation**: Document your exact voice and common responses. Train a VA to handle the first touchpoint of all new subscriber interactions based on this documented voice.
