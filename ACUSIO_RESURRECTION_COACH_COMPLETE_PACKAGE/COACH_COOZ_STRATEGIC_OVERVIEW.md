**Mitigation**: The Resurrection Lexicon (above) is a hard guardrail. Every piece of content must pass the test: *"Does this make the reader feel powerful and seen ‚Äî or shamed and small?"* Resurrection = reclamation, never shame.

### Risk 2: Premature $10K Pricing

**Risk**: Posting strong content for 2 weeks, then going silent for 3 weeks. LinkedIn rewards consistency.
**Mitigation**: The playbook has 20 posts ready to deploy. That's 4+ weeks of content with zero additional writing needed.

### Risk 4: Trying to Serve Everyone

**Risk**: Taking non-executive clients because revenue is tight, then diluting the brand.
**Mitigation**: The $3K validation clients MUST be executives. Even at a discounted price, the audience must match. The Resurrection brand only works if every story is an *executive* resurrection.

**Risk**: Using the elevated "Resurrection = dormant power" language but keeping the old gritty, gym-bro visual brand. The words say $10K but the visuals say $50/month.
**Mitigation**: Oren's "Private Club" visual refresh is essential. The mythic weight of "Resurrection" demands a sophisticated visual container. Navy, slate, cream. Composed power, not sweaty intensity.
