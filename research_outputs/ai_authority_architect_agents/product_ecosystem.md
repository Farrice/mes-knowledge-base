## Executive Summary

Built a 4-tier ascension model that leads from free content to premium service. Tech stack: ThriveCart for checkout/payments, Notion for client portal, Zoom for brain dumps. Minimal overhead, maximum leverage.

## Tech Stack Recommendation

### Payment & Delivery: ThriveCart
**Why ThriveCart**:
- One-time payment ($495-$690), no monthly fees
- Subscription billing built-in
- Tax compliance handled
- Bump orders for upsells
- Affiliate program (for referrals later)

**Alternative**: Stripe + manual invoicing (more work)

### Client Portal: Notion
**Why Notion**:
- Free for under 10 clients
- Content calendar visibility
- Shared docs, revision tracking
- Client onboarding checklist
- Template library

### Brain Dump Calls: Zoom
**Why Zoom**:
- Record + transcribe
- Familiar to clients
- AI summary integration

### AI Processing: Claude
**Why Claude**:
- Superior voice matching
- Longer context windows
- MES 3.0 integration
