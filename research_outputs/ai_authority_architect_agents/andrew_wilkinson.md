## Executive Summary

Applied the "ship fast, iterate faster" methodology. The 90-day sprint gets you from zero to 3 paying clients. Key insight: Perfect is the enemy of shipped. Launch "ugly" and refine based on real client feedback.

---

## The 90-Day Sprint

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)
**Goal**: Proof of concept with 1-2 beta clients

#### Week 1: MES 3.0 Formalization
- [ ] Document the 17 extraction questions
- [ ] Create brain dump protocol template
- [ ] Set up Notion client workspace template
- [ ] Test AI prompts for voice replication

**Time Budget**: 2-3 hours/day

#### Week 2: Beta Client Acquisition
- [ ] Reach out to 3-5 founders in network (personal asks)
- [ ] Offer free 90-day beta (in exchange for testimonial)
- [ ] Conduct first brain dump
- [ ] Generate first batch of content

**Time Budget**: 1-2 hours/day

#### Week 3: Iteration
- [ ] Get feedback on voice accuracy
- [ ] Refine extraction questions based on what works
- [ ] Document what's working vs. not
- [ ] Generate second batch for beta client

**Time Budget**: 2 hours/day

#### Week 4: Case Study Development
- [ ] Collect testimonial from beta client
- [ ] Create before/after voice comparison
- [ ] Document results (engagement, inbound)
- [ ] Write case study draft

**Time Budget**: 1-2 hours/day

---

### Phase 2: Content Engine (Days 31-60)
**Goal**: Build personal brand + 2 beta clients

#### Week 5-6: LinkedIn Content Ramp
- [ ] Post 5x/week minimum
- [ ] Share methodology insights
- [ ] Tell origin story
- [ ] Engage on ICP content

**Content Mix**:
- 3 educational posts (methodology)
- 1 story post (personal)
- 1 case study/testimonial

#### Week 7-8: Referral + Outreach
- [ ] Ask beta clients for referrals
- [ ] "Help first" outreach to 20 target founders
- [ ] Podcast pitch to 5 niche shows
- [ ] Follow up on all warm leads

---

### Phase 3: Client Acquisition (Days 61-90)
**Goal**: 3 paying clients at $2K+/month

#### Week 9-10: Sales Conversations
- [ ] Convert referrals + warm leads to calls
- [ ] Conduct discovery using NEPQ framework
- [ ] Present proposals
- [ ] Close first paying client

#### Week 11-12: Scale + Systematize
- [ ] Onboard paying clients
- [ ] Create repeatable onboarding process
- [ ] Document standard operating procedures
- [ ] Build waiting list for overflow

## What to Ship Ugly

Don't wait for perfect:

| Wait For | Ship Now |
|----------|----------|
| Perfect website | LinkedIn profile optimization |
| Full client portal | Google Doc + Notion share |
| Automated onboarding | Manual onboarding for first 3 |
| Custom ThriveCart setup | Stripe link or manual invoice |
| Polished methodology doc | 1-page process PDF |

---

## The First Client Checklist

Requirements for Client #1:
- [ ] 17-question brain dump protocol documented
- [ ] AI prompts for voice matching tested
- [ ] Notion template for client content
- [ ] Pricing structure decided (start at $2K)
- [ ] Contract template (simple scope + terms)
- [ ] Invoicing method (Stripe or ThriveCart)

---

## What to Postpone

Save for after Client #3:
- Full website
- Complex funnel
- Affiliate program
- Tier 4 offering
- Podcast appearances (beyond first 2-3)
- Course/digital product
