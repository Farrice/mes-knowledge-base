## Executive Summary

The LinkedIn ghostwriting market is fragmented across 4 distinct categories: Agencies, Solo Creators, Educator-Trainers, and AI Tools. No one owns the "Heart + Psychology" positioning. Most sell "growth" and "leads" (vanity metrics). The "Conscious Founder" niche is underserved.

---

| Competitor | Positioning | Price Range | Weakness |

| **Cleverly** | B2B LinkedIn lead gen + ghostwriting | $1,500-$3,500/mo | Generic content focus, lead-first not voice-first |
