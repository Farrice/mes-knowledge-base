### 1. Voice Capture Failure is the #1 Complaint
- "The primary mistake ghostwriters make with AI is **forgetting about voice**, leading to content that feels unauthentic" — Industry analysis

- Ghostwriters who don't train AI on client's specific thinking style produce generic work

### 2. "Robot" Content is Actively Rejected
- Forbes (April 2025): "Professionals using generative AI often trade time savings for a **robotic tone** that can be off-putting to customers"
- Common objection: "AI content feels robotic" — cited as top concern for executives
- 14 expert strategies now published just to make AI content "more natural"

### 3. Generic Content Commoditization
- Reddit r/copywriting: "Blog mill work declining due to AI making generic content much cheaper"
- Writers report: "Everything AI looks so similar" — lack of differentiation
- Content displaying experience, opinion, and first-person perspective now valued MORE as AI floods market

### 4. The Voice Training Gap
- Without tailored voice extraction, "even AI-assisted ghostwriting produces content clients feel didn't sound like me"
- Industry now emphasizing need to "train AI tools on client's specific writing and thinking style, values, and quirks"
- **This is EXACTLY what MES 3.0 solves** — systematic voice extraction
